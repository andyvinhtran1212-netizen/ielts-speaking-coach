This is a faithful simulation of the British Council / IDP computer-delivered Reading interface. The passage is on the left and the questions on the right; both panes scroll independently. Use the numbered bar at the bottom to move between questions, tick Review to flag a question (its marker becomes a circle), and use Settings to change text size or colours. The timer warns you at 10 and 5 minutes remaining; when it reaches zero the test is submitted automatically. After you finish you will get an automatic score, band estimate and Vietnamese explanations for every question.

How to use this test

Navigation. The bottom bar shows all 40 questions grouped by Part 1–3. Click a number to jump to it; use Previous / Next to step through. Answered questions turn white.

Review. Tick the Review box (lower-left) for the current question — its marker in the bar changes from a square to a circle so you can find it again.

Highlight & notes. Select text in the passage, then right-click and choose Highlight, Notes, or Clear. Keyboard: select text and press Alt+H to highlight or Alt+N to add a note.

Answering. Multiple choice = click an option. True/False/Not Given and matching = choose from the drop-down. Completion = type into the box (you may copy-paste from the passage).

Time. 60 minutes, counting down; it warns at 10 and 5 minutes and submits automatically at zero.
