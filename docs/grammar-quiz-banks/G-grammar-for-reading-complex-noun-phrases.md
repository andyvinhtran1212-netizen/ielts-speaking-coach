# ===== item_key 1 · Tìm danh từ trung tâm (head noun) =====

---
id: "cnp_head_b1"
type: "mcq"
input: "choice"
headword: "cnp-head-noun"
skill: "form"
subtype: "basic"
prompt: "Identify the head noun in: 'the recently discovered fossil fuel reserves beneath the Arctic ice'"
options: ["fossil", "fuel", "reserves", "ice"]
answer: 2
grammar_article_slug: "complex-noun-phrases"
explain: "Head noun là 'reserves' — đây là danh từ trung tâm mà mọi thứ khác bổ nghĩa cho. 'Fossil fuel' là modifier đứng trước; 'beneath the Arctic ice' là modifier đứng sau."

# ===== item_key 2 · Modifier trước (pre-modifier) và sau (post-modifier) =====

---
id: "cnp_mod_b1"
type: "mcq"
input: "choice"
headword: "cnp-premod-postmod"
skill: "form"
subtype: "basic"
prompt: "Which phrase is a post-modifier? In 'a large government research grant awarded last year'"
options: ["large", "government", "research", "awarded last year"]
answer: 3
grammar_article_slug: "complex-noun-phrases"
explain: "Pre-modifier: 'large', 'government', 'research' (đứng trước head noun 'grant'). Post-modifier: 'awarded last year' (đứng sau head noun, bổ nghĩa bằng mệnh đề)."
---
