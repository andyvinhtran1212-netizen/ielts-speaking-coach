# ===== item_key 1 · Tương phản (contrast): however, but, yet, in contrast, whereas =====

---

subtype: "basic"

options: ["the second idea opposes the first", "the second idea adds to the first", "the second idea explains the first", "both ideas are equally important"]
answer: 0
grammar_article_slug: "logical-connectors-in-reading"
