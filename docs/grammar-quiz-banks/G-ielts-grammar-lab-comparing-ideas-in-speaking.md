# ===== item_key 1 · Comparative forms (hơn kém) — mã lỗi: comparatives_error =====

---
id: "cis_comp_b1"
type: "mcq"

headword: "cis-comparative-forms"
skill: "form"
subtype: "basic"
prompt: "City life is ____ than village life in terms of cost."
options: ["expensive", "more expensive", "expensiver", "most expensive"]
answer: 1
grammar_article_slug: "comparing-ideas-in-speaking"
