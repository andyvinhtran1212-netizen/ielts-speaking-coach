# ===== item_key 1 · ", which" bình luận cuối câu (non-defining comment — Band 7+ device) =====

---
id: "rciw_com_b1"
type: "mcq"
input: "choice"
headword: "rciw-comment-which"
skill: "form"
subtype: "basic"
prompt: "Governments should subsidise public transport, ____ would reduce traffic congestion."
options: ["which", "who", "that", "what"]
answer: 0
grammar_article_slug: "relative-clauses-in-writing"
explain: "Sau dấu phẩy, dùng 'which' để bình luận về CẢ MỆNH ĐỀ TRƯỚC ('subsidise public transport' → hệ quả là giảm ùn tắc), không dùng 'that' vì 'that' không đứng sau dấu phẩy."

# ===== item_key 2 · Gộp hai câu ngắn bằng mệnh đề quan hệ (giảm lặp ý trong essay) =====

---
id: "rciw_cmb_b1"

skill: "form"
subtype: "basic"
prompt: "Gộp hai câu: 'Many students take part-time jobs. These jobs reduce their study time.' → câu nào ĐÚNG?"
options: ["Many students take part-time jobs, which reduce their study time.", "Many students take part-time jobs. Which reduce their study time.", "Many students take part-time jobs, these reduce their study time.", "Many students take part-time jobs, reduce their study time."]
answer: 0
grammar_article_slug: "relative-clauses-in-writing"
explain: "Gộp câu bằng dấu phẩy + 'which' thay cho 'these jobs' (tránh lặp danh từ) — biến hai câu ngắn thành một câu phức, nâng Grammatical Range."

---

answer: 0
