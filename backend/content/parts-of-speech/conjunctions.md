## Tóm tắt

Liên từ (Conjunction) là từ dùng để **nối** các thành phần trong câu — từ với từ, cụm từ với cụm từ, mệnh đề với mệnh đề. Không có liên từ, ta chỉ có thể viết những câu đơn ngắn, rời rạc.

- I like coffee **and** tea.
- She was tired **but** she kept studying.
- He stayed home **because** it was raining.

## Tại sao quan trọng

Liên từ là chìa khóa để tạo câu ghép (compound) và câu phức (complex). Trong IELTS, dùng đa dạng liên từ thể hiện khả năng sử dụng Grammatical Range and Accuracy, giúp điểm Writing và Speaking tốt hơn.

## Loại 1: Coordinating Conjunctions — Liên từ kết hợp

Nối hai thành phần **ngang hàng** (cùng loại: từ + từ, hoặc mệnh đề độc lập + mệnh đề độc lập):

**Từ gợi nhớ: FANBOYS**

| Liên từ | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|-------|
| **F**or | vì (lý do — khá trang trọng) | She left early, **for** she had a meeting. |
| **A**nd | và | I like coffee **and** tea. |
| **N**or | cũng không | He doesn't smoke, **nor** does he drink. |
| **B**ut | nhưng | She's young **but** experienced. |
| **O**r | hoặc | You can walk **or** take a taxi. |
| **Y**et | nhưng (dù vậy) | It was expensive, **yet** worth it. |
| **S**o | vì vậy | It rained, **so** we stayed inside. |

**Quan trọng:** Khi nối hai mệnh đề độc lập, đặt dấu phẩy trước coordinating conjunction:
- I was hungry, **so** I made a sandwich. ✅
- I was hungry **so** I made a sandwich. ✅ (cũng chấp nhận được)
- I was hungry **so**, I made a sandwich. ❌

## Loại 2: Subordinating Conjunctions — Liên từ phụ

Nối mệnh đề phụ (subordinate clause) với mệnh đề chính. Mệnh đề phụ **không đứng một mình được** — nó phụ thuộc vào mệnh đề chính.

### Chỉ Nguyên nhân / Lý do

| Liên từ | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|
| **because** | She stayed home **because** she was sick. |
| **since** | **Since** it was raining, we cancelled the trip. |
| **as** | **As** the traffic was heavy, I was late. |

### Chỉ Sự tương phản / Nhượng bộ

| Liên từ | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|
| **although / though / even though** | **Although** it was cold, she wore a T-shirt. |
| **while** | **While** some people prefer cities, others prefer rural life. |
| **whereas** | He likes jazz, **whereas** his sister prefers pop. |

> **Phân biệt:** "although" và "despite/in spite of" diễn đạt cùng ý, nhưng:
> - **although** + mệnh đề (S + V): **Although** it was cold...
> - **despite / in spite of** + cụm danh từ: **Despite** the cold weather...

### Chỉ Điều kiện

| Liên từ | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|
| **if** | **If** you study hard, you will pass. |
| **unless** | **Unless** you hurry, you'll be late. (= if not) |
| **provided / as long as** | **As long as** you practise daily, you'll improve. |
| **in case** | Take an umbrella **in case** it rains. |

### Chỉ Thời gian

| Liên từ | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|
| **when** | Call me **when** you arrive. |
| **while** | She studied **while** I cooked dinner. |
| **before / after** | Brush your teeth **before** you go to bed. |
| **until / till** | Wait **until** I get back. |
| **as soon as** | I'll call you **as soon as** I know. |
| **since** | I've lived here **since** I was a child. |
| **once** | **Once** you understand the grammar, it becomes easier. |

### Chỉ Mục đích

| Liên từ | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|
| **so that** | She practised daily **so that** she could improve. |
| **in order that** | He saved money **in order that** he could travel. |

### Chỉ Kết quả

| Liên từ | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|
| **so...that** | It was **so** cold **that** we couldn't go out. |
| **such...that** | It was **such** a cold day **that** we stayed inside. |

### Vị trí của mệnh đề phụ

Mệnh đề phụ có thể đứng **trước hoặc sau** mệnh đề chính:

- **Because** it was raining, we stayed inside. ✅ (đứng đầu → có dấu phẩy)
- We stayed inside **because** it was raining. ✅ (đứng sau → không cần dấu phẩy)

## Loại 3: Correlative Conjunctions — Cặp liên từ tương quan

Hai liên từ đi cặp với nhau:

| Cặp | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|-----|-------|-------|
| **both...and** | cả...lẫn | She speaks **both** English **and** French. |
| **either...or** | hoặc...hoặc | You can use **either** a pen **or** a pencil. |
| **neither...nor** | không...cũng không | He speaks **neither** Chinese **nor** Japanese. |
| **not only...but also** | không chỉ...mà còn | She's **not only** smart **but also** hardworking. |
| **whether...or** | dù...hay | **Whether** you like it **or** not, you must attend. |

**Quan trọng về chia số:** Với "either...or" và "neither...nor", chia động từ theo chủ ngữ gần nhất:
- **Either** the teacher **or** the students **are** responsible. (students → are)
- **Neither** the students **nor** the teacher **is** here. (teacher → is)

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Dùng "although" và "but" cùng lúc

- ❌ **Although** he was tired, **but** he kept working.
- ✅ **Although** he was tired, he kept working.
- ✅ He was tired, **but** he kept working.
- **Tại sao:** Một câu chỉ cần một liên từ tương phản. Dùng cả hai là thừa.

### Lỗi 2: Dùng "because" và "so" cùng lúc

- ❌ **Because** it was raining, **so** we stayed inside.
- ✅ **Because** it was raining, we stayed inside.
- ✅ It was raining, **so** we stayed inside.

### Lỗi 3: Câu mảnh (Fragment) — mệnh đề phụ đứng một mình

- ❌ Although she was tired. (không có mệnh đề chính)
- ✅ **Although** she was tired, she finished the work.
- ❌ Because I like music.
- ✅ **Because** I like music, I practise every day.

### Lỗi 4: Nhầm "despite" với "although"

- ❌ Despite she was tired, she kept going.
- ✅ **Despite** being tired, she kept going. (despite + -ing)
- ✅ **Despite** her tiredness, she kept going. (despite + danh từ)
- ✅ **Although** she was tired, she kept going. (although + mệnh đề)

### Lỗi 5: Nhầm "so" (vì vậy) và "so that" (để)

- ❌ She studied hard **so** she could pass. → nghĩa không rõ
- ✅ She studied hard **so that** she could pass. (mục đích → so that)
- ✅ She studied hard, **so** she passed. (kết quả → so)

## Ứng dụng trong IELTS

### Nối ý kiến trong Writing Task 2:

**Thêm ý:**
- Education is important, **and** it gives people opportunities to succeed.
- **Not only** does education improve career prospects, **but it also** develops critical thinking.

**Tương phản:**
- **Although** technology has many benefits, it also has drawbacks.
- Some people prefer city life, **whereas** others value the peace of rural areas.

**Nguyên nhân:**
- Many young people move to cities **because** they are looking for better job opportunities.
- **Since** the internet became widely available, people's lives have changed dramatically.

**Điều kiện:**
- **If** governments invested more in education, inequality would decrease.
- Urbanisation will continue **unless** rural areas receive more support.

### Trong IELTS Speaking:

- I enjoy travelling **and** learning about different cultures.
- **Although** I was nervous at first, I gradually became more confident.
- I chose this career **because** I've always been passionate about it.

## Bài tập luyện

### Điền liên từ phù hợp

1. She was cold, ___ she put on a coat. (kết quả)
2. ___ he studied hard, he failed the exam. (tương phản)
3. You'll miss the bus ___ you leave now. (điều kiện)
4. I haven't spoken to her ___ we graduated. (thời gian)
5. She bought a dictionary ___ she could look up words easily. (mục đích)

### Sửa lỗi

1. Although it was a long journey, but we enjoyed it.
2. Because he was late, so the teacher was angry.
3. Despite she had a headache, she went to work.
4. He's not only intelligent but also works hard.
5. I'll call you when I will arrive.

### Đáp án

**Điền liên từ:**
1. so | 2. Although / Even though | 3. unless / if you don't | 4. since | 5. so that

**Sửa lỗi:**
1. Although it was a long journey, we enjoyed it. (bỏ "but")
2. Because he was late, the teacher was angry. (bỏ "so") — hoặc: He was late, so the teacher was angry. (bỏ "because")
3. **Despite** having a headache, she went to work. — hoặc: **Although** she had a headache...
4. He's not only intelligent **but also** hardworking. (not only...but also + cùng cấu trúc ngữ pháp)
5. I'll call you when I **arrive**. (sau when chỉ thời gian → hiện tại, không phải tương lai)

## Tóm tắt nhanh

**Coordinating (FANBOYS):** for, and, nor, but, or, yet, so — nối hai thành phần ngang hàng

**Subordinating:** nối mệnh đề phụ với mệnh đề chính
- Nguyên nhân: because, since, as
- Tương phản: although, though, even though, while, whereas
- Điều kiện: if, unless, provided, as long as
- Thời gian: when, while, before, after, until, as soon as, since

**Correlative:** both...and | either...or | neither...nor | not only...but also

**Bẫy phổ biến:**
- Không dùng "although" + "but" cùng lúc
- Không dùng "because" + "so" cùng lúc
- Mệnh đề phụ không đứng một mình
- despite + noun/-ing (không + mệnh đề)
- Sau when/if (chỉ thời gian tương lai) → dùng present simple
