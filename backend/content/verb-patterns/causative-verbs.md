## Tóm tắt

Causative verbs diễn tả việc **A khiến / nhờ / cho phép B làm gì**.

| Causative | Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| **have** | have + O + bare inf / V3 | nhờ ai làm / để gì bị làm |
| **get** | get + O + to-inf / V3 | thuyết phục / để gì bị làm |
| **make** | make + O + bare inf | bắt buộc ai phải làm |
| **let** | let + O + bare inf | cho phép ai làm |
| **help** | help + O + (to) inf | giúp ai làm |

## Tại sao quan trọng

Causative structures thường xuất hiện trong IELTS khi:
- Thảo luận về chính sách: *"The government should make people..."*
- Giáo dục: *"Teachers let students..."*
- Cuộc sống hằng ngày: *"I got my car repaired."*
- Phân tích trách nhiệm: *"Parents should not let children..."*

Dùng đúng causative structures thể hiện Grammatical Range in Band 7+.

## Causative HAVE

### 1. have + Object + bare infinitive — Nhờ ai làm gì

```
Subject + have + Object (person) + bare infinitive
```

A sắp xếp, chỉ đạo để B làm gì cho A.

- I **had** the plumber **fix** the leak. (Tôi nhờ thợ ống nước sửa)
- She **had** her assistant **prepare** the report.
- He **has** his driver **pick** him up every morning.
- The manager **had** everyone **stay** late on Friday.

### 2. have + Object + past participle (V3) — Để gì được làm

```
Subject + have + Object (thing) + past participle (V3)
```

Nhấn mạnh vào kết quả — vật được làm (không đề cập ai làm).

- I **had** my car **serviced** last week. (xe được bảo dưỡng)
- She **had** her hair **cut** and **dyed**.
- We **had** the house **painted** before the event.
- He **had** his laptop **repaired** at the shop.

> **Phân biệt:**
> - **have sb do sth** = nhờ người cụ thể làm
> - **have sth done** = để vật được làm (không quan tâm ai làm)

## Causative GET

### 1. get + Object + to-infinitive — Thuyết phục / mất công nhờ

```
Subject + get + Object (person) + to-infinitive
```

Nghĩa tương tự "have sb do sth" nhưng ngụ ý **phải cố gắng, thuyết phục** hơn.

- I **got** my brother **to help** me move. (thuyết phục anh ấy giúp)
- She finally **got** the manager **to approve** the budget.
- He **got** his kids **to clean** their rooms.

### 2. get + Object + past participle — Để gì được làm (thường informal)

```
Subject + get + Object (thing) + past participle
```

- I need to **get** my glasses **fixed**. (nhờ sửa kính)
- She **got** her nails **done** at the salon.
- He **got** his phone **replaced** under warranty.

> **Have vs Get + sth done:**
> - Cả hai đều diễn đạt "để vật được làm"
> - **have** = trung tính, cả formal và informal
> - **get** = informal hơn, thường trong spoken English

## Causative MAKE

### make + Object + bare infinitive — Bắt buộc, cưỡng ép

```
Subject + make + Object + bare infinitive
```

Nghĩa mạnh hơn: **bắt buộc, không có lựa chọn**.

- The teacher **made** the students **rewrite** their essays.
- The noise **made** me **wake** up at 3 am.
- Peer pressure **makes** teenagers **follow** trends.
- The law **makes** drivers **wear** seat belts.

> **Lưu ý:** Trong passive voice, "make" dùng **to-infinitive**:
> - Active: She **made** him **apologise**.
> - Passive: He **was made to apologise** (by her).

## Causative LET

### let + Object + bare infinitive — Cho phép

```
Subject + let + Object + bare infinitive
```

Cho phép ai làm gì — không ngăn cản.

- She **let** her children **stay up** late on weekends.
- The teacher **let** students **use** dictionaries during the test.
- Don't **let** this opportunity **slip** away.
- Parents should **let** children **make** their own decisions.

> **let vs allow:**
> - **let + bare inf:** Let me know. (informal)
> - **allow + to-inf:** He was allowed to leave. (formal)

## Causative HELP

### help + Object + (to) infinitive

```
Subject + help + Object + (to-)infinitive
```

Cả bare infinitive và to-infinitive đều chấp nhận được, nhưng bare infinitive nghe tự nhiên hơn trong spoken English.

- She **helped** her friend **(to) find** a job.
- Technology **helps** students **learn** more effectively.
- Exercise **helps** people **maintain** a healthy weight.
- Can you **help** me **(to) solve** this problem?

## So sánh toàn bộ causative structures

| Causative | Dạng V theo sau | Ý nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|-----------|----------------|---------|-------|
| **make** | bare infinitive | Bắt buộc, cưỡng ép | She made him apologise. |
| **let** | bare infinitive | Cho phép | He let her leave early. |
| **have** (người) | bare infinitive | Nhờ / chỉ đạo | I had him fix it. |
| **have** (vật) | V3 | Để được làm | I had it fixed. |
| **get** (người) | to-infinitive | Thuyết phục | She got him to help. |
| **get** (vật) | V3 | Để được làm | I got it repaired. |
| **help** | (to) infinitive | Giúp | She helped me (to) study. |

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Dùng "to" sau make và let

- ❌ The teacher made him **to** stay after class.
- ✅ The teacher **made** him **stay** after class.
- ❌ She let me **to** borrow her book.
- ✅ She **let** me **borrow** her book.

### Lỗi 2: Nhầm have sth done với have sb do sth

- ❌ I had the mechanic fixed my car. (sai — cần bare inf sau person)
- ✅ I had the mechanic **fix** my car. (have + person + bare inf)
- ❌ I had my car fix. (sai — cần V3 sau thing)
- ✅ I had my car **fixed**. (have + thing + V3)

### Lỗi 3: Quên passive form của make trong câu bị động

- ❌ He was made apologise.
- ✅ He was made **to** apologise. (passive → to-infinitive)

### Lỗi 4: Nhầm let và make

- ❌ My parents **let** me study every day. (không ai "bắt" học)
- ✅ My parents **made** me study every day. (bắt buộc)
- ❌ She **made** her daughter wear anything she wanted. (không cưỡng ép)
- ✅ She **let** her daughter wear anything she wanted. (cho phép)

## Ứng dụng trong IELTS

### Writing Task 2 — Thảo luận chính sách:

- "Governments should **make** corporations **pay** higher environmental taxes."
- "Schools should **let** students **choose** their own subjects to a greater extent."
- "Parents who **let** children **spend** unlimited time on screens are doing them a disservice."
- "Technology **helps** people **access** information that was previously unavailable."

### Writing Task 2 — Nguyên nhân-kết quả:

- "Urbanisation **has made** many people **abandon** traditional lifestyles."
- "The media **makes** consumers **believe** they need things they don't."
- "Social pressure **gets** teenagers **to conform** even when they disagree."

### Speaking Part 3 — Thảo luận:

- "I think parents should **let** children **make** some of their own decisions from an early age."
- "Strict teachers who **make** students **memorise** everything don't encourage creativity."
- "I had my essay **checked** by a native speaker before submitting it."

### Speaking Part 2 — Kể chuyện:

- "My manager **had** us **work** overtime to meet the deadline."
- "My teacher **got** me **to rewrite** the essay from scratch — it was painful but helpful."

## Bài tập luyện

### Điền dạng động từ đúng

1. She made her son ___ (clean) his room before dinner.
2. I need to get my laptop ___ (repair) — it keeps crashing.
3. The doctor let the patient ___ (leave) after two days.
4. Can you help me ___ (carry) these boxes?
5. She had the photographer ___ (take) photos at the event.

### Sửa lỗi

1. He made his employees to work on weekends.
2. I had my car fix yesterday.
3. She got her friend helped her move.
4. Let him to use your phone for a moment.
5. He was made apologise publicly.

### Đáp án

**Dạng động từ:**
1. clean | 2. repaired | 3. leave | 4. (to) carry | 5. take

**Sửa lỗi:**
1. He made his employees **work** on weekends. (make + bare inf)
2. I had my car **fixed** yesterday. (have + thing + V3)
3. She got her friend **to help** her move. (get + person + to-inf)
4. Let him **use** your phone. (let + bare inf)
5. He was made **to** apologise publicly. (passive make → to-inf)

## Tóm tắt nhanh

| Muốn nói | Dùng | Cấu trúc |
|----------|------|---------|
| Bắt buộc | make | make + O + bare inf |
| Cho phép | let | let + O + bare inf |
| Nhờ (người) | have | have + O + bare inf |
| Để (vật) được làm | have/get | have/get + O + V3 |
| Thuyết phục | get | get + O + to-inf |
| Giúp đỡ | help | help + O + (to) inf |

**Quy tắc vàng:**
- **make / let / have (người)** → bare infinitive (không "to")
- **get (người)** → to-infinitive
- **have / get (vật)** → past participle (V3)
- Passive của make: **was/were made to + inf** (thêm "to")
