## Tóm tắt

Bốn cách nói về tương lai — khác nhau về mức độ chắc chắn và loại kế hoạch:

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Mức chắc chắn | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|--------------|-------|
| **will + V** | Quyết định ngay lúc nói / Dự đoán | Trung bình–Cao | I'll probably move abroad. |
| **be going to + V** | Kế hoạch đã có sẵn / Dự đoán có bằng chứng | Cao | I'm going to apply next year. |
| **Present Continuous** | Sắp xếp cụ thể đã được định | Rất cao | I'm starting work on Monday. |
| **hope/plan/intend to** | Ý định, mong muốn, dự định | Thấp–Trung bình | I hope to study abroad. |

---

## Tại sao quan trọng

Trong IELTS Speaking, câu hỏi về tương lai rất phổ biến:
- Part 1: "What do you plan to do after you finish studying?"
- Part 1: "Do you think your hometown will change in the future?"
- Part 2: "Describe something you would like to do in the future."
- Part 3: "How do you think education will change in the next 20 years?"

Dùng "will" cho mọi tình huống là lỗi phổ biến. Biết phân biệt và phối hợp các cấu trúc tương lai thể hiện **Grammatical Range** — một trong bốn tiêu chí IELTS Speaking.

---

## PHẦN 1: WILL + V — Quyết định, dự đoán, lời hứa

### 1a. Quyết định tức thì (spontaneous decision)

"Will" dùng khi bạn quyết định ngay tại thời điểm nói — không phải kế hoạch có sẵn từ trước.

- "That question is interesting — **I'll think** about it more carefully."
- "**I'll say** that travel is probably the most transformative experience you can have."
- "**I'll try** to answer that from a personal perspective."

> **Lưu ý:** Trong IELTS Speaking, bạn không cần chuẩn bị trước câu trả lời — vì vậy "will" xuất hiện tự nhiên khi bạn đang suy nghĩ và quyết định ngay.

### 1b. Dự đoán (prediction)

"Will" diễn đạt dự đoán về tương lai — thường kèm with adverbs như probably, certainly, likely.

```
will + probably / certainly / definitely + V
won't + V (dự đoán tiêu cực)
```

- "I think remote work **will become** the norm within the next decade."
- "Technology **will probably change** education dramatically in the coming years."
- "Cities **will certainly** face more pressure as the population grows."
- "I don't think traditional shopping malls **will survive** much longer."
- "Young people **will need** to adapt constantly as the job market evolves."

### 1c. Lời hứa, đề nghị, lời mời

- "**I'll** try my best to explain this clearly."
- "**I'll** give you a personal example to illustrate my point."

---

## PHẦN 2: BE GOING TO + V — Kế hoạch đã có, dự đoán hiển nhiên

### 2a. Kế hoạch đã có từ trước (prior intention)

"Be going to" dùng cho kế hoạch **đã quyết định trước thời điểm nói** — bạn đã có ý định rõ ràng.

```
am/is/are + going to + V (base form)
```

- "**I'm going to apply** for a master's degree next year — I've already researched the programmes."
- "**I'm going to start** my own business once I have enough experience."
- "**I'm going to move** to a bigger city after graduation."
- "She **is going to study** medicine — she's wanted to be a doctor since childhood."

### 2b. Dự đoán dựa trên bằng chứng hiện tại

Khi bạn thấy có bằng chứng rõ ràng chứng tỏ điều gì đó sắp xảy ra:

- "Based on current trends, the environment **is going to** face even more serious challenges."
- "With so much investment in AI, the job market **is going to** change significantly."
- "If governments don't act now, the problem **is going to** get much worse."

---

## PHẦN 3: PRESENT CONTINUOUS — Sắp xếp cụ thể đã định

Present Continuous dùng cho kế hoạch **đã được sắp xếp cụ thể** — có ngày giờ, địa điểm hoặc sự xác nhận từ người khác.

```
am/is/are + V-ing
```

- "**I'm starting** a new job next month." (đã confirm với nhà tuyển dụng)
- "**I'm meeting** my supervisor on Friday to discuss my research proposal."
- "**I'm travelling** to Japan in the summer — I've already booked the flights."
- "**We're moving** to a new apartment in June."

> **So sánh be going to vs Present Continuous:**
> - "I'**m going to** look for a new job." (ý định, chưa có cụ thể)
> - "I'**m starting** a new job on Monday." (đã sắp xếp cụ thể)

---

## PHẦN 4: HOPE / PLAN / INTEND / WOULD LIKE TO — Ý định và mong muốn

Đây là nhóm cấu trúc diễn đạt **ý định, mong muốn, hoặc dự định** — thường dùng khi bạn chưa hoàn toàn chắc chắn.

### hope to + V (mong đợi, không chắc hoàn toàn)

- "**I hope to** study abroad in the next two or three years."
- "**I hope to** become a specialist in environmental law."
- "**I'm hoping to** find a position in a company that values work-life balance."

### plan to + V (có kế hoạch cụ thể hơn)

- "**I plan to** pursue a postgraduate degree after working for a few years."
- "**I'm planning to** set up my own practice once I qualify."
- "The government **plans to** invest more in renewable energy infrastructure."

### intend to + V (quyết tâm, mục đích rõ)

- "**I intend to** keep developing my English even after I finish this course."
- "**I intend to** build a career in public health — it's something I feel strongly about."

### would like to / would love to + V (mong muốn, ước nguyện)

- "**I would like to** travel more extensively before settling down."
- "**I'd love to** live abroad for a year or two — just to experience a different culture."
- "**I would like to** eventually work in a role where I can make a real social impact."

### expect to + V (dự kiến, có cơ sở để tin)

- "**I expect** the situation **to** improve once new policies are implemented."
- "Economists **expect** inflation **to** fall next year."

## PHẦN 5: THÊM ĐỘ TỰ NHIÊN — Hedging future plans

Câu trả lời về tương lai nghe tự nhiên hơn khi có **mức độ chắc chắn phù hợp** thay vì cam đoan tuyệt đối:

```
I'm fairly certain that I'll...
I think I'll probably...
It's quite likely that I'll...
I'm not sure exactly, but I think...
That's a tough question — I suppose I'll...
```

- "**I think I'll probably** stay in my current field, but I'd like to explore some new areas."
- "**I'm fairly certain** I'm going to continue studying — I really enjoy learning."
- "**I'm not entirely sure**, but I'd say I'm **most likely going to** pursue further qualifications."
- "**It's quite likely that** I'll move back to my hometown at some point, though not in the immediate future."

## Mẫu câu trả lời Speaking

### Part 1: "What are your plans after you finish your studies?"

> "Well, **I'm planning to** work for a couple of years first before going back to study. **I'd like to** gain some practical experience in my field before committing to a master's degree. **I'm actually going to** start applying for graduate positions quite soon — I've already done some research into which companies I want to target. In the longer term, **I hope to** eventually manage my own team, though **I think** that **will probably** take five or six years to achieve."

### Part 3: "Do you think the way people work will change in the future?"

> "Yes, **I think** it **will change** dramatically. **Remote work will probably** become the default for most knowledge workers — the pandemic showed that it's perfectly possible. **I think** AI **is going to** take over many routine tasks, which **will mean** people **will need to** focus on more creative and interpersonal skills. **I expect** this transition **to** happen gradually, but **I wouldn't be surprised** if the traditional nine-to-five office job **becomes** quite rare within the next generation."

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Dùng "will" cho kế hoạch đã có sẵn

- ❌ "I **will apply** for university next year." (nghe như quyết định ngay lúc này)
- ✅ "**I'm going to apply** for university next year." (kế hoạch đã có)

### Lỗi 2: Dùng "going to" cho dự đoán về xã hội nói chung

- ❌ "Technology **is going to change** how we communicate." (không có bằng chứng cụ thể rõ ràng)
- ✅ "Technology **will change** how we communicate." (dự đoán chung → will)
- Cả hai đều đúng ngữ pháp nhưng "will" tự nhiên hơn cho dự đoán tổng quát.

### Lỗi 3: "Will" + "ing" (không tồn tại)

- ❌ "I will going to study."
- ✅ "I **am going to** study." / "I **will** study."

### Lỗi 4: Quên chia "be" trong "be going to"

- ❌ "I going to travel next year."
- ✅ "**I'm** going to travel next year."

### Lỗi 5: "Hope" + will (redundant)

- ❌ "I hope I will to pass." / "I hope I'll to pass."
- ✅ "I hope **to** pass." / "I hope **I'll** pass."
- "hope to" + V (base) / "hope" + that + clause (với will)

## Bài tập luyện

### Chọn cấu trúc phù hợp

1. (Spontaneous decision) "This question is very interesting — ___ give you a personal example."
2. (Prior plan, not yet confirmed) "After I graduate, ___ look for a job overseas."
3. (Arranged, confirmed) "I can't meet tomorrow — ___ see my supervisor at 2pm."
4. (Hope/wish, not certain) "I ___ travel the world someday."
5. (Prediction, general) "I think AI ___ change most industries within the next decade."

### Gợi ý đáp án

1. **I'll** give you a personal example.
2. **I'm going to** look for a job overseas.
3. **I'm seeing** my supervisor at 2pm.
4. **I hope to** travel the world someday. / **I'd love to** travel...
5. AI **will** change most industries. / AI **is going to** change... (cả hai đều đúng)

## Tóm tắt nhanh

```
WILL + V:
  → Quyết định ngay lúc nói: "I'll say that..."
  → Dự đoán chung: "Cities will grow..."
  → Probably/certainly/definitely: "I'll probably..."

BE GOING TO + V:
  → Kế hoạch đã quyết định trước: "I'm going to apply..."
  → Dự đoán có bằng chứng: "It's going to rain." (thấy mây đen)

PRESENT CONTINUOUS:
  → Sắp xếp đã định sẵn: "I'm starting on Monday."

HOPE/PLAN/INTEND/WOULD LIKE TO:
  → Ý định và mong muốn:
    hope to = muốn nhưng không chắc
    plan to = có kế hoạch cụ thể hơn
    intend to = quyết tâm
    would like to = mong muốn lịch sự
    expect to = dự kiến có cơ sở

CHIẾN LƯỢC IELTS SPEAKING:
  → Kết hợp ít nhất 2 cấu trúc tương lai khác nhau
  → Thêm hedging (probably, fairly certain, I think) để nghe tự nhiên
  → Tránh "will" cho tất cả mọi thứ
```
