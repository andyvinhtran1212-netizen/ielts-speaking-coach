## Tóm tắt

| Cấu trúc | Dùng khi |
|----------|---------|
| **the highest / lowest** | So sánh nhất với số cụ thể hoặc trong nhóm |
| **the most / least** | Với tính từ/trạng từ |
| **topped / led** | Đứng đầu, vị trí cao nhất |
| **ranked first / last** | Thứ hạng chính thức |
| **the peak was / reached a peak of** | Giá trị cao nhất trên timeline |
| **the lowest point was / fell to a low of** | Giá trị thấp nhất trên timeline |
| **by far the [superlative]** | Nhấn mạnh khoảng cách rõ ràng |

---

## Tại sao quan trọng

Mô tả dữ liệu tốt không chỉ là đọc số — mà là **diễn giải vị trí tương đối** của các giá trị: cái gì nhiều nhất, cái gì ít nhất, vị trí thứ mấy. Biết dùng đa dạng cấu trúc cho extremes và rankings giúp tránh lặp từ và tăng điểm Lexical Resource.

---

## PHẦN 1: THE HIGHEST / LOWEST — Superlative cơ bản

```
[Subject] had the highest / lowest [noun] (of all / among all / in [year]).
[Subject] recorded the highest / lowest figure at [value].
[Subject] had by far the highest / lowest [noun].
```

**Ví dụ:**

- "**The USA had the highest** level of car ownership at 850 vehicles per 1,000 people."
- "**Bangladesh recorded the lowest** average income among the countries shown."
- "**China had by far the largest** share of global manufacturing output, accounting for nearly 30%."
- "**The UK had the lowest** unemployment rate in 2019, standing at just 3.8%."

> "By far" thêm vào trước superlative để nhấn mạnh khoảng cách đáng kể: "by far the highest", "by far the most popular".

---

## PHẦN 2: TOP / LEAD / RANK — Diễn đạt thứ hạng

### Topped / led the list:

```
[Subject] topped the list / the rankings with [value].
[Subject] led all other [categories/countries] with [value].
[Subject] was the top [performer/category] with [value].
```

- "**Japan topped the list** for life expectancy, with an average of 84 years."
- "**The USA led all other nations** in terms of defence spending."
- "**Coffee was the top-selling beverage**, with sales reaching 2.4 billion units."

### Ranked first / second / last:

```
[Subject] ranked first / second / third among [group].
[Subject] came first / last in [category].
[Subject] was ranked [position] out of [total] countries / categories.
```

- "**Germany ranked first** in terms of renewable energy adoption among EU nations."
- "**Iceland came last** in terms of population density among the countries surveyed."
- "**Canada was ranked third** out of eight countries in the index."

## PHẦN 3: PEAK AND TROUGH — Giá trị cao nhất và thấp nhất theo thời gian

Dùng khi mô tả line chart — giá trị cao nhất (peak) hoặc thấp nhất (trough) tại một thời điểm.

### Diễn đạt peak (đỉnh):

```
[Subject] peaked at [value] in [year].
[Subject] reached a peak / high of [value] in [year].
[Subject] hit a peak of [value] before declining.
The peak [figure/value] was [value], recorded in [year].
```

- "**Oil prices peaked at** approximately $140 per barrel in 2008 before falling sharply."
- "**Visitor numbers reached a high of** 4.2 million in 2015."
- "**The peak figure was** recorded in 2010, when exports reached $500 billion."

### Diễn đạt trough (đáy):

```
[Subject] fell to a low of / reached its lowest point at [value] in [year].
[Subject] hit a trough / bottomed out at [value] in [year].
The lowest figure was [value], recorded in [year].
```

- "**Unemployment fell to a low of** 4.1% in 2019, before rising again during the pandemic."
- "**Share prices hit a trough of** $12 in March 2020."
- "**The lowest figure was** recorded in 2002, when only 15% of households had internet access."

---

## PHẦN 4: MOST / LEAST — Với tính từ và trạng từ

```
[Subject] was the most / least [adjective] (of all / among all).
[Subject] grew the most / least rapidly.
[Subject] was the most / least frequently [verb-ed].
```

- "**Shopping was the most commonly cited** reason for internet use, followed by email."
- "**The elderly were the least likely** to use social media regularly."
- "**Transport costs grew the most rapidly** of all household expenditure categories."

## PHẦN 5: KẾT HỢP TRONG MÔ TẢ ĐOẠN — TRÁNH LẶP

### Ví dụ đoạn mô tả bar chart:

> "Among the five countries shown, **the USA had by far the highest** level of per capita energy consumption at 310 GJ annually. **Canada ranked second**, with consumption **reaching** 270 GJ. At the other end of the scale, **India recorded the lowest figure** at just 25 GJ, **well below** the global average. **Japan and Germany came third and fourth** respectively, with broadly similar consumption levels of around 170 and 150 GJ."

---

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Dùng "most highest" hoặc "most lowest"

- ❌ "The most highest figure was recorded in 2020."
- ✅ "**The highest** figure was recorded in 2020."

### Lỗi 2: Không có số liệu kèm theo superlative

- ❌ "Japan had the highest rate." *(thiếu số cụ thể)*
- ✅ "Japan had the highest rate **at 84 years** / **of approximately 84 years**."

### Lỗi 3: Nhầm "peaked" với "peaked at"

- ❌ "Oil prices peaked $140." *(thiếu "at")*
- ✅ "Oil prices **peaked at** $140."

### Lỗi 4: Lặp "the highest/lowest" trong cả đoạn

- ❌ "The USA had the highest... Japan had the second highest... Germany had the third highest..."
- ✅ Xen kẽ: "topped the list" → "ranked second" → "came third" → "recorded the lowest" → "fell to a low of"

---

## Bài tập luyện

### Viết lại bằng cấu trúc được chỉ định:

1. "China was the first country in car production." → Dùng **topped the list**
2. "Australia had the most expensive housing." → Dùng **by far the highest**
3. "In 2008, oil prices were at their highest, $140." → Dùng **peaked at**
4. "In 2020, tourism was at its lowest." → Dùng **fell to a low of / hit a trough**

### Đáp án:

1. "China **topped the list** for car production."
2. "Australia had **by far the highest** housing costs among the countries shown."
3. "Oil prices **peaked at** $140 in 2008."
4. "Tourism figures **hit a trough** in 2020, falling to their lowest recorded level."

## Tóm tắt nhanh

```
VỊ TRÍ CAO NHẤT:
  → "had the highest [noun]"
  → "topped the list / the rankings with [value]"
  → "ranked first / came first among [group]"
  → "peaked at [value] in [year]"
  → "by far the highest" (khoảng cách rõ)

VỊ TRÍ THẤP NHẤT:
  → "had the lowest [noun]"
  → "came last / ranked last"
  → "fell to a low of [value] in [year]"
  → "recorded the lowest figure at [value]"

VỊ TRÍ GIỮA:
  → "ranked second / third"
  → "came second among [group]"
  → "was closely followed by [category]"

TRÁNH: most highest / most lowest / lặp "the highest" nhiều lần
```
