## Tóm tắt

| Loại | Cấu trúc | Khi dùng trong Speaking |
|------|---------|------------------------|
| **Zero conditional** | If + present, present | Sự thật chung, quy luật |
| **First conditional** | If + present, will | Khả năng thực tế trong tương lai |
| **Second conditional** | If + past, would | Giả định, không có thật hiện tại |
| **Third conditional** | If + past perfect, would have | Giả định về quá khứ đã xảy ra |
| **Mixed conditional** | If + past perfect, would | Hệ quả hiện tại của quá khứ |

---

## Tại sao quan trọng

Người học ở Band 5-6 thường chỉ dùng zero hoặc first conditional. Band 7+ đặc trưng bởi:
- Second conditional để **speculate** (suy đoán giả định)
- Third conditional để **reflect on past situations** (nhìn lại điều đã không xảy ra)
- Mixed conditionals để **link past and present**
- "If" trong câu hedging và tư duy phân tích

Examiner chú ý đến việc bạn có thể **chuyển đổi linh hoạt** giữa các loại conditional.

---

## PHẦN 1: ZERO VÀ FIRST CONDITIONAL — Nền tảng

### Zero conditional — sự thật, quy luật

```
If + present simple, present simple
```

Dùng khi nói về **sự thật luôn đúng** hoặc **quy luật chung**:

- **If** you don't sleep enough, your concentration **drops**.
- **If** a country invests in education, productivity **tends to increase**.
- **If** young people lack job opportunities, social problems **inevitably follow**.

> Trong Speaking Part 3 khi generalise về xã hội, zero conditional nghe rất tự nhiên.

### First conditional — khả năng thực tế

```
If + present simple, will / can / may + verb
```

Dùng khi nói về điều **có thể xảy ra trong tương lai**:

- **If** the government invests more in public transport, traffic congestion **will decrease** significantly.
- **If** students develop strong reading habits early, they **can** achieve much better academic results.
- **If** we don't act on climate change now, the consequences **may be** irreversible.

---

## PHẦN 2: SECOND CONDITIONAL — Giả định hiện tại

```
If + past simple, would / could / might + verb
```

Đây là loại conditional quan trọng nhất cho IELTS Speaking Part 2 và Part 3.

### Dùng để speculate (suy đoán, giả định):

- **If** I had more free time, I **would** probably take up a new hobby like photography.
- **If** the government banned private cars in city centres, people **would** rely more on public transport.
- **If** everyone had access to quality education, social inequality **would** be much less severe.

### Dùng để trả lời câu hỏi "What would you do if...?":

Câu hỏi dạng này rất phổ biến trong Part 2 và Part 3:

- "What would you do if you had a year off work?"
  → **If I had** a year off, I **would** travel through Southeast Asia and try to learn a new language. I **might** also volunteer with an environmental organisation, which is something I've always wanted to do.

- "If you could change one thing about your city, what would it be?"
  → **If I could** change one thing, I **would** invest heavily in green spaces. I think **if** there were more parks and pedestrian areas, people **would** be both healthier and happier.

### Dùng để đưa ra đề xuất/kiến nghị:

- **If** more companies **offered** flexible working hours, employee wellbeing **would improve** considerably.
- **If** the school curriculum **included** financial literacy, young people **would** make better decisions as adults.

---

## PHẦN 3: THIRD CONDITIONAL — Nhìn lại quá khứ

```
If + past perfect, would have / could have / might have + past participle
```

Dùng khi nói về điều **đã không xảy ra** — "nếu ngày đó tôi đã... thì..."

### Trong Part 2 — kể chuyện, reflect:

- **If** I **had studied** harder during secondary school, I **might have** been accepted into a better university.
- **If** I **had** taken that job opportunity, my career **would have** developed very differently.
- She told me that **if** she **had known** about the scholarship, she **would have** applied.

### Trong Part 3 — phân tích vấn đề xã hội:

- **If** governments **had invested** in renewable energy twenty years ago, we **would not have** such a serious climate crisis today.
- **If** more attention **had been paid** to mental health in schools, many problems **could have been** prevented.

> Third conditional trong Part 3 thể hiện khả năng **critical thinking** — phân tích nguyên nhân và hệ quả lịch sử.

---

## PHẦN 4: MIXED CONDITIONAL — Liên kết quá khứ và hiện tại

Mixed conditional dùng khi **nguyên nhân ở quá khứ nhưng hệ quả ảnh hưởng đến hiện tại**:

```
If + past perfect (quá khứ hoàn thành), would + verb (hiện tại)
```

- **If** I **had chosen** a different career path, I **would** probably be doing something completely different now.
- **If** the government **had implemented** stricter environmental policies earlier, we **would** be in a much better position today.
- **If** she **hadn't worked** so hard in her early career, she **wouldn't be** where she is now.

Và ngược lại — **tình huống hiện tại → hệ quả trong quá khứ**:

```
If + past simple (hiện tại giả định), would have + past participle (quá khứ)
```

- **If** I **were** more ambitious by nature, I **would have** pursued a senior management role years ago.
- **If** he **weren't** so cautious, he **would have** taken that investment opportunity.

## PHẦN 5: "IF" TRONG HEDGING VÀ TƯ DUY PHÂN TÍCH

Ngoài câu điều kiện truyền thống, "if" còn dùng để hedge và phân tích:

### If anything — để điều chỉnh ý kiến:

- The situation has, **if anything**, become worse over the past decade.
- This policy **if anything** highlights the need for more comprehensive reform.

### If you ask me — ý kiến cá nhân:

- **If you ask me**, the real problem lies in a lack of investment in education.
- **If you ask me**, working from home is only sustainable with the right infrastructure.

### Even if — nhượng bộ:

- **Even if** this policy were implemented, I'm not sure it would be effective without public support.
- I'd still support renewable energy **even if** it were slightly more expensive.

### As long as / Provided that — điều kiện có điều kiện:

- This approach **could work**, **provided that** sufficient funding is available.
- Remote learning **can be effective**, **as long as** students have reliable internet access.

## PHẦN 6: CÁC MẪU CÂU TỰ NHIÊN CHO SPEAKING

### Part 2 — Mô tả trải nghiệm hoặc sự kiện giả định:

- "I think **if I had** the chance to visit again, I **would** definitely spend more time exploring the local area."
- "**If** the weather **had been** better, we **would have** been able to see the view from the top."
- "I sometimes wonder what **would have** happened **if** I **had** taken that opportunity."

### Part 3 — Phân tích vấn đề:

- "**If** the government **were** to introduce free childcare, more women **would** be able to return to the workforce."
- "I think **if** more people **were** educated about nutrition from an early age, obesity rates **would** fall significantly."
- "**If** companies **had** been held accountable for their environmental impact earlier, we **might not** be facing such a serious climate crisis."

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Dùng "will" trong mệnh đề "if"

- ❌ "If it **will** rain tomorrow, I won't go."
- ✅ "If it **rains** tomorrow, I won't go."

### Lỗi 2: Dùng "would" trong mệnh đề "if" của second conditional

- ❌ "If I **would have** more time, I would travel."
- ✅ "If I **had** more time, I would travel."

### Lỗi 3: Nhầm second và third conditional

- ❌ "If I studied harder, I would pass the exam." *(khi đang nói về kỳ thi trong quá khứ đã không vượt qua)*
- ✅ "If I **had studied** harder, I **would have** passed the exam."

### Lỗi 4: Không hoàn thành câu conditional sau khi bắt đầu

- ❌ "If the government invested more in education... it's very important."
- ✅ "If the government invested more in education, young people **would have** better opportunities."

### Lỗi 5: "Could of / Would of" — lỗi viết sai khi phát âm

- ❌ "I would of helped if I had known." *(chỉ lỗi khi viết)*
- ✅ "I **would have** helped if I had known."

## Bài tập luyện

### Hoàn thành câu với đúng dạng conditional

1. If I ___ (be) the prime minister, I ___ (invest) more in renewable energy.
2. If she ___ (study) medicine, she ___ (become) a doctor by now.
3. If people ___ (recycle) more, there ___ (be) less waste in landfills.
4. The company ___ (not fail) if the management ___ (make) better decisions.
5. If I ___ (know) about the event, I ___ (come).

### Đáp án

1. **were / would invest** (second — giả định hiện tại)
2. **had studied / would have become** (mixed — quá khứ → hệ quả hiện tại)
3. **recycled / would be** (second — giả định chung)
4. **would not have failed / had made** (third — giả định quá khứ)
5. **had known / would have come** (third — quá khứ)

## Tóm tắt nhanh

```
ZERO: If + present → present (sự thật chung)
  If you don't sleep, you feel tired.

FIRST: If + present → will (khả năng thực)
  If we act now, we will prevent the worst.

SECOND: If + past → would (giả định hiện tại)
  If I had more time, I would travel.

THIRD: If + past perfect → would have (giả định quá khứ)
  If I had known, I would have helped.

MIXED: If + past perfect → would (hệ quả hiện tại)
  If I had studied harder, I would be a doctor now.

HEDGING:
  if anything → điều chỉnh mức độ
  even if → nhượng bộ
  provided that / as long as → điều kiện
  if you ask me → ý kiến cá nhân

KỸ THUẬT Part 3:
  → Second conditional để propose + analyse
  → Third conditional để reflect on policy failures
  → Mixed để link past decisions to present situation
```
