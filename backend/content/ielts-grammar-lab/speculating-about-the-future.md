## Tóm tắt

| Cấu trúc | Mức độ chắc chắn | Phù hợp |
|----------|-----------------|---------|
| **will / will not** | Chắc chắn cao | Writing, Speaking cả hai |
| **is likely / unlikely to** | Có khả năng cao/thấp | Writing, Speaking Part 3 |
| **may / might** | Không chắc lắm | Cả hai, đặc biệt khi nêu ý thận trọng |
| **could possibly / could well** | Có thể (khá chắc) | Speaking, Writing informal |
| **there is a (strong/real) chance that** | Xác suất vừa phải | Cả hai |
| **is expected / predicted / projected to** | Dự báo chuyên môn | Writing, formal speaking |

## Tại sao quan trọng

Trong IELTS Speaking Part 3, examiner hay hỏi:
- "What do you think will happen to [X] in the future?"
- "Do you think [trend] will continue?"
- "How might [issue] change in the next decade?"

Câu trả lời "I think it will be better" quá đơn giản. Band 7+ cần dùng các cấu trúc suy đoán đa dạng, kết hợp với lý do giải thích.

---

## PHẦN 1: WILL — Dự đoán chắc chắn

```
[Subject] will [verb].
[Subject] will not / won't [verb] unless [condition].
It is clear / almost certain that [subject] will [verb].
```

"Will" dùng khi bạn khá chắc chắn về điều gì sẽ xảy ra — hoặc khi nêu hậu quả logic.

**Speaking:**

- "I think technology **will continue** to reshape the job market over the next decade."
- "**It's almost certain that** electric vehicles **will replace** petrol cars within the next twenty years."
- "The population of cities **will grow** significantly as more people move away from rural areas."

**Writing:**

- "If current trends continue, the global average temperature **will rise** by 1.5 degrees Celsius before 2050."
- "Without urgent intervention, antibiotic resistance **will become** one of the most serious public health crises of the 21st century."

---

## PHẦN 2: IS LIKELY / UNLIKELY TO — Mức độ xác suất rõ ràng

```
[Subject] is likely / unlikely to [verb].
[Subject] is highly / fairly likely to [verb].
It is likely / unlikely that [clause].
```

"Likely" thể hiện xác suất cao nhưng không hoàn toàn chắc chắn — rất phổ biến trong IELTS Writing.

**Speaking:**

- "Remote working **is likely to become** the norm in many industries, at least for knowledge-based jobs."
- "I think traditional shops **are unlikely to survive** unless they offer something online retailers can't."
- "**It's fairly likely that** more countries **will adopt** renewable energy policies in the next few years."

**Writing:**

- "**It is likely that** automation will displace a significant number of low-skilled jobs within the next two decades."
- "Urban populations **are unlikely to decline** in the near future, given continued rural-to-urban migration."
- "**It is highly likely that** AI will transform the healthcare sector, enabling faster and more accurate diagnoses."

---

## PHẦN 3: MAY / MIGHT — Suy đoán dè dặt, thận trọng

```
[Subject] may / might [verb].
[Subject] may / might not [verb].
[Subject] may well [verb]. (= probably will)
```

"May" và "might" thể hiện khả năng không chắc — rất hữu ích khi bạn không muốn khẳng định quá mạnh.

**Speaking:**

- "Climate change **might** make some parts of the world uninhabitable in the long run."
- "Younger generations **may** have very different views on marriage and family life."
- "This trend **may well reverse** as governments begin to introduce stricter regulations."

**Writing:**

- "Advances in biotechnology **may** eventually enable the elimination of certain genetic diseases."
- "The widespread adoption of AI in education **might** widen the gap between well-resourced and under-resourced schools."
- "While some jobs **may** be lost to automation, new roles **may well emerge** in sectors that do not yet exist."

---

## PHẦN 4: COULD / COULD POSSIBLY / COULD WELL — Khả năng vừa phải

```
[Subject] could [verb].
[Subject] could possibly [verb].
[Subject] could well [verb]. (= có khả năng cao)
```

**Speaking:**

- "I suppose this **could possibly lead to** a significant shift in how young people view work."
- "If more countries adopt electric vehicles, it **could** dramatically reduce air pollution in cities."
- "**It could well be** that the next major technological breakthrough comes from an unexpected field."

**Writing:**

- "The expansion of urban public transport networks **could** encourage more people to abandon their cars."
- "A failure to invest in education **could** have severe long-term consequences for economic productivity."

---

## PHẦN 5: IS EXPECTED / PREDICTED / PROJECTED TO — Văn phong học thuật

Những cấu trúc này nghe trang trọng và học thuật hơn — phù hợp với Writing Task 2 và Speaking Part 3 ở mức cao.

```
[Subject] is expected / predicted / projected to [verb].
It is expected / predicted / projected that [clause].
Experts / analysts predict / forecast that [clause].
```

**Writing:**

- "The global population **is projected to** reach approximately 10 billion by 2050."
- "**It is predicted that** the demand for skilled workers in the technology sector **will outstrip** supply significantly in the coming decade."
- "Renewable energy **is expected to** account for over 50% of global electricity generation by 2035."

**Speaking:**

- "**Experts predict that** AI-driven tools **will transform** most professional industries within a generation."
- "The economy **is expected to** recover gradually, although uncertainties remain."

---

## PHẦN 6: THERE IS A CHANCE THAT — Tự nhiên trong Speaking

```
There is a (real / strong / good / slight) chance that [clause].
There is little / no chance that [clause].
```

**Speaking:**

- "**There's a real chance that** we'll see major cities completely powered by renewables within our lifetimes."
- "**There's a good chance that** social media platforms will face much stricter regulation in the near future."
- "Honestly, **there's very little chance** that people will give up their smartphones willingly."

## PHẦN 7: KẾT HỢP SỰ SUY ĐOÁN VỚI LÝ DO

Một câu suy đoán đơn giản không đủ cho Band 7+. Kết hợp với lý do:

```
[Speculation], because / since / as [reason].
[Speculation], given that [evidence/trend].
[Speculation], particularly if [condition].
[Speculation], unless [obstacle/counter-condition].
```

**Speaking:**

- "Remote working **is likely to become** more widespread, **particularly as** companies realise they can reduce overheads without sacrificing productivity."
- "Electric vehicles **will probably replace** petrol cars within two decades, **given that** battery technology is improving rapidly and costs are falling."
- "The population of cities **may continue to grow**, **unless** governments invest significantly in improving quality of life in rural areas."

**Writing:**

- "**It is likely that** income inequality will worsen in the short term, **given the current pace** of automation and the lack of adequate retraining programmes."
- "Global temperatures **will almost certainly continue to rise**, **unless** radical and immediate action is taken by both governments and individuals."

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Dùng "will" cho mọi câu suy đoán

- ❌ "Technology will change everything. People will use AI every day. Companies will be more productive."
- ✅ Xen kẽ: will → is likely to → might → could → is expected to

### Lỗi 2: "Might could" hoặc "might will" (không tồn tại)

- ❌ "This might could happen." / "It might will change."
- ✅ "This **might** happen." / "It **could** change."

### Lỗi 3: Không giải thích lý do sau suy đoán

- ❌ "I think electric cars will be popular in the future."
- ✅ "Electric cars **are likely to become** the dominant form of personal transport, **given the rapid improvement** in battery technology and the growing pressure on governments to reduce emissions."

### Lỗi 4: Nhầm "may" và "might" (sự khác biệt nhỏ)

- "May" = khả năng cao hơn một chút
- "Might" = khả năng thấp hơn một chút / giả định hơn
- Trong IELTS, cả hai đều chấp nhận được và sự khác biệt không đáng kể.

## Bài tập luyện

### Trả lời các câu hỏi Speaking sau (2-3 câu, dùng ít nhất 2 cấu trúc suy đoán):

1. "Do you think people will work shorter hours in the future?"
2. "How do you think cities will change in the next 50 years?"

### Đáp án gợi ý:

1. "**It's quite likely that** working hours **will decrease** in many industries, **particularly as** automation takes over repetitive tasks. **There's a good chance** that a four-day work week **will become** the norm in many countries within the next generation, though this **may not** apply equally across all sectors."

2. "Cities **are expected to** grow considerably more dense and technologically advanced. Public spaces **will probably** be redesigned around sustainability — with more green areas, better cycling infrastructure, and reduced car use. **It's possible that** some cities **might** be designed from scratch as entirely carbon-neutral environments, though **it's equally likely that** upgrading existing cities **will remain** the more realistic challenge."

## Tóm tắt nhanh

```
TỪ CHẮC ĐẾN KHÔNG CHẮC:

CHẮC CHẮN NHẤT:
  will → "Technology will transform healthcare."
  is expected/predicted/projected to → "The population is projected to reach 10 billion."

CÓ KHẢ NĂNG CAO:
  is likely to → "This trend is likely to continue."
  could well → "This could well lead to major changes."
  may well → "Attitudes may well shift over time."

KHÔNG CHẮC LẮM:
  may / might → "This might create new challenges."
  there is a chance that → "There's a chance that..."

CÓ THỂ:
  could possibly → "This could possibly happen."
  could → "This could have serious consequences."

CÔNG THỨC ĐẦY ĐỦ:
  [speculation] + because/given that/unless/particularly if + [reason/condition]
```
