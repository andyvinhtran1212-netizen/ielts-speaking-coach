## Tóm tắt

| Loại | Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|------|---------|-------|
| **Nguyên nhân (verb)** | cause, lead to, result in, give rise to | Poverty **leads to** social unrest. |
| **Nguyên nhân (conjunction)** | because, since, as, given that | **Because** funding was cut, the programme failed. |
| **Nguyên nhân (preposition)** | due to, owing to, as a result of, because of | **Due to** a lack of resources, progress was slow. |
| **Kết quả (adverb)** | therefore, consequently, as a result, hence | **Consequently**, many people lost their jobs. |
| **Kết quả (verb)** | result in, lead to, cause, produce, generate | This **resulted in** a significant decline in productivity. |

---

## Tại sao quan trọng

Trong IELTS Writing Task 2, dù là opinion essay, discussion essay hay problem-solution essay, bạn luôn cần giải thích **tại sao** (nguyên nhân) và **điều gì xảy ra tiếp theo** (kết quả). Chỉ dùng "because" và "so" là Band 5. Band 7+ đòi hỏi đa dạng hoá cấu trúc và biết khi nào dùng conjunction vs preposition.

---

## PHẦN 1: DIỄN ĐẠT NGUYÊN NHÂN — CONJUNCTIONS

### because (liên từ — nối hai mệnh đề)

```
[Result clause] + because + [cause clause]
Because + [cause clause], + [result clause]
```

- The project **failed** **because** the team lacked the necessary expertise.
- **Because** public transport is expensive and unreliable, many people prefer to drive.
- Crime rates have risen **because** unemployment has increased dramatically.

### since / as (lý do đã biết, ít nhấn mạnh hơn because)

```
Since/As + [known cause], + [result]
```

- **Since** the city lacks a reliable public transport system, residents are heavily dependent on private vehicles.
- **As** unemployment rates remain high, the demand for social welfare services has increased.
- **Since** this is a widely recognised problem, urgent action is required.

> **Phân biệt:** "since" và "as" thường đứng đầu câu và diễn đạt lý do đã ngầm hiểu. "because" mạnh hơn và nhấn mạnh nguyên nhân trực tiếp.

### given that / in view of the fact that (vì xét rằng)

```
Given that / In view of the fact that + [cause], + [result]
```

- **Given that** the population is ageing rapidly, investment in elderly care is essential.
- **In view of the fact that** carbon emissions continue to rise, more aggressive climate policies are urgently needed.

## PHẦN 2: DIỄN ĐẠT NGUYÊN NHÂN — PREPOSITIONS

Khác với conjunction (nối mệnh đề), preposition nối với **noun phrase**.

### due to / owing to / because of / as a result of / on account of

```
[Result clause] + due to / owing to / because of + [noun phrase]
Due to / Owing to + [noun phrase], + [result clause]
```

| Giới từ | Mức trang trọng | Ghi chú |
|--------|----------------|---------|
| **due to** | Formal | Phổ biến nhất trong academic writing |
| **owing to** | Very formal | Phổ biến trong formal reports |
| **because of** | Neutral | Dùng được ở mọi ngữ cảnh |
| **as a result of** | Formal | Nhấn mạnh hơn |
| **on account of** | Formal/old-fashioned | Ít dùng hơn |

**Ví dụ:**

- **Due to** rapid urbanisation, many cities are struggling with overcrowding and inadequate infrastructure.
- **Owing to** a lack of political will, environmental legislation has been slow to progress.
- **Because of** economic pressures, many young people are delaying starting a family.
- **As a result of** technological advances, many traditional industries have been replaced.
- The scheme was cancelled **on account of** insufficient funding.

> **Lỗi phổ biến:** "Due to" vs "Because" — xem thêm bài because-vs-because-of.
> - ❌ "Due to the team lacked expertise, the project failed." *(due to + mệnh đề là sai)*
> - ✅ "Due to the **team's lack of** expertise, the project failed." *(due to + noun phrase)*

## PHẦN 3: DIỄN ĐẠT KẾT QUẢ — ADVERBS / CONNECTORS

### therefore / consequently / as a result / hence / thus

```
[Cause]. + Therefore / Consequently / As a result, + [result].
[Cause]; consequently, + [result].
```

| Connector | Ghi chú |
|----------|--------|
| **therefore** | Phổ biến nhất, logic trực tiếp |
| **consequently** | Formal, nhấn mạnh hậu quả |
| **as a result** | Neutral, rõ ràng |
| **hence** | Rất formal, thường trong academic prose |
| **thus** | Very formal, dùng cẩn thận |
| **for this reason** | Thân thiện hơn, dùng được mọi chỗ |

**Ví dụ:**

- Obesity rates have risen sharply over the past decade. **Consequently**, the burden on healthcare systems has increased significantly.
- Many young people are graduating with significant debt. **As a result**, they are delaying major life decisions such as buying a home or starting a family.
- The government failed to invest in infrastructure; **therefore**, the country's economic competitiveness declined.
- Deforestation continues at an alarming rate. **Hence**, biodiversity loss has accelerated.
- Public transport is unreliable. **For this reason**, most residents prefer to use private vehicles.

## PHẦN 4: DIỄN ĐẠT KẾT QUẢ — VERBS

### lead to / result in / cause / give rise to / contribute to

```
[Cause] + leads to / results in / causes / gives rise to + [noun phrase/gerund]
[Cause] + contributes to / is responsible for + [noun phrase/gerund]
```

| Động từ | Nghĩa | Ghi chú |
|--------|-------|--------|
| **lead to** | dẫn đến | Phổ biến nhất |
| **result in** | dẫn đến kết quả | Nhấn mạnh hơn lead to |
| **cause** | gây ra | Mạnh, trực tiếp |
| **give rise to** | làm nảy sinh | Formal, cho abstract nouns |
| **contribute to** | góp phần vào | Không phải nguyên nhân duy nhất |
| **generate** | tạo ra | Cho outcomes tích cực hoặc trung tính |
| **produce** | tạo ra, sản sinh | Tương tự generate |
| **trigger** | kích hoạt | Cho sự kiện đột ngột |

**Ví dụ:**

- Rapid urbanisation **leads to** increased demand for housing, transport, and public services.
- Air pollution **results in** thousands of premature deaths each year.
- A lack of physical activity **causes** a range of health problems, including cardiovascular disease.
- Economic instability **gives rise to** social tensions and political unrest.
- Poor nutrition in childhood **contributes to** long-term developmental difficulties.
- The new policy **generated** considerable controversy among business leaders.
- The financial crisis of 2008 **triggered** widespread unemployment across Europe.

## PHẦN 5: CHUỖI NGUYÊN NHÂN — KẾT QUẢ (CAUSAL CHAIN)

Để viết phân tích sâu, cần diễn đạt được chuỗi nhân quả dài:

```
[A] leads to [B], which in turn leads to [C], ultimately resulting in [D].
[A] → [B] → [C] → [D]
```

**Ví dụ chuỗi nhân quả:**

> "**The increasing pressure** on students to achieve high academic results **leads to** chronic stress, **which in turn** can result in burnout and declining mental health. **This, combined with** a lack of access to professional support, **may ultimately contribute to** a rise in youth depression and anxiety disorders."

> "**A failure to invest** in renewable energy **gives rise to** continued dependence on fossil fuels, **which not only** perpetuates carbon emissions **but also** leaves countries vulnerable to volatile global oil prices, **thereby** creating long-term economic instability."

## PHẦN 6: NGUYÊN NHÂN NHIỀU CHIỀU (MULTIPLE CAUSES)

Khi một vấn đề có nhiều nguyên nhân, dùng cấu trúc phân chia:

### Nguyên nhân chính / phụ:

```
The primary cause of [problem] is [cause A].
A secondary factor is [cause B].
[Problem] is largely attributable to [cause], although [other factors] also play a role.
```

- "**The primary cause** of rising obesity rates is poor dietary habits, particularly the widespread availability of cheap, calorie-dense food. **A secondary factor** is the decline in physical activity, **owing to** increasingly sedentary lifestyles."

### Kết hợp nhiều nguyên nhân:

```
[Problem] is the result of a combination of factors, including [A], [B], and [C].
[Problem] can be attributed to both [cause A] and [cause B].
[Problem] stems from [root cause], compounded by [additional factors].
```

- "Youth unemployment **is the result of a combination of** structural economic changes, inadequate educational systems, and, **to some extent**, a lack of vocational training opportunities."
- "The housing crisis **stems from** decades of underinvestment in social housing, **compounded by** rapidly rising property prices and insufficient regulatory oversight."

## PHẦN 7: VÍ DỤ ĐOẠN BODY HOÀN CHỈNH

**Câu hỏi:** "What are the main causes of traffic congestion in modern cities, and what are the effects?"

**Body paragraph — causes:**

> "**The primary cause** of urban traffic congestion is the overdependence on private vehicles, which **stems from** inadequate investment in public transport infrastructure. **Because** bus and train networks are often unreliable, overcrowded, or expensive, many commuters **opt for** the convenience of personal cars. **In addition**, rapid population growth in urban areas **has led to** a significant increase in vehicle ownership, **thereby** placing greater strain on road networks that were designed for much smaller populations."

**Body paragraph — effects:**

> "Traffic congestion **has far-reaching consequences** for both individuals and society as a whole. **At the individual level**, prolonged commuting times **result in** increased stress and reduced productivity, **contributing to** a deterioration in overall quality of life. **At a broader level**, heavy traffic **gives rise to** elevated levels of air pollution, **which in turn** has been linked to respiratory diseases and premature mortality. **Furthermore**, economic output is undermined **due to** the inefficiency of goods and services delivery, with some estimates suggesting that congestion **costs** urban economies billions of dollars annually."

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: "Due to" theo sau bởi mệnh đề

- ❌ "Due to the government didn't invest enough, ..."
- ✅ "Due to **insufficient government investment**, ..."
- ✅ "Because the government didn't invest enough, ..."

### Lỗi 2: "Lead to" + verb thay vì noun

- ❌ "This leads to people suffer."
- ✅ "This leads to **people suffering**." (gerund)
- ✅ "This leads to **increased suffering** among the population." (noun phrase)

### Lỗi 3: Dùng "so" thay vì academic connector

- ❌ "The economy grew, **so** living standards improved." *(quá informal)*
- ✅ "The economy grew; **consequently**, living standards improved."
- ✅ "Economic growth **led to** an improvement in living standards."

### Lỗi 4: "Result" nhầm giữa "result in" và "result from"

- **result IN** → nguyên nhân gây ra kết quả: "Poverty **results in** social problems."
- **result FROM** → kết quả đến từ nguyên nhân: "Social problems often **result from** poverty."

## Bài tập luyện

### Nối câu bằng cấu trúc phù hợp

1. Many people are unemployed. Many families struggle to afford food. (consequence)
2. The city has poor air quality. Traffic volumes are very high. (cause)
3. The government increased taxes. Investment in infrastructure improved. (result)

### Gợi ý đáp án

1. High unemployment rates **result in** financial hardship for many families, who **consequently** struggle to afford basic necessities such as food.
2. The city's poor air quality is **largely attributable to** extremely high traffic volumes, **owing to** an underdeveloped public transport network.
3. The government's decision to increase taxes **led to** improved investment in infrastructure, **which in turn** contributed to stronger economic growth.

## Tóm tắt nhanh

```
NGUYÊN NHÂN (conjunction):
  because + clause | since + clause | as + clause
  given that + clause | in view of the fact that...

NGUYÊN NHÂN (preposition + noun phrase):
  due to / owing to / because of / as a result of / on account of

KẾT QUẢ (adverb connector):
  therefore / consequently / as a result / hence / thus / for this reason

KẾT QUẢ (verb):
  lead to / result in / cause / give rise to / contribute to
  generate / produce / trigger

CHUỖI NHÂN QUẢ:
  [A] leads to [B], which in turn results in [C]
  [A] → [B] → [C], ultimately resulting in [D]

NHIỀU NGUYÊN NHÂN:
  The primary cause is [A]. A secondary factor is [B].
  This stems from [A], compounded by [B] and [C].
  This is attributable to a combination of [A], [B], and [C].

PHÂN BIỆT:
  result IN sth → nguyên nhân → kết quả
  result FROM sth → kết quả ← nguyên nhân
  due to + NOUN (not clause)
```
