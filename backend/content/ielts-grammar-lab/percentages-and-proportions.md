## Tóm tắt

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|----------|-------|-------|
| **accounted for** | chiếm (tỷ lệ) | Oil accounted for 40% of energy use. |
| **made up** | cấu thành, chiếm | Women made up just over half of all graduates. |
| **stood at** | ở mức, đạt | The figure stood at approximately 35%. |
| **represented** | đại diện cho, chiếm | Agriculture represented the smallest share. |
| **comprised** | bao gồm, chiếm | Students comprised 60% of the population. |
| **constituted** | cấu thành | The elderly constituted roughly a quarter. |

---

## Tại sao quan trọng

Khi mô tả pie chart, table hoặc bar chart, người học thường chỉ viết lặp đi lặp lại một cấu trúc: "X was 40%... Y was 30%..." Điều này bị trừ điểm về Lexical Resource và Grammatical Range. Dùng đa dạng các cấu trúc mô tả tỷ lệ giúp bài viết tự nhiên hơn và đạt Band 7+.

## PHẦN 1: CÁC VERB CHÍNH ĐỂ MÔ TẢ TỶ LỆ

### 1. account for — chiếm (tỷ lệ trong tổng thể)

```
[Subject] accounted for [percentage / fraction] of [total].
[Subject] accounted for the largest / smallest share / proportion.
```

- "Coal **accounted for** approximately 38% of total electricity generation."
- "Young adults aged 18–24 **accounted for** the largest proportion of social media users."
- "Transport costs **accounted for** nearly a quarter of all household expenditure."

> "Account for" là động từ tự nhiên nhất để mô tả một phần trong tổng thể.

### 2. make up — cấu thành, chiếm

```
[Subject] made up [percentage] of [total].
[Subject] made up the majority / minority / bulk of [total].
```

- "Renewable sources **made up** just over a fifth of total energy production."
- "Female employees **made up** the majority of the workforce in the healthcare sector."
- "International students **made up** approximately 30% of all university enrolments."

### 3. stand at — ở mức, đạt (đặc biệt dùng với số cụ thể)

```
[Subject] stood at [figure / percentage] in [year].
The figure / proportion / share stood at approximately [value].
```

- "The unemployment rate **stood at** around 7.5% in 2010."
- "By 2020, car ownership in the region **stood at** 85 vehicles per 100 households."
- "The proportion of households with internet access **stood at** just under 60%."

> "Stand at" thường dùng để mô tả số liệu tại một thời điểm cụ thể.

### 4. represent — đại diện cho, chiếm

```
[Subject] represented [percentage] of [total].
[Subject] represented the [largest / smallest / dominant] share.
```

- "Nuclear energy **represented** only a small fraction of total power output."
- "Tourism **represented** the fastest-growing sector in the national economy."
- "Children under five **represented** roughly 12% of the total population."

### 5. comprise / constitute — cấu thành, bao gồm

```
[Subject] comprised [percentage] of [total].
[Subject] constituted [fraction] of the [whole].
```

- "Women **comprised** just over half of all university graduates in 2019."
- "The working-age population **constituted** approximately 65% of the national total."

## PHẦN 2: MÔ TẢ BẰNG PHÂN SỐ VÀ CỤM TỪ TỶ LỆ

Không phải lúc nào cũng cần dùng số phần trăm cụ thể. Các cụm từ phân số và tỷ lệ ước lượng giúp bài viết tự nhiên hơn:

| Tỷ lệ | Cụm từ mô tả |
|-------|-------------|
| ~50% | approximately half / just over/under half / roughly half |
| ~25% | a quarter / one in four |
| ~33% | a third / one in three |
| ~75% | three quarters / the majority |
| ~10% | a tenth / a small minority / one in ten |
| >50% | the majority / more than half / over half |
| <50% | less than half / under half / a minority |
| ~100% | almost all / nearly all / the vast majority |

**Ví dụ dùng phân số:**

- "**Just over a third** of respondents reported feeling dissatisfied with their working conditions."
- "**Nearly three quarters** of households in the survey owned at least one car."
- "**Fewer than one in ten** participants had accessed the service more than twice."

## PHẦN 3: KẾT HỢP CÁC CẤU TRÚC — TRÁNH LẶP

Trong một đoạn mô tả, thay vì dùng cùng một cấu trúc nhiều lần, kết hợp để tạo sự đa dạng:

### Ví dụ đoạn mô tả pie chart:

> "The pie chart illustrates the distribution of household energy expenditure in 2022. **Coal accounted for** the largest proportion, **representing** approximately 35% of total spending. Natural gas **made up** a further 28%, while electricity **stood at** around 20%. Renewable energy sources, by contrast, **comprised** only a small fraction of the total, **constituting** just 5% of all household energy costs."

### Chú ý: Tránh lặp "was/were + percentage" quá nhiều:

- ❌ "Coal was 35%. Gas was 28%. Electricity was 20%. Renewables were 5%."
- ✅ Kết hợp: account for / make up / stand at / represent / comprise

## PHẦN 4: CẤU TRÚC SO SÁNH TỶ LỆ

```
[A] accounted for a significantly larger / smaller proportion than [B].
[A] represented nearly double / three times the proportion of [B].
While [A] made up [X]%, [B] accounted for only [Y]%.
[A]'s share was [X] percentage points higher / lower than [B]'s.
```

**Ví dụ:**

- "**Fossil fuels accounted for a significantly larger proportion** of energy consumption than renewables, at 70% compared to just 12%."
- "**The share of** mobile internet users **was nearly double that of** desktop users by 2020."
- "**While** food and beverages **made up** 40% of spending, entertainment **accounted for** only 8%."
- "**The proportion of** female graduates **was 15 percentage points higher** than that of male graduates."

---

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Chỉ dùng "was/were" để mô tả tỷ lệ

- ❌ "The percentage of oil was 40%. The percentage of gas was 30%. Renewables were 15%."
- ✅ "Oil **accounted for** 40% of total energy use, while gas **made up** 30%. Renewables **represented** a relatively minor share at 15%."

### Lỗi 2: Nhầm subject và object

- ❌ "40% accounted for oil." *(sai thứ tự)*
- ✅ "Oil **accounted for** 40%." *(subject = Oil)*

### Lỗi 3: Không dùng approximators khi số không chẵn

- ❌ "Oil accounted for 37.8% of energy use."
- ✅ "Oil **accounted for** approximately 38% of energy use." / "**just under** 40%"

### Lỗi 4: Dùng "compose" thay cho "comprise/constitute"

- ❌ "Women composed 55% of the workforce." *(không tự nhiên)*
- ✅ "Women **comprised** 55% of the workforce." / "Women **constituted** 55%..."

## Bài tập luyện

### Viết lại các câu sau dùng verb được chỉ định:

1. "Transport was 25% of the budget." → Dùng **accounted for**
2. "Women were 48% of voters." → Dùng **made up**
3. "The rate was 12% in 2015." → Dùng **stood at**
4. "Healthcare was the largest category." → Dùng **represented**

### Đáp án:

1. "Transport **accounted for** 25% of the budget."
2. "Women **made up** approximately 48% of all voters."
3. "The rate **stood at** around 12% in 2015."
4. "Healthcare **represented** the largest category of government spending."
