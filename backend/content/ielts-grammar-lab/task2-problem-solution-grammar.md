## Tóm tắt

Bài Problem-Solution có cấu trúc điển hình:

```
INTRODUCTION:  Paraphrase + thesis (identifying the problem)
BODY 1:        Causes / extent of the problem
BODY 2:        Solutions (individual + government/societal level)
CONCLUSION:    Restate + outlook
```

---

## Tại sao quan trọng

Dạng bài Problem-Solution yêu cầu bạn không chỉ mô tả vấn đề mà còn **phân tích nguyên nhân, đề xuất giải pháp cụ thể, và thể hiện thái độ qua modal verbs**. Thiếu ngữ pháp đặc thù dẫn đến bài viết đơn điệu, ý thiếu chiều sâu, và không đạt Band 7+ về Grammatical Range and Accuracy.

---

## PHẦN 1: GIỚI THIỆU VẤN ĐỀ (Introduction)

### Paraphrase + nhận diện tính nghiêm trọng:

```
[Topic] has become an increasingly [serious/pressing/acute] issue/problem/concern
in [many countries / the modern world / recent decades].
```

- Unemployment among young graduates **has become an increasingly pressing concern** in many developed economies.
- Traffic congestion in major cities **has emerged as one of the most significant challenges** facing urban planners today.
- The rising cost of housing **has become an acute problem** for a large proportion of the population.

### Thesis statement — xác định trọng tâm bài:

```
This essay will examine the [main causes / key factors] behind this trend
and propose [several / a number of] [practical / effective / realistic] solutions.
```

- **This essay will examine** the primary causes of youth unemployment and **suggest** several targeted policy responses.
- **This essay will explore** the factors contributing to urban congestion and **outline** potential solutions at both individual and governmental levels.

---

## PHẦN 2: MÔ TẢ VÀ PHÂN TÍCH VẤN ĐỀ (Body 1: Problem/Causes)

### Mô tả mức độ nghiêm trọng:

```
The [problem] is [particularly / especially / disproportionately] acute in...
[The problem] affects / impacts [noun phrase] significantly.
[Noun phrase] are [most] severely affected by [this issue].
```

- The housing crisis is **particularly acute** in major metropolitan areas, where demand consistently outstrips supply.
- Obesity **disproportionately affects** children from low-income backgrounds, where access to nutritious food is limited.
- Rural communities **are most severely affected by** the decline in public services.

### Nêu nguyên nhân:

**Sử dụng các cấu trúc nguyên nhân — tham khảo bài task2-cause-effect-grammar:**

```
One of the primary causes of [X] is [Y].
[X] can be largely attributed to [Y].
[X] stems from / arises from [Y].
A key contributing factor is [Y].
[Y] has given rise to / led to / resulted in [X].
```

- One of the **primary causes** of youth unemployment is the **mismatch between** the skills taught in universities and those demanded by the modern job market.
- The housing shortage **can be largely attributed to** a combination of rapid urbanisation, insufficient construction, and restrictive zoning regulations.
- The rise in obesity **stems from** increasingly sedentary lifestyles and the proliferation of processed, calorie-dense foods.
- **A key contributing factor** is the lack of affordable childcare, which prevents many parents from re-entering the workforce.

### Liệt kê nhiều nguyên nhân:

```
There are several factors that contribute to this problem.
Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Furthermore, ... Finally, ...
Not only does [X] contribute to this issue, but [Y] also plays a significant role.
```

- There are **several interrelated factors** that contribute to rising youth unemployment. **Firstly**, educational institutions often fail to equip graduates with practical, employable skills. **Secondly**, an increasingly competitive labour market means that entry-level positions attract far more applicants than there are vacancies. **Furthermore**, the expansion of temporary and part-time contracts has eroded the availability of stable employment.

## PHẦN 3: ĐỀ XUẤT GIẢI PHÁP (Body 2: Solutions)

### Modal verbs để đề xuất:

```
[Subject] should / must / ought to + verb
[Subject] could / might + verb    (less certain, hedged)
It is essential / vital / crucial that + [subject] + verb
```

**Mạnh (bắt buộc):**
- Governments **must** invest significantly more in vocational training programmes.
- It is **essential** that urban planners prioritise affordable housing in their development strategies.
- Employers **should** be required to offer apprenticeships as part of their operating licences.

**Trung bình (đề xuất):**
- Authorities **could** introduce congestion charging schemes to reduce the number of private vehicles on the road.
- Schools **might** consider incorporating financial literacy into the standard curriculum.
- Local governments **should** be encouraged to streamline the planning permission process.

**Nhẹ (gợi ý):**
- One possible approach **would be** to provide tax incentives for companies that hire young graduates.
- Another option **might involve** redirecting subsidies from fossil fuels towards renewable energy infrastructure.

### Cấu trúc giải pháp hai cấp (individual + government):

```
At the individual level, [people / individuals / citizens] can/should + verb...
At the governmental / societal / institutional level, [governments / authorities / schools] should + verb...
```

- **At the individual level**, people can reduce their carbon footprint by choosing more sustainable modes of transport, such as cycling or using public transit. **At the governmental level**, authorities should invest in expanding and improving public transport networks, making them a genuinely viable alternative to private car use.

### Giải thích tại sao giải pháp hiệu quả:

```
This would [result in / lead to / help to / ensure that]...
By doing so, [subject] would...
Such measures would [significantly / substantially] [reduce / alleviate / address]...
```

- Investing in renewable energy infrastructure **would not only** reduce carbon emissions **but also** create thousands of skilled jobs in the green economy.
- **By implementing** stricter regulations on food advertising targeted at children, governments **could** significantly reduce the normalisation of unhealthy dietary habits.
- **Such measures would** go a long way towards alleviating the housing shortage and making home ownership accessible to a broader section of the population.

## PHẦN 4: KẾT BÀI (Conclusion)

### Tóm tắt + dự báo:

```
In conclusion, [problem] is a complex issue with [multiple / several] contributing factors.
[Summary of solutions]. If [these / such] measures are [implemented / adopted / taken],
[positive outcome]. Ultimately, [broader point].
```

- **In conclusion**, youth unemployment is a multifaceted problem rooted in structural weaknesses in both the education system and the labour market. **Addressing** this issue **will require** coordinated action from governments, employers, and educational institutions. **If such reforms are implemented**, it is reasonable to expect a significant improvement in youth employment rates over the coming decade.

## PHẦN 5: TEMPLATE ĐẦY ĐỦ — Problem-Solution

```
INTRODUCTION:
In recent years, [topic] has become an increasingly [serious/pressing] issue 
in [context]. This essay will examine the [key causes] of this problem 
and propose [several/a number of] [practical] solutions.

BODY 1 — PROBLEM + CAUSES:
The [problem] is particularly [severe/acute] in [context]. 
One of the primary causes is [X], which [explanation].
Furthermore, [another cause] has contributed significantly to [this trend].
[A third cause / As a result], [consequence of the problem].

BODY 2 — SOLUTIONS:
There are several measures that [governments/individuals/schools] could take 
to address this issue.
Firstly, [solution 1]. This would [result/benefit].
Secondly, [solution 2]. By doing so, [subject] would [outcome].
[In addition / Furthermore], [solution 3 if needed].

CONCLUSION:
In conclusion, [problem] is a complex issue with multiple contributing factors.
If [the above solutions / these measures] are [implemented/adopted], 
[positive outcome]. Ultimately, [broader statement about the future/importance].
```

## PHẦN 6: NGỮ PHÁP ĐẶC THÙ — Danh từ hóa (Nominalization)

Trong problem-solution essays, nominalization giúp câu văn học thuật hơn:

| Verbal form | Nominalized form |
|------------|-----------------|
| the government **decided** | the government's **decision** |
| people **are unemployed** | **unemployment** / the **prevalence of** unemployment |
| costs **have risen** | the **rise in** costs / an **increase in** costs |
| it is **necessary** to | **the necessity of** / **the need for** |
| to **solve** the problem | **a solution to** the problem |
| to **access** education | **access to** education |
| roads are **congested** | road **congestion** / traffic **congestion** |

**Ví dụ so sánh:**

- ❌ "The government needs to solve this because people are poor."
- ✅ "**Addressing** poverty requires substantial government intervention, particularly in the areas of social welfare and employment creation."

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Liệt kê giải pháp mà không giải thích tại sao hiệu quả

- ❌ "Governments should invest in education. They should also improve healthcare. Public transport should be better."
- ✅ "Governments **should** invest significantly in vocational education. **This would** equip graduates with the skills demanded by the modern labour market, thereby **reducing** the structural mismatch that underlies youth unemployment."

### Lỗi 2: Quá nhiều modal verbs giống nhau

- ❌ "Governments should... Schools should... Parents should... Employers should..."
- ✅ Xen kẽ: "must / could / might / ought to / need to / are required to / would benefit from"

### Lỗi 3: Giải pháp không liên kết với nguyên nhân đã nêu

Nếu Body 1 nêu nguyên nhân là thiếu kỹ năng thực tế, Body 2 phải đề xuất giải pháp liên quan đến đào tạo kỹ năng — không đề xuất chủ đề khác.

### Lỗi 4: Conclusion chỉ lặp lại Body thay vì tóm tắt và hướng ra ngoài

- ❌ "In conclusion, the causes are X and Y, and the solutions are A and B."
- ✅ "In conclusion, while [problem] is undeniably complex, targeted intervention at both individual and governmental levels **offers a realistic pathway** to meaningful improvement."

## Bài tập luyện

### Hoàn thành câu theo đúng cấu trúc

1. "One of the primary causes of homelessness ___" *(nêu nguyên nhân)*
2. "Governments should ___, which would ___." *(đề xuất + kết quả)*
3. "At the individual level, people can ___, while at the governmental level, ___." *(giải pháp hai cấp)*

### Viết lại câu sau bằng nominalization:

4. "People are increasingly obese because they eat too much junk food."
5. "It is difficult to access healthcare in rural areas."

### Gợi ý đáp án

1. "One of the primary causes of homelessness **is the shortage of affordable housing**, which **has been exacerbated by** rising property prices and insufficient government investment in social housing."
2. "Governments **should** introduce mandatory financial literacy programmes in schools, which **would** equip young people with the skills needed to manage debt responsibly."
3. "At the individual level, people **can** reduce energy consumption by making sustainable choices at home, while at the governmental level, **authorities should** introduce stricter building codes that mandate energy-efficient designs."
4. "**The rise in obesity** can be attributed to **the increasing consumption of** energy-dense, nutritionally poor foods."
5. "**Access to** healthcare in rural areas **remains** severely limited."

## Tóm tắt nhanh

```
INTRODUCTION PATTERNS:
  [X] has become an increasingly [pressing/serious] issue...
  This essay will examine... and propose...

PROBLEM/CAUSES:
  One of the primary causes of [X] is [Y].
  [X] can be largely attributed to [Y].
  [X] stems from / arises from [Y].
  A key contributing factor is...

SOLUTIONS — MODAL VERBS:
  must (strong obligation) | should (recommendation)
  could / might (suggestion) | would (conditional)
  It is essential / vital / crucial that...

SOLUTION STRUCTURE:
  At the individual level... | At the governmental level...
  This would result in / lead to / help to...
  By doing so, [subject] would...
  Such measures would significantly [reduce/alleviate/address]...

CONCLUSION:
  If these measures are implemented, [positive outcome].
  Ultimately, [broader point].

NOMINALIZATION:
  decided → decision | unemployed → unemployment
  solve → solution | congested → congestion
```
