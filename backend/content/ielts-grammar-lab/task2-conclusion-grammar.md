## Tóm tắt

| Chức năng | Cấu trúc |
|-----------|---------|
| **Mở kết bài** | In conclusion, / To conclude, / Overall, / On balance, |
| **Khẳng định lại quan điểm** | I maintain that... / it remains my view that... / the evidence suggests... |
| **Tóm tắt ý chính** | While [concession], the key argument is that... |
| **Forward-looking (optional)** | Unless [X] is addressed... / If [action], then [outcome]... |

---

## Tại sao quan trọng

Kết bài là phần cuối cùng examiner đọc — nó để lại ấn tượng cuối. Hai lỗi phổ biến nhất:

1. **Lặp nguyên văn mở bài** → nhàm chán, không có giá trị tổng hợp
2. **Đưa ý mới vào kết bài** → vi phạm nguyên tắc cơ bản của kết bài

Kết bài tốt làm được 3 việc trong 2-3 câu: **tóm tắt nhanh** → **khẳng định quan điểm bằng từ mới** → **kết thúc chắc chắn**.

---

## PHẦN 1: MỞ ĐẦU KẾT BÀI

### Các từ kết nối mở kết bài:

```
In conclusion, ...
To conclude, ...
Overall, ...
On balance, ...
To summarise, ...
In summary, ...
```

> **Lưu ý:** Chỉ dùng MỘT trong số này. Không viết "In conclusion, to summarise..." — thừa và lặp.

"**In conclusion**" và "**To conclude**" phổ biến nhất.
"**Overall**" và "**On balance**" nghe tự nhiên hơn và hơi informal hơn một chút — phù hợp khi muốn tạo cảm giác cân bằng.

---

## PHẦN 2: KHẲNG ĐỊNH LẠI QUAN ĐIỂM — Bằng ngôn ngữ mới

Đây là phần quan trọng nhất của kết bài. Không sao chép mở bài — **diễn đạt lại quan điểm bằng từ và cấu trúc khác**.

### Cấu trúc khẳng định lại:

```
I maintain that [position restated in new words].
It remains my view / firm belief that [position].
The evidence presented strongly supports the view that [position].
It is clear / evident that [position].
[X] is ultimately / undeniably [adjective/better/more important].
```

### So sánh weak vs strong:

| Weak (lặp cơ học) | Strong (tổng hợp + từ mới) |
|-------------------|---------------------------|
| "I agree that social media is bad." | "**I maintain that** the adverse effects of social media on mental health and social cohesion **far outweigh** its communicative benefits." |
| "The government should do more." | "**It remains my firm belief that** targeted government intervention is both necessary and justified to address this growing problem." |
| "Technology is both good and bad." | "**On balance**, while technology has undeniably transformed the way we live and work, **its unchecked use poses risks that cannot be ignored**." |

## PHẦN 3: TÓM TẮT Ý CHÍNH — Không liệt kê lại toàn bộ

Kết bài không phải là chỗ liệt kê lại hết body paragraphs. Chỉ cần một câu tóm tắt tinh gọn.

### Cấu trúc tóm tắt:

```
While [concession / other side], [main argument] ultimately [verb].
[Main point 1] and [main point 2] together demonstrate that...
By [doing X], [outcome follows].
Without [action], [negative consequence] is likely to worsen/continue.
```

**Ví dụ:**

- "**While** the economic arguments for deregulation are not without merit, the social and environmental costs **ultimately outweigh** the short-term financial gains."
- "**By investing** in renewable energy infrastructure now, governments **can** avoid far greater economic and environmental costs in the future."
- "**Without** adequate investment in mental health services, the long-term burden on healthcare systems **is likely to increase** considerably."

---

## PHẦN 4: FORWARD-LOOKING STATEMENT (tùy chọn)

Một câu nhìn về phía trước sau phần tóm tắt có thể nâng kết bài lên Band 7+. Nhưng không bắt buộc.

```
Governments / Individuals / Society must [action] if [positive outcome] is to be achieved.
Unless [X] is addressed urgently, [negative consequence] will persist.
Only by [doing X] can [desired outcome] be realised.
```

- "**Governments must** take decisive action to regulate the technology sector **if** the benefits of innovation **are to be** enjoyed equitably across society."
- "**Unless** educational systems adapt to the demands of a digital economy, many young people **will find themselves** ill-equipped for the workforce of the future."
- "**Only by** fostering genuine international cooperation **can** the world hope to address the existential challenge of climate change."

## PHẦN 5: KẾT BÀI HOÀN CHỈNH — CÁC MẪU ĐẦY ĐỦ

### Mẫu 1 — Opinion essay (agree/disagree):

```
In conclusion, [restate position in new words]. While [brief concession],
[main argument + key reason]. [Optional: forward-looking statement.]
```

> "**In conclusion**, I maintain that the widespread use of social media poses significant risks to individual wellbeing and social cohesion that cannot be dismissed. **While** its role in facilitating global communication is undeniable, the psychological harms — particularly for younger users — are too severe to overlook. **Governments and technology companies alike must take greater responsibility** for creating safer, more regulated digital environments."

### Mẫu 2 — Discussion essay:

```
In conclusion, although both sides have merit, [one side] is ultimately more compelling
because [key reason]. [Summary of key point.]
```

> "**In conclusion**, although both competition and cooperation have value in a child's development, **I believe that** nurturing collaborative skills is ultimately more beneficial, as it prepares young people for the realities of the modern workplace and a globally interconnected society. A well-rounded education system should, therefore, prioritise both — but with cooperation as its foundation."

### Mẫu 3 — Problem-solution essay:

```
In conclusion, [restate problem briefly]. However, [proposed solutions],
if implemented effectively, [positive outcome].
```

> "**In conclusion**, youth unemployment remains a deeply complex challenge with roots in both structural economic shifts and educational misalignment. **However**, a combination of vocational training programmes, business incentives for youth hiring, and curriculum reform represents a viable path forward. **If these measures are implemented thoughtfully**, the long-term economic and social benefits will be considerable."

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Kết bài là bản sao của mở bài

- ❌ Mở bài: "I firmly believe that social media is harmful to society."
  Kết bài: "In conclusion, I believe that social media is harmful to society."
- ✅ "**In conclusion**, the evidence presented in this essay **strongly supports** the view that digital social platforms have had a predominantly damaging effect on mental health and public discourse, and this issue demands urgent attention."

### Lỗi 2: Đưa ý mới vào kết bài

- ❌ "In conclusion, social media is harmful. Also, I think governments should tax technology companies more."
  *(thuế công nghệ = ý mới, không đề cập trong thân bài)*
- ✅ Kết bài chỉ tổng hợp và nhấn mạnh — không bổ sung luận điểm mới.

### Lỗi 3: Kết bài quá ngắn (1 câu)

- ❌ "In conclusion, I agree."
- ✅ 2-3 câu: mở kết bài + restate + tóm tắt/forward-looking.

### Lỗi 4: Kết bài quá dài (>4 câu)

- Kết bài không phải thân bài thứ ba. Tối đa 3-4 câu là đủ.

## Bài tập luyện

### Viết kết bài cho các đề sau (2-3 câu):

1. "Some people think that older employees are more valuable to companies than younger ones. Do you agree?"

2. "Many cities are expanding public transport systems. What are the advantages and disadvantages of this?"

### Đáp án gợi ý:

1. "**In conclusion**, I maintain that experience and institutional knowledge **do** make older employees highly valuable, particularly in roles that require judgment and leadership. **However**, organisations that fail to integrate younger workers risk losing innovation and adaptability — two qualities that are increasingly essential in a rapidly changing economy. **A workforce that balances both** is ultimately the most resilient."

2. "**Overall**, the expansion of public transport systems **offers considerable benefits** in terms of reduced congestion, lower carbon emissions, and improved accessibility for those who cannot afford private vehicles. **While** the financial and logistical challenges of such projects are significant, the long-term social and environmental gains **justify** the investment. **Effective planning and community consultation** are key to ensuring these systems serve their intended purpose."

## Tóm tắt nhanh

```
KẾT BÀI = MỞ + RESTATE + TÓM TẮT (+ FORWARD-LOOKING)

MỞ: In conclusion, / To conclude, / Overall, / On balance,

RESTATE (bằng từ mới):
  "I maintain that..."
  "It remains my view that..."
  "The evidence suggests that..."
  "X ultimately outweighs Y."

TÓM TẮT:
  "While [concession], [main argument]."
  "By [action], [outcome]."
  "Without [X], [consequence]."

FORWARD-LOOKING (optional):
  "Governments must... if [outcome] is to be achieved."
  "Unless [X] is addressed, [negative result]."

TRÁNH:
  → Sao chép mở bài
  → Đưa ý mới
  → Quá ngắn (1 câu) hoặc quá dài (>4 câu)
```
