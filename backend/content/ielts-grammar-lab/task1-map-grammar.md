## Tóm tắt

| Loại map | Thì chính | Đặc điểm |
|---------|---------|---------|
| **Map tại một thời điểm** | Present simple | Mô tả vị trí hiện tại |
| **Map thay đổi (past → present)** | Past simple / Present perfect | Mô tả sự thay đổi theo thời gian |
| **Two maps cùng thời điểm** | Present simple | So sánh vị trí, đặc điểm |

---

## Tại sao quan trọng

Map question khác các dạng Task 1 khác vì không có số liệu — bạn mô tả **vị trí, cấu trúc, và thay đổi**. Hai nhóm ngôn ngữ quan trọng nhất là:
1. **Prepositions of location** — để mô tả chính xác vị trí
2. **Change language** — để mô tả những gì đã thêm, bị xoá, hoặc được thay thế

Không có hai nhóm này, bài map sẽ rất nghèo nàn về từ vựng.

---

## PHẦN 1: PREPOSITIONS OF LOCATION — Từ vị trí cốt lõi

### Vị trí cơ bản:

| Giới từ | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|-------|
| **in the north/south/east/west** | ở phía bắc/nam/đông/tây | A lake is situated **in the north** of the island. |
| **in the centre / in the middle** | ở trung tâm | The town hall is located **in the centre** of the town. |
| **in the north-east / south-west** | ở góc đông bắc / tây nam | A car park **in the north-east** corner |
| **to the north of** | ở phía bắc của | The school is located **to the north of** the main road. |
| **next to / beside** | kề bên | The library is **next to** the post office. |
| **adjacent to** | liền kề | The new building is **adjacent to** the old museum. |
| **opposite** | đối diện | The supermarket is **opposite** the park. |
| **between A and B** | giữa A và B | The station is **between** the hotel **and** the bus terminal. |
| **along** | dọc theo | Shops are located **along** the main street. |
| **on the outskirts of** | ở vùng ngoại ô | A new housing estate was built **on the outskirts of** the town. |
| **surrounding** | bao quanh | Farmland **surrounding** the village was converted to residential use. |

### Hướng đi, tuyến đường:

- **runs from [A] to [B]** — A new road **runs from** the town centre **to** the industrial zone.
- **leads to** — The main road **leads to** the harbour.
- **connects [A] with [B]** — A bridge **connects** the two islands.
- **crosses** — The railway line **crosses** the river near the eastern boundary.
- **extends along** — The beach **extends along** the western coastline.

## PHẦN 2: NGÔN NGỮ THAY ĐỔI — Map qua thời gian

### Đã xây dựng thêm (new addition):

```
A [building/road/area] was built / constructed / created / developed
A [building/road/area] has been built / added / established
A new [feature] appeared / was introduced
```

- A new **shopping centre was constructed** to the north of the town.
- A **car park has been built** adjacent to the railway station.
- **Several residential areas were developed** on what was previously farmland.
- A **pedestrian zone was introduced** along the high street.

### Đã bị phá bỏ / biến mất:

```
[Feature] was demolished / removed / cleared / pulled down
[Feature] disappeared / was replaced by [new feature]
[Feature] no longer exists
```

- The old factory **was demolished** and replaced by a housing estate.
- The woodland **was cleared** to make way for the new bypass.
- The small cottages that previously occupied this area **no longer exist**.
- The original farmhouse **was torn down** and the land **was converted** to commercial use.

### Đã được mở rộng hoặc thay đổi:

```
[Feature] was extended / expanded / widened / enlarged
[Feature] was converted into [new use]
[Feature] was upgraded / renovated / modernised
[Area] changed significantly / underwent significant development
```

- The main road **was widened** to accommodate increased traffic flow.
- The old hospital **was converted into** a museum.
- The town centre **underwent significant redevelopment** between 1990 and 2020.
- The industrial zone **was expanded** eastward.

### Vẫn không thay đổi:

```
[Feature] remained / stayed the same
[Feature] was retained / preserved
[Feature] continued to occupy [location]
```

- The church in the town centre **remained unchanged** throughout the period.
- The river **continued to flow** along the northern boundary.
- The school **was retained**, though its surrounding area changed considerably.

## PHẦN 3: THAY ĐỔI — BẢNG TỔNG HỢP

| Loại thay đổi | Cấu trúc |
|--------------|---------|
| Xây mới | was built / was constructed / was developed |
| Phá bỏ | was demolished / was removed / was torn down |
| Thay thế | was replaced by / gave way to |
| Mở rộng | was extended / was expanded / was enlarged |
| Chuyển đổi công năng | was converted into / was transformed into |
| Không đổi | remained / was retained / continued to |

## PHẦN 4: CẤU TRÚC CÂU MÔ TẢ VỊ TRÍ

### Mẫu câu chủ động:

```
[Feature] is located in/to the [direction] of [reference point].
[Feature] can be found [location description].
[Feature] lies [location].
[Feature] stands [location].
```

- The library **is located** in the north-east of the town, near the park.
- A petrol station **can be found** on the outskirts, adjacent to the motorway.
- The old cathedral **lies** in the heart of the city.

### Mẫu câu bị động:

```
[Feature] is situated [location].
[Feature] is positioned [location].
[Feature] is surrounded by [other features].
```

- The hotel **is situated** to the south of the town centre, opposite the beach.
- The industrial estate **is positioned** to the east, separated from residential areas by a green belt.
- The city hall **is surrounded by** parks and gardens on three sides.

### Mẫu câu với mệnh đề quan hệ:

```
[Feature], which is located [direction], ...
[Feature] that lies [location] ...
```

- The school, **which is located** in the western part of the town, was demolished in 2010.
- The bridge **that connects** the two banks of the river was upgraded during the redevelopment.

## PHẦN 5: OVERVIEW CHO MAP QUESTION

Overview cho map cần bao gồm:
1. Loại thay đổi tổng thể (nhiều thay đổi hay ít?)
2. Xu hướng chung (phát triển về hướng nào? Khu vực nào thay đổi nhiều nhất?)

### Mẫu Overview (map thay đổi):

```
Overall, [place] underwent significant/considerable development between [year1] and [year2].
The most notable changes occurred in the [area/direction], where...
While some features were retained, many new [buildings/roads/areas] were added/constructed.
```

**Ví dụ:**

- "**Overall**, the town **underwent considerable development** between 1990 and 2020. **The most significant changes** occurred in the eastern and northern areas, where new residential zones and commercial facilities **were introduced**. **The town centre**, however, **retained** several original features, including the church and the market square."

- "**Overall**, while the basic layout of the island **remained largely unchanged**, the southern coastal area **saw substantial development**, with the addition of tourist facilities and a new road network."

## PHẦN 6: VÍ DỤ ĐOẠN BODY HOÀN CHỈNH

**Bản đồ thị trấn năm 1990 và 2020:**

> "**In 1990**, the town was relatively small, with most buildings **concentrated** in the central area. A school **occupied** the land to the north of the high street, **surrounded by** open farmland. **To the south**, a small residential area **extended** along the river bank.
>
> **By 2020**, the town **had expanded** considerably. **The farmland to the north** of the school **was converted into** a large housing estate, with a new supermarket **constructed** at its western edge. **The residential area to the south** was significantly **enlarged**, and a leisure centre **was built** adjacent to the river. The school itself **was retained**, though it **was modernised** and expanded. **The only feature that remained completely unchanged** was the parish church in the town centre."

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Dùng sai giới từ vị trí

- ❌ "The school is **at** the north of the town."
- ✅ "The school is **in** the north of the town." *(dùng "in" cho vùng, khu vực)*
- ✅ "The school is **to** the north of the river." *(dùng "to" khi có điểm tham chiếu cụ thể)*

### Lỗi 2: Không dùng passive khi mô tả thay đổi

- ❌ "They built a new hospital in 2005."
- ✅ "A new hospital **was built** in 2005."

### Lỗi 3: Không phân biệt thì khi mô tả map thay đổi

- Map 1990: dùng **past simple** — "was located", "occupied"
- Map 2020: dùng **present simple** — "is located", "stands"
- Thay đổi: **was built, was demolished, was replaced**

### Lỗi 4: Liệt kê mà không tổ chức theo khu vực

Thay vì mô tả từng feature một cách ngẫu nhiên, tổ chức theo **khu vực địa lý** (north, south, centre...) để bài có cấu trúc rõ ràng.

### Lỗi 5: Bỏ qua Overview

Map question cũng cần Overview — tóm tắt thay đổi chính, không mô tả chi tiết.

```
  along / on the outskirts of / surrounding

THAY ĐỔI:
  Xây mới:    was built / was constructed / was developed
  Phá bỏ:     was demolished / was removed / was torn down
  Thay thế:   was replaced by / gave way to
  Mở rộng:    was extended / was enlarged / was expanded
  Chuyển đổi: was converted into / was transformed into
  Không đổi:  remained / was retained / continued to

THÌ:
  Map hiện tại: present simple
  Map quá khứ: past simple
  Thay đổi: was built, was demolished (past passive)

OVERVIEW:
  [Place] underwent significant development between [year1] and [year2].
  The most notable changes occurred in the [area].
  While [old feature] was retained, [new feature] was added.
```
