## Tóm tắt

| Phần mở bài | Cấu trúc cốt lõi |
|-------------|-----------------|
| **Paraphrase chủ đề** | It is widely argued / debated / recognised that... |
| **Bối cảnh** | In recent years / decades, [topic] has become... |
| **Quan điểm rõ (opinion essay)** | This essay will argue that... / I firmly believe that... |
| **Giới thiệu trung lập (discussion essay)** | This essay will examine both sides... / This essay will discuss... |

---

## Tại sao quan trọng

Mở bài Task 2 không cần dài hay phức tạp — examiner đọc hàng trăm bài mỗi năm và họ biết ngay từ 2-3 câu đầu người viết có hiểu đề hay không. Hai lỗi phổ biến nhất:

1. **Sao chép nguyên văn đề bài** → bị trừ điểm Task Response
2. **Quan điểm mờ nhạt hoặc không có** → examiner không biết bài muốn nói gì

Các cấu trúc trong bài này giúp bạn **paraphrase tự nhiên** và **nêu lập trường rõ ràng** ngay từ đầu bài.

---

## PHẦN 1: PARAPHRASE CHỦ ĐỀ — Diễn giải lại, không sao chép

### Công thức bối cảnh + chủ đề:

```
It is widely argued / believed / recognised that [paraphrase of topic].
[Topic] has become an increasingly [contentious / complex / pressing] issue in recent years.
In recent decades, [topic] has attracted growing attention from [the public / governments / researchers].
The question of whether [topic] has long been a subject of considerable debate.
```

**Ví dụ đề bài:** *"Many people believe that social media has had a negative effect on society."*

| Đề gốc | Paraphrase |
|--------|-----------|
| social media | online platforms / digital social networks |
| has had a negative effect on | has adversely affected / has done more harm than good to |
| society | communities / public life / individuals and communities |

- "**It is widely debated whether** the rise of online platforms has done more harm than good to communities and individuals."
- "**In recent years**, digital social networking **has become** an increasingly controversial aspect of modern life."
- "**The question of whether** social media is ultimately damaging to society **has attracted growing attention** from researchers, parents, and policymakers alike."

### Kỹ thuật paraphrase:

| Phương pháp | Ví dụ |
|-------------|-------|
| Đổi từ (synonym) | *negative* → *detrimental / harmful / damaging* |
| Đổi từ loại (word form) | *negative effect* → *negatively affecting* |
| Đổi cấu trúc câu | *A affects B* → *B is affected by A* |
| Generalise / Specificise | *young people* → *the younger generation / teenagers* |

## PHẦN 2: THESIS STATEMENT — Nêu quan điểm rõ ràng

### Dạng 1: Opinion essay ("To what extent do you agree?")

```
This essay will argue that [your position].
I firmly believe that [your position], [optional nuance].
It is my contention that [your position].
In my view, [your position], although [acknowledgement of other side].
While [opposing view], I would argue that [your position].
```

- "**This essay will argue that**, while social media does offer some communicative benefits, its overall impact on society is predominantly negative."
- "**I firmly believe that** governments should take greater responsibility for public health, **provided that** individual freedoms are not unduly restricted."
- "**While** some may argue that economic growth should take priority, **I contend that** environmental sustainability is the more pressing concern in the long term."

### Dạng 2: Discussion essay ("Discuss both views")

```
This essay will examine both sides of the argument before concluding that [position].
This essay will discuss [side A] and [side B], arguing that [your view].
Both perspectives will be considered in this essay, though [your leaning].
```

- "**This essay will examine both** the potential advantages and the significant risks of [topic], **before concluding that** the latter outweigh the former."
- "**Both perspectives will be considered**, though **it will be argued that** a balanced approach is ultimately more effective."

### Dạng 3: Problem-solution / Cause-effect essay

```
This essay will first examine [cause/problem] before suggesting [solution/effect].
The causes of [X] and possible solutions will be explored in this essay.
```

- "**This essay will first examine** the key factors contributing to rising youth unemployment **before suggesting** a range of government-led and individual measures to address this issue."

## PHẦN 3: CẤU TRÚC MỞ BÀI HOÀN CHỈNH

Mở bài Task 2 lý tưởng thường có **2-3 câu**:

```
Câu 1: Paraphrase + bối cảnh
Câu 2: (Optional) Mở rộng hoặc nêu hai phía vắn tắt
Câu 3: Thesis statement — quan điểm rõ ràng
```

### Ví dụ hoàn chỉnh — Opinion essay:

**Đề:** *"In many countries, more and more people are choosing to live alone. Do you think this is a positive or negative development?"*

> "**The growing trend** of single-person households **has become** a notable feature of modern urban life in many parts of the world. **While** this shift reflects a greater degree of personal freedom and independence, **I believe that**, on balance, it raises significant concerns about social isolation and community cohesion. **This essay will argue that** the disadvantages of living alone outweigh the benefits, particularly for younger and elderly populations."

### Ví dụ hoàn chỉnh — Discussion essay:

**Đề:** *"Some people think that children should be taught to compete, while others believe cooperation is more important."*

> "**The question of whether** competitive or collaborative values should be instilled in children from an early age **is one that** has long divided educators and parents. **On one hand**, competition can drive motivation and personal achievement. **On the other**, cooperation is widely seen as essential for building harmonious communities. **This essay will discuss both perspectives before arguing that** a balanced approach, one that nurtures both skills, is the most effective approach to children's development."

## PHẦN 4: CẢNH BÁO TEMPLATE — Khi mẫu câu giúp và khi không

Các cấu trúc trong bài này là **scaffold hữu ích**, không phải công thức bảo đảm điểm cao. Examiner đọc hàng trăm bài dùng "This essay will argue that..." và "In recent years, X has become..." mỗi năm — những cấu trúc này không tạo ấn tượng xấu, nhưng cũng không tạo ấn tượng tốt. Điều quyết định band là **nội dung và lập trường**, không phải vỏ câu.

### Dấu hiệu intro bị "template hóa" quá mức:

- ❌ Thesis lặp lại chủ đề mà không nêu được lập trường: *"This essay will discuss the advantages and disadvantages."*
- ❌ Paraphrase chỉ thay từ đồng nghĩa, giữ nguyên cấu trúc câu đề bài
- ❌ Intro đúng form nhưng thesis không cụ thể — examiner đọc xong không biết bạn nghĩ gì

### Làm cho thesis tự nhiên và thuyết phục hơn:

Thêm **điều kiện, phạm vi, hoặc góc nhìn** vào thesis — thay vì chỉ nêu lập trường đơn giản:

| Mẫu template | Cụ thể hơn |
|-------------|-----------|
| "I agree that technology is beneficial." | "Technology is broadly beneficial, **though its impact depends heavily on how it is used and by whom**." |
| "This essay will argue that education should be free." | "Free higher education is a worthwhile goal, **provided governments address the systemic underfunding it would create**." |
| "There are advantages and disadvantages." | "**While the short-term benefits are clear**, the long-term consequences deserve considerably more scrutiny." |

> Examiner không đánh giá bạn vì dùng hay không dùng "This essay will argue that" — họ đánh giá bạn vì lập trường sau đó có **rõ ràng, có thuyết phục, và nhất quán với thân bài** không.

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Sao chép nguyên câu đề bài

- ❌ "Many people believe that social media has had a negative effect on society." *(y nguyên đề)*
- ✅ "The influence of digital social platforms on community life **has become** an increasingly debated topic in recent years."

### Lỗi 2: Không có thesis statement

- ❌ "This is an interesting topic. There are many aspects to consider."
- ✅ "**This essay will argue that** the benefits of [X] outweigh the drawbacks, provided adequate safeguards are in place."

### Lỗi 3: Quan điểm mâu thuẫn hoặc lật ngược trong bài

- Nếu mở bài viết "I agree", toàn bài phải nhất quán theo lập trường đó. Nếu "largely agree", thừa nhận một ý đối lập nhỏ là ổn.

### Lỗi 4: Mở bài quá dài

- Mở bài chỉ cần 2-3 câu. Không cần nêu luận điểm chi tiết ngay ở đây — để cho thân bài.

## Bài tập luyện

### Paraphrase những đề sau (đừng sao chép):

1. "Many governments spend large amounts of money on art and culture."
2. "Young people today are less fit than previous generations."
3. "Technology is making people more isolated."

### Viết thesis statement cho:

4. "Some say university education should be free for all. To what extent do you agree?"
5. "Some people think working from home has more advantages than disadvantages. Discuss."

### Đáp án gợi ý:

1. "The allocation of significant public funding to **the arts and cultural sector** has become a contentious issue in a number of countries."
2. "**A decline in physical fitness** among today's youth, relative to earlier generations, is an increasingly recognised public health concern."
3. "**Despite its many benefits**, modern technology has been linked to growing levels of social disconnection in contemporary society."
4. "**I strongly believe that** tertiary education should be made available to all citizens regardless of financial means, as this would benefit both individuals and society as a whole."
5. "**This essay will explore both perspectives**, ultimately arguing that remote work offers substantial advantages, though its effectiveness depends heavily on the nature of the work and individual circumstances."

## Tóm tắt nhanh

```
MỞ BÀI = PARAPHRASE + THESIS

PARAPHRASE — Đổi từ, đổi cấu trúc, KHÔNG sao chép:
  "It is widely debated whether..."
  "In recent years, [topic] has become..."
  "The question of whether [X] has attracted..."

THESIS — Nêu rõ lập trường:
  Opinion: "This essay will argue that..."
           "I firmly believe that..."
           "While [A], I contend that [B]..."
  Discussion: "This essay will examine both sides before..."
  Problem-solution: "This essay will first examine [X] before suggesting..."

ĐỘ DÀI: 2-3 câu là đủ. Không cần dài.

TRÁNH:
  → Sao chép đề
  → Quan điểm không rõ
  → Mở bài quá dài (>4 câu)
```
