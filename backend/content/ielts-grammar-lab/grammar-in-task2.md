## Tóm tắt

IELTS Writing Task 2 là một bài luận học thuật dài 250+ từ. Điểm Grammar phụ thuộc vào:

- **Grammatical Range:** dùng đa dạng cấu trúc — simple, compound, complex, mixed
- **Grammatical Accuracy:** không mắc lỗi cơ bản lặp đi lặp lại
- **Hedging:** dùng modal verbs để tránh nói tuyệt đối — cần thiết trong văn học thuật
- **Cohesion:** discourse markers kết nối ý mạch lạc

## Tại sao quan trọng

Grammatical Range and Accuracy chiếm 25% điểm Task 2. Để đạt Band 7, bài viết cần "use a variety of complex structures with some flexibility and accuracy." Band 6 chỉ cần "use a mix of simple and complex sentence forms." Nắm được các cấu trúc dưới đây sẽ tạo ra sự khác biệt rõ rệt.

## Phần 1: Câu điều kiện (Conditionals) trong lập luận

Conditionals giúp xây dựng lập luận mạch lạc — đặt ra điều kiện và dự đoán kết quả.

### Type 1 — Lập luận thực tế có thể xảy ra

```
If + Present Simple, will + V
```

Dùng khi đề xuất giải pháp hoặc cảnh báo hậu quả:

- **If** governments invest in renewable energy, carbon emissions **will** decrease significantly.
- The problem **will** worsen **if** no action is taken in the coming years.
- **If** young people receive better vocational training, unemployment **will** fall.

### Type 2 — Giả định lý tưởng / đề xuất chính sách

```
If + Past Simple, would + V
```

Dùng khi giả định điều hiện chưa xảy ra nhưng đáng được xem xét:

- **If** education were free for all citizens, social inequality **would** be dramatically reduced.
- **If** companies paid workers a living wage, productivity **would** likely improve.
- **If** I were in charge of policy, I **would** prioritise public transport over road expansion.

> **Lưu ý "were":** Trong văn học thuật, dùng "were" cho tất cả các ngôi trong Type 2:
> - If this **were** the case, the consequences would be severe. ✅

### Type 3 — Đánh giá chính sách / lịch sử

```
If + Past Perfect, would have + V3
```

Dùng khi phân tích điều đã xảy ra trong quá khứ:

- **If** stricter regulations **had been introduced** earlier, many environmental problems **would not have developed**.
- The financial crisis **might have been avoided** **if** banks **had been** more carefully regulated.

## Phần 2: Modal Verbs — Hedging trong Task 2

Văn học thuật không nói tuyệt đối. Modal verbs giúp thể hiện mức độ chắc chắn một cách tinh tế.

### Tại sao cần hedging?

Nói quá tuyệt đối nghe thiếu học thuật và dễ bị phản bác:
- ❌ "Social media **destroys** relationships." (quá tuyệt đối)
- ✅ "Social media **can have** a damaging effect on personal relationships." (hedging)

### Thang độ chắc chắn

| Mức độ | Modal / Cụm từ | Ví dụ |
|--------|----------------|-------|
| Chắc chắn cao | will, is likely to | This trend **will** continue. |
| Khá chắc | should, would | Education **should** be accessible to all. |
| Trung bình | may, could | This **may** lead to increased inequality. |
| Thấp / Giả định | might, could possibly | Some people **might** argue that... |
| Khách quan | it is argued that, it is suggested that | **It is suggested that** technology is the main cause. |

### Cách hedging quan điểm

**Hedging mức độ:**
- Technology **can** transform education in ways we haven't yet imagined.
- This approach **may** not be suitable for all contexts.
- Urban migration **could** place enormous strain on infrastructure.

**Hedging quan điểm người khác:**
- **It could be argued that** social media does more harm than good.
- **Some people believe that** governments should take full responsibility.
- **There is a school of thought that** economic growth always benefits everyone.

**Khẳng định quan điểm cá nhân có kiểm soát:**
- I **would argue that** education is the most powerful tool against poverty.
- In my view, this policy **is likely to** achieve limited results.
- **It seems clear that** more funding is needed in this area.

## Phần 3: Relative Clauses — Viết câu phức

Relative clauses giúp gộp ý vào một câu dài hơn và phức tạp hơn — điều cần thiết cho Band 7+.

### Defining relative clause (không có dấu phẩy)

- Cities **that invest heavily in public transport** tend to have lower pollution levels.
- Students **who receive early childhood education** are more likely to succeed academically.
- The policies **which were introduced in 2010** have had limited success.

### Non-defining relative clause (có dấu phẩy)

Dùng khi thêm thông tin bổ sung — không bỏ được mà câu vẫn hiểu:

- Singapore, **which has invested heavily in education**, consistently ranks among the world's most competitive economies.
- The global population, **which is expected to reach 10 billion by 2050**, will place enormous pressure on food systems.

### Reduced relative clause — viết gọn hơn

- Countries **investing in renewables** (= that invest) tend to reduce emissions faster.
- The report **published last year** (= that was published) highlighted several key findings.
- Students **enrolled in the programme** (= who are enrolled) showed significant improvements.

### Sentence relative clause với "which"

Dùng "which" để bình luận về cả mệnh đề trước:

- Governments have significantly increased education spending, **which has led to** improved literacy rates.
- More young people are choosing to study abroad, **which suggests that** they value international experience.

## Phần 4: Câu bị động — Tăng tính học thuật

Câu bị động phù hợp hơn trong văn học thuật vì không cần xác định chủ ngữ và tạo khoảng cách khách quan.

### Khi nào dùng bị động

- Không cần hoặc không biết chủ ngữ: *The report **was published** in 2022.*
- Muốn nhấn mạnh kết quả hơn tác nhân: *Significant progress **has been made** in renewable energy.*
- Tạo tông giọng học thuật khách quan: *It **is widely believed** that...*

### Passive reporting verbs — rất phổ biến trong Task 2

```
It is + V3 + that + clause
```

| Công thức | Ví dụ |
|-----------|-------|
| It **is argued** that | It is argued that urban living increases stress. |
| It **is believed** that | It is believed that education is a human right. |
| It **is widely acknowledged** that | It is widely acknowledged that climate change poses a major threat. |
| It **has been shown** that | It has been shown that early intervention improves outcomes. |
| It **is often claimed** that | It is often claimed that technology reduces employment. |

## Phần 5: Discourse Markers — Tổ chức đoạn văn

Discourse markers giúp bài viết mạch lạc và dễ theo dõi.

### Cấu trúc đoạn thân bài (body paragraph)

```
Topic sentence → Explanation → Evidence/Example → Link back
```

**Ví dụ đoạn văn hoàn chỉnh với discourse markers:**

*One significant advantage of remote working is the positive effect it has on employee wellbeing. **Specifically**, workers who avoid long commutes **tend to** report lower stress levels and greater job satisfaction. **Furthermore**, studies have shown that flexible working arrangements **can lead to** increased productivity, as employees are able to work during their most productive hours. **However**, it should be noted that not all roles are suited to remote work, **which suggests that** a hybrid model **may be** the most practical solution for many organisations.*

### Cấu trúc mở đoạn đa dạng (tránh luôn bắt đầu bằng "Firstly")

| Cách mở đoạn | Ví dụ |
|-------------|-------|
| Topic sentence đơn giản | One key advantage of this approach is... |
| Câu hỏi tu từ | Why do so many young people choose to leave rural areas? |
| Thừa nhận quan điểm đối lập | While it is true that technology creates some risks, the benefits are considerable. |
| Số liệu / sự kiện | According to recent studies, over 60% of workers prefer flexible working arrangements. |
| Câu điều kiện | If governments fail to act, the consequences for future generations will be severe. |

## Lỗi thường gặp trong Task 2

### Lỗi 1: Dùng "will" trong if-clause

- ❌ If the government **will invest** more in education, the results will improve.
- ✅ If the government **invests** more in education, the results will improve.

### Lỗi 2: Lạm dụng một cấu trúc

Dùng đi dùng lại "In addition, ... In addition, ... In addition" → đơn điệu. Xen kẽ: *Furthermore, Moreover, What is more, Not only... but also.*

### Lỗi 3: Câu quá ngắn và đơn giản

- ❌ Technology is bad. It causes many problems. People use it too much.
- ✅ **Although** technology offers many benefits, **it is widely acknowledged that** excessive use **can have** a detrimental effect on mental health, **particularly** among younger generations.

### Lỗi 4: Nói tuyệt đối — thiếu học thuật

- ❌ Social media always has a negative effect on society.
- ✅ Social media **can have** a negative effect on society, **particularly when** used excessively.

### Lỗi 5: Mở đoạn bằng "I think" (không học thuật)

- ❌ I think that education is very important.
- ✅ **It is widely acknowledged that** education plays a fundamental role in social development.
- ✅ **I would argue that** education is the single most effective tool for reducing inequality.

## Bài tập luyện

### Cải thiện câu sau bằng cách thêm relative clause

1. Governments should invest in renewable energy. Renewable energy is becoming cheaper every year.
2. Social media has transformed communication. It was developed primarily for entertainment.
3. Many students struggle financially. They cannot afford university tuition fees.

### Thêm hedging vào câu tuyệt đối

1. Technology destroys traditional jobs.
2. Children who use social media become depressed.
3. Urban living is more stressful than rural living.

### Đáp án

**Relative clause:**
1. Governments should invest in renewable energy, **which is becoming cheaper every year**.
2. Social media, **which was developed primarily for entertainment**, has transformed global communication.
3. Many students **who cannot afford university tuition fees** struggle financially.

**Hedging:**
1. Technology **can** destroy / **may displace** traditional jobs.
2. Children who use social media **may be more likely to** experience depression.
3. Urban living **is often considered** more stressful than rural living / Urban living **tends to be** more stressful.

## Tóm tắt nhanh

| Mục đích | Cấu trúc |
|----------|---------|
| Đề xuất giải pháp thực tế | If + Present Simple, will + V (Type 1) |
| Giả định lý tưởng | If + Past Simple, would + V (Type 2) |
| Đánh giá quá khứ | If + Past Perfect, would have + V3 (Type 3) |
| Hedging nhẹ | may, might, could + V |
| Hedging mạnh | it is argued/believed/widely acknowledged that |
| Thêm thông tin vào câu | relative clause (who/which/that/where) |
| Tính học thuật khách quan | Passive reporting verbs (it is shown that...) |
| Kết nối ý | discourse markers (furthermore, however, consequently) |

**Quy tắc vàng cho Task 2:**
- **Đừng bao giờ** dùng "will" trong if-clause
- **Đa dạng cấu trúc câu** — xen kẽ simple, compound, complex
- **Hedging** mọi quan điểm — tránh nói tuyệt đối
- **Discourse markers** phản ánh đúng mối quan hệ logic (thêm ý vs tương phản vs kết quả)
