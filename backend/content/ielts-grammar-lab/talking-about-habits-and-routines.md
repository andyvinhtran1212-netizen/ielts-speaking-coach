## Tóm tắt

| Loại thói quen | Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|----------------|---------|-------|
| **Thói quen hiện tại — thường xuyên** | usually / always / often + V | I usually wake up at 7. |
| **Thói quen hiện tại — thỉnh thoảng** | sometimes / occasionally / now and then | I occasionally cook at home. |
| **Thói quen thường xuyên (emphasis)** | tend to / make a point of | I tend to exercise in the morning. |
| **Thói quen mới** | I've started + V-ing / I've been + V-ing | I've started cycling to work. |
| **Thói quen trong quá khứ** | used to + V / would + V (past habit) | I used to watch a lot of TV. |
| **Thói quen đang thay đổi** | I'm trying to / I'm cutting down on | I'm trying to read more. |

---

## Tại sao quan trọng

IELTS Speaking Part 1 thường hỏi về daily routines: "Do you usually cook at home?", "How often do you exercise?", "Did you read much as a child?". Người học thường trả lời quá ngắn hoặc chỉ dùng "I always..." / "I never...". Bài này giúp bạn đa dạng hóa cách nói về thói quen và mở rộng câu trả lời một cách tự nhiên.

---

## PHẦN 1: THÓI QUEN HIỆN TẠI — Present Simple + Frequency Adverbs

### Trạng từ tần suất:

```
always → usually → often → sometimes → occasionally → rarely → never
(100%)                                                              (0%)
```

- I **always** have coffee in the morning — I can't function without it.
- I **usually** cook dinner at home, though I order takeaway on Fridays.
- I **often** go for a walk after work — it helps me clear my head.
- I **sometimes** stay up too late reading.
- I **occasionally** go to the cinema, maybe once a month or so.
- I **rarely** watch TV — I find it hard to sit still for that long.
- I **hardly ever** skip breakfast.
- I **never** exercise at night — it keeps me awake.

> **Vị trí:** Trạng từ tần suất thường đứng **trước động từ chính** nhưng **sau "be"**:
> - "I **usually** walk to work." ✅
> - "I **am always** tired on Mondays." ✅

### Cụm thời gian thay thế:

Thay vì chỉ dùng trạng từ, thêm cụm thời gian để cụ thể hơn:

- "I go to the gym **three times a week**."
- "I cook at home **most evenings**."
- "I check my phone **first thing in the morning**."
- "I try to read **for at least half an hour** before bed."
- "I meet up with friends **every couple of weeks**."

---

## PHẦN 2: TEND TO — Nói về thói quen tự nhiên hơn

"Tend to" nghe tự nhiên hơn "usually" và không quá tuyệt đối:

- "I **tend to** wake up quite early — usually around 6."
- "I **tend to** prefer mornings for anything that requires focus."
- "I **tend to** eat out more during the week when I'm busy."
- "I **tend to** be quite active — I don't like sitting still for too long."

### Kết hợp với "quite" / "fairly" để mềm hóa:

- "I'm **quite** disciplined about exercise — I **tend to** go every other day."
- "I'm **fairly** consistent with my sleep schedule during the week."

---

## PHẦN 3: THÓI QUEN TRONG QUÁ KHỨ — Used to / Would

### used to + bare infinitive:

Dùng để nói về thói quen hoặc trạng thái **trong quá khứ, không còn đúng nữa**:

- "I **used to** watch a lot of TV as a child, but I barely watch it now."
- "I **used to** cycle to school every day."
- "I **didn't use to** enjoy cooking — it's only something I've gotten into recently."
- "I **used to** be quite shy about speaking English, but I'm more confident now."

### would + bare infinitive (past habits — more literary):

Dùng để kể về thói quen cụ thể trong quá khứ (thường kết hợp với bối cảnh):

- "When I was at university, I **would** stay up until 2am studying."
- "Growing up, we **would** eat dinner together as a family every evening."
- "Every summer, my family **would** drive to the coast for a week."

> **Lưu ý:** "Would" **không dùng** cho trạng thái (state verbs như be, have, know, believe):
> - ❌ "I **would be** very shy." → ✅ "I **used to be** very shy."

---

## PHẦN 4: THÓI QUEN ĐANG THAY ĐỔI HOẶC MỚI

### I've started / I've been + V-ing:

- "I've **started** cycling to work recently — it saves time and keeps me fit."
- "I've **been trying to** read more — I'm aiming for one book a month."
- "I've **been making an effort to** cook at home more often."

### I'm trying to / I'm working on:

- "I'm **trying to** wake up earlier, but it's a work in progress."
- "I'm **working on** building a better morning routine."
- "I'm **cutting down on** caffeine — I was drinking way too much coffee."

### I've gradually / slowly:

- "I've **gradually** stopped watching TV and replaced it with podcasts."
- "I've **slowly** gotten into meditation over the past year."

---

## PHẦN 5: PHÁT TRIỂN CÂU TRẢ LỜI PART 1

Câu trả lời Part 1 lý tưởng: **2-4 câu** — không quá ngắn, không quá dài.

### Cấu trúc: Habit + Reason/Context + Detail/Contrast

```
[Describe habit] + [reason/context] + [extra detail or contrast].
```

### Ví dụ Part 1:

> Q: "Do you usually cook at home?"
>
> ❌ "Yes, I usually cook at home."
>
> ✅ "Yes, I **tend to** cook most evenings — I find it quite relaxing after a long day at work. I'm not a very adventurous cook, though. I **usually** stick to simple Vietnamese dishes, and I **occasionally** try something new at weekends."

> Q: "How do you usually spend your evenings?"
>
> ✅ "It **depends** on the day, honestly. On weekdays, I **tend to** keep things quite low-key — I **usually** read or listen to a podcast for a bit before bed. At weekends, I'm more likely to go out, though I **don't** do that every week. I've **been trying to** spend less time on my phone in the evenings — with mixed results."

> Q: "Did you watch a lot of TV when you were younger?"
>
> ✅ "Definitely — I **used to** watch TV for hours after school. There were a few shows I was completely obsessed with. These days, I **barely** watch it; I **tend to** stream things when I do, and I'm much more selective about what I watch."

## PHẦN 6: TỪ VỰNG BỔ SUNG

### Nói về tần suất cụ thể hơn:

- "every day / every other day / every few days"
- "once a week / twice a month / a few times a year"
- "on weekdays / at weekends / on Friday evenings"
- "in the morning / last thing at night / first thing when I wake up"

### Nói về độ nhất quán:

- "I'm **quite consistent** about it."
- "I **try** to do it regularly, but I'm not always disciplined."
- "It's become **second nature** to me."
- "It's **part of my routine** — I don't really think about it anymore."
- "I'm **hit or miss** — sometimes I stick to it, sometimes I don't."

---

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Chỉ dùng "always" / "never" — nghe quá tuyệt đối

- ❌ "I always wake up at 6. I always have coffee. I never skip exercise."
- ✅ "I **tend to** wake up around 6, and I **usually** have coffee first thing. I **try** not to skip exercise, though it does happen occasionally."

### Lỗi 2: Quên dùng "used to" cho thói quen quá khứ

- ❌ "When I was a child, I watched TV a lot." *(đúng nhưng không phân biệt rõ với hiện tại)*
- ✅ "I **used to** watch a lot of TV as a child, but I **barely** watch it now."

### Lỗi 3: Dùng "would" cho trạng thái

- ❌ "I **would have** a lot of free time at university."
- ✅ "I **used to have** a lot of free time at university."

### Lỗi 4: Trả lời quá ngắn

- ❌ "Yes, I usually cook." *(1 câu — quá ngắn cho Part 1)*
- ✅ Thêm lý do, ngữ cảnh, ví dụ, hoặc contrast (xem Phần 5)

```
  three times a week / most evenings / first thing in the morning

  I used to + V (thói quen & trạng thái — không còn nữa)

  I've started + V-ing
  I've been trying to + V
  I'm gradually + V-ing
  I'm cutting down on + noun/V-ing

MỞ RỘNG CÂU TRẢ LỜI:
  Habit + Reason + Detail/Contrast (2-4 câu cho Part 1)
```
