## Tóm tắt

IELTS Speaking được chấm theo 4 tiêu chí, mỗi tiêu chí 25%:
- **Fluency and Coherence** — nói liên tục, mạch lạc
- **Lexical Resource** — vốn từ đa dạng
- **Grammatical Range and Accuracy (GRA)** — đa dạng cấu trúc, ít lỗi
- **Pronunciation**

Bài viết này tập trung vào **GRA** — cách dùng ngữ pháp đúng và đa dạng trong cả 3 Parts.

## Tại sao quan trọng

Band 6 GRA: "uses a mix of simple and complex structures, but with limited flexibility." Band 7 GRA: "uses a range of complex structures with some flexibility and accuracy." Sự khác biệt chính là **variety** (đa dạng) và **accuracy** (ít lỗi hệ thống). Bạn không cần nói hoàn hảo — thỉnh thoảng sửa lại tự nhiên là bình thường — nhưng cần tránh cùng một lỗi lặp đi lặp lại.

## Phần 1: Ngữ pháp theo từng Part

### Speaking Part 1 — Câu hỏi cá nhân

Part 1 hỏi về bản thân, sở thích, thói quen hàng ngày. Thì chính:
- **Present Simple** — thói quen, sự thật hiện tại
- **Present Continuous** — hoạt động đang diễn ra
- **Past Simple** — kinh nghiệm quá khứ
- **Present Perfect** — trải nghiệm đến hiện tại

**Ví dụ câu trả lời Part 1:**

*Q: Do you enjoy cooking?*

- ❌ "Yes, I enjoy cooking." (quá ngắn, không có range)
- ✅ "Yes, I really enjoy cooking, **although** I **didn't use to** when I was younger. These days I **tend to** cook at the weekends, **which** I find really relaxing. **In fact**, I **have been trying** to learn more Asian recipes lately."

Chú ý: câu trả lời tốt dùng: although (contrast), didn't use to (past habit), tend to (present habit), which (relative clause), have been trying (present perfect continuous).

### Speaking Part 2 — Long Turn (1-2 phút)

Part 2 yêu cầu nói về một chủ đề trong 1-2 phút, dùng thẻ gợi ý. Cần:
- **Mạch thời gian rõ ràng** — kết hợp Past Simple, Past Continuous, Past Perfect
- **Discourse markers** để cấu trúc câu chuyện
- **Relative clauses** để thêm chi tiết

**Ví dụ Part 2 — Describe a memorable journey:**

*"I'd like to talk about a trip I took to Hội An about two years ago. **To begin with**, I **had never visited** Central Vietnam before, so I **was really looking forward to** it. When we **arrived**, the old town **was lit up** with lanterns, **which was absolutely breathtaking**. **What struck me most** was how well the historic architecture **had been preserved**. **By the time** we left, I **had completely fallen** in love with the place. **If I could**, I **would go back** there every year."*

Cấu trúc được dùng: Past Perfect (had never visited), Past Continuous (was lit up), relative clause (which was), inversion cảm thán (what struck me most), conditional Type 2 (if I could, would go).

### Speaking Part 3 — Thảo luận trừu tượng

Part 3 yêu cầu ý kiến về các vấn đề xã hội rộng hơn. Đây là nơi thể hiện range ngữ pháp cao nhất:
- **Conditionals** — lập luận giả định
- **Modal verbs** — hedging quan điểm
- **Passive voice** — tông giọng học thuật nhẹ
- **Complex sentences** — nối nhiều ý

**Ví dụ Part 3 — "Do you think technology has improved education?"**

*"I think it **depends** a lot on how it's used. Technology **can** be an incredibly powerful tool — for instance, students **are now able to** access lectures from universities around the world, **which would have been unimaginable** thirty years ago. **That said**, there **are** concerns about screen time and distraction, **which is why** I **would argue** that technology **should be** integrated carefully, **rather than** simply replacing traditional teaching methods. **If schools were to** invest more in teacher training alongside new technology, the results **would probably be** far better."*

## Phần 2: Cách thể hiện range ngữ pháp

### Đa dạng thì (Tense Variety)

Ngay cả khi trả lời câu hỏi hiện tại, cố gắng dùng nhiều thì:

| Thì | Ví dụ trong Speaking |
|-----|---------------------|
| Present Simple | I **work** in marketing. |
| Present Continuous | I**'m currently working** on a new project. |
| Present Perfect | I**'ve lived** here for five years. |
| Present Perfect Continuous | I**'ve been studying** English since I was ten. |
| Past Simple | I **studied** abroad for two years. |
| Past Continuous | I **was working** when I got the call. |
| Past Perfect | I **had never tried** sushi before that trip. |
| Future (will) | I think remote work **will become** the norm. |
| Future (going to) | I**'m going to** start a new course next month. |
| Conditional | **If I had more time**, I **would** travel more. |

### Modal Verbs để diễn đạt sắc thái

Trong Speaking, tránh nói quá tuyệt đối:

| Muốn nói | Cách nói tự nhiên |
|----------|-----------------|
| "I don't know the exact number" | It **might be** around 50%, I'm not sure. |
| "I think this is probably true" | I **would say** this is largely true. |
| "It's possible that..." | It **could** be because people are more stressed. |
| "I believe this is important" | I **would argue** that this is crucial. |
| "Maybe in the future" | This **may well** become more common. |

### Câu điều kiện trong Part 3

Conditionals rất tự nhiên trong thảo luận Part 3:

**Type 1 — Thực tế:**
- **If** governments invest more in public transport, air quality **will** definitely improve.

**Type 2 — Giả định:**
- **If** I could change one thing about my city, I **would** definitely improve the roads.
- **If** everyone worked from home, we **would** see a dramatic reduction in commuting.

**Type 3 — Nhìn lại quá khứ:**
- **If** we **had invested** in renewable energy earlier, we **wouldn't be facing** such a severe climate crisis now.

## Phần 3: Discourse Markers trong Speaking

Trong Speaking, discourse markers nên nghe **tự nhiên** hơn trong Writing. Tránh dùng "Furthermore" hay "Nevertheless" — nghe quá cứng nhắc khi nói.

### Speaking vs Writing equivalents

| Writing (trang trọng) | Speaking (tự nhiên) |
|-----------------------|---------------------|
| Furthermore / Moreover | Also, On top of that, What's more, Plus |
| However / Nevertheless | But, Though, Then again, That said |
| In addition | And also, As well as that |
| Consequently / Therefore | So, As a result, That's why |
| For example / For instance | Like, For example, For instance, Say |
| In conclusion | So overall, All in all, At the end of the day |
| It is worth noting | The thing is, Actually, In fact |

### Fillers tự nhiên (mua thời gian suy nghĩ)

Không nên im lặng dài. Dùng fillers để mua thời gian:

- "**That's an interesting question** — let me think about that..."
- "**Well**, I suppose it depends on..."
- "**To be honest**, I haven't really thought about this before, but..."
- "**I mean**, there are so many factors to consider..."
- "**Off the top of my head**, I'd say..."

> Tránh lạm dụng "um", "uh". Thay bằng các cụm trên để vừa mua thời gian vừa nghe chuyên nghiệp.

## Phần 4: Sửa lỗi trong khi nói

Trong Speaking, tự sửa lỗi là hoàn toàn bình thường và thậm chí được đánh giá tích cực nếu làm tự nhiên.

### Cách sửa tự nhiên

- "I **went** — **I mean I've been** to Japan twice."
- "She **works** — **well, she used to work** at a hospital."
- "The problem **is** — **or rather, was** more serious ten years ago."

### Cấu trúc "reformulation" — diễn đạt lại

Khi không chắc từ, diễn đạt theo cách khác:

- "It's... **what's the word**... it's like a feeling of being completely lost."
- "I'd call it a kind of **restlessness**, you know, when you can't sit still."

## Lỗi thường gặp trong Speaking

### Lỗi 1: Chỉ dùng Present Simple

Nhiều thí sinh trả lời mọi câu hỏi bằng Present Simple dù tình huống cần thì khác.

- Q: "Have you always lived here?"
- ❌ "Yes, I live here for 20 years." (sai thì — Present Perfect cần thiết)
- ✅ "Yes, **I've lived** here for about 20 years now."

### Lỗi 2: Câu quá ngắn — thiếu range

- ❌ "Yes I like music. I listen to music every day. It is relaxing."
- ✅ "Yes, I'm really into music — **I find it incredibly relaxing**, especially after a long day. I **tend to** listen to jazz **when I'm working**, **which** helps me concentrate."

### Lỗi 3: Dùng "because" kép

- ❌ Although it is expensive, but people still buy it. (although + but = sai)
- ✅ **Although** it is expensive, people still buy it. (bỏ "but")
- ✅ It is expensive, **but** people still buy it.

### Lỗi 4: Quên chia động từ sau modal

- ❌ Technology can **to** help students learn faster.
- ✅ Technology can help students learn faster. (không có "to" sau modal)

### Lỗi 5: "Make" vs "Do" trong collocations

- ❌ I **make** my homework every evening.
- ✅ I **do** my homework every evening.
- ❌ I **do** a mistake.
- ✅ I **make** a mistake.

## Chiến lược nâng cấp câu trả lời

### Công thức PEEL cho Part 3

**P**oint → **E**xplanation → **E**xample → **L**ink

*"I **think** (P) that technology **has had** a largely positive effect on education. **This is because** (E) students **are now able to** access information instantly, **which would have been impossible** twenty years ago. **For instance**, (E) in remote areas **where** there **are** no good schools, children **can** still receive quality education through online platforms. **So overall**, (L) I **would say** technology **has opened up** opportunities **that** many people **would never have had** otherwise."*

### Template câu Part 3 có range cao

| Mục đích | Template |
|----------|---------|
| Đồng ý một phần | "To some extent, I agree that X, **but** I think it's more complicated than that." |
| Đưa ra hai mặt | "**On the one hand**, X. **On the other**, Y. **Personally**, I **would argue** that..." |
| Lập luận giả định | "**If** we **were to** solve this problem, we **would need to** first understand the root causes." |
| Nhìn lại quá khứ | "Looking back, **if** more resources **had been invested** earlier, the situation today **might be** very different." |

## Bài tập luyện

### Mở rộng câu trả lời

Mở rộng câu trả lời ngắn sau bằng cách thêm: một relative clause, một modal verb, một discourse marker.

1. "I enjoy reading." → (thêm: what kind of books, how long you've read, why)
2. "My hometown is small." → (thêm: comparison, change over time, opinion)
3. "People use technology too much." → (thêm: hedge, example, conditional)

### Đáp án gợi ý

1. "I really enjoy reading, **especially** fiction — **I've been an avid reader since** I was about eight. **What I love most** is that books **can transport** you to completely different worlds."

2. "My hometown **is** quite small, **which** I **used to find** boring when I was younger. It **has changed** a lot over the past decade though — **in fact**, it's **becoming** increasingly popular with tourists, **which** I think **is partly because** of its beautiful countryside."

3. "I **would say** people **do** tend to rely on technology quite heavily these days. **For instance**, most people **can't seem to** get through a meal without checking their phones. **If** this trend **continues**, I think we **might see** some serious consequences for face-to-face communication skills."

## Tóm tắt nhanh

| Part | Thì chính | Cấu trúc nổi bật |
|------|-----------|-----------------|
| Part 1 | Present Simple, Past Simple, Present Perfect | Modal verbs, relative clauses |
| Part 2 | Past tenses (simple, continuous, perfect) | Discourse markers, conditionals |
| Part 3 | Mixed — Past + Present + Future + Conditional | Conditionals, hedging, complex sentences |

**Công thức tăng điểm GRA trong Speaking:**
- Sau mỗi câu ngắn → **mở rộng** bằng relative clause, reason, or example
- Dùng **ít nhất 1 conditional** trong Part 3
- Thay "very" bằng adverbs mạnh hơn: **remarkably, incredibly, considerably**
- Xen kẽ **active và passive** để tạo tông giọng đa dạng
- Tự sửa lỗi tự nhiên — không phải điểm trừ, mà là dấu hiệu của accuracy awareness
