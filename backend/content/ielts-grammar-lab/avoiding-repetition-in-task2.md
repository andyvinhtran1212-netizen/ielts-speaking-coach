## Tại sao quan trọng

Grammatical Range and Accuracy chiếm 25% tổng điểm Task 2. Examiner đánh giá không chỉ bạn có **dùng đúng** ngữ pháp không, mà bạn có dùng **đa dạng** các cấu trúc không. Viết 5 câu đều dạng "Subject does/is/has + object" — dù không sai — vẫn bị giới hạn điểm.

## PHẦN 1: THAY ĐỔI CÁCH MỞ ĐẦU CÂU

### Vấn đề: Mọi câu đều bắt đầu bằng Subject

- ❌ "*Technology* is changing the world. *It* is used by millions of people. *It* helps people communicate. *Technology* also creates new jobs."

### Giải pháp: Xen kẽ 5 kiểu mở đầu

#### 1. Bắt đầu bằng trạng từ / trạng ngữ thời gian:
- "**In recent years**, technology has transformed the way people communicate."
- "**Over the past two decades**, smartphone usage has grown exponentially."
- "**Increasingly**, individuals are turning to social media for news and information."

#### 2. Bắt đầu bằng cụm trạng từ nguyên nhân/nhượng bộ:
- "**Despite the obvious benefits**, excessive screen time raises serious concerns."
- "**Due to rapid urbanisation**, many cities are struggling with overcrowded public transport."
- "**Given the environmental pressures**, governments must act decisively."

#### 3. Bắt đầu bằng mệnh đề phụ (adverbial clause):
- "**Although technology has improved access to information**, it has also facilitated the spread of misinformation."
- "**While individual choices matter**, systemic change requires government action."

#### 4. Bắt đầu bằng gerund phrase:
- "**Investing in early childhood education** is one of the most effective long-term strategies."
- "**Reducing carbon emissions** requires both individual and collective action."

#### 5. Bắt đầu bằng it-cleft hoặc what-cleft:
- "**It is the lack of affordable housing** that drives many young people to leave major cities."
- "**What governments need to recognise** is that short-term costs often lead to long-term savings."

## PHẦN 2: NOMINAL PHRASES — Rút gọn mệnh đề thành cụm danh từ

Đây là một trong những dấu hiệu rõ nhất của Band 7+ writing.

### Before (dài, lặp):

- ❌ "When people don't exercise enough, they tend to have health problems. This means that the healthcare system becomes more burdened."

### After (cô đọng, học thuật):

- ✅ "**A sedentary lifestyle** is closely linked to a range of chronic health conditions, placing increasing pressure on healthcare infrastructure."

### Các chuyển đổi phổ biến:

| Mệnh đề dài | Nominal phrase |
|-------------|---------------|
| "the fact that many young people are unemployed" | **youth unemployment** |
| "when people use social media too much" | **excessive social media use** |
| "the number of people who are living alone is increasing" | **the rising number of single-person households** |
| "the way in which technology has changed communication" | **the technological transformation of communication** |
| "because there is a lack of qualified teachers" | **a shortage of qualified teachers** |

**Ví dụ before/after:**

- ❌ "Because there are not enough hospitals in rural areas, people who live there can't get good medical care."
- ✅ "**The inadequate provision of healthcare facilities in rural areas** continues to deprive many communities of essential medical services."

## PHẦN 3: PASSIVE VOICE — Thay đổi trọng tâm và tránh lặp chủ ngữ

Passive voice không chỉ là câu bị động — nó là công cụ để thay đổi điểm nhấn.

```
[Object] + is/are/was/were + past participle + (by [agent]).
[Object] + has been / have been + past participle.
It is widely believed / argued / recognised that...
```

### Before/after:

- ❌ "Researchers have shown that exercise reduces stress. Doctors recommend exercise to patients. Many people now do exercise regularly."
- ✅ "**Exercise has been shown** to reduce stress significantly, and **is now widely recommended** by medical professionals as a cornerstone of preventive healthcare."

**Thêm ví dụ:**

- "**It is widely argued that** the current educational system fails to prepare students for the modern workplace."
- "**Considerable attention has been paid to** the role of diet in preventing chronic disease."
- "**Significant progress has been made** in reducing carbon emissions, though more action **is still needed**."

## PHẦN 4: RELATIVE CLAUSES — Gộp thông tin vào một câu

Thay vì viết hai câu ngắn, dùng relative clause để gộp:

```
[Noun] + which/who/that/whose + [relative clause]
[Noun] + where/when + [relative clause]
```

### Before (hai câu rời):

- ❌ "Finland has an excellent education system. This system consistently ranks among the best in the world."

### After (gộp bằng relative clause):

- ✅ "Finland has an education system **that consistently ranks among the best in the world**."

**Thêm ví dụ:**

- ❌ "Many young people move to cities. Cities offer better career opportunities."
- ✅ "Many young people are drawn to cities, **where career opportunities are more abundant**."

- ❌ "The government introduced new policies. These policies have been criticised by economists."
- ✅ "The government's newly introduced policies, **which have been widely criticised by economists**, risk destabilising the housing market."

---

## PHẦN 5: PARTICIPLE PHRASES — Rút gọn mệnh đề phụ

Participle phrase (V-ing hoặc past participle ở đầu câu) là công cụ nâng cấp câu nhanh.

```
[Present participle phrase], [main clause].
[Past participle phrase], [main clause].
```

### Before:

- ❌ "The government introduced new regulations. This created uncertainty in the business sector."

### After:

- ✅ "**Introducing new regulations without adequate consultation**, the government created significant uncertainty in the business sector."

**Thêm ví dụ:**

- "**Faced with rising living costs**, many families are cutting back on non-essential spending."
- "**Having invested heavily in public transport**, the city has seen a marked reduction in traffic congestion."
- "**Driven by rapid technological change**, companies are increasingly seeking employees with adaptable skill sets."

---

## PHẦN 6: CLEFT SENTENCES — Nhấn mạnh

```
It is/was [X] that/who [rest of sentence].
What [subject] [verb] is/was [complement].
```

- "**It is the shortage of affordable housing** that is driving many young people out of major cities."
- "**What concerns many educators** is not the technology itself, but the lack of critical thinking skills among students who use it."
- "**It is individual accountability**, combined with systemic reform, **that** will ultimately determine whether lasting change is achievable."

---

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Dùng passive voice không đúng chỗ

Passive voice hữu ích nhưng dùng quá nhiều làm bài khó đọc và mất tự nhiên. Cân bằng active vs passive.

### Lỗi 2: Nominal phrase quá phức tạp

- ❌ "The increasing rapid technological digital transformation of modern urban communication infrastructure..."
- Giữ nominal phrase gọn: không quá 3-4 từ bổ nghĩa.

### Lỗi 3: Relative clause dùng sai đại từ

- ❌ "The problem which it needs solving..." *(thừa "it")*
- ✅ "The problem **that needs** solving..." / "The problem **which requires** addressing..."

### Lỗi 4: Participle phrase lơ lửng (dangling participle)

- ❌ "**Having studied abroad**, the economy improved." *(chủ ngữ của participle ≠ chủ ngữ main clause)*
- ✅ "**Having studied abroad**, many graduates return home with valuable skills and perspectives."

## Bài tập luyện

### Viết lại các câu sau dùng kỹ thuật được chỉ định:

1. "Many people don't have access to clean water. This is a serious global problem." → Dùng **relative clause**
2. "The government needs to address poverty. Poverty affects millions of citizens." → Dùng **nominal phrase**
3. "Researchers discovered that stress causes illness." → Dùng **passive**
4. "The rising cost of living is the main reason. Young people leave rural areas because of it." → Dùng **cleft sentence**

### Đáp án gợi ý:

1. "Access to clean water, **which remains unavailable to millions**, is one of the most pressing global challenges."
2. "**Widespread poverty** — affecting millions of citizens — demands urgent government intervention."
3. "Stress **has been found to be** a significant contributor to a range of serious illnesses."
4. "**It is the rising cost of living** that drives many young people away from rural areas."
