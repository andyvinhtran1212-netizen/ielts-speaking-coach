## Tóm tắt

Trong IELTS Speaking, "so sánh" có ba dạng chính:

| Dạng | Mục đích | Ví dụ connector |
|------|----------|----------------|
| **Similarity** | Hai thứ giống nhau | similarly, likewise, just as, both |
| **Contrast** | Hai thứ khác nhau | whereas, while, however, on the other hand |
| **Preference** | Một thứ tốt hơn | more...than, better than, prefer, rather than |

---

## Tại sao quan trọng

Trong Part 2 (cue card) và Part 3 (discussion), examiner thường hỏi bạn so sánh:
- "Compare your country now with 20 years ago."
- "Is it better to work from home or in an office?"
- "How are young people today different from previous generations?"

Câu trả lời có cấu trúc so sánh rõ ràng nghe **thông minh, có chiều sâu** và đạt điểm cao về Coherence & Cohesion. Câu trả lời chỉ liệt kê hai điểm rời rạc nghe yếu và thiếu tổ chức.

## PHẦN 1: SO SÁNH HƠN — Comparative Forms

### Tính từ / Trạng từ ngắn

```
adjective-er + than
```

- City life is **more expensive** / **busier** **than** village life.
- Young people today are **better educated** **than** those of previous generations.
- The second option is **cheaper** and **easier** to implement.
- Remote work is **more flexible** **than** working in an office.

### Tính từ / Trạng từ dài

```
more + adjective/adverb + than
less + adjective/adverb + than
```

- Social media is **more influential** **than** traditional media in shaping opinions.
- People are **less likely** to read physical books **than** they were 20 years ago.
- Working from home can be **more productive**, but it's also **more isolating**.

### Danh từ và số lượng

```
more + noun + than
fewer + countable noun + than
less + uncountable noun + than
```

- Cities offer **more job opportunities** **than** rural areas.
- **Fewer** young people own homes **than** in previous generations.
- People spend **less time** with family because of busy work schedules.

## PHẦN 2: ĐỐI LẬP — Contrast Structures

### Dùng "while" và "whereas" (trong cùng một câu)

```
While [A], [B] ...
[A], whereas [B] ...
```

- **While** city living offers convenience and career opportunities, **it also comes with** high costs and stress.
- Young people prioritise career development, **whereas** older generations tend to value stability and security.
- **While** technology has connected people globally, **it has also** made face-to-face communication less common.
- Online learning is flexible and affordable, **whereas** traditional classroom learning offers more structure and personal interaction.

### Dùng "however" và "on the other hand" (giữa hai câu)

```
[Point A]. However, [Point B].
[Point A]. On the other hand, [Point B].
```

- "City life has many advantages in terms of career opportunities and entertainment. **However**, it can also be extremely stressful and expensive."
- "Working from home increases productivity for many people. **On the other hand**, it can blur the boundary between work and personal life."
- "Social media allows people to stay connected. **That said**, excessive use can lead to anxiety and feelings of inadequacy."

### Dùng "although" và "even though"

```
Although/Even though [less expected fact], [main point].
[Main point], although [less expected fact].
```

- **Although** public transport has improved significantly, many people still prefer to drive.
- **Even though** technology makes many jobs easier, it has also created new forms of stress.
- The traditional approach is still effective, **although** newer methods tend to produce faster results.

## PHẦN 3: SỞ THÍCH VÀ LỰA CHỌN — Preference Patterns

### Diễn đạt sở thích rõ ràng

```
I prefer [A] to [B] because...
I would rather [A] than [B]...
[A] suits me better than [B] because...
I find [A] more [adj] than [B]...
```

- "**I prefer** living in a city **to** the countryside, mainly because I enjoy having access to cultural events and a wider social circle."
- "**I would rather** work in a team **than** on my own, because I find collaboration more stimulating."
- "City life **suits** me **better than** rural life — I'm quite an active person and I need that energy around me."
- "**I find** online learning **more convenient than** attending physical classes, especially for people with busy schedules."

### So sánh hai lựa chọn một cách cân bằng

```
Both [A] and [B] have their advantages...
[A] is better for [some people], while [B] suits [others]...
It depends on...; for some, [A] is preferable, but for others, [B] makes more sense.
```

- "**Both** city life and rural life have their appeal — it really depends on your lifestyle and priorities."
- "Online education **suits** self-motivated learners, **while** traditional schooling works better for those who need structure."
- "**It depends on** the individual — for someone who values independence, working from home is ideal; for others who need social interaction, the office is better."

## PHẦN 4: SO SÁNH QUA CÁC THỜI KỲ — Comparing Time Periods

Đây là dạng so sánh quan trọng trong Part 2 và Part 3 khi đề hỏi về thay đổi xã hội.

### Mô tả sự thay đổi

```
[Subject] used to [verb], but now [subject] [verb]...
In the past, ... Nowadays, however, ...
Compared with/to [time period], [subject] is/has...
[Subject] has changed significantly over the past [time]...
```

- "**In the past**, most people worked in one job for their entire career. **Nowadays, however**, job-hopping is much more common and even encouraged."
- "**Compared with** 20 years ago, young people today have **far more** educational opportunities but also **significantly more** financial pressure."
- "My hometown **used to be** a quiet farming community, **but** over the past decade **it has transformed into** a bustling urban centre."
- "The way people socialise **has changed dramatically** — **whereas** previous generations would meet in person, many now maintain friendships primarily through social media."

### Nhấn mạnh mức độ thay đổi

```
[Subject] is far/much/significantly [more/less] [adj] than before.
The change has been dramatic / gradual / remarkable.
There has been a considerable shift in...
```

- "Work culture is **far less formal** than it was even ten years ago."
- "There has been a **remarkable shift** in attitudes towards mental health — it's discussed much more openly now."
- "The pace of technological change has been **extraordinary** — things that seemed futuristic in 2000 are now everyday tools."

## Mẫu câu trả lời hoàn chỉnh (Part 3)

**Q: Do you think people's lifestyles have changed compared to 50 years ago?**

> "Yes, dramatically. **In the past**, most people led much simpler lives — they lived near where they were born, worked in physical jobs, and socialised primarily with neighbours and family. **Whereas** previous generations rarely travelled far, people today are **far more mobile** and connected globally. **However**, I think this comes at a cost — **while** modern life is **more convenient and stimulating**, it's also **more stressful and isolating** in some ways. **On the other hand**, access to healthcare, education, and technology has improved enormously, so **overall**, I'd say the changes have been positive, **although** not without trade-offs."

**Q: Is it better to live in the city or the countryside?**

> "I think **it really depends on** what you value. **City life** offers far more career opportunities, better infrastructure, and a wider social life — **whereas** rural areas tend to be **quieter, more affordable**, and **closer to nature**. **For** ambitious, career-focused people, cities are probably **better**. **On the other hand**, for families with children or people who prioritise wellbeing over career advancement, the countryside often **suits** them **better**. **Personally**, I prefer the city, **mainly because** I'm quite career-focused at this stage of my life, **although** I could see myself moving somewhere quieter eventually."

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: So sánh không đầy đủ (incomplete comparison)

- ❌ "City life is more convenient."
- ✅ "City life is **more convenient than** rural life." (hơn cái gì?)

### Lỗi 2: Dùng "more" với tính từ ngắn

- ❌ "It is more big than before."
- ✅ "It is **bigger** than before."

### Lỗi 3: Liệt kê mà không có connector

- ❌ "Cities have jobs. Rural areas have nature. Cities are busy. Rural areas are quiet."
- ✅ "**While** cities offer more employment opportunities, rural areas provide a calmer environment and a closer connection to nature."

### Lỗi 4: "Different than" (không chuẩn)

- ❌ "It's **different than** before."
- ✅ "It's **different from** before." (British English)
- ✅ "It's **different to** before." (informal British)

### Lỗi 5: Lạm dụng "but" thay vì các connector đa dạng hơn

- ❌ "I like cities but I also like the countryside but there are advantages but..."
- ✅ Thay bằng: however, whereas, on the other hand, that said, although, even so

## Bài tập luyện

### Nối hai ý bằng connector phù hợp

1. "Online shopping is convenient." / "You can't try on clothes before buying."
   → Use: *while/whereas/although*

2. "My hometown used to be very rural." / "Now it's a modern city."
   → Use: *used to... but now / whereas*

3. "Working from home saves commuting time." / "It can make people feel isolated."
   → Use: *while/however/that said*

### Gợi ý đáp án

1. "**While** online shopping is convenient, you can't try on clothes before buying." / "Online shopping is convenient, **although** you can't try on clothes before buying."
2. "My hometown **used to be** very rural, **but** it **has transformed into** a modern city over the past two decades." / "**Whereas** my hometown was once entirely rural, it is now a thriving urban centre."
3. "Working from home saves commuting time. **However**, it can make people feel isolated." / "**While** working from home saves commuting time, it can also lead to feelings of isolation."

## Tóm tắt nhanh

```
COMPARISON (hơn):
  adjective-er + than / more + adj + than
  less + adj + than / fewer/more + noun + than

CONTRAST (đối lập, cùng câu):
  while / whereas + clause
  although / even though + clause

CONTRAST (giữa câu):
  However, ... / On the other hand, ...
  That said, ... / Even so, ...

PREFERENCE (sở thích):
  prefer [A] to [B] / would rather [A] than [B]
  [A] suits me better than [B]

TIME COMPARISON (theo thời gian):
  used to... but now...
  In the past,... Nowadays, however,...
  Compared with [time], [subject] is...
  There has been a significant shift in...
```
