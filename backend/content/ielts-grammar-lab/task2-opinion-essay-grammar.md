## Tóm tắt

| Phần bài | Cấu trúc ngữ pháp chính |
|---------|------------------------|
| **Mở bài** | It is widely argued / Some people believe... / This essay will argue that... |
| **Luận điểm chính** | One significant reason is that... / Furthermore, ... |
| **Ví dụ / bằng chứng** | For instance, / This can be illustrated by / As evidenced by... |
| **Nhượng bộ** | Admittedly, ... / While it is true that... |
| **Kết bài** | In conclusion, / On balance, / Overall, ... |

---

## Tại sao quan trọng

Bài opinion essay yêu cầu bạn **thể hiện quan điểm rõ ràng và nhất quán**. Lỗi phổ biến nhất không phải là sai ngữ pháp mà là:
- Quan điểm không rõ ở mở bài
- Luận điểm không được hỗ trợ bằng bằng chứng / giải thích
- Kết bài chỉ lặp lại mở bài mà không tổng hợp

Các cấu trúc ngữ pháp trong bài này giúp **dẫn dắt examiner** qua logic của bài viết.

## PHẦN 1: MỞ BÀI — Giới thiệu chủ đề và quan điểm

### Bước 1: Paraphrase câu hỏi (không sao chép)

```
It is widely argued / debated / believed that [topic paraphrase].
[Topic] has become an increasingly [contentious/important] issue in recent years.
In recent decades, [topic] has attracted significant attention from [policymakers/researchers/the public].
```

- "**It is widely argued that** the rise of social media has had a predominantly negative impact on society. This essay will argue that, **while** there are some benefits, **the drawbacks outweigh them**."

- "**In recent years**, the question of **whether** governments should take greater responsibility for citizens' health **has become increasingly prominent**."

### Bước 2: Nêu quan điểm rõ ràng (thesis statement)

```
This essay will argue that [position].
I firmly believe that [position].
It is my contention that [position].
In my view, [position].
While [opposing view], I would argue that [your position].
```

- "**This essay will argue that** the advantages of living in a multicultural society **far outweigh** the challenges."
- "**I firmly believe that** technology has done more good than harm, **provided that** it is used responsibly."
- "**While** some may argue that capital punishment acts as a deterrent, **I contend that** it is fundamentally unjust and should be abolished."

> **Lưu Ý:** Với "To what extent do you agree?" — thường viết một quan điểm được support "largely" hoặc "to a large extent" kèm nuance là tốt nhất.

## PHẦN 2: THÂN BÀI — Cấu trúc luận điểm

### Mô hình PEEL cho mỗi đoạn thân bài:

```
P — Point (luận điểm)
E — Explanation (giải thích)
E — Evidence / Example (bằng chứng / ví dụ)
L — Link (liên kết về luận điểm hoặc luận điểm tiếp theo)
```

### Câu mở đoạn (Topic sentence):

```
One of the main reasons why [position] is that [reason].
A key argument in favour of / against [topic] is that [reason].
Perhaps the most significant [advantage/disadvantage] of [topic] is [point].
[Topic] has a profound impact on [area], particularly because [reason].
```

- "**One of the main reasons why** remote work should be encouraged is that it **significantly reduces** commuting times and associated stress levels."
- "**A key argument against** the widespread use of artificial intelligence in healthcare is the potential for bias in algorithmic decision-making."
- "**Perhaps the most significant benefit of** universal basic income is its ability to provide a financial safety net for vulnerable members of society."

### Giải thích luận điểm:

```
This is because + [reason].
This means that + [consequence].
As a result, + [outcome].
Consequently, + [outcome].
The reason for this is that + [explanation].
```

- "**This is because** prolonged screen use can disrupt the body's natural sleep cycle by suppressing melatonin production."
- "**This means that** individuals with fewer financial resources may be disproportionately affected by this policy."

### Đưa ví dụ và bằng chứng:

```
For instance, / For example, ...
This can be seen in [country/case], where...
Research suggests that / Studies have shown that...
A clear illustration of this is...
Take [X] as an example: ...
```

- "**For instance**, a study conducted in Finland found that students who had access to outdoor learning spaces demonstrated significantly higher engagement levels."
- "**This can be seen in** countries such as Sweden and Denmark, where strong social welfare systems have been linked to higher levels of citizen happiness."
- "**Take** Singapore **as an example**: its emphasis on education and meritocracy has enabled it to achieve remarkable economic growth despite limited natural resources."

## PHẦN 3: NHƯỢNG BỘ VÀ PHẢN BIỆN (Concession + Counter-argument)

Đây là dấu hiệu của bài Band 7+ — thừa nhận quan điểm đối lập trước khi bác bỏ nó.

### Công thức cơ bản:

```
Admittedly, [opposing view]. However, [your counter-argument].
While it is true that [opposing view], [your position] is stronger because...
Although [opposing view], the evidence suggests that [your position].
It cannot be denied that [opposing view]; nevertheless, [your counter-argument].
```

**Ví dụ:**

- "**Admittedly**, introducing stricter environmental regulations may impose additional costs on businesses. **However**, the long-term economic benefits of preventing environmental degradation **far outweigh** these short-term expenses."

- "**While it is true that** social media can facilitate communication, **it is equally important to recognise that** it can also amplify misinformation and increase anxiety among young users."

- "**It cannot be denied that** a degree of individual freedom is sacrificed when governments introduce mandatory vaccination programmes. **Nevertheless**, the collective benefit to public health **justifies** this intervention."

## PHẦN 4: CÁC CẤU TRÚC CÂU ĐẶC TRƯNG BAND 7+

### Nhấn mạnh bằng cleft sentence:

```
It is [X] that + [clause]
What [subject] + verb is + [noun phrase]
```

- "**It is the lack of investment in public education** that perpetuates cycles of poverty."
- "**What concerns many people** is not the technology itself, but the speed at which it is being adopted without adequate regulation."

### Câu giả định và điều kiện để phân tích:

- "**If** governments **were to** invest more in mental health services, the overall burden on healthcare systems **would decrease** significantly."
- "**Were this policy to be implemented**, it **could have** far-reaching consequences for the labour market."

### Passive voice để tạo văn phong học thuật:

- "**It is widely believed that** economic growth and environmental protection are mutually exclusive goals."
- "**A great deal of attention has been paid to** the role of social media in political discourse."
- "This issue **has been debated** extensively in both academic and public spheres."

### Nominal phrases thay vì mệnh đề:

Thay vì: "The fact that many young people are unemployed is a problem."
→ "**Youth unemployment** represents a significant challenge for developing economies."

Thay vì: "When people use social media too much, it damages their mental health."
→ "**Excessive social media use** has been linked to deteriorating mental health outcomes."

## PHẦN 5: KẾT BÀI — Tổng hợp và khẳng định lại

Kết bài không chỉ lặp lại — nó **tổng hợp** và khẳng định lại quan điểm với ngôn ngữ mới.

### Cấu trúc kết bài:

```
In conclusion / To conclude / Overall, ...
[Restate position in new words].
[Summarise main points briefly].
[Optional: forward-looking statement or recommendation].
```

**Ví dụ:**

- "**In conclusion**, while there are legitimate concerns about the impact of technological unemployment, **I maintain that** the long-term benefits of automation — in terms of productivity, quality of life, and the creation of new industries — **outweigh** the transitional challenges. **Governments**, however, **must** invest in retraining programmes to ensure that displaced workers are not left behind."

- "**To conclude**, **it is my firm belief that** a universal basic income represents a viable and humane response to growing economic inequality. **By providing** a financial floor for all citizens, societies **can** foster both individual dignity and collective resilience."

- "**Overall**, **although** the arguments on both sides have merit, the evidence **suggests** that stricter regulation of the food industry is both necessary and justified. **Without** such measures, the public health consequences **are likely to worsen**."

## PHẦN 6: CẤU TRÚC LIÊN KẾT Ý TRONG OPINION ESSAY

| Chức năng | Cấu trúc |
|----------|---------|
| **Thêm ý** | Furthermore, / Moreover, / In addition, / What is more, |
| **Tương phản** | However, / Nevertheless, / On the other hand, / By contrast, |
| **Nguyên nhân** | because / as / since / owing to / due to |
| **Kết quả** | Therefore, / As a result, / Consequently, / Hence, |
| **Ví dụ** | For instance, / For example, / Such as / To illustrate, |
| **Nhượng bộ** | Admittedly, / While it is true that... / Although... |
| **Kết luận** | In conclusion, / Overall, / To conclude, / On balance, |

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Quan điểm không rõ ràng trong mở bài

- ❌ "There are advantages and disadvantages to this issue." *(không nêu rõ lập trường)*
- ✅ "**While there are some merits** to this approach, **I firmly believe** that the disadvantages are more significant."

### Lỗi 2: Kết bài chỉ lặp mở bài nguyên xi

- ❌ "In conclusion, I agree with the statement because it has advantages."
- ✅ "**In conclusion**, the evidence presented **strongly supports** the view that [position]. **By** [action], societies **can** [benefit]. **Without** [intervention], the consequences **are likely to be** severe."

### Lỗi 3: Thiếu câu liên kết giữa bằng chứng và luận điểm

Sau example: phải có câu giải thích tại sao example đó hỗ trợ luận điểm.
- "For example, in Japan, birth rates have declined sharply. **This demonstrates that** economic pressures can have a direct impact on family planning decisions."

### Lỗi 4: Dùng "I think / I believe" quá nhiều và đơn điệu

Xen kẽ với: "It is my contention that...", "I would argue that...", "In my view...", "It seems clear that...", "The evidence suggests..."

## Bài tập luyện

### Viết mở bài cho đề sau:

"In many countries, the gap between rich and poor is widening. To what extent do you agree that governments should take responsibility for reducing this inequality?"

### Gợi ý đáp án:

"**The growing divide** between the wealthy and those in poverty **has emerged as** one of the most pressing social issues of our time. **While it is true that** individual effort plays a role in determining economic outcomes, **I firmly believe** that governments have a fundamental responsibility to address systemic inequality through targeted policy intervention. **This essay will argue** that, **while** personal agency is important, structural factors make government action both necessary and justified."

## Tóm tắt nhanh

```
MỞ BÀI:
  Paraphrase + thesis statement
  "This essay will argue that..."
  "I firmly believe / contend that..."

THÂN BÀI (PEEL):
  Point: "One key reason is that..."
  Explain: "This is because... / This means that..."
  Example: "For instance, / This can be seen in..."
  Link: "Therefore, / This demonstrates that..."

NHƯỢNG BỘ:
  "Admittedly, ... However, ..."
  "While it is true that..., ..."
  "It cannot be denied that...; nevertheless, ..."

KẾT BÀI:
  "In conclusion, I maintain that..."
  Tổng hợp lại + khuyến nghị / forward-looking

CẤU TRÚC BAND 7+:
  → Cleft: It is [X] that...
  → Nominal phrases thay mệnh đề dài
  → Passive voice cho văn học thuật
  → Second conditional cho phân tích chính sách
```
