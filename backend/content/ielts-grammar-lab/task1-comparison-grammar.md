## Tóm tắt

Ba loại so sánh trong Task 1:

| Loại | Mục đích | Cấu trúc chính |
|------|---------|---------------|
| **So sánh hơn** | A > B hoặc A < B | higher/lower than, more/less than |
| **So sánh nhất** | A cao/thấp nhất trong nhóm | the highest/lowest, the most/least |
| **Đối lập** | A ≠ B về xu hướng hoặc đặc điểm | whereas, while, by contrast, compared with |

---

## Tại sao quan trọng

Task 1 đòi hỏi bạn **select and report key features** — và key features thường là:
- Danh mục cao nhất / thấp nhất
- So sánh giữa các danh mục
- Sự đối lập giữa xu hướng

Bài viết chỉ mô tả từng số một mà không so sánh nghe như đọc số liệu, không phải phân tích. Câu trả lời Band 7+ biết chọn thông tin quan trọng và trình bày chúng qua lens của so sánh.

## PHẦN 1: SO SÁNH HƠN KÉM

### Higher/Lower than (so sánh tuyệt đối)

```
[Subject A] was higher/lower/greater/smaller than [Subject B]
[Subject A] exceeded/surpassed [Subject B]
[Subject A] was [amount] higher/lower than [Subject B]
```

- The figure for France was **higher than** that for Germany throughout the period.
- Sales in Asia were **significantly lower than** those in Europe in 2010.
- The proportion of male students **exceeded** that of female students by around 10 percentage points.
- The unemployment rate in Country A was **roughly twice as high as** that in Country B.

### More/Less than (so sánh số lượng)

```
[Subject A] accounted for more/less than [amount]
[Subject A] was more than/less than [number]
[Subject A] was [X] times more/less than [Subject B]
```

- The figure for oil consumption was **more than** three times that for natural gas.
- In 2020, online sales accounted for **more than** half of all transactions.
- The number of car owners was **less than** 10% of the population in rural areas.
- Coal generated **three times more** electricity **than** nuclear power.

### As...as (so sánh ngang bằng)

```
[A] was (approximately/roughly/nearly/almost) as [adjective] as [B]
[A] was (not) as [adjective] as [B]
```

- The figure for Category A was **roughly the same as** that for Category B.
- By 2010, the two figures had become **almost as large as** each other.
- Category C was **not as significant as** Category A throughout the period.
- In 2015, both groups were **approximately equal in size**.

## PHẦN 2: SO SÁNH NHẤT

### The Highest / The Lowest

```
[Subject] had the highest/lowest + [noun]
[Subject] recorded/achieved the highest/lowest + [noun] of [amount]
[Subject] accounted for the largest/smallest proportion/share
```

- **The highest** figure was recorded in Japan, at approximately 80%.
- China **had the largest** share of the market, accounting for 35%.
- **The lowest** unemployment rate was found in Switzerland, at just 3%.
- The UK **accounted for the smallest** proportion of total exports.
- **The most popular** category by far was transport, which represented nearly half of all spending.

### Ranking patterns

- **The second highest** figure belonged to Germany.
- India ranked **third**, with a figure of approximately 15%.
- **Among** all the countries shown, Japan consumed **the most** energy per capita.
- **Of** the three categories, healthcare represented **the largest** share.

## PHẦN 3: ĐỐI LẬP XU HƯỚNG

### Whereas / While (trong cùng câu)

```
While/Whereas [Subject A + trend], [Subject B + contrasting trend].
```

- **While** car ownership increased substantially over the period, public transport usage **declined**.
- **Whereas** energy consumption in developed countries **fell**, it **rose** dramatically in developing nations.
- Sales in Europe **grew steadily**, **while** those in Asia **fluctuated** considerably.
- **While** Category A **showed** an upward trend, Categories B and C **remained relatively stable**.

### By contrast / In contrast (giữa hai câu)

```
[Subject A] + [trend]. By/In contrast, [Subject B] + [contrasting trend].
```

- Sales in the domestic market grew by 20%. **By contrast**, exports **declined** by 15% over the same period.
- The proportion of young people in employment **rose** throughout the decade. **In contrast**, the figures for older workers **showed** a consistent decline.
- The first half of the period saw strong growth. **By contrast**, the second half was marked by significant fluctuation.

### Compared with / Compared to

```
[Subject A] + [trend/level], compared with/to [Subject B]
Compared with/to [Subject B], [Subject A] + [trend/level]
```

- The figure for the UK was considerably lower, **compared with** that of the US.
- **Compared with** the 1990s, energy consumption in the 2010s was **significantly higher**.
- Japan's spending on education was modest **compared to** that of Nordic countries.
- **Compared to** the previous year, production **increased** by approximately 25%.

### On the other hand / However (đối lập ở mức đoạn)

- The 1990s saw sustained economic growth. **On the other hand**, the following decade was characterised by volatility and decline.
- The proportion of workers in manufacturing **fell** sharply. **However**, the service sector **experienced** significant growth during the same period.

## PHẦN 4: SO SÁNH SỐ LIỆU CỤ THỂ

### Nói về khoảng cách (gap)

```
[A] was [amount] higher/lower than [B]
The gap between [A] and [B] was approximately [amount]
The difference between [A] and [B] narrowed/widened over the period
```

- Sales in Region A were approximately **$50 million higher than** those in Region B.
- **The gap between** male and female participation rates **was around** 15 percentage points.
- **The difference between** the two figures **narrowed** significantly by the end of the period.
- By 2020, the two categories **had converged**, with a gap of less than 2%.

### Nói về tỷ lệ (ratio/proportion)

```
[A] was [X] times as [adj] as [B]
[A] was double/triple/half the figure for [B]
[A] accounted for [X]% of the total
```

- The figure for Category A was **almost double** that of Category B.
- In 2010, coal **accounted for** 45% of total energy production.
- The proportion of elderly residents was **three times** as high in Japan as in India.
- Spending on education represented **roughly a quarter** of the total budget.

## PHẦN 5: LINKING COMPARISONS IN SENTENCES

Câu so sánh mạnh nhất thường kết hợp thông tin về **mức độ** và **ngữ cảnh**:

```
[Subject A] was [considerably/significantly/slightly/marginally] 
[higher/lower/greater/smaller] than [Subject B], 
[standing at/reaching/recording] [figure] compared with [figure] for [B].
```

**Ví dụ câu hoàn chỉnh:**

- "The proportion of university graduates in Country A was **considerably higher** than in Country B, **standing at** 45% **compared with** just 22% for the latter."
- "Energy consumption per capita in the USA was **roughly twice as high** as that in China, **at** approximately 80 units **versus** 40 units."
- "**While** the figures for Categories A and B **were broadly similar** throughout the period, Category C was **consistently lower**, **recording** figures that were on average **30% below** those of the other two."

---

## Ví dụ đoạn Overview và Body hoàn chỉnh

**Biểu đồ:** Tỷ lệ người dùng internet ở 5 quốc gia năm 2000 và 2020.

**Overview:**
> "**Overall**, internet usage increased in all five countries between 2000 and 2020. **The most striking change** was seen in China, **which** experienced **the greatest** growth. **By contrast**, the USA and UK **already had** high levels of internet penetration in 2000, and **while** their figures continued to rise, the increase was **less dramatic compared with** those in developing nations."

**Body paragraph:**
> "In 2000, **the highest** rate of internet use was recorded in the USA, at approximately 45%, **followed closely by** the UK at 40%. **By contrast**, only 2% of the Chinese population used the internet at this time — a figure **considerably lower than** all other countries shown. **However**, **by** 2020, China's rate **had surged** to 75%, **overtaking** the UK (92%) and **approaching** the USA's figure of 90%. **The gap between** China and the other nations **narrowed dramatically** over the twenty-year period."

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: So sánh không song song (comparing different things)

- ❌ "The figure for France was higher than Germany." (đang so sánh figure với Germany)
- ✅ "The figure for France was higher than **that** for Germany."
- ✅ "France's figure was higher than Germany's."

### Lỗi 2: Dùng "more higher / more lower"

- ❌ "Sales were **more higher** in 2020."
- ✅ "Sales were **higher** in 2020."
- ✅ "Sales were **significantly higher** in 2020."

### Lỗi 3: Quên "the" với superlatives

- ❌ "Japan had **highest** consumption."
- ✅ "Japan had **the highest** consumption."

### Lỗi 4: "Compare with" vs "Compare to"

- **compared with** → so sánh hai thứ tương tự (thường dùng trong academic writing)
- **compared to** → thường xuyên hơn trong văn không chính thức; cũng đúng trong Task 1
- Cả hai đều acceptable trong IELTS, nhưng "compared with" nghe học thuật hơn.

### Lỗi 5: Không dùng proxy noun để tránh lặp

- ❌ "The figure for China was higher than the figure for Japan."
- ✅ "The figure for China was higher than **that** for Japan."
- ✅ "China's figure was higher than Japan's."

## Tóm tắt nhanh

```
SO SÁNH HƠN:
  higher/lower than / more/less than
  exceeded / surpassed
  [X] times as high as / double/triple...
  roughly the same as / approximately equal

SO SÁNH NHẤT:
  the highest/lowest / the most/least
  the largest/smallest (proportion)
  ranked first/second/third

ĐỐI LẬP:
  while / whereas (cùng câu)
  By contrast / In contrast (giữa câu)
  compared with / compared to
  However (đối lập mức đoạn)

SỐ LIỆU CỤ THỂ:
  standing at X, compared with Y for...
  the gap between A and B was/narrowed/widened
  accounted for X% / represented [fraction]

LƯU Ý:
  → Dùng "that/those" thay vì lặp lại noun
  → "compared with" trang trọng hơn "compared to"
  → Thêm adverbs: considerably, significantly, marginally, roughly
```
