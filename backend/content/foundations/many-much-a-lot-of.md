## Tóm tắt

| Từ | Dùng với | Formal/Informal | Ví dụ |
|----|---------|----------------|-------|
| **many** | Đếm được số nhiều | Neutral / Formal | many books, many students |
| **much** | Không đếm được | Neutral / Formal | much time, much money |
| **a lot of** | Cả hai | Informal / Neutral | a lot of books, a lot of time |
| **lots of** | Cả hai | Informal | lots of ideas, lots of water |

---

## Tại sao quan trọng

Trong IELTS Writing, dùng "a lot of" quá nhiều làm giảm điểm Lexical Resource vì nó mang tính informal. Examiner muốn thấy bạn biết phân biệt và sử dụng đa dạng: many, much, a great deal of, numerous, a significant number of. Trong Speaking, "a lot of" hoàn toàn tự nhiên.

---

## PHẦN 1: MANY — Dùng với danh từ đếm được số nhiều

```
many + countable noun (plural)
```

**Với danh từ đếm được:**
- **Many** students struggle with time management.
- She has **many** responsibilities at work.
- There are **many** reasons why this policy may fail.
- I don't have **many** friends in this city.
- How **many** people attended the event?

**Vị trí trong câu:**
- Câu khẳng định: "There are **many** advantages."
- Câu phủ định: "There aren't **many** options left."
- Câu hỏi: "How **many** times have you visited?"

> **Lưu ý:** "many" có thể đứng một mình như đại từ: "**Many** of the students passed." / "**Many** have tried, but few have succeeded."

---

## PHẦN 2: MUCH — Dùng với danh từ không đếm được

```
much + uncountable noun
```

**Với danh từ không đếm được:**
- She doesn't have **much** time to prepare.
- There isn't **much** water left in the bottle.
- He showed **much** patience throughout the process.
- How **much** money do you need?
- We haven't made **much** progress.

> **Lưu ý quan trọng:** Trong câu khẳng định, "much" nghe hơi formal/cứng khi đứng một mình:
> - ❌ (informal) "I have **much** work to do." *(sounds unnatural in speech)*
> - ✅ "I have **a lot of** work to do." *(natural spoken)*
> - ✅ "There is **much** to be done." *(formal/written)*
>
> Trong câu phủ định và câu hỏi, "much" rất tự nhiên: "I don't have **much** time."

---

## PHẦN 3: A LOT OF / LOTS OF — Dùng được cả hai

```
a lot of / lots of + countable (plural) OR uncountable noun
```

**Với đếm được:**
- **A lot of** students are applying for the scholarship.
- She has **lots of** ideas for the project.

**Với không đếm được:**
- He spends **a lot of** time on social media.
- There is **lots of** information available online.

**A lot of vs. Lots of:**
- Về nghĩa: tương đương nhau
- "Lots of" hơi informal hơn "a lot of" một chút
- Cả hai thích hợp cho speaking và informal writing, **tránh trong IELTS Task 2**

### Thay thế trong academic writing:

| Informal | Academic (đếm được) | Academic (không đếm được) |
|---------|-------------------|--------------------------|
| a lot of | a large number of / numerous / many | a great deal of / a significant amount of |
| lots of | a considerable number of | a considerable amount of |

---

## PHẦN 4: TOO MANY / TOO MUCH — Quá nhiều (tiêu cực)

"Too" thêm nghĩa **tiêu cực** — quá nhiều đến mức có vấn đề:

```
too many + countable plural (negative)
too much + uncountable (negative)
```

- There are **too many** cars on the road. *(gây tắc đường)*
- She has **too many** responsibilities. *(quá tải)*
- He spends **too much** time playing video games.
- There is **too much** noise in the office.
- **Too much** screen time can harm children's eyesight.

> **So sánh:** "She has **many** responsibilities." *(trung tính)* vs. "She has **too many** responsibilities." *(quá tải)*

---

## PHẦN 5: SO MANY / SO MUCH — Rất nhiều (nhấn mạnh, thường ngạc nhiên)

```
so many + countable plural (emphasis)
so much + uncountable (emphasis)
```

- There are **so many** things to consider.
- I had **so much** fun at the festival.
- She showed **so much** courage in a difficult situation.
- There were **so many** people at the event that we couldn't get in.
- I have **so much** work this week — I don't know where to start.

---

## PHẦN 6: HOW MANY / HOW MUCH — Câu hỏi về số lượng

```
How many + countable plural + ?
How much + uncountable + ?
```

- **How many** languages do you speak?
- **How many** people live in your city?
- **How much** water should we drink per day?
- **How much** does this course cost?
- **How much** time do we have left?

> **Lưu ý thực tế:** "How much?" cũng dùng để hỏi giá tiền — đây là dạng rút gọn của "How much does it cost?"

---

## PHẦN 7: MUCH / MANY TRONG FORMAL WRITING

Trong IELTS Writing Task 2 và academic writing, dùng nhiều biến thể phong phú hơn:

### Thay thế cho "many":

| Từ/cụm | Mức trang trọng | Ví dụ |
|--------|----------------|-------|
| **numerous** | Formal | **Numerous** studies have shown... |
| **a large number of** | Formal | **A large number of** young people... |
| **a considerable number of** | Formal | **A considerable number of** respondents... |
| **a significant number of** | Formal | **A significant number of** countries... |
| **the majority of** | Formal | **The majority of** students... |
| **a wide range of** | Formal | **A wide range of** factors... |
| **countless** | Emphatic | **Countless** attempts have been made... |

### Thay thế cho "much":

| Từ/cụm | Mức trang trọng | Ví dụ |
|--------|----------------|-------|
| **a great deal of** | Formal | **A great deal of** evidence supports... |
| **a significant amount of** | Formal | **A significant amount of** time is wasted... |
| **a considerable amount of** | Formal | **A considerable amount of** funding is needed... |
| **substantial** | Formal (adj) | **Substantial** progress has been made. |
| **extensive** | Formal (adj) | **Extensive** research has been conducted. |

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Dùng "much" với danh từ đếm được

- ❌ "There are **much** students in the class."
- ✅ "There are **many** students in the class."

### Lỗi 2: Dùng "many" với danh từ không đếm được

- ❌ "She doesn't have **many** time."
- ✅ "She doesn't have **much** time."

### Lỗi 3: Dùng "a lot of" quá nhiều trong IELTS Writing

- ❌ "There are **a lot of** reasons for this. **A lot of** research has been done. **A lot of** people are affected."
- ✅ "There are **numerous** reasons for this. **Extensive** research has been conducted. **A large number of** people are affected."

### Lỗi 4: "Too many/much" dùng trong ngữ cảnh trung tính

- ❌ "She has **too many** qualifications." *(nghe như là vấn đề)*
- ✅ "She has **many** qualifications."

### Lỗi 5: "So much/many" ở văn học thuật

"So much/many" nghe informal nếu dùng trong academic writing mà không có mệnh đề kết quả:
- Informal: "There is **so much** inequality in the world."
- Formal: "**Significant** inequality persists across many societies."

## Tóm tắt nhanh

```
MANY + đếm được số nhiều:
  many students, many reasons, many countries

MUCH + không đếm được:
  much time, much money, much progress

A LOT OF + cả hai (nhưng informal):
  a lot of books, a lot of information

TOO MANY/MUCH = quá nhiều (tiêu cực):
  too many cars, too much noise

SO MANY/MUCH = rất nhiều (nhấn mạnh):
  so many options, so much work

HOW MANY/MUCH = câu hỏi số lượng:
  How many languages? / How much time?

ACADEMIC WRITING:
  many → numerous / a large number of / a significant number of
  much → a great deal of / a considerable amount of / extensive
```
