Tea is one of the oldest drinks in the world. According to legend, it was
discovered in China thousands of years ago when a few leaves blew into a pot of
boiling water. Today it is the most popular **beverage** on Earth after plain
water.

From China, tea travelled along trade routes to Japan, India, and eventually
Europe. Each culture made it its own. In Japan, preparing and serving tea grew
into a careful **ritual**, with precise steps and quiet attention. In Britain,
it became a daily habit shared with milk and conversation.

Wherever it went, tea did more than quench thirst — it brought people together.
