## Tóm tắt

Mô tả xu hướng cần hai thứ: **từ vựng đúng** và **cấu trúc ngữ pháp đúng**. Tất cả xu hướng đều rơi vào một trong năm loại:

| Xu hướng | Verb | Noun |
|----------|------|------|
| Tăng | rise, increase, grow, climb, surge | a rise, an increase, growth, a surge |
| Giảm | fall, decrease, decline, drop, plunge | a fall, a decrease, a decline, a drop |
| Dao động | fluctuate | fluctuation |
| Ổn định | remain stable/steady, level off, plateau | stability, a plateau |
| Đạt đỉnh / chạm đáy | peak, reach a peak / bottom out, reach a low | a peak, a low point |

---

## Tại sao quan trọng

Task 1 chiếm 1/3 điểm Writing. Điểm **Lexical Resource** và **Grammatical Accuracy** phụ thuộc nhiều vào:
- Có dùng được nhiều từ đồng nghĩa không (rise/increase/climb/surge)?
- Có dùng đúng cả dạng verb và noun không?
- Có kết hợp tính từ/trạng từ để diễn tả mức độ không?

Câu chỉ dùng "increased" và "decreased" cho mọi xu hướng không thể đạt Band 7+.

---

## PHẦN 1: TĂNG — Rise / Increase / Grow

### Động từ (Verb forms)

```
Subject + rose/increased/grew/climbed/surged + (by + amount) + (to + level) + (in + period)
```

| Động từ | Sắc thái |
|--------|---------|
| **rise** (rose) | trung tính, phổ biến nhất |
| **increase** | trung tính, chuẩn mực |
| **grow** | thường dùng với tỷ lệ/số lượng |
| **climb** | tăng dần, từng bước |
| **surge** | tăng mạnh, đột ngột |
| **soar** | tăng rất cao, ấn tượng |
| **jump** | tăng đột ngột |
| **rocket** / **skyrocket** | tăng cực nhanh |

**Ví dụ:**
- Sales **rose** from 200 to 350 units between 2010 and 2015.
- The number of users **increased** by 40% over the decade.
- The population **grew** steadily throughout the period.
- Temperatures **climbed** gradually from January to July.
- Demand **surged** in 2020, reaching an all-time high.
- Profits **soared** after the new product launch.

### Danh từ (Noun forms)

```
There was a [adj] rise/increase/growth in [noun] + (from X to Y) + (in period)
[Subject] saw/experienced/recorded a [adj] rise/increase
```

- **There was a significant rise** in unemployment during the recession.
- The data shows **a steady increase** in average temperatures.
- **There was a dramatic surge** in online shopping during the pandemic.
- The country **experienced** **rapid growth** in GDP throughout the 1990s.
- The graph shows **a gradual climb** from 2000 to 2010.

## PHẦN 2: GIẢM — Fall / Decrease / Decline

### Động từ

| Động từ | Sắc thái |
|--------|---------|
| **fall** (fell) | trung tính, phổ biến nhất |
| **decrease** | trung tính |
| **decline** | thường dùng cho xu hướng dài hạn |
| **drop** | giảm rõ rệt, đột ngột |
| **plunge** / **plummet** | giảm mạnh, rất đột ngột |
| **dip** | giảm nhẹ, tạm thời |
| **slip** | giảm nhẹ, từ từ |

```
Subject + fell/decreased/declined/dropped + (by + amount) + (to + level) + (in + period)
```

- The number of smokers **fell** significantly between 1990 and 2000.
- Attendance **decreased** by around 15% over the period.
- Manufacturing output **declined** steadily throughout the 1980s.
- Profits **dropped** sharply in the final quarter.
- The share price **plunged** following the announcement.
- Sales **dipped** briefly in mid-2019 before recovering.

### Danh từ

- **There was a sharp fall** in sales in the third quarter.
- The period saw **a gradual decline** in birth rates.
- **A significant drop** in temperatures was recorded in November.
- The data shows **a dramatic plunge** following the financial crisis.

## PHẦN 3: ĐẠT ĐỈNH VÀ CHẠM ĐÁY

### Đạt đỉnh — Peak / Reach a peak

```
Subject + peaked at + [level] + in/at [time]
Subject + reached a peak of + [level] + in [time]
Subject + hit an all-time high of + [level]
```

- Sales **peaked at** 500 units in 2018.
- The unemployment rate **reached a peak of** 12% during the recession.
- Temperatures **hit an all-time high** of 45°C in the summer of 2021.
- The figure **reached its highest point** of $2 billion in 2015.

### Chạm đáy — Bottom out / Reach a low

```
Subject + bottomed out at + [level] + in [time]
Subject + reached its lowest point of + [level]
Subject + fell to a low of + [level]
```

- Production **bottomed out at** 100 units in 2008 before recovering.
- The stock price **reached its lowest point** of $5 in March 2020.
- Exports **fell to a low of** $10 million before gradually recovering.

---

## PHẦN 4: DAO ĐỘNG — Fluctuate

```
Subject + fluctuated (between X and Y) + over [period]
Subject + showed some fluctuation + in [period]
There were fluctuations in + subject + throughout the period
```

- Prices **fluctuated** considerably throughout the year, ranging from $20 to $35.
- The data **fluctuated between** 400 and 600 units over the decade.
- **There were significant fluctuations** in rainfall from month to month.
- Attendance **showed considerable fluctuation** throughout the period.

---

## PHẦN 5: ỔN ĐỊNH — Remain Stable / Level Off / Plateau

```
Subject + remained stable/steady + at + [level]
Subject + levelled off + at + [level]
Subject + plateaued + at + [level]
Subject + stayed at roughly + [level]
```

- The population **remained stable** at around 5 million throughout the 1990s.
- After rising sharply, growth **levelled off** at around 20% in 2015.
- Production **plateaued** at 1,000 units per month for several years.
- Temperatures **stayed at roughly** 25°C for most of the summer.
- There was **little change** in the figures between 2010 and 2015.
- The figures **remained relatively constant** over the period.

## PHẦN 6: TRẠNG TỪ VÀ TÍNH TỪ MỨC ĐỘ

Kết hợp với trạng từ/tính từ để diễn tả **mức độ thay đổi**:

### Mạnh (Strong)

| Tính từ (+ noun) | Trạng từ (+ verb) |
|----------------|------------------|
| a **dramatic** increase | **dramatically** |
| a **sharp** rise | **sharply** |
| a **significant** fall | **significantly** |
| a **substantial** decline | **substantially** |
| a **considerable** drop | **considerably** |
| a **steep** surge | **steeply** |
| a **rapid** growth | **rapidly** |

### Nhẹ (Gentle / Moderate)

| Tính từ (+ noun) | Trạng từ (+ verb) |
|----------------|------------------|
| a **gradual** increase | **gradually** |
| a **slight** decline | **slightly** |
| a **modest** rise | **modestly** |
| a **marginal** decrease | **marginally** |
| a **gentle** climb | **gently** |

### Ổn định (Steady)

| Tính từ | Trạng từ |
|---------|---------|
| a **steady** growth | **steadily** |
| a **consistent** rise | **consistently** |
| a **stable** level | — |

## PHẦN 7: CẤU TRÚC CÂU HAY DÙNG

### Dùng Verb (Subject + Verb)

```
[Subject] rose/fell/fluctuated [adverb] [by amount] [to level] [in period]
```
- "The figure **rose sharply** by 50% to reach 300 in 2010."
- "Sales **fell gradually** from $500 to $300 million between 2008 and 2012."

### Dùng Noun (There was a + Noun phrase)

```
There was a [adjective] [noun] in [subject] [from/between X and Y]
```
- "**There was a dramatic increase** in oil prices between 2005 and 2008."
- "**There was a gradual decline** in the number of manufacturing jobs."

### Dùng Noun (Subject saw/experienced/recorded)

```
[Subject] saw/experienced/recorded a [adjective] [noun]
```
- "The UK **experienced** **a sharp rise** in immigration during this period."
- "The chart **shows** **a steady climb** in temperatures over the century."

### Linking câu mô tả xu hướng

- "**After** rising sharply in the early years, the figure **levelled off**..."
- "**Having peaked** at X in [year], the number **then fell**..."
- "**Following** a brief dip in 2008, sales **recovered** and **continued** to grow."
- "**Despite** fluctuating throughout the period, the overall trend was upward."

---

## Ví dụ câu mô tả biểu đồ hoàn chỉnh

**Biểu đồ:** Số lượng xe điện bán ra từ 2010 đến 2020.

> "The number of electric vehicles sold **rose steadily** throughout the period. **Having started at** around 50,000 units in 2010, sales **grew gradually** to reach 200,000 by 2015, before **surging dramatically** to **peak at** 800,000 in 2020. **There was a brief dip** in 2016, when sales **fell slightly** by around 10%, before recovering. **Overall**, the data shows **a substantial and consistent upward trend** over the decade, with a particularly **sharp acceleration** in the latter half of the period."

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Dùng "increased" cho mọi xu hướng tăng

- ❌ "Sales increased. Prices increased. Population increased." (lặp lại)
- ✅ Dùng: rise, grow, climb, surge, soar, jump để thay đổi

### Lỗi 2: Nhầm noun và verb trong cùng cấu trúc

- ❌ "There was a **rose** in sales." (rose là verb)
- ✅ "There was a **rise** in sales." (rise là noun)
- ❌ "Sales **increase** significantly." (dùng noun thay verb)
- ✅ "Sales **increased** significantly." (verb → past tense)

### Lỗi 3: "Reach the peak" thay vì "reach a peak" / "peak at"

- ❌ "Sales reached the peak at 500."
- ✅ "Sales **peaked at** 500." / "Sales **reached a peak of** 500."

### Lỗi 4: Không có adverb → mô tả không cụ thể

- ❌ "The figure fell."
- ✅ "The figure **fell sharply**." / "The figure **fell gradually**."

### Lỗi 5: Nhầm "by" và "to" khi mô tả mức tăng

- "increased **by** 50%" → tăng thêm 50%
- "increased **to** 500" → đạt đến mức 500
- ❌ "increased to 50%" (khi muốn nói "tăng 50%")
- ✅ "increased **by** 50%" (tăng thêm 50%)

## Tóm tắt nhanh

```
TĂNG:   rise/increase/grow/climb/surge/soar + (sharply/gradually/steadily)
        a rise/increase/growth + (dramatic/gradual/steady)

GIẢM:   fall/decrease/decline/drop/plunge/dip + (sharply/gradually)
        a fall/decrease/decline/drop + (sharp/gradual/slight)

ĐỈNH:   peaked at X / reached a peak of X / hit an all-time high of X

ĐÁY:    bottomed out at X / reached its lowest point of X

DAO ĐỘNG: fluctuated (between X and Y) / showed fluctuation

ỔN ĐỊNH: remained stable/steady / levelled off / plateaued

MỨC ĐỘ:
  Mạnh:  dramatically/sharply/significantly/rapidly/steeply
  Nhẹ:   gradually/slightly/modestly/marginally/gently
  Đều:   steadily/consistently

LƯU Ý:
  by = tăng/giảm thêm bao nhiêu (by 50%)
  to = đạt đến mức nào (to 500 units)
  from X to Y = từ mức X đến mức Y
```
