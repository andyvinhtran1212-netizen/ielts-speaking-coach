## Tóm tắt

Balanced argument essays yêu cầu bạn:

1. **Trình bày Quan điểm A** với bằng chứng/lý do
2. **Thừa nhận Quan điểm B** (concession)
3. **Phản bác hoặc cân bằng** Quan điểm B
4. **Kết luận** với lập trường rõ ràng (hoặc trung dung)

---

## Tại sao quan trọng

Trong IELTS Writing Task 2, "discuss both views" và partial agreement ("to some extent") chiếm phần lớn đề thi. Người học thường mắc hai lỗi: hoặc trình bày một phía quá thiên về một hướng, hoặc cố gắng cân bằng hoàn toàn mà không đưa ra lập trường rõ ràng. Ngữ pháp phù hợp giúp bạn **thể hiện lập trường trong khi vẫn thừa nhận sự phức tạp** của vấn đề — đây là đặc điểm của Band 7+.

## PHẦN 1: GIỚI THIỆU HAI PHÍA (Introduction)

### Đặt vấn đề với hai góc nhìn:

```
[Topic] is a subject that has attracted considerable debate.
While some argue that [View A], others contend that [View B].
```

- The role of technology in education is a subject that **has attracted considerable debate**. **While** some argue that digital tools enhance learning outcomes, **others contend that** they distract students and undermine critical thinking.
- **Proponents of** stricter immigration controls argue that they protect domestic employment, **whereas critics** maintain that immigration is essential for economic growth.
- Whether governments should prioritise economic growth over environmental protection **is a matter of ongoing debate**, with convincing arguments on both sides.

### Thesis statement — thể hiện lập trường:

**Partial agreement:**
```
This essay will argue that, while [View A has merit], [View B is ultimately more convincing / the advantages outweigh the disadvantages].
```

**Discuss both, no clear stance:**
```
This essay will explore both perspectives before reaching a reasoned conclusion.
```

- **This essay will argue that**, while globalisation undeniably creates economic opportunities, **its social and environmental costs are too significant to be dismissed**.
- **This essay will examine** both the advantages and disadvantages of remote working **before concluding that** a hybrid model represents the most balanced approach.

## PHẦN 2: TRÌNH BÀY QUAN ĐIỂM A (Body 1)

### Mở đầu đoạn:

```
Those who support / advocate / favour [X] argue that...
Proponents of [X] contend / maintain / assert / claim that...
From the perspective of [supporters], [X] offers...
There are several compelling arguments in favour of [X].
```

- **Those who advocate** stricter regulation of social media **argue that** it would protect vulnerable users, particularly children, from harmful content.
- **Proponents of** a universal basic income **contend that** it would eliminate poverty and provide individuals with greater freedom to pursue meaningful work.
- **From the perspective of** environmentalists, renewable energy is not merely preferable but essential if catastrophic climate change is to be averted.

### Phát triển luận điểm với bằng chứng/lý do:

```
This is because / This can be explained by the fact that...
[Point]. As a result / consequently, [effect].
[Evidence / example]. This suggests / indicates / demonstrates that...
```

- Supporters argue that universal healthcare reduces inequality. **This is because** it ensures that access to medical treatment is determined by need rather than financial capacity. **As a result**, preventable diseases are less likely to go untreated in lower-income communities.
- Research consistently shows that children who attend pre-school perform better academically throughout their education. **This suggests that** early childhood investment yields significant long-term benefits for both individuals and society as a whole.

## PHẦN 3: THỪA NHẬN VÀ PHẢN BÁC (Concession + Counter-Argument)

Đây là phần ngữ pháp quan trọng nhất trong balanced argument essays.

### Bước 1 — Thừa nhận (Concession):

```
Admittedly, [View B has some merit / there is some truth in the claim that...]
It is true that / It cannot be denied that [X].
Granted, [concession point].
There is certainly some validity to the argument that...
```

- **Admittedly**, there is some merit in the argument that stricter immigration controls can protect local wages in certain industries.
- **It cannot be denied that** social media has provided unprecedented opportunities for communication and community building.
- **Granted**, economic growth does generate employment and raise living standards in the short term.

### Bước 2 — Phản bác (Counter-argument):

```
However, / Nevertheless, / Nonetheless, [main argument].
Yet, this argument fails to account for [X].
This view, however, overlooks the fact that [X].
While this may be true in some cases, [counter-point].
```

- **However**, such controls risk depriving economies of the skilled workers needed to sustain growth and fill critical shortages in areas such as healthcare and technology.
- **Nevertheless**, the evidence suggests that the harms associated with unregulated social media — including cyberbullying, misinformation, and addiction — significantly outweigh these benefits.
- **While this may be true in certain contexts**, the long-term environmental and social costs of unchecked economic growth often exceed its short-term benefits.

### Cấu trúc kết hợp (Concession + Counter trong một câu):

```
Although / While / Even though [concession], [counter-argument].
[Concession], but [counter-argument].
[Concession]; however, [counter-argument].
```

- **Although** economic growth generates employment, **its benefits are not evenly distributed**, and inequality tends to widen as economies expand.
- **While** technology undeniably improves access to information, **it also introduces new risks** in terms of privacy, misinformation, and social isolation.
- The tax cuts may stimulate short-term consumer spending; **however**, their long-term impact on public services is likely to be deeply damaging.

## PHẦN 4: CÁC CÔNG CỤ CÂN BẰNG (Balancing Devices)

### On one hand... on the other hand:

```
On one hand, [View A]. On the other hand, [View B].
```

- **On one hand**, homeschooling allows parents to tailor education to their child's individual needs. **On the other hand**, it may deprive children of the social development that comes from interacting with peers in a structured environment.

### While / Whereas (trực tiếp đối chiếu):

```
While [View A], [View B].
[View A], whereas [View B].
```

- **While** proponents argue that nuclear energy is a necessary transition fuel, **critics point out** that the unresolved issue of nuclear waste poses unacceptable long-term risks.
- Younger generations tend to embrace technological innovation readily, **whereas** older demographics often express concern about the pace and implications of such change.

### To some extent... but:

```
To some extent, [partial agreement]. However, [qualification].
[X] is partly true / partially justified. Nevertheless, [limitation].
```

- **To some extent**, it is true that increased competition encourages innovation. **However**, it is equally true that in markets dominated by a small number of powerful corporations, competition is often more apparent than real.

## PHẦN 5: HEDGING — Diễn đạt thận trọng trong lập luận cân bằng

Không nên quả quyết tuyệt đối khi trình bày một phía — dùng hedging để thể hiện tính học thuật:

```
It could be argued that...
There is reason to believe that...
[X] appears to / seems to / tends to...
[X] may / might / can [result in...]
In many cases / In some instances...
```

- **It could be argued that** the benefits of globalisation are unevenly distributed, with wealthier nations capturing the majority of economic gains.
- **There is reason to believe that** the long-term consequences of automation will be far more disruptive to the labour market than current projections suggest.
- **Stricter environmental regulations may** impose short-term costs on businesses, but they **tend to** generate substantial long-term savings through reduced pollution and improved public health.

## PHẦN 6: KẾT BÀI — Balanced Conclusion

### Cấu trúc kết bài cân bằng:

```
In conclusion, both perspectives have merit.
However, the weight of evidence suggests that [one side is stronger].
A balanced approach, incorporating elements of both positions, may offer the most viable path forward.
Ultimately, [broader statement].
```

**Kết bài với lập trường rõ ràng (preferred for Band 7+):**

- **In conclusion**, while there are legitimate arguments on both sides of this debate, I would argue that **the case for** greater government investment in public education is ultimately more compelling. The long-term social and economic returns of a well-educated workforce **substantially outweigh** the short-term fiscal costs involved.

**Kết bài trung dung (discuss both views):**

- **In conclusion**, both increased economic integration and the protection of national industries offer genuine advantages. **The most effective approach** is likely to involve a carefully calibrated strategy that maximises the benefits of international trade while implementing targeted measures to protect vulnerable domestic sectors.

## PHẦN 7: TEMPLATE ĐẦY ĐỦ — Balanced Argument

```
INTRODUCTION:
[Topic paraphrase]. While some argue that [View A], others contend that [View B].
This essay will [examine both views / argue that, while X has merit, Y is ultimately more convincing].

BODY 1 — VIEW A:
Proponents of [A] argue that [main point].
[Explanation / evidence]. As a result, [effect].
Furthermore, [supporting point], which suggests that [implication].

BODY 2 — VIEW B + CONCESSION/COUNTER:
However, those who favour [B] contend that [main point].
[Evidence / explanation]. This indicates that [implication].
Admittedly, [concession about View A]. Nevertheless, [counter-argument that supports B or your position].

CONCLUSION:
In conclusion, while [both views have merit / View A has some validity],
[your final position / recommendation].
Ultimately, [broader outlook or implication].
```

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Không đưa ra lập trường rõ ràng trong kết bài

- ❌ "In conclusion, there are advantages and disadvantages to both views."
- ✅ "In conclusion, while there are merits on both sides, **I believe that** the long-term benefits of [X] **outweigh** the drawbacks, provided that appropriate safeguards are in place."

### Lỗi 2: Dùng "on the other hand" mà không có "on one hand" hoặc dùng không đúng cặp

- ❌ "**On the other hand**, education improves quality of life." *(không có "one hand")*
- ✅ "**On one hand**, education improves career prospects. **On the other hand**, it also enhances quality of life beyond the workplace."

### Lỗi 3: Concession quá yếu hoặc không có counter-argument

- ❌ "Admittedly, there are some benefits. However, there are also disadvantages."
- ✅ "**Admittedly**, remote working does offer workers greater flexibility. **However**, research consistently indicates that prolonged physical isolation from colleagues **undermines** collaborative creativity and erodes team cohesion — outcomes that are **difficult to replicate** in a fully remote environment."

### Lỗi 4: Dùng "both sides have good points" trong kết bài của bài "to what extent"

Với đề "to what extent do you agree", kết bài phải có lập trường rõ ràng — không trung dung hoàn toàn.

### Lỗi 5: Lặp cấu trúc concession quá nhiều lần

Tránh dùng "Admittedly... However" trong mỗi đoạn. Xen kẽ:
- Admittedly... However
- While it is true that... Nevertheless
- Granted... Yet
- There is some validity in... That said

## Bài tập luyện

### Hoàn thành câu concession + counter-argument

1. "Admittedly, renewable energy currently costs more to install. However, ___."
2. "While it is true that immigration puts pressure on public services, ___."
3. "Granted, stricter censorship may protect children from harmful content. Nevertheless, ___."

### Viết câu "on one hand... on the other hand" cho chủ đề sau:

4. **Chủ đề:** Advantages and disadvantages of living in a city.

### Viết kết bài cho câu hỏi:

5. "To what extent do you agree that governments should prioritise economic growth over environmental protection?"

### Gợi ý đáp án

1. "...the long-term savings on energy bills and the stabilisation of energy prices more than compensate for the initial investment."
2. "...the economic contribution of immigrants — through taxes, entrepreneurship, and filling critical skills shortages — substantially outweighs this strain."
3. "...the precedent set by government censorship poses a significant threat to freedom of expression and the open exchange of ideas that underpins democratic societies."
4. "On one hand, urban living offers unparalleled access to employment, cultural amenities, and social opportunities. On the other hand, cities are associated with higher costs of living, congestion, pollution, and a sense of social isolation that is paradoxically more pronounced in densely populated areas."
5. "In conclusion, while economic development undeniably plays a vital role in raising living standards, I firmly believe that **prioritising growth at the expense of environmental protection is ultimately self-defeating**. A degraded natural environment imposes enormous long-term costs on economies and societies alike, and the most resilient path forward is one in which sustainable development is pursued as an integrated goal rather than as a secondary consideration."

## Tóm tắt nhanh

```
GIỚI THIỆU HAI PHÍA:
  While some argue that [A], others contend that [B].
  Proponents of [X] argue... / Critics of [X] contend...

TRÌNH BÀY QUAN ĐIỂM:
  Those who support [X] argue that... This is because...
  Research/Evidence suggests that... As a result,...

CONCESSION + COUNTER:
  Admittedly, [X]. However, [Y].
  While it is true that [X], [counter].
  Granted, [concession]. Nevertheless, [main point].
  There is some validity to [X]. That said, [counter].

BALANCING DEVICES:
  On one hand... On the other hand...
  While [A], [B]. | [A], whereas [B].
  To some extent... However...

HEDGING:
  It could be argued that... | There is reason to believe...
  [X] appears to / tends to / may / might...

KẾT BÀI:
  While both views have merit, [clear position].
  Ultimately, [broader implication].
  → Luôn có lập trường rõ ràng với đề "to what extent"
```
