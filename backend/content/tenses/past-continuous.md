## Tóm tắt

Past Continuous diễn tả **hành động đang diễn ra** tại một thời điểm xác định trong quá khứ.

| | Khẳng định | Phủ định | Nghi vấn |
|--|-----------|---------|---------|
| **I/He/She/It** | was working | was not working | Was ... working? |
| **You/We/They** | were working | were not working | Were ... working? |

- At 8 pm last night, I **was studying** for the exam.
- They **were not sleeping** when we arrived.
- **Were** you **watching** TV when I called?

## Tại sao quan trọng

Past Continuous là công cụ kể chuyện quan trọng trong IELTS:
- **Speaking Part 2:** mô tả bối cảnh khi kể về sự kiện quá khứ
- **Writing Task 1:** diễn tả xu hướng đang diễn ra tại một thời điểm
- Tạo sự phong phú về ngữ pháp khi kết hợp với Past Simple

## Cấu trúc và cách dùng

### Cấu trúc

```
Subject + was/were + V-ing
```

**Lưu ý:** Dùng **was** cho I/he/she/it; **were** cho you/we/they.

### 4 cách dùng chính

**1. Hành động đang diễn ra tại một thời điểm cụ thể trong quá khứ**

Thường đi với: *at 8 o'clock, at this time yesterday, at noon last Tuesday...*

- This time last year, she **was living** in London.
- At midnight, the children **were still sleeping**.
- What **were** you **doing** at 3 pm on Monday?

**2. Hành động đang diễn ra thì bị gián đoạn (Past Continuous + Past Simple)**

```
Past Continuous (background) + when + Past Simple (interruption)
```

- I **was reading** a book when the phone **rang**.
- She **was cooking** dinner when he **arrived** home.
- They **were discussing** the plan when the power **went** out.

> **Mẹo:** Past Continuous = hành động nền dài; Past Simple = sự kiện ngắn bất ngờ xảy đến.

**3. Hai hành động song song trong quá khứ**

```
Past Continuous + while + Past Continuous
```

- She **was reading** while he **was watching** TV.
- The students **were taking** notes while the teacher **was explaining**.
- I **was cooking** while my sister **was setting** the table.

**4. Mô tả bối cảnh / tạo không khí trong kể chuyện**

- The sun **was shining**, birds **were singing**, and children **were playing** in the park.
- When we **arrived**, everyone **was enjoying** themselves.
- The city **was buzzing** with excitement — it **was** the night before the festival.

### Dấu hiệu nhận biết

| Từ/Cụm từ | Ví dụ |
|-----------|-------|
| at + thời điểm + yesterday/last... | at 5 pm yesterday |
| this time + last week/year | this time last month |
| when (+ Past Simple) | when I arrived |
| while | while they were working |
| all morning/evening/day | all morning yesterday |

## So sánh Past Continuous vs Past Simple

| | Past Continuous | Past Simple |
|--|---------------|------------|
| **Ý nghĩa** | Đang xảy ra (chưa xong) | Đã xảy ra và kết thúc |
| **Thời gian** | Kéo dài / nền | Điểm thời gian / hoàn thành |
| **Kết hợp** | "when + PS" = bị gián đoạn | "when + PC" = sự kiện bất ngờ |

- I **was walking** home when it **started** to rain. (đang đi thì trời bắt đầu mưa)
- I **walked** home when it **stopped** raining. (đợi tạnh mưa rồi đi bộ về)

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Dùng was/were nhầm

- ❌ You **was** sleeping.
- ✅ You **were** sleeping.
- ❌ They **was** playing football.
- ✅ They **were** playing football.

### Lỗi 2: Quên -ing

- ❌ She was study when I called.
- ✅ She **was studying** when I called.

### Lỗi 3: Dùng state verbs (stative verbs) với Past Continuous

Các động từ chỉ trạng thái **không dùng** với continuous tenses: know, believe, understand, want, need, like, love, hate, have (sở hữu), see (nhìn thấy), hear...

- ❌ I was knowing the answer.
- ✅ I **knew** the answer.
- ❌ She was loving the film.
- ✅ She **loved** the film.

### Lỗi 4: Nhầm khi dùng "when" và "while"

- **When** thường đi với Past Simple: *When she **arrived**, I was cooking.*
- **While** thường đi với Past Continuous: *While she **was cooking**, I was reading.*

- ❌ While she arrived, I was cooking.
- ✅ **When** she arrived, I was cooking.
- ❌ When I was sleeping, he knocked.
- ✅ While I **was sleeping**, he knocked. / When I was sleeping, he **knocked**.

### Lỗi 5: Dùng Past Continuous thay vì Past Simple cho hành động đã hoàn thành

- ❌ Yesterday, I was finishing my homework at 9 pm. (hàm ý chưa xong)
- ✅ Yesterday, I **finished** my homework at 9 pm.
- ✅ Yesterday, at 9 pm, I **was finishing** my homework. (đang làm tại thời điểm đó)

## Ứng dụng trong IELTS

### Speaking Part 2 — Kể chuyện sự kiện:

*"I'd like to talk about a memorable trip to Hội An. When we **arrived**, the sun **was setting** and the lanterns **were glowing** everywhere. While my friends **were taking** photos, I **was just standing** there, taking it all in. It **was** one of the most magical moments I've ever experienced."*

### Speaking Part 1 — Mô tả quá khứ:

- "When I **was studying** at university, I **was living** near the campus."
- "This time last year, I **was preparing** for my IELTS exam."

### Writing Task 1 — Mô tả xu hướng tại thời điểm:

- "In 2005, the rate **was rising** steadily before it peaked in 2008."
- "While the number of urban residents **was increasing**, rural populations **were declining**."

### Writing Task 2 — Mô tả bối cảnh lịch sử:

- "In the early 20th century, scientists **were beginning** to understand the link between diet and health."
- "While governments **were focusing** on economic growth, environmental concerns **were being** largely ignored."

## Bài tập luyện

### Chia động từ đúng (Past Simple hoặc Past Continuous)

1. I (read) ___ a book when she (call) ___ me.
2. They (play) ___ football when it (start) ___ raining.
3. At 10 pm, he (sleep) ___.
4. While she (cook) ___ dinner, I (do) ___ my homework.
5. What (you/do) ___ at midnight last night?

### Chọn "when" hoặc "while"

1. ___ I was studying, my phone rang.
2. ___ she arrived, we were having dinner.
3. He was reading ___ his wife was watching TV.
4. The alarm went off ___ I was sleeping.

### Sửa lỗi

1. She was know the truth all along.
2. You was working very hard yesterday.
3. While he arrived, I was cooking.
4. I was finishing the report at 6 pm yesterday.

### Đáp án

**Chia động từ:**
1. was reading / called | 2. were playing / started | 3. was sleeping | 4. was cooking / was doing | 5. were you doing

**When/While:**
1. While | 2. When | 3. while | 4. while / when

**Sửa lỗi:**
1. She **knew** the truth all along. (stative verb)
2. **You were** working very hard yesterday.
3. **When** he arrived, I was cooking.
4. I **finished** the report at 6 pm yesterday. (hành động hoàn thành → Past Simple)

## Tóm tắt nhanh

| Cách dùng | Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| Hành động đang xảy ra tại thời điểm QK | was/were + V-ing | At noon, I was eating. |
| Hành động bị gián đoạn | PC + when + PS | I was studying when she called. |
| Hai hành động song song | PC + while + PC | He was reading while she was cooking. |
| Mô tả bối cảnh | PC (nền) + PS (sự kiện) | The rain was falling when we left. |

**Quy tắc vàng:**
- **was** = I / he / she / it
- **were** = you / we / they
- Stative verbs (know, want, love...) **không dùng** Past Continuous
- Past Continuous = **nền/bối cảnh**; Past Simple = **sự kiện xảy đến**
