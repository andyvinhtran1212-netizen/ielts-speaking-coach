## Tóm tắt

"I think" không sai — nhưng dùng nó liên tục trong cùng một câu trả lời là dấu hiệu của Lexical Resource thấp.

| Mức độ tự tin | Patterns | Ghi chú |
|---------------|----------|---------|
| **Mạnh / Chắc chắn** | I strongly believe, I am convinced that, I have no doubt that | Dùng khi bạn chắc chắn về quan điểm |
| **Trung bình** | In my opinion, From my point of view, As far as I'm concerned | Trang trọng hơn "I think" |
| **Nhẹ / Thận trọng** | I would say, It seems to me that, I tend to think that | Mềm hơn "I think" — hữu ích khi không chắc |
| **Cá nhân hóa** | Personally, I believe... / For me, ... | Thêm góc nhìn cá nhân |

---

## Tại sao "I think" lặp lại làm yếu câu trả lời

### Vấn đề 1: Nghe đơn điệu và thiếu từ vựng

Examiner đánh giá **Lexical Resource** — đa dạng từ vựng. Khi bạn dùng "I think" 5 lần trong một câu trả lời Part 3, examiner thấy bạn không có công cụ ngôn ngữ để diễn đạt quan điểm theo nhiều cách khác nhau.

❌ "I think people should exercise more. I think the government can help. I think schools should teach this. I think it's very important. I think we need to act now."

✅ "People should definitely exercise more. **In my opinion**, the government has a responsibility to create policies that make this easier. **From my point of view**, schools are actually the best starting point, **since** habits formed early tend to last a lifetime. **I would say** this is one of the most pressing public health issues of our time."

### Vấn đề 2: "I think" ở đầu MỌI câu — mất tính tự nhiên

Người bản ngữ không bắt đầu mỗi câu bằng "I think". Họ dùng nhiều kiểu khác nhau — đôi khi nói thẳng vào quan điểm mà không cần intro phrase, đôi khi dùng "personally", "for me", "honestly".

### Vấn đề 3: Mọi câu đều cùng mức độ tự tin

"I think" không phân biệt được bạn rất chắc chắn hay chỉ đoán thử. Trong IELTS Speaking Part 3, examiner muốn thấy bạn **defend quan điểm** — dùng "I strongly believe" hay "I am convinced that" cho thấy bạn có quan điểm rõ ràng, không chỉ đang đoán.

## NHÓM 1: Phrases trang trọng trung bình (thay "I think" trực tiếp)

### IN MY OPINION / IN MY VIEW

Hai phrases này hoàn toàn thay thế "I think" và nghe academic hơn:

```
In my opinion, [S + V]
In my view, [S + V]
```

- **In my opinion**, governments should prioritise investment in public education over defence spending.
- **In my view**, the rise of social media has had a largely negative effect on how young people perceive themselves.

> "In my opinion" và "In my view" đứng đầu câu, theo sau là dấu phẩy. Không cần thêm "I think" sau đó.
> ❌ "In my opinion, I think..." (lặp — bỏ "I think")
> ✅ "In my opinion, ..." (đủ rồi)

### FROM MY POINT OF VIEW / FROM MY PERSPECTIVE

Nhấn mạnh đây là **góc nhìn cá nhân**, thường dùng để đưa ra một góc nhìn khác sau khi đã trình bày quan điểm chung:

- **From my point of view**, the most important factor is access — not motivation.
- **From my perspective**, the issue is not about technology itself but how we use it.

### AS FAR AS I'M CONCERNED

Diễn đạt ý kiến cá nhân mạnh hơn, đôi khi ngụ ý "có người không đồng ý nhưng đây là quan điểm của tôi":

- **As far as I'm concerned**, working from home is simply more productive for most people.
- **As far as I'm concerned**, stricter environmental laws are not just desirable — they're essential.

## NHÓM 2: Phrases mạnh hơn (khi bạn chắc chắn)

### I STRONGLY BELIEVE / I FIRMLY BELIEVE

Dùng khi bạn muốn thể hiện quan điểm dứt khoát, đặc biệt trong phần tranh luận hay câu hỏi về giá trị:

```
I strongly believe (that) [S + V]
I firmly believe (that) [S + V]
```

- **I strongly believe** that access to education is a fundamental human right, not a privilege.
- **I firmly believe** that no government policy can succeed without addressing root causes of poverty.

### I AM CONVINCED THAT

Ngụ ý bạn đã suy nghĩ kỹ và đưa ra kết luận — phù hợp cho câu trả lời Part 3 phức tạp:

- **I am convinced that** urban planning is one of the most critical issues facing governments in the coming decades.
- **I am convinced that** early childhood education has a far greater impact than people realise.

### I HAVE NO DOUBT THAT

Diễn đạt sự chắc chắn tuyệt đối — dùng tiết kiệm, chỉ khi quan điểm thực sự rõ ràng:

- **I have no doubt that** technology will continue to transform the workplace — the only question is how quickly.

---

## NHÓM 3: Phrases nhẹ hơn (khi không chắc chắn hoàn toàn)

### I WOULD SAY (THAT)

Nhẹ hơn "I think" một chút — diễn đạt quan điểm với sắc thái thận trọng, nghe tự nhiên trong Speaking:

```
I would say (that) [S + V]
I'd say [S + V]
```

- **I would say** the biggest challenge facing young people today is probably job insecurity.
- **I'd say** that most people underestimate how much their daily habits affect their long-term health.

> "I would say" / "I'd say" — phổ biến, tự nhiên, phù hợp cả Part 1 lẫn Part 3.

### IT SEEMS TO ME THAT

Diễn đạt quan điểm như một nhận xét/quan sát — phù hợp khi bạn đưa ra kết luận từ bằng chứng:

- **It seems to me that** people are increasingly prioritising experiences over material possessions.
- **It seems to me that** the education system hasn't kept pace with changes in the job market.

### I TEND TO THINK THAT

Ngụ ý đây là thiên hướng chung của bạn, không phải quan điểm cứng nhắc — nghe rất tự nhiên trong Speaking:

- **I tend to think that** smaller class sizes lead to better educational outcomes, though I know the research is mixed.
- **I tend to think that** people learn more from failure than from success.

## NHÓM 4: Cá nhân hóa (bắt đầu bằng "Personally" hoặc "For me")

### PERSONALLY, I BELIEVE / PERSONALLY, I THINK

"Personally" thêm sắc thái cá nhân trước quan điểm — và thay "I think" bằng "I believe" tạo sự đa dạng:

- **Personally, I believe** that the relationship between mental health and physical activity is underappreciated.
- **Personally, I think** the pressure on students has become completely unsustainable.

### FOR ME, ...

Không cần thêm "think" hay "believe" — "For me" đã đủ để giới thiệu quan điểm cá nhân:

- **For me**, the most rewarding part of travelling is meeting local people rather than visiting tourist attractions.
- **For me**, the defining issue of this generation is climate change — everything else is secondary.

### HONESTLY, / FRANKLY, ...

Nghe rất tự nhiên và conversational — phù hợp Part 1 và Part 2:

- **Honestly**, I find it quite hard to stay motivated when working from home.
- **Frankly**, I think most people care more about salary than job satisfaction, even if they say otherwise.

---

## Không cần phrase nào cả — đôi khi nói thẳng tốt hơn

Không phải câu nào cũng cần intro phrase. Đặc biệt trong Part 3, sau khi đã giới thiệu quan điểm, bạn có thể phát triển ý mà không cần lặp lại "I think" hay "In my opinion":

❌ "I think exercise is important. I think it helps people mentally. I think it also helps physically."

✅ "Exercise is fundamentally important — both mentally and physically. **In fact**, research consistently shows that regular physical activity reduces rates of anxiety and depression, sometimes as effectively as medication. **For this reason**, I believe governments should do far more to make sport and exercise accessible to everyone."

## Phân loại theo mức độ trang trọng

| Register | Phrases | Phù hợp |
|----------|---------|---------|
| **Formal / Academic** | In my opinion, In my view, I am convinced that, I strongly believe, From my perspective | Writing Task 2, Speaking Part 3 (câu phức tạp) |
| **Semi-formal** | I would say, It seems to me that, I tend to think that, As far as I'm concerned | Part 3, Part 2 |
| **Informal / Conversational** | Personally, For me, Honestly, Frankly, I'd say | Part 1, Part 2, phần trả lời ngắn |

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Lặp "I think" ở đầu mọi câu

- ❌ "I think cities are better. I think there are more jobs. I think young people prefer cities. I think the countryside is boring."
- ✅ "Cities generally offer more opportunities for young people. **In my opinion**, the job market is simply far more diverse in urban areas. **For me**, the access to culture and nightlife is also a significant draw — though I'd admit **it seems to me** that many people underestimate how stressful city life can be."

### Lỗi 2: "In my opinion, I think..."

Lặp hai lần — "In my opinion" đã bao gồm ý "I think".

- ❌ In my opinion, I think the government should invest more in education.
- ✅ **In my opinion**, the government should invest more in education.
- ✅ **I think** the government should invest more in education.

### Lỗi 3: Dùng phrases quá mạnh cho quan điểm không chắc

- ❌ "I am absolutely convinced that eating vegetables every day makes you smarter." (quá mạnh cho claim không có evidence)
- ✅ "I **tend to think** that a balanced diet plays a role in cognitive function, though the exact relationship is complex."

### Lỗi 4: Dùng "From my point of view" nhưng không đưa ra quan điểm khác biệt

"From my point of view" ngụ ý góc nhìn cá nhân — đừng dùng nó cho những điều ai cũng đồng ý:

- ❌ "From my point of view, clean water is important." (hiển nhiên — không cần phrase này)
- ✅ "From my point of view, the most overlooked solution to urban pollution is simply reducing private car use."

### Lỗi 5: Dùng phrases trang trọng quá trong Part 1

- ❌ "I am firmly convinced that coffee is my preferred morning beverage." (Part 1 — quá formal)
- ✅ "Honestly, I can't really function without a coffee in the morning." (Part 1 — tự nhiên hơn)

## Ứng dụng trong IELTS Speaking

### Part 1 — Câu trả lời thân mật:

*"Do you prefer the city or the countryside?"*

❌ "I think the city is better. I think there are more things to do. I think it's more exciting."

✅ "**For me**, cities just have so much more to offer — the restaurants, the events, the career opportunities. **Honestly**, the countryside sounds peaceful, but **I'd say** I'd get bored quite quickly. **That said**, I think I'd appreciate it more as I get older."

### Part 2 — Kết hợp trong monologue:

*"Describe a person you admire."*

"...She was remarkably resilient. **In my view**, the most impressive thing about her was not her talent but her persistence. **Personally, I believe** that kind of attitude is far more important than raw ability. **As far as I'm concerned**, she's been one of the most influential people in my life."

### Part 3 — Tranh luận và phân tích:

*"Do you think social media has had a positive or negative impact on society?"*

"**From my point of view**, the picture is genuinely mixed. **I would say** social media has undeniably improved access to information and connected people across distances in remarkable ways. **However, I am convinced that** the design of most platforms is fundamentally at odds with mental wellbeing — they're engineered to maximise engagement, not happiness. **It seems to me that** regulation is going to be unavoidable at some point, though **I tend to think** most governments have been far too slow to act."

## Bài tập luyện

### Rewrite — thay thế "I think" bằng phrase phù hợp

Viết lại câu sau, thay thế "I think" bằng một phrase khác phù hợp với mức độ tự tin hoặc ngữ cảnh. Có thể viết 2 phiên bản khác nhau.

1. "I think the government should spend more on public healthcare."
2. "I think learning a second language is very beneficial."
3. "I think I prefer studying in the morning, but I'm not sure."
4. "I think climate change is the biggest problem humanity faces."
5. "I think my city is quite modern but lacks green spaces."

### Chọn phrase phù hợp nhất

Chọn phrase phù hợp nhất cho mỗi tình huống:

1. Bạn rất chắc chắn rằng giáo dục sớm là quan trọng: ___
2. Bạn đang trả lời Part 1 về thức uống yêu thích một cách tự nhiên: ___
3. Bạn đưa ra quan điểm thận trọng về một vấn đề phức tạp: ___
4. Bạn muốn thể hiện đây là quan điểm cá nhân trong Part 3: ___

*(in my opinion / honestly / I tend to think that / I strongly believe that)*

### Viết câu trả lời mẫu

Viết câu trả lời cho câu hỏi sau (4-5 câu) mà **không dùng "I think" một lần nào**:

*"Do you think people today are more stressed than people in the past? Why?"*

---

## Đáp án

### Rewrite (gợi ý):

1. **In my opinion** / **I strongly believe** the government should spend more on public healthcare.
2. **I am convinced that** / **From my point of view**, learning a second language is extremely beneficial.
3. **I would say** / **I tend to think** I prefer studying in the morning, though I'm not entirely sure.
4. **I firmly believe** / **As far as I'm concerned**, climate change is the single greatest threat facing humanity.
5. **Personally** / **From my perspective**, my city is quite modern but frustratingly lacks green spaces.

### Chọn phrase:

1. I strongly believe that / I am convinced that
2. Honestly / For me
3. I tend to think that / It seems to me that
4. In my opinion / From my point of view / As far as I'm concerned

### Viết câu trả lời mẫu (gợi ý):

*"Do you think people today are more stressed than people in the past? Why?"*

"**In my view**, yes — modern life has become significantly more stressful in several ways, even if we have more material comfort than previous generations. **From my perspective**, the constant connectivity that smartphones create means that people are never truly 'off work' — there's always another message, another notification, another expectation. **Personally**, I find that quite exhausting. **That said**, I tend to think that stress in the past was just as real — people faced economic hardship, war, and illness in ways that are hard to imagine today. **I would say** the nature of stress has changed more than its intensity."

## Tóm tắt nhanh

```
THAY THẾ "I THINK":

Trang trọng / Mạnh:
→ In my opinion, ...
→ In my view, ...
→ From my point of view, ...
→ As far as I'm concerned, ...
→ I strongly believe that ...
→ I am convinced that ...

Trung bình / Tự nhiên:
→ I would say (that) ...
→ It seems to me that ...
→ I tend to think that ...
→ Personally, I believe ...

Informal / Part 1:
→ For me, ...
→ Honestly, ...
→ Frankly, ...
```

**Quy tắc vàng:**
- Không dùng "I think" quá 1 lần trong một câu trả lời Part 3
- Xen kẽ ít nhất 3 phrases khác nhau khi trả lời câu hỏi dài
- "In my opinion" và "I think" = cùng ý nghĩa — đừng dùng cả hai trong một câu
- Đôi khi không cần phrase nào — đặc biệt khi bạn đang phát triển ý sau câu đầu
- Part 1 → informal phrases; Part 3 → có thể formal hơn
