## Tóm tắt

| Loại lỗi | Ví dụ | Vấn đề |
|----------|-------|--------|
| **"It" không rõ chỉ gì** | "Technology changed society. It is not always good." | "It" = technology hay sự thay đổi? |
| **"They" mơ hồ** | "Parents and teachers disagree. They are often too strict." | "They" = parents hay teachers? |
| **"This" / "That" không rõ** | "Pollution is rising. Traffic is increasing. This is worrying." | "This" = pollution, traffic, hay cả hai? |
| **"He/She" sai đối tượng** | "The teacher told the student that he was wrong." | "He" = teacher hay student? |

## Pronoun reference là gì?

**Pronoun** (đại từ) như *it, they, he, she, this, that, these, those* được dùng để thay thế cho danh từ đã đề cập trước đó — nhằm tránh lặp lại.

Đại từ **chỉ hoạt động tốt** khi người đọc/nghe biết ngay lập tức nó đang thay thế cho danh từ nào. Nếu có thể hiểu nhiều hơn một cách → **lỗi tham chiếu mơ hồ (ambiguous reference)**.

> "The teacher praised the student because **he** worked so hard."

Câu trên mơ hồ vì "he" có thể là teacher hoặc student.

---

## KIỂU 1: "IT" MƠ HỒ

"It" là đại từ thay thế cho một danh từ số ít, không người. Lỗi xảy ra khi câu trước có nhiều danh từ và không rõ "it" chỉ cái nào.

### Ví dụ và cách sửa:

**Ví dụ 1:**
- ❌ "The company introduced a new policy. **It** caused a lot of confusion."
- ✅ "The company introduced a new policy. **The policy** caused a lot of confusion."
- ✅ "The company introduced a new policy **that** caused a lot of confusion."

**Ví dụ 2:**
- ❌ "The report discussed the budget and the timeline. **It** was unclear."
- → Không rõ "it" = report, budget hay timeline
- ✅ "The report discussed the budget and the timeline. **The timeline** was unclear."
- ✅ "The report discussed the budget and the timeline, but **neither** was clearly explained."

**Ví dụ 3:**
- ❌ "Technology has improved education. But **it** has also created problems."
- → Câu này thực ra ổn vì "it" = technology là rõ nhất
- ✅ Nếu muốn rõ hơn: "Technology has improved education, but **technology** has also created problems." *(hoặc giữ "it" — tuỳ ngữ cảnh)*

**Quy tắc:** "It" nên chỉ đến **danh từ gần nhất** hoặc **chủ đề rõ ràng nhất** trong câu trước. Nếu có thể hiểu nhiều hơn một cách → viết lại với danh từ cụ thể.

---

## KIỂU 2: "THEY" MƠ HỒ

"They" thay thế cho danh từ số nhiều. Lỗi xảy ra khi câu trước có nhiều nhóm danh từ số nhiều.

### Ví dụ và cách sửa:

**Ví dụ 1:**
- ❌ "Parents and teachers both care about children. **They** are often too strict."
- → "They" = parents, teachers, hay cả hai?
- ✅ "Parents and teachers both care about children. However, **parents** are often too strict about screen time."
- ✅ "Parents and teachers both care about children, but **teachers** tend to be stricter in academic matters."

**Ví dụ 2:**
- ❌ "The government and businesses need to cooperate. **They** should invest more."
- → Ai đầu tư? Cả hai? Chỉ một bên?
- ✅ "The government and businesses need to cooperate. **Both parties** should invest more in infrastructure."

**Ví dụ 3:**
- ❌ "Students who study abroad often struggle at first. **They** eventually adapt."
- → Câu này ổn — "they" rõ ràng là "students who study abroad"
- Không phải mọi "they" đều mơ hồ — chỉ khi có nhiều nhóm danh từ số nhiều.

## KIỂU 3: "THIS" / "THAT" MƠ HỒ

"This" và "that" thường dùng để chỉ đến một **ý/tình huống** vừa được đề cập. Lỗi xảy ra khi đoạn văn trước đó có nhiều ý và không rõ "this" đang gộp lại cái gì.

### Ví dụ và cách sửa:

**Ví dụ 1:**
- ❌ "Pollution is rising. Traffic is increasing. Deforestation is getting worse. **This** is a serious concern."
- → "This" = pollution? traffic? deforestation? cả ba?
- ✅ "Pollution is rising. Traffic is increasing. Deforestation is getting worse. **These environmental problems** are a serious concern."
- ✅ "**All of these issues** represent a serious threat to the environment."

**Ví dụ 2:**
- ❌ "Many young people prefer working from home. **This** makes employers uncomfortable."
- → Câu này ổn — "this" rõ ràng chỉ "the fact that young people prefer working from home"
- ✅ Có thể viết rõ hơn: "**This preference for remote work** makes some employers uncomfortable."

**Mẹo hay:** Thay "this" bằng **"this + noun"** — "this trend", "this problem", "this approach", "this finding" — để luôn rõ ràng và nâng band.

---

## KIỂU 4: "HE / SHE" SAI ĐỐI TƯỢNG

Khi câu có hai người cùng giới tính, "he" hoặc "she" có thể gây nhầm lẫn.

### Ví dụ và cách sửa:

**Ví dụ 1:**
- ❌ "The teacher told the student that **he** was wrong."
- → "He" = teacher hay student?
- ✅ "The teacher told the student that **the student** was wrong."
- ✅ "The teacher said to the student, '**You** were wrong.'"

**Ví dụ 2:**
- ❌ "After the manager spoke to the employee, **he** felt better."
- → Ai cảm thấy tốt hơn — manager hay employee?
- ✅ "After the manager spoke to the employee, **the employee** felt better."
- ✅ "After their meeting, **the employee** felt more confident."

**Ví dụ 3:**
- ❌ "Maria called Anna because **she** was worried."
- → Ai lo lắng — Maria hay Anna?
- ✅ "**Maria** called Anna because **Maria** was worried about the situation."
- ✅ "Maria called Anna, worried about **her friend's** wellbeing."

## Chiến lược viết rõ pronoun reference

### 1. Dùng "this/that + noun" thay vì chỉ "this/that"

| Mơ hồ | Rõ ràng |
|-------|---------|
| "This is a problem." | "**This situation** is a problem." |
| "This has changed a lot." | "**This attitude** has changed a lot." |
| "This shows that..." | "**This finding** shows that..." |

### 2. Lặp danh từ khi cần thiết

Không phải lúc nào lặp danh từ cũng xấu — đôi khi cần để tránh nhầm lẫn:

- ✅ "The government introduced a policy. **The policy** was controversial." (rõ hơn "It was controversial")

### 3. Dùng relative clause để gắn pronoun vào danh từ

- Thay: "She gave the report to her manager. **It** was late."
- Bằng: "She gave the report — **which was already late** — to her manager."

---

## Ứng dụng trong IELTS

IELTS Examiner chấm **Coherence & Cohesion** — pronoun reference mơ hồ là lỗi trực tiếp ảnh hưởng đến tiêu chí này.

**Band 6:** "uses cohesive devices effectively but there may be some inaccuracies or over/underuse"
**Band 7+:** "uses a range of cohesive devices appropriately"

Một cách nhanh để nâng band là thay "this/that" bằng "this + specific noun":

- "This **trend** / this **issue** / this **approach** / this **phenomenon**"

## Bài tập luyện

### Xác định lỗi và viết lại cho rõ:

1. "The teacher spoke to the student about his performance. He seemed nervous."
2. "Air pollution and water contamination are both serious. This needs to be addressed immediately."
3. "Scientists and politicians discussed climate change. They couldn't agree on a solution."
4. "She told her sister that she should apply for the job."
5. "Many countries have introduced new laws. It has been effective in some places."

### Đáp án gợi ý:

1. "The teacher spoke to the student about **the student's** performance. **The student** seemed nervous." / "...performance. **He** seemed nervous" *(ổn nếu rõ "he" = student từ ngữ cảnh)*
2. "Air pollution and water contamination are both serious. **Both problems** need to be addressed immediately." / "**These issues** need to be addressed..."
3. "Scientists and politicians discussed climate change. **The two sides** couldn't agree on a solution." / "**Neither group** could agree..."
4. "She told her sister that **her sister** should apply for the job." / "She encouraged **her sister** to apply for the job."
5. "Many countries have introduced new laws. **These laws** have been effective in some places."
