## Tóm tắt

Mỗi từ trong tiếng Anh thường có nhiều dạng tương ứng với các từ loại khác nhau:

| Noun | Verb | Adjective | Adverb |
|------|------|-----------|--------|
| success | succeed | successful | successfully |
| beauty | beautify | beautiful | beautifully |
| education | educate | educational | educationally |
| economy | economise | economic / economical | economically |

Lỗi word form = dùng đúng từ nhưng sai dạng (sai từ loại cho vị trí đó trong câu).

## Tại sao quan trọng

Trong IELTS:
- **Lexical Resource (LR):** Dùng đúng word form = biết dùng từ chính xác và linh hoạt
- **Grammatical Accuracy (GRA):** Sai word form thường kéo theo sai cấu trúc câu
- **Band 7+:** Yêu cầu ít lỗi word form, và biết dùng đa dạng word families

## Nguyên tắc xác định từ loại cần dùng

Trước khi chọn dạng từ, hỏi: **"Vị trí này trong câu cần từ loại gì?"**

| Vị trí | Từ loại cần | Ví dụ |
|--------|------------|-------|
| Trước danh từ | Adjective | a **beautiful** city |
| Sau be/seem/look/feel/become | Adjective | She seems **happy** |
| Chủ ngữ, tân ngữ | Noun | **Education** is important |
| Sau động từ | Adverb (nếu bổ nghĩa V) | She sings **beautifully** |
| Vị ngữ (hành động) | Verb | They **succeed** every time |

## Phần 1: Suffixes thường gặp — nhận diện từ loại

### Noun suffixes (đuôi tạo danh từ):

| Suffix | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|
| -tion / -sion | education, pollution, decision, conclusion |
| -ment | development, achievement, government, improvement |
| -ness | happiness, awareness, effectiveness, kindness |
| -ity / -ty | ability, creativity, equality, poverty, quality |
| -ence / -ance | importance, evidence, independence, significance |
| -er / -or | teacher, worker, investor, competitor |
| -ist | economist, environmentalist, scientist |
| -ism | capitalism, environmentalism, socialism |
| -al (noun) | proposal, approval, refusal, arrival |
| -age | advantage, shortage, coverage |
| -ship | leadership, relationship, friendship |

### Adjective suffixes (đuôi tạo tính từ):

| Suffix | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|
| -ful | successful, beautiful, powerful, harmful, useful |
| -less | harmless, jobless, homeless, careless, hopeless |
| -ous / -ious | dangerous, famous, serious, obvious, ambitious |
| -al | educational, environmental, economic, national |
| -ive | effective, creative, productive, competitive |
| -ic | economic, scientific, dramatic, academic |
| -able / -ible | sustainable, comparable, responsible, visible |
| -ent / -ant | significant, independent, relevant, important |
| -ing / -ed | interesting/interested, boring/bored, challenging |

### Adverb suffixes:

Hầu hết trạng từ = tính từ + **-ly**:
- quick → **quickly** | economic → **economically** | significant → **significantly**
- Ngoại lệ: fast, hard, well, late, early (không thêm -ly hoặc có dạng riêng)

### Verb suffixes:

| Suffix | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|
| -ise / -ize | modernise, globalise, emphasise, realise |
| -ify | simplify, justify, clarify, qualify |
| -en | strengthen, widen, broaden, deepen, shorten |
| -ate | educate, create, evaluate, communicate |

## Phần 3: Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Dùng danh từ thay tính từ

- ❌ This is a **success** programme. (success = noun)
- ✅ This is a **successful** programme. (successful = adjective)
- ❌ She is very **beauty**.
- ✅ She is very **beautiful**.
- ❌ The policy has **significance** impact.
- ✅ The policy has **significant** impact.
- ❌ This is an **economy** decision.
- ✅ This is an **economic** decision. (liên quan đến kinh tế)
- ✅ This is an **economical** decision. (tiết kiệm tiền)

### Lỗi 2: Dùng tính từ thay danh từ

- ❌ The **educational** of children is important.
- ✅ The **education** of children is important.
- ❌ **Successful** requires hard work.
- ✅ **Success** requires hard work.
- ❌ The **environmental** is getting worse.
- ✅ The **environment** is getting worse.

### Lỗi 3: Dùng tính từ thay trạng từ

- ❌ She speaks English **fluent**.
- ✅ She speaks English **fluently**.
- ❌ The economy has grown **rapid**.
- ✅ The economy has grown **rapidly**.
- ❌ He performed **extreme** well.
- ✅ He performed **extremely** well.
- ❌ This **significant** affects students.
- ✅ This **significantly** affects students.

### Lỗi 4: Nhầm "economic" và "economical"

- **economic** = thuộc về kinh tế (relating to the economy)
  - **economic** growth, **economic** development, **economic** policy
- **economical** = tiết kiệm, không lãng phí (cost-effective)
  - an **economical** car, an **economical** approach

- ❌ She drives an **economic** car.
- ✅ She drives an **economical** car.
- ❌ The country's **economical** performance improved.
- ✅ The country's **economic** performance improved.

### Lỗi 5: Nhầm "affect" (verb) và "effect" (noun)

- **affect** = động từ "ảnh hưởng đến"
- **effect** = danh từ "tác động, kết quả"

- ❌ Pollution **effects** our health.
- ✅ Pollution **affects** our health.
- ❌ The **affect** of pollution on health is serious.
- ✅ The **effect** of pollution on health is serious.

**Cách nhớ:** "affect" = Action (verb), "effect" = End result (noun)

**Ngoại lệ:** "effect" đôi khi dùng như verb nghĩa "thực hiện": to **effect** change = to bring about change (rất trang trọng, ít dùng).

### Lỗi 6: Nhầm "effective" và "efficient"

- **effective** = có tác dụng (produces the desired result)
- **efficient** = hiệu quả về thời gian/năng lượng/chi phí (minimises waste)

- A treatment can be **effective** (chữa được bệnh) but not **efficient** (tốn kém quá).
- Solar panels are becoming more **efficient** (tạo ra nhiều điện hơn với ít chi phí hơn).
- This new law is **effective** in reducing crime.

### Lỗi 7: Nhầm "historic" và "historical"

- **historic** = có tầm quan trọng lịch sử (significant in history)
- **historical** = thuộc về lịch sử, liên quan đến quá khứ (relating to history)

- This is a **historic** moment. (khoảnh khắc đáng nhớ trong lịch sử)
- She is reading a **historical** novel. (tiểu thuyết có bối cảnh lịch sử)

### Lỗi 8: Quên biến đổi sau "make/keep/find + object"

Sau "make/keep/find + object", cần **adjective** không phải adverb hoặc noun:
- ❌ Exercise makes you **healthily**.
- ✅ Exercise makes you **healthy**.
- ❌ She finds the work **challenge**.
- ✅ She finds the work **challenging**.
- ❌ The government should keep prices **stability**.
- ✅ The government should keep prices **stable**.

## Phần 4: Từ loại sau các cấu trúc quan trọng

| Cấu trúc | Từ loại tiếp theo | Ví dụ |
|----------|------------------|-------|
| be/seem/look/feel/become/appear | **Adjective** | She seems **confident** |
| very/quite/rather/extremely | **Adjective / Adverb** | very **important** / very **quickly** |
| make/keep/find + O | **Adjective** | find it **difficult** |
| a/an/the + __ + noun | **Adjective** | a **significant** change |
| verb + __ | **Adverb** (modifies verb) | works **efficiently** |
| Chủ ngữ / tân ngữ | **Noun** | **Education** matters |
| despite / because of + | **Noun/Gerund** | despite **failing** |
| is/are/was/were | **Noun hoặc Adjective** | She is **a teacher** / is **intelligent** |

## Ứng dụng trong IELTS

### Writing Task 2 — ví dụ sửa lỗi:

**Trước khi sửa (nhiều lỗi word form):**
> *"In today's society, **technology** has become **importance** for **economy** development. Many people believe that **technological** can help solve problems like **pollute** and **unemploy**."*

**Sau khi sửa:**
> *"In today's society, **technology** has become **important** for **economic** development. Many people believe that **technology** can help solve problems like **pollution** and **unemployment**."*

### Đoạn văn Band 7+ — word forms đúng:

> The **environmental** impact of rapid **industrialisation** has been **significant**. As economies **develop**, they tend to produce more carbon emissions, which **adversely** affects global temperatures. However, **innovative** solutions such as renewable energy are becoming increasingly **economical**, suggesting that **sustainable** development is not only **achievable** but also **economically** viable in the long term.

### Speaking Part 3 — sử dụng đúng word form:

- "I think **education** is really **important** for **economic** development..."
- "The **environmental** problems we face are **increasingly** **serious**..."
- "**Technology** has **significantly** changed the way people **communicate**..."

## Bài tập luyện

### Chọn dạng từ đúng

1. The government needs to take (effect/effective/effectively) action on climate change.
2. She is a very (success/successful/successfully) businesswoman.
3. (Economic/Economy/Economical) growth has slowed significantly.
4. Pollution (affect/effect/effective) millions of people worldwide.
5. He speaks very (fluent/fluency/fluently) in three languages.
6. The results were (significance/significant/significantly) better than expected.
7. We need to find an (economy/economic/economical) solution to reduce waste.
8. The company has made a huge (invest/investment/investor) in renewable energy.

### Sửa lỗi word form

1. She works very hard and achieves many success in her career.
2. The environment damage caused by factories is a serious concern.
3. He is very talent and creativity in his approach to problem-solving.
4. The govern should provide more support for unemployed people.
5. This policy has been extreme beneficial for low-income families.

### Đáp án

**Chọn dạng từ:**
1. effective | 2. successful | 3. Economic | 4. affects | 5. fluently | 6. significantly | 7. economical | 8. investment

**Sửa lỗi:**
1. She works very hard and achieves many **successes** in her career. (hoặc: achieves **great success**)
2. The **environmental** damage caused by factories is a serious concern.
3. He is very **talented** and **creative** in his approach to problem-solving.
4. The **government** should provide more support for **unemployed** people.
5. This policy has been **extremely** beneficial for low-income families.

## Tóm tắt nhanh

**Cách xác định word form cần dùng:**
1. Vị trí **trước noun** → cần **adjective**
2. Vị trí **chủ ngữ/tân ngữ** → cần **noun**
3. Vị trí **sau verb** (bổ nghĩa verb) → cần **adverb**
4. Vị trí **vị ngữ** (hành động) → cần **verb**
5. Sau **be/seem/look/feel/become** → cần **adjective**
6. Sau **make/keep/find + object** → cần **adjective**

**Cặp từ hay nhầm:**
- affect (V) vs effect (N)
- economic (thuộc kinh tế) vs economical (tiết kiệm)
- effective (có tác dụng) vs efficient (hiệu quả về chi phí/thời gian)
- historic (đáng nhớ) vs historical (thuộc lịch sử)
- success (N) vs successful (Adj) vs successfully (Adv)

**Quy tắc vàng:** Trước khi điền từ, hỏi: "Vị trí này trong câu cần DANH TỪ, ĐỘNG TỪ, TÍNH TỪ hay TRẠNG TỪ?" — rồi mới chọn dạng từ phù hợp.
