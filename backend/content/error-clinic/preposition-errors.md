## Tóm tắt

Giới từ trong tiếng Anh thường phải học theo **collocation** (cụm từ cố định) vì không có quy tắc logic hoàn toàn. Các lỗi phổ biến nhất là:
1. Nhầm giới từ thời gian: at/on/in
2. Nhầm giới từ vị trí: at/on/in
3. Sai giới từ theo sau động từ: depend **on** (không phải "to")
4. Sai giới từ theo sau tính từ: interested **in** (không phải "about")
5. Sai giới từ theo sau danh từ: reason **for** (không phải "of")

## Tại sao quan trọng

Giới từ sai tạo ra "unnatural English" — câu đúng nghĩa nhưng nghe lạ tai. IELTS examiners đánh giá collocational accuracy như một phần của Lexical Resource (LR) và Grammatical Accuracy (GRA).

## Phần 1: Giới từ thời gian — at / on / in

| Giới từ | Dùng cho | Ví dụ |
|---------|---------|-------|
| **at** | Giờ cụ thể, buổi, thời điểm | at 7pm, at noon, at midnight, at the weekend (BrE) |
| **on** | Ngày, thứ, ngày đặc biệt | on Monday, on 15 April, on New Year's Day, on my birthday |
| **in** | Tháng, năm, thế kỷ, thập kỷ, buổi (morning/afternoon/evening) | in May, in 2020, in the 21st century, in the morning |

**Ngoại lệ cần nhớ:**
- at night (không phải "in the night" khi nói chung chung)
- at the weekend (British) / on the weekend (American)
- in the morning / afternoon / evening → NHƯNG: **on** Monday morning
- at Christmas / at Easter (dịp lễ)

### Lỗi thường gặp — thời gian:

- ❌ She was born **in** 15 April.
- ✅ She was born **on** 15 April.
- ❌ I'll see you **on** the morning.
- ✅ I'll see you **in** the morning.
- ❌ He arrived **on** 2019.
- ✅ He arrived **in** 2019.
- ❌ The meeting is **in** Monday.
- ✅ The meeting is **on** Monday.

## Phần 2: Giới từ vị trí — at / on / in

| Giới từ | Dùng cho | Ví dụ |
|---------|---------|-------|
| **at** | Điểm cụ thể, địa chỉ, địa điểm (được nhìn là điểm) | at the bus stop, at the corner, at 15 Oxford Street, at school (chức năng) |
| **on** | Bề mặt, tầng, phương tiện (xe buýt, máy bay) | on the table, on the second floor, on the bus, on the wall |
| **in** | Không gian bên trong, thành phố/quốc gia | in the room, in a box, in London, in Vietnam, in the car |

**Ngoại lệ quan trọng:**
- in/on the bus: **on** the bus (đang ở trên xe) vs get **in** the car (bước vào xe)
- at/in school: **at school** (đang học, chức năng) vs **in** the school (bên trong tòa nhà)
- at/in the corner: **at the corner** (ngoài góc đường) vs **in the corner** (góc trong phòng)

### Lỗi thường gặp — vị trí:

- ❌ She is **on** the kitchen.
- ✅ She is **in** the kitchen.
- ❌ The book is **in** the shelf.
- ✅ The book is **on** the shelf.
- ❌ I live **in** 10 Downing Street.
- ✅ I live **at** 10 Downing Street.
- ❌ He's studying **on** Oxford University.
- ✅ He's studying **at** Oxford University.

## Phần 3: Verb + Preposition Collocations

### Các động từ hay bị dùng sai giới từ:

| Verb | Preposition đúng | Lỗi thường gặp |
|------|-----------------|----------------|
| depend **on** | on | ❌ depend to/from/of |
| rely **on** | on | ❌ rely to |
| consist **of** | of | ❌ consist in/from |
| result **in** (gây ra kết quả) | in | ❌ result to |
| result **from** (xuất phát từ) | from | ❌ result of |
| contribute **to** | to | ❌ contribute for |
| suffer **from** | from | ❌ suffer of/with |
| benefit **from** | from | ❌ benefit of/by |
| respond **to** | to | ❌ respond on |
| agree **with** (người) | with | ❌ agree to someone |
| agree **to** (kế hoạch) | to | ❌ agree with a plan |
| disagree **with** | with | ❌ disagree to |
| concentrate **on** | on | ❌ concentrate in |
| focus **on** | on | ❌ focus in |
| insist **on** | on | ❌ insist to |
| apologise **for** | for | ❌ apologise about |
| search **for** | for | ❌ search about |
| apply **for** (xin việc) | for | ❌ apply to a job |
| apply **to** (nộp vào trường) | to | apply to university |
| aim **at/for** | at/for | ❌ aim to (when using as noun) |

**Ví dụ đúng:**
- The success of the project **depends on** several factors.
- This **results in** greater inequality.
- Poverty **results from** a combination of factors.
- Many people **suffer from** stress in the workplace.
- Young people can **benefit from** mentoring programmes.
- The government **contributed to** the problem by cutting funding.
- She **concentrated on** her studies for the final exam.

## Phần 4: Adjective + Preposition Collocations

| Adjective | Preposition | Ví dụ |
|-----------|-------------|-------|
| interested **in** | in | She is interested **in** environmental issues. |
| good/bad **at** | at | He is good **at** mathematics. |
| responsible **for** | for | Who is responsible **for** this decision? |
| aware **of** | of | Many people are not aware **of** the risks. |
| capable **of** | of | She is capable **of** achieving great things. |
| proud **of** | of | They are proud **of** their achievements. |
| afraid **of** | of | He is afraid **of** failure. |
| similar **to** | to | This approach is similar **to** the one used in Finland. |
| different **from** | from | British English is different **from** American English. |
| dependent **on** | on | Children are dependent **on** their parents. |
| typical **of** | of | This is typical **of** the problems in the region. |
| relevant **to** | to | This is directly relevant **to** the argument. |
| concerned **about** | about | Many people are concerned **about** climate change. |
| enthusiastic **about** | about | She is enthusiastic **about** learning. |
| familiar **with** | with | Are you familiar **with** this approach? |
| satisfied **with** | with | Consumers are not satisfied **with** the service. |
| associated **with** | with | Smoking is associated **with** many diseases. |
| involved **in** | in | She is involved **in** several projects. |
| dedicated **to** | to | He is dedicated **to** improving education. |

### Lỗi thường gặp — adjective + preposition:

- ❌ She is interested **about** history.
- ✅ She is interested **in** history.
- ❌ He is good **in** sports.
- ✅ He is good **at** sports.
- ❌ The results are different **with** what we expected.
- ✅ The results are different **from** what we expected.
- ❌ She is proud **about** her achievements.
- ✅ She is proud **of** her achievements.

## Phần 5: Noun + Preposition Collocations

| Noun | Preposition | Ví dụ |
|------|-------------|-------|
| reason **for** | for | The main reason **for** this problem is... |
| solution **to** | to | There is no easy solution **to** this issue. |
| answer **to** | to | The answer **to** this question is... |
| increase **in** | in | There has been an increase **in** crime. |
| decrease **in** | in | A decrease **in** funding led to... |
| rise **in** | in | The rise **in** temperatures is alarming. |
| fall **in** | in | A significant fall **in** unemployment |
| demand **for** | for | There is growing demand **for** renewable energy. |
| need **for** | for | There is an urgent need **for** change. |
| impact **on** | on | Technology has a significant impact **on** our lives. |
| effect **on** | on | What effect does stress have **on** health? |
| influence **on** | on | Parents have a strong influence **on** children. |
| approach **to** | to | A new approach **to** education |
| attitude **towards** | towards | The attitude **towards** immigrants has changed. |
| relationship **between** | between | The relationship **between** diet and health |
| access **to** | to | Access **to** education should be universal. |
| advantage **of/over** | of/over | The advantage **of** online learning is flexibility. |

### Lỗi thường gặp — noun + preposition:

- ❌ The reason **of** this problem is inequality.
- ✅ The reason **for** this problem is inequality.
- ❌ There is no solution **for** this problem.
- ✅ There is no solution **to** this problem.
- ❌ Technology has a great impact **to** our society.
- ✅ Technology has a great impact **on** our society.
- ❌ The increase **of** pollution is worrying.
- ✅ The increase **in** pollution is worrying.
- ❌ We need better access **of** healthcare.
- ✅ We need better access **to** healthcare.

## Lỗi thường gặp (tổng hợp)

### Lỗi 1: Dịch sát từ tiếng Việt

Tiếng Việt: "phụ thuộc vào" — người học dùng "depend to"
- ❌ This **depends to** many factors.
- ✅ This **depends on** many factors.

Tiếng Việt: "quan tâm đến" — người học dùng "care to"
- ❌ She **cares to** the environment.
- ✅ She **cares about** the environment.

### Lỗi 2: Nhầm "result in" và "result from"

- ❌ Poverty **results in** poor policies. (nghĩa ngược)
- ✅ Poor policies **result in** poverty. (result in = gây ra)
- ✅ Poverty **results from** poor policies. (result from = xuất phát từ)

### Lỗi 3: "Different from" (không phải "than" hay "to")

- ❌ My opinion is different **than** yours.
- ❌ My opinion is different **to** yours. (British dùng được nhưng ít formal)
- ✅ My opinion is different **from** yours. (academic writing — standard)

### Lỗi 4: "Married to" (không phải "with")

- ❌ She is married **with** a doctor.
- ✅ She is married **to** a doctor.

### Lỗi 5: "Arrive at/in" (không phải "arrive to")

- ❌ We arrived **to** London.
- ✅ We arrived **in** London. (thành phố/quốc gia)
- ✅ We arrived **at** the airport. (địa điểm cụ thể)

## Ứng dụng trong IELTS

### Writing Task 2 — các collocations quan trọng:

**Môi trường:**
- The increase **in** carbon emissions has led **to** global warming.
- Governments must find solutions **to** environmental problems.
- This policy will have a significant impact **on** the environment.
- Many species are **at** risk **of** extinction.

**Giáo dục:**
- Access **to** quality education should be guaranteed **for** all children.
- There is an urgent need **for** educational reform.
- Investment **in** education is essential **for** economic development.
- Students **benefit from** a structured learning environment.

**Xã hội:**
- The reason **for** rising inequality is complex.
- Technology has changed our attitude **towards** work.
- Young people are particularly concerned **about** their career prospects.
- The relationship **between** education and employment is well-established.

### Speaking — collocations tự nhiên:

- "I'm quite interested **in** environmental issues..."
- "I think the main reason **for** this is..."
- "This has had a really positive impact **on** my life..."
- "I'm not very good **at** cooking, to be honest..."
- "It depends **on** the situation, I suppose..."

## Bài tập luyện

### Điền giới từ đúng

1. The success of any policy depends ___ strong implementation.
2. There has been a significant increase ___ the number of people studying abroad.
3. She is very interested ___ environmental science.
4. Technology has had a profound impact ___ modern communication.
5. The rise ___ unemployment resulted ___ the economic crisis.
6. He arrived ___ the station just in time.
7. She is dedicated ___ helping disadvantaged communities.
8. This is different ___ what we discussed previously.

### Sửa lỗi

1. Many people suffer of stress in their daily lives.
2. The reason of climate change is human activity.
3. We need better access of clean water in rural areas.
4. She is married with a teacher who works at the local school.
5. The effect to pollution on health is well documented.

### Đáp án

**Điền giới từ:**
1. on | 2. in | 3. in | 4. on | 5. in / from | 6. at | 7. to | 8. from

**Sửa lỗi:**
1. Many people suffer **from** stress in their daily lives.
2. The reason **for** climate change is human activity.
3. We need better access **to** clean water in rural areas.
4. She is married **to** a teacher who works at the local school.
5. The effect **of** pollution **on** health is well documented.

## Tóm tắt nhanh

**Giới từ thời gian:**
- **at** + giờ (7pm, noon, midnight)
- **on** + ngày, thứ, ngày đặc biệt
- **in** + tháng, năm, thập kỷ, buổi (morning/afternoon/evening)

**Giới từ vị trí:**
- **at** + điểm cụ thể, địa chỉ, địa điểm (là điểm)
- **on** + bề mặt, tầng
- **in** + không gian bên trong, thành phố/quốc gia

**Collocations quan trọng nhất:**
- depend/rely/focus/concentrate/insist **on**
- result/suffer/benefit/different **from**
- contribute/respond/similar/dedicated **to**
- consist/proud/aware/capable/typical **of**
- impact/effect/influence **on** | reason/demand/need **for**
- solution/access/answer/attitude **to/towards**
- increase/decrease/rise/fall **in**

**Quy tắc vàng:** Học giới từ theo **cụm từ cố định** (collocations), không học rời từng giới từ. Khi nghi ngờ, hỏi: "Động từ/tính từ/danh từ này thường đi với giới từ gì?"
