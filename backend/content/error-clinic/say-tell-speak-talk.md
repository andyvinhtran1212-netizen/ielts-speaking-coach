## Tóm tắt

Bốn động từ, bốn vị trí khác nhau trong câu:

| Động từ | Có tân ngữ người nghe? | Cấu trúc chính | Ngữ cảnh |
|---------|------------------------|----------------|----------|
| **say** | Không bắt buộc | say (that) / say something | Nội dung lời nói |
| **tell** | **Bắt buộc** | tell **someone** (that/to) | Nội dung + người nghe |
| **speak** | Không | speak to/with someone / speak a language | Trang trọng hơn, khả năng ngôn ngữ |
| **talk** | Không | talk to/with / talk about | Hội thoại, thân mật |

---

## Tại sao quan trọng

Đây là lỗi rất phổ biến trong Speaking IELTS: thí sinh nói "She told that it was difficult" (thiếu tân ngữ người nghe), hoặc "Can I speak you for a moment?" (thiếu giới từ). Cả bốn động từ đều có cấu trúc riêng — học sai cấu trúc là lỗi ngữ pháp, không chỉ là lỗi từ vựng.

---

## SAY — nói gì, không cần nói với ai

### Cấu trúc:

```
say + something
say + that + clause
say + "trích dẫn trực tiếp"
```

- She **said** it was a great idea. ✅
- He **said** that he would be late.
- "I'm tired," she **said**.

### Khi nào KHÔNG dùng say:

- ❌ She said me that she was happy.
- ✅ She **told me** that she was happy. (cần "me" — dùng tell)

> **Quy tắc vàng:** "Say" **không** đi kèm tân ngữ chỉ người nghe trực tiếp.
> Muốn thêm người nghe vào, phải dùng giới từ: **say to** someone.

- She **said to me** that she was happy. ✅ (nhưng nghe trang trọng — thường dùng "told me")

### Collocations với SAY:

| Cụm từ | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|
| say hello / goodbye | She **said** goodbye and left. |
| say yes / no | He finally **said** yes to the offer. |
| say sorry / thank you | Don't forget to **say** thank you. |
| say a word | He didn't **say** a word during the meeting. |
| say your name | Can you **say** your name slowly? |
| say a prayer | She **said** a prayer before the meal. |

---

## TELL — nói với ai, bắt buộc có người nghe

### Cấu trúc:

```
tell + someone + (that) + clause
tell + someone + to + V
tell + someone + something
```

- She **told me** that she was tired.
- He **told** us **to** wait outside.
- Can you **tell** me the way to the station?

> **Quy tắc vàng:** "Tell" **luôn luôn** đi kèm tân ngữ chỉ người nghe. Nếu không có người nghe, không dùng tell.

### Lỗi thường gặp:

- ❌ She **told** that the exam was hard.
- ✅ She **said** that the exam was hard.
- ✅ She **told me** that the exam was hard.

### Collocations với TELL:

| Cụm từ | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|
| tell the truth | Always **tell** the truth. |
| tell a lie / a story / a joke | He **told** a funny story about his childhood. |
| tell the time | Can you **tell** me the time? |
| tell the difference | It's hard to **tell** the difference between the two. |
| tell someone off | She **told** him **off** for being late. |
| tell someone apart | The twins are so similar I can't **tell** them **apart**. |

## SPEAK — trang trọng hơn, gắn với khả năng ngôn ngữ

### Cấu trúc:

```
speak to/with + someone
speak about/on + topic
speak + language
```

- I'd like to **speak with** the manager, please.
- She **spoke** about climate change at the conference.
- He **speaks** three languages fluently.
- Can I **speak to** Dr. Johnson?

### Ngữ cảnh của SPEAK:

- Formal / professional settings
- Presentations, speeches, meetings
- Khả năng dùng ngôn ngữ (speak English, speak Vietnamese)
- Khi đề cập đến khả năng giao tiếp

### Lỗi thường gặp:

- ❌ Can I **speak** you for a moment?
- ✅ Can I **speak to** you for a moment? (cần giới từ "to")

### Collocations với SPEAK:

| Cụm từ | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|
| speak a language | She **speaks** Japanese fluently. |
| speak to/with someone | I need to **speak** with you urgently. |
| speak at/in a meeting | He **spoke** at the annual conference. |
| speak highly of | Everyone **speaks** highly of her work. |
| speak your mind | Don't be afraid to **speak** your mind. |
| speak up | Please **speak up** — I can't hear you. |
| generally/broadly speaking | **Generally speaking**, people prefer consistency. |

## TALK — thân mật, hội thoại hai chiều

### Cấu trúc:

```
talk to/with + someone
talk about / on + topic
```

- We **talked** for hours last night.
- Can we **talk** about this later?
- She's **talking** to her friend on the phone.
- Let's **talk** about your progress so far.

### Ngữ cảnh của TALK:

- Informal / casual conversations
- Two-way interaction (không chỉ một chiều)
- Thảo luận, trao đổi ý kiến

### So sánh SPEAK vs TALK:

| | SPEAK | TALK |
|--|-------|------|
| **Mức độ** | Trang trọng hơn | Thân mật hơn |
| **Hướng** | Có thể một chiều | Thường hai chiều |
| **Ví dụ** | "I'd like to **speak** with the principal." | "We need to **talk**." |
| **Collocations** | speak at a conference, speak a language | talk it over, small talk |

---

## So sánh trực tiếp — cùng tình huống

| Tình huống | Cách nói |
|-----------|---------|
| Truyền đạt nội dung | She **said** (that) she was busy. |
| Truyền đạt cho ai | She **told me** (that) she was busy. |
| Gọi điện hỏi gặp ai | Can I **speak** to Mr. Smith? |
| Trao đổi bình thường | Can we **talk** about this? |
| Nói về một chủ đề | He **spoke/talked** about renewable energy. |
| Lệnh gián tiếp | She **told** us **to** sit down. |
| Dẫn lời nói | He **said**, "I'm not coming." |

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: "Tell that" — thiếu người nghe

- ❌ He **told** that the meeting was cancelled.
- ✅ He **said** that the meeting was cancelled.
- ✅ He **told us** that the meeting was cancelled.

### Lỗi 2: "Speak" không có giới từ

- ❌ I need to **speak** you about something.
- ✅ I need to **speak to/with** you about something.

### Lỗi 3: "Say me"

- ❌ She **said** me to stop talking.
- ✅ She **told me** to stop talking.

### Lỗi 4: Nhầm tell/say trong câu tường thuật

- ❌ He **said** me the answer.
- ✅ He **told me** the answer.
- ❌ She **told** that it was wrong.
- ✅ She **said** that it was wrong.

### Lỗi 5: "Talk" với tân ngữ trực tiếp

- ❌ She **talked** me about her trip.
- ✅ She **talked to me** about her trip.
- ✅ She **told me** about her trip.

## Ứng dụng trong IELTS

### Speaking Part 1 (thân mật, dùng talk/say):

- "My friends and I often **talk about** music and films."
- "My teacher **said** that I should practise more."
- "I usually **talk to** my family on the phone every evening."

### Speaking Part 2 (kể chuyện):

- "She **told** me that the concert was absolutely incredible."
- "He **spoke** about his experiences working abroad."
- "We **talked** about it for hours — I'll never forget that conversation."

### Speaking Part 3 (thảo luận, phân tích):

- "Politicians need to **speak** more honestly about these issues."
- "It's important to **talk** openly about mental health."
- "Many people **say** that technology is making us less social."

### Writing Task 2:

- Research **suggests** / Studies **indicate** (thay cho "say" trong văn viết trang trọng)
- Experts **argue / claim / assert** (thay cho "say" trong học thuật)
- The government **announced / stated** (thay cho "said" khi formal)

> **IELTS Writing tip:** Trong Writing, tránh dùng "say" quá nhiều — thay bằng **argue, claim, suggest, state, assert, maintain**.

## Bài tập luyện

### Điền say / tell / speak / talk

1. Can I ___ to the manager, please?
2. She ___ me that the flight was delayed.
3. He didn't ___ a word during the whole meeting.
4. They need to ___ about what happened.
5. She ___ three languages fluently.
6. I tried to ___ her to stay calm.
7. He ___ that he would help us.
8. Let's ___ about your future plans.
9. Could you ___ more slowly? I can't follow.
10. My mother always ___ me to be honest.

### Sửa lỗi

1. She told that the exam was difficult.
2. Can I speak you for a minute?
3. He said me to wait outside.
4. They talked me that the shop was closed.
5. She said me the directions.

### Đáp án

**Điền:**
1. speak | 2. told | 3. say | 4. talk | 5. speaks | 6. tell | 7. said | 8. talk | 9. speak | 10. tells

**Sửa lỗi:**
1. She **said** that the exam was difficult. / She **told us** that the exam was difficult.
2. Can I **speak to** you for a minute?
3. He **told me** to wait outside.
4. They **told me** that the shop was closed.
5. She **told me** the directions. / She **gave me** the directions.

## Tóm tắt nhanh

| Động từ | Cấu trúc chính | Đặc điểm |
|---------|----------------|-----------|
| **say** | say (that) + clause | Không cần người nghe; dẫn lời nói |
| **tell** | tell **someone** (that/to) | **Bắt buộc** có người nghe |
| **speak** | speak **to/with** someone | Trang trọng; dùng với ngôn ngữ |
| **talk** | talk **to/with** / talk about | Thân mật; hội thoại hai chiều |

**Quy tắc vàng:**
- **Say** = nội dung; **Tell** = nội dung + ai
- **Tell** luôn cần tân ngữ người nghe → "tell *me* / tell *him* / tell *someone*"
- **Speak** cần giới từ → speak **to** / speak **with**
- **Talk** thân mật hơn speak; cũng cần giới từ → talk **to** / talk **with** / talk **about**
