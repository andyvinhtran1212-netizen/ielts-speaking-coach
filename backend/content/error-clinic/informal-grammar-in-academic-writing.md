## Tóm tắt

| Loại lỗi informal | Ví dụ sai | Phiên bản academic |
|------------------|-----------|-------------------|
| **Contractions** | can't, don't, it's | cannot, do not, it is |
| **Colloquial quantifiers** | a lot of, loads of | a great deal of, a significant number of |
| **Vague language** | kind of, sort of, stuff | somewhat, to some extent, aspects |
| **Câu hỏi tu từ sai chỗ** | Isn't it true that...? | It can be argued that... |
| **Viết tắt** | e.g. không đúng vị trí, etc. | for example, and so on |
| **Slang / phong cách chat** | gonna, wanna, a big deal | are going to, want to, significant |

---

## Tại sao quan trọng

IELTS Writing Band descriptors đánh giá **Lexical Resource** dựa vào việc bạn có sử dụng ngôn ngữ phù hợp với ngữ cảnh không. Bài viết có contractions, colloquialisms, hoặc câu hỏi tu từ sẽ bị trừ điểm nặng dù nội dung tốt. Điểm tối đa cho Lexical Resource chỉ đạt được khi register (mức độ trang trọng) nhất quán và phù hợp.

## NHÓM LỖI 1: CONTRACTIONS (từ viết tắt bằng dấu nháy)

Contractions là hoàn toàn không phù hợp trong IELTS Writing. Chúng thuộc về văn nói, email thân thiện, hoặc tin nhắn — không phải essay học thuật.

| Contraction ❌ | Dạng đầy đủ ✅ |
|--------------|--------------|
| can't | cannot |
| don't | do not |
| doesn't | does not |
| won't | will not |
| it's | it is |
| they're | they are |
| we've | we have |
| I'd | I would / I had |
| shouldn't | should not |
| couldn't | could not |
| that's | that is |
| there's | there is |
| isn't | is not |
| aren't | are not |

**Ví dụ sửa lỗi:**

- ❌ "Many people **can't** afford to buy a house."
- ✅ "Many people **cannot** afford to buy a house."

- ❌ "**It's** widely believed that education is important."
- ✅ "**It is** widely believed that education is important."

- ❌ "Governments **shouldn't** ignore this issue."
- ✅ "Governments **should not** ignore this issue."

## NHÓM LỖI 2: COLLOQUIAL QUANTIFIERS

Người học thường dùng từ chỉ số lượng của văn nói thay vì dạng academic.

| Informal ❌ | Academic ✅ |
|-----------|-----------|
| a lot of | a great deal of (uncountable) / a large number of (countable) / numerous |
| lots of | considerable, substantial, a significant amount/number of |
| loads of | an enormous number/amount of |
| tons of | a vast number/amount of |
| not many | relatively few / a minority of |
| a few | several / a small number of |
| most | the majority of / a large proportion of |
| some | a number of / certain / some (acceptable in academic) |

**Ví dụ sửa lỗi:**

- ❌ "**A lot of** young people struggle to find employment."
- ✅ "**A large number of** young people struggle to find employment."
- ✅ "**Many** young people struggle to find employment." *(many là acceptable)*

- ❌ "There are **loads of** reasons why this policy might fail."
- ✅ "There are **numerous** reasons why this policy might fail."

- ❌ "**Most** of the evidence supports this view."
- ✅ "**The majority of** the evidence supports this view."
- ✅ "**Most** evidence supports this view." *(most không có "of" là acceptable)*

## NHÓM LỖI 3: VAGUE LANGUAGE (ngôn ngữ mơ hồ)

| Informal / vague ❌ | Academic ✅ |
|-------------------|-----------|
| kind of / sort of | somewhat / to some extent / to a degree |
| pretty [adj] | relatively / fairly / rather / quite |
| really [adj] | extremely / highly / significantly |
| a bit | slightly / marginally / to a minor extent |
| stuff | aspects / factors / elements / issues |
| things | factors / aspects / considerations / matters |
| big / huge | significant / considerable / substantial / major |
| bad | detrimental / harmful / adverse / negative |
| good | beneficial / advantageous / positive / effective |
| get worse | deteriorate / worsen / decline |
| get better | improve / progress / advance |
| show | demonstrate / indicate / reveal / suggest |

**Ví dụ sửa lỗi:**

- ❌ "This is **kind of** a problem for society."
- ✅ "This represents **a significant concern** for society."

- ❌ "The situation has become **really bad**."
- ✅ "The situation has **deteriorated significantly**."

- ❌ "There are many **things** to consider."
- ✅ "There are many **factors** to consider."

- ❌ "This is a **pretty big** issue."
- ✅ "This is a **considerable** issue." / "This is **a matter of significant concern**."

## NHÓM LỖI 4: CÂU HỎI TU TỪ (Rhetorical questions)

Câu hỏi tu từ được chấp nhận trong văn nói hoặc văn viết sáng tạo, nhưng trong IELTS Writing Task 2, chúng **làm giảm tính học thuật** và nghe informal.

**Không nên dùng:**

- ❌ "**Is it not obvious that** governments should take more responsibility?"
- ❌ "**Don't we all** want a cleaner environment?"
- ❌ "**Why should** young people bear the burden of an ageing population?"
- ❌ "**Who would** disagree that education is important?"

**Thay bằng:**

- ✅ "**It is widely recognised that** governments have a responsibility to address this issue."
- ✅ "**There is broad consensus** that environmental protection is a shared responsibility."
- ✅ "**It can be argued that** young people should not disproportionately bear the burden of demographic change."
- ✅ "**Few would dispute** the importance of education in shaping individual outcomes."

## NHÓM LỖI 5: FIRST-PERSON OVERUSE (dùng "I" quá nhiều)

Trong academic writing, không tránh hoàn toàn "I" nhưng cần tránh dùng informal first-person.

| Informal ❌ | Academic ✅ |
|-----------|-----------|
| "I think that..." (lặp quá nhiều) | "It can be argued that... / It is widely believed that..." |
| "In my opinion, I believe..." | "In my view, / I would argue that..." (chọn một) |
| "Personally, I feel like..." | "It is my contention that... / I maintain that..." |
| "Me and my friends..." | "Many young people... / Individuals in this age group..." |
| "As we all know..." | "It is widely acknowledged that..." |
| "I'm going to talk about..." | "This essay will examine / discuss / argue..." |

## NHÓM LỖI 6: PHRASAL VERBS INFORMAL

Phrasal verbs thường mang tính informal. Trong writing, thay bằng động từ Latin gốc.

| Phrasal verb ❌ | Academic verb ✅ |
|--------------|----------------|
| go up / come up | increase / rise / grow |
| go down / come down | decrease / decline / fall |
| bring up | raise / introduce / mention |
| find out | discover / determine / establish |
| look into | investigate / examine / explore |
| put off | postpone / delay |
| deal with | address / tackle / manage / handle |
| make up | constitute / comprise / account for |
| come up with | develop / devise / propose / generate |
| get rid of | eliminate / remove / abolish |
| carry out | conduct / implement / undertake |
| set up | establish / create / found / introduce |

**Ví dụ sửa lỗi:**

- ❌ "Prices have **gone up** significantly."
- ✅ "Prices have **risen** significantly."

- ❌ "Governments need to **deal with** this problem."
- ✅ "Governments need to **address** this problem."

- ❌ "Researchers **found out** that..."
- ✅ "Researchers **established** / **determined** that..."

## NHÓM LỖI 7: VIẾT TẮT VÀ CỤM KHÔNG TRANG TRỌNG

| Không dùng ❌ | Dùng thay ✅ |
|------------|-----------|
| e.g. (ở đầu câu) | For example, / For instance, |
| i.e. (ở đầu câu) | That is, / In other words, |
| etc. | and so on / and so forth / among others |
| vs. | versus / compared with |
| & | and |
| % (viết tắt khi không có số) | percentage / proportion |

> **Lưu ý:** e.g. và i.e. chấp nhận được **trong dấu ngoặc đơn** giữa câu, nhưng không bao giờ ở đầu câu.
> - ✅ "Several factors contribute to this problem (e.g., poverty, inequality, and lack of education)."
> - ❌ "**E.g.,** poverty is a major factor."

## NHÓM LỖI 8: CÁC CỤM KHÔNG PHÙ HỢP KHÁC

| Informal ❌ | Academic ✅ |
|-----------|-----------|
| nowadays | in contemporary society / in the modern era / today |
| in today's world | in the contemporary world / in the modern era |
| more and more | an increasing number of / increasingly |
| less and less | a decreasing number of / decreasingly |
| very unique | unique (unique đã là tuyệt đối) |
| for sure | certainly / undoubtedly |
| a big deal | a matter of significant importance |
| no way | it is impossible / it is highly unlikely |
| you know | (bỏ hoàn toàn) |
| basically | fundamentally / essentially / in essence |
| at the end of the day | ultimately / in the final analysis |

## Bài tập luyện

### Viết lại theo phong cách academic

1. "A lot of people can't afford to buy houses these days."
2. "Governments should deal with this problem because it's really bad."
3. "Isn't it obvious that we need to find out more about this issue?"
4. "Things have gotten worse lately, and it's kind of a big deal."
5. "More and more countries are set up systems to help homeless people."

### Gợi ý đáp án

1. "**A large number of** people **are unable** to afford to buy homes **in the modern era**."
2. "Governments **should address** this issue given that it has become **increasingly detrimental** to society."
3. "**It is widely accepted** that further **research** is needed to fully **understand** this issue."
4. "The situation has **deteriorated considerably** in recent years, **representing** a matter of **significant concern**."
5. "**An increasing number of** countries **have established** systems to **support** homeless populations."
