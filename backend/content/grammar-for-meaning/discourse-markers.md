## Tóm tắt

Discourse markers là những từ, cụm từ dùng để:
- **Kết nối ý** trong câu và giữa các câu/đoạn
- **Tổ chức thông tin** theo trình tự, tương phản, nguyên nhân-kết quả...
- **Báo hiệu** cho người đọc/nghe biết ý nào sắp được nói đến

Chúng khác với liên từ (conjunctions) ở chỗ:
- Conjunctions (and, but, because) **nằm bên trong câu**, nối hai mệnh đề
- Discourse markers thường **đứng đầu câu** hoặc đoạn, nối các ý lớn hơn

## Tại sao quan trọng

IELTS Writing được chấm **Coherence and Cohesion** — khả năng tổ chức ý mạch lạc. Dùng đúng và đa dạng discourse markers là yêu cầu cốt lõi để đạt Band 7+. Trong Speaking, chúng giúp bài nói tự nhiên, có cấu trúc và dễ theo dõi.

## Nhóm 1: Thêm ý (Addition)

Thêm thông tin, lý do, ví dụ bổ sung:

| Discourse Marker | Vị trí | Ví dụ |
|-----------------|--------|-------|
| **Furthermore** | Đầu câu | **Furthermore**, regular exercise improves mental health. |
| **Moreover** | Đầu câu | **Moreover**, the cost of living has risen significantly. |
| **In addition (to this)** | Đầu câu | **In addition**, there are environmental concerns to consider. |
| **Additionally** | Đầu câu | **Additionally**, students need access to reliable internet. |
| **Besides (this)** | Đầu câu | **Besides**, it would be unfair to other candidates. |
| **What is more** | Đầu câu | **What is more**, the evidence suggests a long-term trend. |
| **Also** | Đầu câu / giữa câu | Online learning is flexible. It is **also** cost-effective. |
| **Not only...but also** | Trong câu | It is **not only** affordable **but also** highly effective. |

> **Thang bậc nhấn mạnh:** also < furthermore < moreover < what is more
> 
> Tránh lạm dụng "also" — thay bằng "furthermore" hoặc "moreover" trong IELTS Writing.

## Nhóm 2: Tương phản (Contrast)

Giới thiệu quan điểm đối lập hoặc ngoại lệ:

| Discourse Marker | Ví dụ |
|-----------------|-------|
| **However** | Technology has many benefits. **However**, it also poses risks. |
| **Nevertheless / Nonetheless** | The task was difficult. **Nevertheless**, they completed it on time. |
| **On the other hand** | City life is exciting. **On the other hand**, it can be stressful. |
| **In contrast** | Developed nations consume more energy. **In contrast**, developing nations use far less. |
| **Whereas** | Some prefer working from home, **whereas** others prefer office environments. |
| **While / Although** | **While** technology saves time, it also reduces human interaction. |
| **Despite this / In spite of this** | **Despite this**, many people continue to smoke. |
| **Even so** | The evidence is limited. **Even so**, the findings are promising. |
| **That said** | Online learning is convenient. **That said**, face-to-face interaction is irreplaceable. |

> **Phân biệt:**
> - **However** — phổ biến nhất, dùng được mọi lúc
> - **Nevertheless / Nonetheless** — mạnh hơn, ngụ ý "dù vậy vẫn cứ..."
> - **On the other hand** — dùng để trình bày hai mặt của vấn đề (không dùng nếu chỉ có một mặt)
> - **In contrast** — nhấn mạnh sự khác biệt rõ rệt, thường dùng trong Task 1

## Nhóm 3: Nguyên nhân và Kết quả (Cause and Effect)

| Discourse Marker | Ví dụ |
|-----------------|-------|
| **As a result** | Pollution levels rose. **As a result**, many residents experienced health problems. |
| **Therefore** | The population is ageing. **Therefore**, healthcare costs will increase. |
| **Consequently** | Investment was cut. **Consequently**, research was delayed. |
| **Hence** | Demand increased. **Hence**, prices rose. |
| **Thus** | The data is inconclusive. **Thus**, further research is needed. |
| **This means that** | Fewer people are having children. **This means that** the workforce will shrink. |
| **This leads to** | Urbanisation **leads to** increased pressure on infrastructure. |
| **Because of this / Due to this** | **Because of this**, many families struggle to afford housing. |
| **As a consequence** | **As a consequence**, the company had to lay off staff. |

> **Thang độ trang trọng:** as a result ≈ consequently > therefore > hence > thus
> 
> "Hence" và "thus" rất trang trọng — phù hợp IELTS Writing hơn Speaking.

## Nhóm 4: Trình tự (Sequencing)

### Liệt kê lý do, ý tưởng:

| Thứ tự | Discourse Marker |
|--------|-----------------|
| Mở đầu | **First of all / Firstly / To begin with / Initially** |
| Tiếp theo | **Secondly / Furthermore / Next / Then** |
| Cuối cùng | **Finally / Lastly / Last but not least** |

**Ví dụ đoạn văn:**

*There are several reasons why people migrate to cities. **Firstly**, urban areas offer more employment opportunities. **Furthermore**, cities provide better access to healthcare and education. **Finally**, many people are drawn by the cultural and social opportunities that cities offer.*

### Trình tự thời gian:

| | Discourse Marker |
|-|-----------------|
| Trước | **Previously / Before this / Prior to this / Initially** |
| Đồng thời | **Meanwhile / At the same time / Simultaneously** |
| Sau đó | **Subsequently / Afterwards / Later / Following this** |
| Cuối cùng | **Eventually / In the end / Ultimately** |

## Nhóm 5: Ví dụ và Minh chứng (Exemplification)

| Discourse Marker | Ví dụ |
|-----------------|-------|
| **For example** | Many countries face water shortages. **For example**, large parts of Africa and the Middle East receive very little rainfall. |
| **For instance** | Technology has transformed education. **For instance**, students can now access lectures from universities worldwide. |
| **Such as** | Renewable energy sources, **such as** solar and wind power, are becoming more affordable. |
| **In particular / Particularly** | Young people, **in particular**, are affected by social media pressure. |
| **To illustrate** | **To illustrate**, a study found that reading for 20 minutes a day can add years to your life. |
| **Specifically** | The problem affects developing countries **specifically**. |

## Nhóm 6: Tổng kết và Kết luận (Summary/Conclusion)

| Discourse Marker | Ví dụ |
|-----------------|-------|
| **In conclusion** | **In conclusion**, both individuals and governments must act to address climate change. |
| **To conclude / To sum up** | **To sum up**, the advantages of remote work outweigh the disadvantages. |
| **Overall** | **Overall**, the data suggests a positive trend. |
| **In summary** | **In summary**, there are three main causes of urban poverty. |
| **To summarise** | **To summarise**, the evidence strongly supports the need for policy reform. |
| **All in all** | **All in all**, it was a highly productive year for the company. |
| **Ultimately** | **Ultimately**, the decision rests with the individual. |

## Nhóm 7: Nhấn mạnh và Làm rõ (Emphasis/Clarification)

| Discourse Marker | Ví dụ |
|-----------------|-------|
| **In fact / In reality** | Technology is often seen as a distraction. **In fact**, it can enhance focus when used correctly. |
| **Indeed** | The problem is serious. **Indeed**, many experts describe it as a crisis. |
| **In other words** | The policy failed. **In other words**, it did not achieve its intended goals. |
| **That is to say / That is** | The tax will affect the wealthy. **That is to say**, those earning over £100,000. |
| **Clearly / Obviously** | **Clearly**, more needs to be done to address inequality. |
| **It is worth noting that** | **It is worth noting that** the data covers only a five-year period. |

## Nhóm 8: Nhượng bộ và Giới hạn (Concession/Limitation)

| Discourse Marker | Ví dụ |
|-----------------|-------|
| **Admittedly** | **Admittedly**, there are some downsides to this approach. |
| **It is true that...however** | **It is true that** social media connects people; **however**, it also spreads misinformation. |
| **While this may be true** | **While this may be true**, the benefits outweigh the costs. |
| **Of course...but** | **Of course**, everyone is entitled to their opinion, **but** this argument lacks evidence. |
| **To some extent** | **To some extent**, poverty is a result of poor decision-making, but structural factors also play a role. |

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Lạm dụng một cụm từ

- ❌ *However, ... However, ... However, ...*
- ✅ Xen kẽ: *However... / Nevertheless... / On the other hand... / That said...*

### Lỗi 2: Dùng "In addition" để bắt đầu câu TIẾP THEO nhưng không liên quan

"In addition" phải thêm ý cùng hướng với ý vừa nêu, không phải thêm ý khác chủ đề.

### Lỗi 3: Dùng "Moreover" trong Speaking thông thường

"Moreover" và "Furthermore" nghe rất trang trọng — phù hợp hơn cho Writing. Trong Speaking, dùng "Also", "On top of that", "Plus" nghe tự nhiên hơn.

### Lỗi 4: Dấu phẩy sau discourse marker

Hầu hết discourse markers đứng đầu câu **cần dấu phẩy:**
- **However,** prices have risen. ✅
- **In addition,** the policy has side effects. ✅
- **As a result,** unemployment fell. ✅

Nhưng "also" và "so" thường **không** cần dấu phẩy:
- She **also** mentioned the environmental impact. ✅

### Lỗi 5: Nhầm "However" với "But"

Cả hai đều diễn tả tương phản, nhưng:
- **But** nối hai mệnh đề trong cùng một câu: *I like it **but** it's expensive.*
- **However** nối hai câu riêng biệt, đứng đầu câu sau: *It is beneficial. **However**, there are risks.*

## Discourse Markers trong IELTS Speaking

Trong Speaking, sử dụng discourse markers nghe tự nhiên hơn — tránh những từ quá trang trọng:

| Writing | Speaking (tương đương) |
|---------|------------------------|
| Furthermore | Also, On top of that, Plus |
| However | But, Though, Then again |
| In addition | Also, And, What's more |
| Consequently | So, As a result |
| For example | Like, For example, For instance |
| In conclusion | So overall, All in all |

**IELTS Speaking Part 2 — sử dụng để kể chuyện có cấu trúc:**

*"I'd like to talk about a trip I took to Da Nang. **First of all**, we arrived early in the morning, so we had the whole day ahead. **Then**, we went straight to the beach, which was absolutely beautiful. **Later that afternoon**, we visited the Marble Mountains. **Overall**, it was one of the best trips I've ever taken."*

## Bài tập luyện

### Điền discourse marker phù hợp

1. The new policy has many benefits. ___, it is not without its drawbacks.
2. There are several reasons for the rise in obesity. ___, people are eating more processed food. ___, they are exercising less.
3. The company invested heavily in technology. ___, productivity increased by 30%.
4. Research shows that reading improves vocabulary. ___, it enhances critical thinking skills.
5. ___, the arguments in favour of renewable energy are compelling.

### Sắp xếp đoạn văn hợp lý

Sắp xếp các câu dưới đây thành đoạn văn mạch lạc, thêm discourse markers thích hợp:

1. People are spending more time on social media.
2. They have less time for face-to-face interactions.
3. Loneliness and depression rates have increased.
4. Social connection is fundamental to mental health.
5. Apps and platforms should design features that encourage real-world meetings.

### Đáp án

**Điền discourse marker:**
1. However / Nevertheless / That said
2. Firstly / In addition / Furthermore / Secondly
3. As a result / Consequently / Therefore
4. Furthermore / Moreover / In addition / What is more
5. In conclusion / Overall / To sum up

**Đoạn văn mẫu:**
*People are spending more time on social media. **As a result**, they have less time for face-to-face interactions. **Consequently**, loneliness and depression rates have increased. **Indeed**, social connection is fundamental to mental health. **Therefore**, apps and platforms should design features that encourage real-world meetings.*

## Tóm tắt nhanh

| Mục đích | Discourse Markers |
|----------|------------------|
| **Thêm ý** | furthermore, moreover, in addition, additionally, also |
| **Tương phản** | however, nevertheless, on the other hand, in contrast, that said |
| **Kết quả** | as a result, therefore, consequently, hence, thus |
| **Trình tự** | firstly, secondly, finally, subsequently, meanwhile |
| **Ví dụ** | for example, for instance, such as, in particular |
| **Kết luận** | in conclusion, overall, to sum up, ultimately |
| **Nhấn mạnh** | in fact, indeed, clearly, it is worth noting |
| **Nhượng bộ** | admittedly, to some extent, while this may be true |

**Quy tắc vàng:**
- Đặt dấu phẩy sau discourse marker khi đứng đầu câu
- Tránh lặp đi lặp lại cùng một từ — dùng đa dạng
- Discourse markers phải phản ánh đúng mối quan hệ logic giữa các ý
- Trong Writing: dùng từ trang trọng; trong Speaking: dùng từ tự nhiên hơn
