## Tóm tắt

Tiếng Anh có ba mức so sánh:

| Mức | Tên gọi | Ý nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|-----|---------|---------|-------|
| Bình thường | Positive | như nhau | big, beautiful |
| So sánh hơn | Comparative | A hơn B | bigger, more beautiful |
| So sánh nhất | Superlative | nhất trong nhóm | the biggest, the most beautiful |

## Tại sao quan trọng

So sánh xuất hiện liên tục trong IELTS:
- Writing Task 1: *Sales were **higher than** the previous year / The UK had **the highest** rate of...*
- Writing Task 2: *Cities are **more polluted than** rural areas / Education is **one of the most important** investments...*
- Speaking: *This city is **much bigger** than my hometown / I prefer reading **more than** watching TV*

## Cấu trúc Comparative

### Tính từ/Trạng từ ngắn (1 âm tiết, hoặc 2 âm tiết kết thúc -y)

```
Adjective + -er + than
```

| Tính từ | Comparative | Quy tắc thêm đuôi |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|
| tall | tall**er** | thêm -er |
| nice | nic**er** | bỏ -e, thêm -er |
| big | bigg**er** | nhân đôi phụ âm cuối |
| happy | happi**er** | -y → -ier |
| easy | easi**er** | -y → -ier |

- This book is **cheaper** than that one.
- She runs **faster** than him.
- My new job is **easier** than the old one.

### Tính từ/Trạng từ dài (2+ âm tiết, không kết thúc -y)

```
more + Adjective + than
```

- This solution is **more efficient** than the previous one.
- She speaks **more fluently** than I do.
- Online shopping is **more convenient** than going to the store.

### Bất quy tắc

| Dạng gốc | Comparative | Superlative |
|----------|-------------|-------------|
| good / well | **better** | **the best** |
| bad / badly | **worse** | **the worst** |
| far | **further / farther** | **the furthest / farthest** |
| little | **less** | **the least** |
| much / many | **more** | **the most** |
| old | **older / elder** | **the oldest / eldest** |

- She's **better** at maths than her brother.
- Things got **worse** before they got better.
- I have **less** time today than yesterday.

## Cấu trúc Superlative

### Tính từ ngắn

```
the + Adjective + -est
```

| Tính từ | Superlative |
|---------|-------------|
| tall | the tall**est** |
| nice | the nic**est** |
| big | the bigg**est** |
| happy | the happi**est** |

- He is **the tallest** student in the class.
- This is **the cheapest** option available.

### Tính từ dài

```
the most + Adjective
```

- This is **the most interesting** book I've ever read.
- She's **the most experienced** candidate for the job.
- Tokyo is **one of the most populated** cities in the world.

> **Superlative + ever:** Dùng Present Perfect sau superlative:
> - It's **the best** film I'**ve ever seen**.
> - That was **the most difficult** exam she'**d ever taken**.

## Các cấu trúc so sánh quan trọng

### 1. As...as (so sánh bằng)

```
as + Adjective/Adverb + as
```

- She's **as talented as** her sister.
- The exam was **not as difficult as** I expected.
- He doesn't earn **as much as** she does.

**Phủ định:** not as...as = less...than
- This hotel is **not as expensive as** that one. = This hotel is **less expensive than** that one.

### 2. Much / Far / A lot / Even / Slightly (nhấn mạnh mức độ so sánh)

```
much / far / a lot / even / slightly + comparative
```

- The new model is **much faster** than the old one.
- She's **far more qualified** than the other applicants.
- Prices are **slightly higher** than last year.
- The second exam was **even harder** than the first.

**Không dùng "very" với comparative:**
- ❌ She's very taller than me.
- ✅ She's **much taller** / **far taller** than me.

### 3. The + comparative, the + comparative

Diễn tả mối quan hệ tỉ lệ thuận:

```
The + comparative..., the + comparative...
```

- **The harder** you work, **the better** your results.
- **The more** you practise, **the more confident** you become.
- **The older** he gets, **the wiser** he becomes.

### 4. Twice / Three times as...as (so sánh bội số)

```
twice / three times / half + as + Adjective + as
```

- The population of China is **four times as large as** that of the United States.
- This laptop costs **twice as much as** the one I bought last year.
- My commute is **half as long as** my colleague's.

### 5. Less / The least (so sánh kém)

```
less + Adjective + than
the least + Adjective
```

- This approach is **less effective** than the traditional method.
- Of all the options, this one is **the least expensive**.

## So sánh trong mô tả dữ liệu (IELTS Task 1)

| Cần diễn đạt | Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|-------------|----------|-------|
| A cao hơn B | A is higher/greater than B | Sales in 2020 were **higher than** in 2019. |
| A thấp hơn B | A is lower/smaller than B | UK rates were **lower than** those in the US. |
| A cao nhất | A has the highest... | China has **the highest** population. |
| A thấp nhất | A has the lowest... | Iceland has **the lowest** crime rate. |
| Gần bằng nhau | A is almost as high as B | France's figures were **almost as high as** Germany's. |
| Gấp đôi | A is twice as high as B | The rate in 2020 was **twice as high as** in 2010. |
| Chênh lệch nhỏ | slightly / marginally higher/lower | Figures were **slightly higher** in urban areas. |
| Chênh lệch lớn | significantly / considerably higher | Costs were **significantly higher** in London. |

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Dùng "more" với tính từ ngắn

- ❌ She is **more tall** than her brother.
- ✅ She is **taller** than her brother.
- ❌ This is **more cheap** than that.
- ✅ This is **cheaper** than that.

### Lỗi 2: Quên "than" sau comparative

- ❌ My city is bigger my hometown.
- ✅ My city is bigger **than** my hometown.

### Lỗi 3: Quên "the" trước superlative

- ❌ She is most intelligent student in class.
- ✅ She is **the** most intelligent student in class.

### Lỗi 4: Dùng "very" thay "much/far" trước comparative

- ❌ He is very smarter than me.
- ✅ He is **much smarter** / **far smarter** than me.

### Lỗi 5: Nhầm "good-better-best" và "well-better-best"

- ❌ She sings more good than him.
- ✅ She sings **better** than him.
- ❌ This is the most good restaurant.
- ✅ This is **the best** restaurant.

### Lỗi 6: Dùng comparative thay superlative khi so sánh 3+ thứ

- ❌ Of the three candidates, she is **more qualified**.
- ✅ Of the three candidates, she is **the most qualified**.

## Ứng dụng trong IELTS

### Writing Task 1 — so sánh dữ liệu:

- Sales in France were **considerably higher than** those in Germany throughout the period.
- The proportion of people aged 65+ was **the highest** in Japan, at **twice as much as** in the UK.
- Urban areas showed **slightly higher** unemployment rates compared to rural regions.
- **The greater** the investment in education, **the lower** the poverty rate tended to be.

### Writing Task 2 — phân tích và lập luận:

- City life is often **far more stressful than** rural living, due to noise, traffic, and high costs.
- Renewable energy is becoming **increasingly competitive** with fossil fuels.
- Education is arguably **one of the most powerful** tools for reducing inequality.
- People in developed countries consume **significantly more** resources than those in developing nations.

### Speaking — mô tả và so sánh:

- My hometown is **much smaller** than Hanoi, but I think it's **far more peaceful**.
- I find speaking **more challenging than** writing, because you can't take time to think.
- **The more** I practise English, **the more confident** I feel using it.

## Bài tập luyện

### Chia dạng đúng: comparative hoặc superlative

1. Paris is one of ___ (beautiful) cities in Europe.
2. He's ___ (tall) than his father.
3. The exam was ___ (difficult) than I expected.
4. Of all the subjects, Maths is ___ (challenging) for me.
5. She works ___ (hard) than anyone else in the office.

### Sửa lỗi

1. She is more old than her colleague.
2. This coffee is the more expensive in the menu.
3. He runs very faster than me.
4. Of the two options, the first is most practical.
5. London is bigger as Paris.

### Đáp án

**Chia dạng:**
1. the most beautiful | 2. taller | 3. more difficult | 4. the most challenging | 5. harder

**Sửa lỗi:**
1. She is **older** than her colleague. (old → older, không dùng "more")
2. This coffee is **the most expensive** on the menu. (the + most + long adj; "in the menu" → "on the menu")
3. He runs **much faster** than me. (dùng much/far, không dùng very)
4. Of the two options, the first is **more** practical. (hai đối tượng → comparative, không superlative)
5. London is bigger **than** Paris. (dùng "than", không "as")

## Tóm tắt nhanh

**Tính từ ngắn (1-2 âm tiết):** -er / the -est
- Thêm -er/-est: tall → taller → the tallest
- Nhân đôi phụ âm: big → bigger → the biggest
- -y → -ier/-iest: happy → happier → the happiest

**Tính từ dài (3+ âm tiết):** more / the most
- more beautiful / the most beautiful

**Bất quy tắc:** good→better→best | bad→worse→worst | much/many→more→most | little→less→least

**Cấu trúc quan trọng:**
- so sánh bằng: as...as
- nhấn mạnh: much / far / considerably / significantly + comparative
- tỉ lệ: the + comparative, the + comparative
- bội số: twice / three times as...as

**Không dùng "very" trước comparative — dùng "much/far/a lot/even" thay thế!**
