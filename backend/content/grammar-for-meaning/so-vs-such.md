## Tóm tắt

Hai cấu trúc nhấn mạnh, một quy tắc cốt lõi:

| | **SO** | **SUCH** |
|--|--------|----------|
| **Theo sau là** | Tính từ / Trạng từ | (a/an) + (adj) + Danh từ |
| **Loại từ** | Trạng từ bổ nghĩa | Tính từ chỉ định |
| **Ví dụ** | so **difficult** | such a **difficult exam** |
| **Result clause** | that... | that... |

---

## Tại sao quan trọng

Cả "so" và "such" đều dùng để nhấn mạnh, nhưng rất nhiều học sinh nhầm:

- ❌ "It was **such** difficult." (thiếu noun)
- ❌ "It was **so** a difficult exam." (so không đứng trước a/an + noun)

Lỗi này xuất hiện ở Band 5-6 và phản ánh người học chưa kiểm soát được cấu trúc cơ bản. Biết dùng đúng "so ... that" và "such ... that" cũng là đặc trưng của Band 7+ trong Writing Task 2.

---

## SO + Tính từ / Trạng từ

"So" bổ nghĩa cho **tính từ** hoặc **trạng từ** — không bổ nghĩa trực tiếp cho danh từ.

```
so + adjective
so + adverb
so + adjective + that + [result clause]
```

**Với tính từ:**
- The exam was **so difficult** (that many students failed).
- She is **so intelligent** (that everyone admires her).
- The weather was **so cold** we couldn't go out.
- His explanation was **so clear** I understood immediately.

**Với trạng từ:**
- He spoke **so quickly** (that I couldn't follow).
- She worked **so hard** she burned out.
- Time passed **so slowly** during the waiting.

**Với much / many / few / little (số lượng):**
- There was **so much** traffic (that we were late).
- She has **so many** friends.
- I have **so little** time these days.
- **So few** people attended the event.

> **Lưu ý:** "So much" và "so many" đi trước danh từ — nhưng đây vẫn là "so + much/many" (determiner), không phải "such".

---

## SUCH + Cụm danh từ

"Such" đứng trước **cụm danh từ** — gồm danh từ, có thể có tính từ và mạo từ đi kèm.

```
such + a/an + adjective + singular noun
such + adjective + plural noun
such + adjective + uncountable noun
such + a/an + noun (không cần adj)
```

**Danh từ đếm được số ít (cần a/an):**
- It was **such a difficult exam** (that many students failed).
- She is **such an intelligent person** (that everyone admires her).
- He is **such a good teacher**.
- It was **such a long day** — I'm exhausted.
- That was **such a great idea**!

**Danh từ đếm được số nhiều (không có a/an):**
- They are **such talented musicians**.
- These are **such important issues**.
- We had **such great times** together.

**Danh từ không đếm được (không có a/an):**
- She showed **such courage** under pressure.
- It was **such hard work**.
- He displayed **such patience** with the students.

**Chỉ có danh từ, không có tính từ:**
- He is **such a genius**!
- It was **such a coincidence** that we met here.
- She made **such a mess**.

## Bảng so sánh trực tiếp

| SO + adj/adv | SUCH + (a/an) + adj + noun |
|-------------|---------------------------|
| so **cold** | such **cold weather** |
| so **talented** | such a **talented musician** |
| so **quickly** | — (adverbs không dùng với such) |
| so **many people** | such **a large number of people** |
| so **much work** | such **hard work** |
| so **difficult** | such a **difficult task** |
| so **slowly** | — |

## SO ... THAT và SUCH ... THAT

Cả hai cấu trúc thường đi kèm với "that" để diễn đạt **kết quả**:

```
so + adj/adv + that + [result]
such + (a/an) + (adj) + noun + that + [result]
```

- The film was **so boring that** I fell asleep.
- It was **such a boring film that** I fell asleep.
- He spoke **so quickly that** no one could understand him.
- He spoke at **such a speed that** no one could understand.
- She worked **so hard that** she finished early.
- She did **such hard work that** she finished early.

> **Ghi nhớ:** "that" thường bị bỏ trong văn nói (optional), nhưng nên giữ trong Writing để rõ cấu trúc.

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: "Such" không có danh từ

- ❌ It was **such difficult**.
- ✅ It was **so difficult**.
- ✅ It was **such a difficult task**.

### Lỗi 2: "So" trước cụm danh từ

- ❌ She is **so a good student**.
- ✅ She is **such a good student**.
- ✅ She is **so good** (at studying).

### Lỗi 3: Quên "a/an" sau "such" với danh từ đếm được số ít

- ❌ It was **such good idea**.
- ✅ It was **such a good idea**.

### Lỗi 4: Dùng "such many / such much"

- ❌ There were **such many** people.
- ✅ There were **so many** people.
- ❌ There was **such much** noise.
- ✅ There was **so much** noise.

### Lỗi 5: "So" trước danh từ đơn thuần

- ❌ He is **so genius**.
- ✅ He is **such a genius**.
- ✅ He is **so intelligent**.

## Ứng dụng trong IELTS

### Speaking — nhấn mạnh cảm xúc tự nhiên:

- "It was **such an incredible experience** — I'll never forget it."
- "The traffic was **so bad** that I almost missed the exam."
- "She's **such a dedicated teacher** that her students always do well."
- "I was **so nervous** during my first presentation."

### Writing Task 2 — nhấn mạnh luận điểm:

- "Air pollution has reached **such alarming levels** that immediate action is required."
- "The issue is **so complex** that no single policy can address it fully."
- "Modern technology has created **such rapid change** that societies struggle to adapt."
- "The disparity between rich and poor is **so pronounced** that social unrest is inevitable."

### Writing Task 1 — mô tả biến động:

- "The increase was **so dramatic** that it doubled in just five years."
- "There was **such a sharp rise** in 2020 that it distorted the overall trend."

## Bài tập luyện

### Chọn SO hoặc SUCH

1. The music was ___ loud I couldn't sleep.
2. He gave ___ a boring speech that half the audience left.
3. It was ___ cold weather that the roads froze.
4. She speaks English ___ fluently.
5. There was ___ much food at the party.
6. They are ___ dedicated teachers.
7. The problem is ___ serious that it requires immediate attention.
8. She showed ___ patience throughout the process.

### Viết lại câu (dùng cấu trúc còn lại)

1. It was so a difficult exam. (sửa lỗi)
2. She's so talented singer. (sửa lỗi)
3. The news was **so shocking** that everyone went silent.
   → It was **such** ___
4. It was **such a long journey** that we were exhausted.
   → The journey was **so** ___

### Đáp án

**Chọn:**
1. so | 2. such | 3. such | 4. so | 5. so | 6. such | 7. so | 8. such

**Viết lại:**
1. It was **such a difficult exam**. (hoặc: The exam was **so difficult**.)
2. She's **such a talented singer**.
3. It was **such shocking news** that everyone went silent.
4. The journey was **so long** that we were exhausted.

## Tóm tắt nhanh

```
SO + adj/adv:
  → so difficult, so quickly, so much, so many

SUCH + (a/an) + (adj) + noun:
  → such a difficult exam
  → such talented people
  → such hard work

KIỂM TRA NHANH:
  Sau so/such là gì?
  - Tính từ/Trạng từ → SO
  - Danh từ (có hoặc không có adj) → SUCH
  - much/many/few/little → SO (luôn luôn)

KẾT QUẢ dùng THAT:
  so + adj/adv + THAT + result
  such + noun phrase + THAT + result
```

**Quy tắc vàng:** Nếu bạn nói được câu bằng cách thêm "a/an" và danh từ, dùng **such**. Nếu chỉ có tính từ hoặc trạng từ, dùng **so**.
