## Tóm tắt

Hai cấu trúc, một quy tắc đơn giản:

| | **BECAUSE** | **BECAUSE OF** |
|--|-------------|----------------|
| **Theo sau là** | Mệnh đề (subject + verb) | Cụm danh từ (noun/noun phrase) |
| **Loại từ** | Liên từ (conjunction) | Giới từ kép (preposition) |
| **Ví dụ** | because it **rained** | because of the **rain** |

---

## Tại sao quan trọng

Đây là lỗi cực phổ biến trong IELTS Writing Band 5-6: học sinh viết "because of it rained" (dùng preposition + clause — sai) hoặc "because the heavy rain" (dùng conjunction + noun phrase — sai). Cả hai đều là lỗi ngữ pháp cơ bản bị trừ điểm nặng.

---

## BECAUSE + Mệnh đề (Subject + Verb)

"Because" là **liên từ** — nó nối hai mệnh đề.

```
[Main clause] + because + [subject] + [verb] + ...
```

- She stayed home **because** she **was** sick.
- He failed the exam **because** he **didn't** study.
- The event was cancelled **because** it **rained** heavily.
- Many people struggle with English **because** the grammar **is** complex.

"Because" cũng có thể đứng đầu câu (nhưng không nên dùng để bắt đầu câu văn trong IELTS Writing — đây là convention văn phong):

- **Because** she **was** tired, she went to bed early.
- **Because** the costs **were** too high, the project was abandoned.

> **Lưu ý:** Trong văn nói và văn viết thông thường, "because" đứng đầu câu được chấp nhận. Nhưng trong IELTS Academic Writing, ưu tiên đặt "because" ở mệnh đề phụ phía sau.

---

## BECAUSE OF + Cụm danh từ

"Because of" là **giới từ kép** — theo sau là danh từ hoặc cụm danh từ (noun phrase).

```
[Main clause] + because of + [noun phrase]
```

- She stayed home **because of** her **illness**.
- He failed the exam **because of** his **lack of preparation**.
- The event was cancelled **because of** the **heavy rain**.
- Many people struggle with English **because of** its complex **grammar**.

Có thể đứng đầu câu:
- **Because of** the **traffic**, I arrived late.
- **Because of** rising **costs**, many businesses had to close.

## Bảng so sánh trực tiếp

| BECAUSE (+ clause) | BECAUSE OF (+ noun phrase) |
|--------------------|---------------------------|
| because it **rained** | because of the **rain** |
| because she **was** ill | because of her **illness** |
| because prices **increased** | because of the **price increase** |
| because they **lacked** resources | because of the **lack of resources** |
| because he **worked** hard | because of his **hard work** |
| because the weather **was** bad | because of the **bad weather** |
| because demand **fell** | because of **falling demand** |

## Cách chuyển đổi giữa hai cấu trúc

Khi có động từ → chuyển thành danh từ tương ứng:

| Câu với BECAUSE | Câu với BECAUSE OF |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| because he **arrived** late | because of his **late arrival** |
| because she **failed** | because of her **failure** |
| because it **improved** | because of the **improvement** |
| because they **refused** | because of their **refusal** |
| because demand **increased** | because of the **increase in demand** |

**Luyện tập chuyển đổi:**

*Because* traffic was congested, we missed the train.
→ *Because of* the **traffic congestion**, we missed the train.

*Because* the company invested wisely, it expanded rapidly.
→ *Because of* its **wise investment**, the company expanded rapidly.

## Các cấu trúc nguyên nhân khác cần biết

Khi viết IELTS, tránh lặp lại "because / because of" — thay bằng các cấu trúc sau:

### + Noun phrase (giống "because of"):

| Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|----------|-------|
| **due to** | The delay was **due to** bad weather. |
| **owing to** | **Owing to** staff shortages, the service suffered. |
| **as a result of** | **As a result of** the recession, unemployment rose. |
| **on account of** | He was absent **on account of** illness. |

### + Clause (giống "because"):

| Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|----------|-------|
| **since** | **Since** prices rose, many consumers cut back. |
| **as** | **As** the economy weakened, spending fell. |
| **given that** | **Given that** resources are limited, priorities must be set. |

> **Lưu ý mức độ trang trọng:**
> - **since / as** — trung tính, thường gặp trong writing học thuật
> - **due to** — rất trang trọng, phổ biến trong writing
> - **on account of** — rất trang trọng, ít dùng hơn
> - **because** — phổ biến nhất, phù hợp cả speaking và writing

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: "Because of" + mệnh đề

- ❌ She left early **because of** she was tired.
- ✅ She left early **because** she was tired.
- ✅ She left early **because of** her tiredness.

### Lỗi 2: "Because" + cụm danh từ

- ❌ The match was delayed **because** bad weather.
- ✅ The match was delayed **because of** bad weather.
- ✅ The match was delayed **because** the weather was bad.

### Lỗi 3: "Due to" + mệnh đề

- ❌ The flight was cancelled **due to** the storm was severe.
- ✅ The flight was cancelled **due to** the severe storm.
- ✅ The flight was cancelled **because** the storm was severe.

### Lỗi 4: "Because of" + verb-ing (gerund — được chấp nhận)

- ✅ He succeeded **because of** working hard. (gerund — acceptable nhưng nghe cứng)
- ✅ He succeeded **because of** his hard work. (noun phrase — tự nhiên hơn)
- ✅ He succeeded **because** he worked hard. (clause — thông dụng nhất)

## Ứng dụng trong IELTS

### Writing Task 1 — mô tả xu hướng:

- Sales increased **because** online shopping **became** more popular.
- The decline in public transport use was partly **due to** rising fares.
- Figures fell in 2008, **because of** the global financial crisis.
- **Because** the population **grew** rapidly, demand for housing increased.

### Writing Task 2 — phân tích nguyên nhân:

- Many young people are unemployed **because** they **lack** practical skills.
- **Because of** the high cost of living, young adults are delaying marriage.
- Childhood obesity is rising **due to** poor diet and reduced physical activity.
- **Since** access to technology **has become** widespread, information is more accessible than ever.

### Speaking Part 3 — giải thích lý do:

- "I think people prefer city life **because** there are more job opportunities."
- "**Because of** social media, young people feel more pressure about their appearance."
- "Crime rates are rising in some areas **because** unemployment is high."

## Bài tập luyện

### Chọn BECAUSE hoặc BECAUSE OF

1. She was promoted ___ her excellent performance.
2. He couldn't attend ___ he was travelling abroad.
3. The project failed ___ a lack of funding.
4. I was nervous ___ it was my first interview.
5. ___ the cold weather, many people stayed indoors.
6. They cancelled the trip ___ heavy snow was forecast.
7. She improved quickly ___ her dedication to practice.
8. ___ studying hard, she passed with distinction.

### Viết lại câu

Dùng "because of" thay cho "because":

1. She cried because she was disappointed.
2. He left because the noise was unbearable.
3. They succeeded because they cooperated effectively.

Dùng "because" thay cho "because of":

4. Because of the poor weather, the match was postponed.
5. Because of his experience, he got the job.
6. Because of rising inflation, people are spending less.

### Đáp án

**Chọn:**
1. because of | 2. because | 3. because of | 4. because | 5. Because of | 6. because | 7. because of | 8. Because of

**Viết lại:**
1. She cried **because of** her disappointment.
2. He left **because of** the unbearable noise.
3. They succeeded **because of** their effective cooperation.
4. **Because** the weather **was** poor, the match was postponed.
5. **Because** he **was** experienced, he got the job.
6. **Because** inflation **is** rising, people are spending less.

## Tóm tắt nhanh

| | BECAUSE | BECAUSE OF |
|--|---------|------------|
| **Theo sau** | Subject + Verb (clause) | Noun / Noun phrase |
| **Loại từ** | Liên từ (conjunction) | Giới từ kép (preposition) |
| **Ví dụ** | because it **rained** | because of the **rain** |
| **Tương đương** | since, as, given that | due to, owing to, as a result of |

**Kiểm tra nhanh:** Sau "because/because of" là gì?
- Có **động từ chia** (rained, was, increased) → dùng **BECAUSE**
- Chỉ có **danh từ/cụm danh từ** (the rain, bad weather, a lack of...) → dùng **BECAUSE OF**
