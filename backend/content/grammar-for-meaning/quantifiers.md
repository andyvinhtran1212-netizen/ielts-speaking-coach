## Tóm tắt

Quantifiers = từ đứng trước danh từ để chỉ **số lượng** nhiều hay ít.

| Quantifier | Dùng với | Ví dụ |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| **many** | countable plural | many students, many problems |
| **much** | uncountable | much time, much evidence |
| **few / a few** | countable plural | few options / a few ideas |
| **little / a little** | uncountable | little money / a little help |
| **some / any** | cả hai | some water / any students |
| **a lot of / lots of** | cả hai | a lot of time / lots of people |
| **several** | countable plural | several reasons |
| **plenty of** | cả hai | plenty of time / plenty of books |
| **a great deal of** | uncountable | a great deal of evidence |
| **a large number of** | countable plural | a large number of people |

## Tại sao quan trọng

Lỗi dùng sai quantifier (much students, few information...) bị đánh giá là lỗi cơ bản trong IELTS. Nắm vững quantifiers giúp:
- Diễn đạt số lượng chính xác trong Writing Task 1 (mô tả dữ liệu)
- Lập luận thuyết phục trong Task 2 (most people, a significant proportion...)
- Nói tự nhiên và đa dạng trong Speaking

## Many vs Much

### Many — đếm được, số nhiều

```
many + countable plural noun
```

- **Many** students struggle with academic writing.
- There are **many** factors contributing to unemployment.
- **How many** people attended the event?

### Much — không đếm được

```
much + uncountable noun
```

- **Much** research has been conducted in this area.
- There isn't **much** time left.
- **How much** money was spent?

> **Lưu ý:** "Much" thường dùng trong câu phủ định và câu hỏi. Trong câu khẳng định, ưu tiên "a lot of" hoặc "a great deal of":
> - ❌ Much research supports this view. (nghe cứng trong văn thông thường)
> - ✅ **A great deal of** research supports this view. (học thuật hơn)
> - ✅ **Much** of the research supports this view. (chấp nhận được trong academic writing)

## Few vs Little — Âm tính (ít đến mức thiếu)

### Few — countable, mang nghĩa âm tính

```
few + countable plural noun = "không đủ / quá ít"
```

- **Few** governments have successfully implemented this policy. (Rất ít — ngụ ý đáng tiếc)
- **Few** people are aware of this problem.
- There are **few** solutions to this crisis.

### Little — uncountable, mang nghĩa âm tính

```
little + uncountable noun = "không đủ / quá ít"
```

- There is **little** evidence to support this claim. (Gần như không có bằng chứng)
- **Little** progress has been made in recent years.
- He showed **little** interest in the project.

## A few vs A little — Dương tính (có một ít)

### A few — countable, dương tính

```
a few + countable plural noun = "có một chút — đủ dùng"
```

- I have **a few** suggestions for improving this. (có vài gợi ý)
- Let me give you **a few** examples.
- She needed **a few** days to recover.

### A little — uncountable, dương tính

```
a little + uncountable noun = "có một ít — đủ dùng"
```

- Could you give me **a little** more time? (xin thêm một chút thời gian)
- There is **a little** hope remaining.
- I have **a little** experience in this field.

### Tóm tắt few vs little

| | Âm tính (quá ít, thiếu) | Dương tính (có một ít, đủ) |
|--|------------------------|--------------------------|
| **Countable** | **few** (ít hơn mức cần) | **a few** (có một số) |
| **Uncountable** | **little** (ít hơn mức cần) | **a little** (có một ít) |

**Ví dụ so sánh:**
- **Few** students passed. (Rất ít em đỗ — kết quả đáng lo ngại)
- **A few** students passed. (Có một vài em đỗ — tốt hơn không có gì)
- **Little** funding was available. (Gần như không có kinh phí)
- **A little** funding was available. (Có một ít kinh phí)

## Some vs Any

### Some — khẳng định, đề nghị, mời mọc

- **Some** countries have adopted this approach. (một số nước)
- Would you like **some** water? (mời/đề nghị)
- **Some** students find writing difficult.

### Any — phủ định, câu hỏi, điều kiện

- Are there **any** questions? (câu hỏi)
- There aren't **any** exceptions. (phủ định)
- If you have **any** concerns, let me know. (điều kiện)

> **Any trong câu khẳng định = "bất kỳ":**
> - You can ask **any** question you like.
> - **Any** country can participate.

## Quantifiers trang trọng — Dùng trong IELTS Writing

| Informal | Formal / Academic |
|---------|------------------|
| a lot of people | a large number of people |
| a lot of research | a great deal of research |
| lots of evidence | a significant amount of evidence |
| many countries | the majority of countries |
| most | the vast majority of |
| some | a proportion of / a number of |

**Ví dụ viết lại:**

- ❌ A lot of studies show that exercise is beneficial.
- ✅ **A considerable number of** studies demonstrate that exercise yields significant health benefits.

- ❌ Many countries have this problem.
- ✅ **The majority of** countries experience this issue to varying degrees.

## Quantifiers quan trọng khác

### Several — một số (nhiều hơn a few, ít hơn many)

Chỉ dùng với countable plural:

- There are **several** reasons for this trend.
- **Several** studies have reached similar conclusions.
- The policy has **several** notable weaknesses.

### Plenty of — đủ nhiều, thừa thãi

Dùng với cả countable và uncountable:

- There is **plenty of** time before the deadline.
- There are **plenty of** opportunities available.
- She has **plenty of** experience in this field.

### A number of — một số (tương đương "some/several")

Dùng với countable plural, động từ số nhiều:

- **A number of** students have raised this concern. (students → động từ số nhiều)
- **A number of** factors contribute to this problem.

> **Phân biệt "the number of" vs "a number of":**
> - **The number of** students **is** increasing. (số lượng → số ít)
> - **A number of** students **are** struggling. (một số học sinh → số nhiều)

### The majority of / A minority of

- **The majority of** participants **agreed** with the proposal.
- **A minority of** people **remain** opposed.

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Dùng "many" với uncountable

- ❌ There is **many** evidence to support this.
- ✅ There is **much** evidence / **a great deal of** evidence to support this.
- ❌ Many information has been gathered.
- ✅ **Much** information / **A great deal of** information has been gathered.

### Lỗi 2: Dùng "much" với countable

- ❌ There are **much** students in the class.
- ✅ There are **many** students in the class.
- ❌ We need **much** more workers.
- ✅ We need **many** more workers.

### Lỗi 3: Nhầm "few" và "a few"

- ❌ The situation is positive — **few** solutions exist. (ngụ ý không đủ)
- ✅ The situation is positive — **a few** solutions exist.
- ❌ The outlook is bleak but **a few** alternatives remain. (cần nhấn mạnh tính âm tính)
- ✅ The outlook is bleak but **few** alternatives remain.

### Lỗi 4: "A number of" + singular verb

- ❌ A number of students **is** struggling.
- ✅ A number of students **are** struggling. (subject thực là "students")

### Lỗi 5: "Several" với uncountable

- ❌ There is **several** evidence...
- ✅ There is **some** evidence... / There are **several** pieces of evidence...

## Ứng dụng trong IELTS

### Writing Task 1 — Mô tả dữ liệu:

- "**A significant number of** respondents indicated a preference for online shopping."
- "**The vast majority of** households in the survey reported owning at least one car."
- "**A small proportion of** participants had **little** or no access to internet services."
- "**Several** countries showed **considerable** improvements in literacy rates."
- "**Few** nations managed to achieve the target by the deadline."

### Writing Task 2 — Lập luận:

- "**A great deal of** research suggests that early childhood education yields long-term benefits."
- "**Many** people argue that technology isolates individuals; however, **a significant number of** studies suggest the opposite."
- "**Few** would dispute that access to clean water is a fundamental human right."
- "There are **several** compelling reasons why governments should invest more in public health."
- "**The majority of** experts agree that immediate action is required."

### Speaking Part 3 — Thảo luận:

- "I think **a lot of** young people are genuinely concerned about the environment."
- "There are **quite a few** barriers that prevent people from getting higher education."
- "**Most** people I know use social media daily, but **a few** of my friends have deliberately avoided it."

## Bài tập luyện

### Chọn quantifier đúng

1. There is (much/many) ___ pollution in the city.
2. (Few/A few) ___ countries have succeeded in reducing poverty significantly.
3. She only had (little/a little) ___ time to prepare.
4. (Many/Much) ___ research has been done on this topic.
5. There are (several/much) ___ solutions to consider.

### Sửa lỗi

1. There is many evidence that exercise improves mental health.
2. A number of research have been conducted.
3. Few solutions exist, so we are optimistic.
4. Much students choose to study online these days.
5. A number of funding is available for this project.

### Đáp án

**Quantifier đúng:**
1. much | 2. Few | 3. a little | 4. Much / A great deal of | 5. several

**Sửa lỗi:**
1. There is **much / a great deal of** evidence... (evidence = uncountable)
2. **A great deal of** research has been conducted. (research = uncountable; "a number of" → countable plural)
3. **A few** solutions exist, so we are optimistic. (positive → "a few")
4. **Many** students choose... (students = countable plural)
5. **A significant amount of** funding is available. (funding = uncountable)

## Tóm tắt nhanh

| | Countable plural | Uncountable |
|--|-----------------|-------------|
| **Nhiều** | many / a large number of | much / a great deal of / a large amount of |
| **Đủ nhiều** | plenty of / several | plenty of |
| **Ít (âm tính)** | **few** | **little** |
| **Có một ít (dương tính)** | **a few** | **a little** |
| **Một số** | some / a number of | some / a proportion of |
| **Không có** | not any / no | not any / no |

**Quy tắc vàng:**
- **many / few / a few / several** → chỉ dùng với countable plural
- **much / little / a little** → chỉ dùng với uncountable
- **few** (âm tính: không đủ) ≠ **a few** (dương tính: có một số)
- **little** (âm tính) ≠ **a little** (dương tính)
- Trong IELTS Writing: thay "a lot of" bằng "a large number of" (countable) hoặc "a great deal of" (uncountable)
