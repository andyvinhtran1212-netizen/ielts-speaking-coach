## Tóm tắt

Hedging là **kỹ thuật diễn đạt thận trọng** — không phải vì người viết thiếu tự tin, mà vì văn phong học thuật đòi hỏi sự chính xác và khiêm tốn tri thức (epistemic humility).

| Loại hedging | Ví dụ |
|-------------|-------|
| **Modal verbs** | may, might, could, would |
| **Verbs thận trọng** | tends to, appears to, seems to, suggests |
| **Adverbs** | generally, often, typically, largely |
| **Cấu trúc impersonal** | It can be argued that, It is widely believed that |
| **Giới hạn phạm vi** | in many cases, under certain conditions, to some extent |

---

## Tại sao quan trọng

Văn phong học thuật **không nói tuyệt đối** trừ khi đó là sự thật không thể tranh cãi. So sánh:

- ❌ "Social media **destroys** family relationships." (quá tuyệt đối)
- ✅ "Social media **can** have a negative impact on family relationships **in some cases**."

Câu hedged không yếu hơn — nó **chính xác hơn** và **khó bác bỏ hơn**. Examiner IELTS đánh giá cao người biết phân biệt giữa sự thật tuyệt đối và nhận định có điều kiện.

## PHẦN 1: MODAL VERBS — Hedging qua động từ khuyết thiếu

Modal verbs là công cụ hedging phổ biến và linh hoạt nhất.

### may / might — có thể (không chắc chắn)

```
Subject + may/might + V (base form)
```

- Technology **may** replace many manual jobs within the next decade.
- Air pollution **might** be a contributing factor to rising rates of asthma.
- These changes **may** not produce the desired results immediately.
- Economic inequality **might** worsen if current trends continue.

> **Phân biệt:** "may" nghe hơi chắc hơn "might", nhưng cả hai đều phù hợp trong academic writing.

### could — có thể (khả năng về mặt lý thuyết)

```
Subject + could + V (base form)
```

- Increased investment in education **could** significantly reduce poverty levels.
- Remote work **could** lead to both benefits and challenges for employees.
- This approach **could** prove more effective than current methods.
- Stricter regulations **could** deter foreign investment.

### would — có điều kiện (trong ngữ cảnh hypothetical)

```
Subject + would + V (thường trong câu điều kiện hoặc generalisation)
```

- Higher taxes **would** discourage consumption of harmful products.
- Implementing such a policy **would** require significant resources.
- **Without** adequate funding, the programme **would** struggle to succeed.

## PHẦN 2: VERBS THẬN TRỌNG — Tends to, Appears to, Seems to

Những động từ này diễn đạt xu hướng chung hoặc nhận xét có mức độ thận trọng.

### tends to — có xu hướng

```
Subject + tends to + V
```

- Wealthier countries **tend to** invest more in healthcare infrastructure.
- Students who read regularly **tend to** perform better in written exams.
- Economic growth **tends to** slow during periods of political instability.
- People **tend to** underestimate the long-term consequences of their choices.

> "Tends to" là một trong những từ hedging đẹp nhất trong academic English — thể hiện rằng đây là xu hướng chung, không phải quy luật tuyệt đối.

### appears to / seems to — có vẻ như (dựa trên quan sát)

```
Subject + appears to / seems to + V
```

- The data **appears to** suggest a correlation between income and health outcomes.
- Current research **seems to** support the view that early education is crucial.
- The policy **appears to** have had limited impact on long-term behaviour change.
- Public opinion **seems to** be shifting in favour of stricter environmental rules.

### suggests — ngụ ý, cho thấy (dữ liệu/nghiên cứu)

```
[Data/Evidence/Research] + suggests (that) + clause
```

- Recent studies **suggest that** regular physical exercise reduces the risk of depression.
- The evidence **suggests** a link between poverty and educational underachievement.
- Available data **suggests** that urban populations are growing at an unprecedented rate.

## PHẦN 3: ADVERBS — Hedging qua trạng từ

Adverbs giới hạn phạm vi của nhận định mà không thay đổi cấu trúc câu.

### Tần suất và mức độ phổ biến

| Adverb | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|-------|
| **generally** | nhìn chung | This **generally** leads to better outcomes. |
| **often** | thường thường | People **often** underestimate these risks. |
| **typically** | điển hình là | Students **typically** struggle with time management. |
| **frequently** | thường xuyên | This issue is **frequently** overlooked. |
| **commonly** | phổ biến | It is **commonly** assumed that... |

### Mức độ chắc chắn

| Adverb | Mức chắc chắn |
|--------|-------------|
| **certainly / undoubtedly** | Rất cao (dùng cẩn thận) |
| **largely / predominantly** | Cao — phần lớn là đúng |
| **arguably** | Trung bình — có thể được lập luận như vậy |
| **probably / likely** | Trung bình |
| **possibly / perhaps** | Thấp |

**Ví dụ:**
- The situation is **largely** the result of poor planning at the governmental level.
- Social inequality is **predominantly** an economic issue.
- This **arguably** represents the most significant shift in the labour market in decades.
- The long-term consequences are **probably** more severe than currently acknowledged.
- **Perhaps** the most concerning aspect is the impact on vulnerable populations.

## PHẦN 4: CẤU TRÚC IMPERSONAL — It can be argued / It is widely believed

Cấu trúc impersonal giúp diễn đạt ý kiến một cách khách quan, tránh dùng "I" quá nhiều.

### It can be argued that...

```
It can be argued that + [clause]
It could be argued that + [clause]
```

- **It can be argued that** globalisation has widened the gap between rich and poor nations.
- **It could be argued that** the benefits of this policy outweigh its limitations.
- **It can reasonably be argued that** stricter environmental legislation is now essential.

### It is widely believed / acknowledged / recognised that...

```
It is widely/generally/commonly + [believed/acknowledged/recognised/accepted] that + [clause]
```

- **It is widely acknowledged that** climate change is one of the greatest challenges of our time.
- **It is generally recognised that** early intervention produces better educational outcomes.
- **It is commonly accepted that** a diverse workforce leads to greater innovation.
- **It is broadly understood that** inequality is both a cause and consequence of poor health.

### It is worth noting / considering that...

```
It is worth noting/considering/emphasising that + [clause]
```

- **It is worth noting that** these figures represent averages and may conceal significant variation.
- **It is important to recognise that** not all communities are equally affected.
- **It should be noted that** correlation does not imply causation.

## PHẦN 5: GIỚI HẠN PHẠM VI — Qualifying clauses

Giới hạn phạm vi là cách hedging bằng cách nói rõ điều kiện hoặc phạm vi áp dụng.

### Giới hạn nhóm đối tượng

```
for many people... / for some... / in certain communities...
among [group], ... / particularly for [group], ...
```

- **For many young people**, social media is the primary source of news and entertainment.
- **Among lower-income households**, the impact of rising food prices is most severe.
- **Particularly for** those in rural areas, access to quality healthcare remains a challenge.

### Giới hạn điều kiện

```
in many cases... / under certain circumstances... / in some contexts...
when [condition], ... / provided that... / assuming that...
```

- **In many cases**, economic development comes at an environmental cost.
- **Under certain circumstances**, short-term financial incentives can produce lasting behavioural change.
- **In some contexts**, traditional approaches may be more effective than modern methods.
- **Assuming that** current trends continue, the situation is likely to worsen.

### Giới hạn mức độ

```
to some extent... / to a certain degree... / in part...
at least in part... / largely, though not entirely...
```

- **To some extent**, these problems are the result of inadequate public investment.
- The decline is **at least in part** attributable to changing consumer preferences.
- This is **largely, though not entirely**, an economic phenomenon.

## PHẦN 6: SO SÁNH STRONG vs. HEDGED

| Strong Claim (overstatement) | Hedged Version (academic) |
|-----------------------------|--------------------------|
| Social media **destroys** relationships. | Social media **can have a negative impact on** relationships **in some cases**. |
| Young people **are lazy**. | Young people **may be less motivated** in certain contexts, **perhaps** due to economic uncertainty. |
| Technology **causes** unemployment. | Technology **tends to** displace certain types of jobs, **although** it **also tends to** create new ones. |
| Education **solves** poverty. | Education **is widely regarded as** one of the most effective tools for **reducing** poverty **over the long term**. |
| Cities **are better** than rural areas. | Cities **generally offer** more economic opportunities, **although** rural areas **may** provide a better quality of life **for certain individuals**. |
| This policy **will** solve the problem. | This policy **could** help address the issue, **provided that** it is properly implemented and funded. |

## Ứng dụng trong IELTS Writing Task 2

### Mở bài (với hedging)

- "**It is widely acknowledged that** the issue of [topic] has become increasingly significant in recent decades."
- "**While it can be argued that** [position A], **many** would contend that [position B]."
- "**It is generally believed that** [topic] has both advantages and disadvantages."

### Thân bài (với hedging tầng)

- "**One of the most significant** consequences **appears to be** the widening gap between skilled and unskilled workers."
- "**In many cases**, the benefits of this approach **tend to** outweigh the costs, **particularly** in developed economies."
- "**It could be argued that** this policy **might** prove more effective **if** supported by adequate public funding."

### Kết bài (cân bằng với hedging)

- "**Overall**, **it seems that** [position], **although** the situation is **arguably** more complex than it first appears."
- "**While** a definitive conclusion is difficult to reach, the evidence **suggests** that [stance]."
- "**In conclusion**, addressing this issue **will likely** require a multi-faceted approach, **combining** [solution A] **with** [solution B]."

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Hedge quá nhiều — câu trở nên vô nghĩa

- ❌ "It might possibly perhaps seem to suggest that there could be some kind of potential link..."
- ✅ "Research **suggests** a possible link between..." (một hedge đủ)

### Lỗi 2: Dùng "maybe" thay vì academic hedges

- ❌ "**Maybe** social media is bad for society."
- ✅ "Social media **may** have negative consequences for social cohesion."

### Lỗi 3: Hedge mọi thứ, kể cả sự thật rõ ràng

- ❌ "The Earth **might** orbit the Sun." (sự thật khoa học — không cần hedge)
- ✅ Chỉ hedge nhận định, quan điểm, dự đoán, và generalisation

### Lỗi 4: Dùng "I think" khi cần impersonal hedge

- ❌ "**I think** poverty is linked to crime." (quá personal trong academic writing)
- ✅ "**It can be argued that** poverty is linked to higher crime rates."
- ✅ "**Research suggests** a correlation between poverty and crime."

## Bài tập luyện

### Thêm hedging vào các câu quá tuyệt đối

1. "Smartphones are ruining young people's ability to concentrate."
2. "Governments must ban fast food advertising."
3. "Higher education guarantees a better salary."
4. "Social media causes depression."

### Gợi ý đáp án

1. "Smartphones **may be contributing to** a decline in concentration spans **among some young people**, **particularly** those who use them excessively."
2. "**It can be argued that** governments **should** consider stricter regulations on fast food advertising, **particularly** content targeting children."
3. "Higher education **tends to** be associated with better long-term earnings, **although** this **varies considerably** depending on the field of study and individual circumstances."
4. "Heavy use of social media **appears to** be linked to higher rates of anxiety and depression **in certain groups**, **particularly** adolescents — **although** the causal relationship is still debated."

  may/might → có thể
  could     → có thể (lý thuyết)
  would     → có điều kiện

VERBS THẬN TRỌNG:
  tends to   → có xu hướng
  appears to / seems to → có vẻ như
  suggests   → dữ liệu cho thấy

ADVERBS GIỚI HẠN:
  generally, often, typically, largely, arguably, possibly

IMPERSONAL STRUCTURES:
  It can be argued that...
  It is widely believed/acknowledged that...
  It is worth noting that...
  It should be recognised that...

GIỚI HẠN PHẠM VI:
  to some extent, in many cases, for some people
  under certain conditions, particularly for...

QUY TẮC VÀNG:
  → Hedge nhận định và generalisation
  → Đừng hedge sự thật khoa học/lịch sử đã được công nhận
  → Một hedge mạnh tốt hơn ba hedge yếu
  → "tends to" + "in many cases" là combo rất mạnh cho Task 2
