## Tóm tắt

Hedging (diễn đạt thận trọng) là kỹ thuật dùng ngôn ngữ để:
- **Tránh khẳng định tuyệt đối** khi bằng chứng chưa đủ chắc chắn
- **Thể hiện sự không chắc chắn** một cách học thuật
- **Trình bày quan điểm** mà không áp đặt
- **Phân biệt sự thật với ý kiến** trong văn bản học thuật

So sánh:
- ❌ "Technology **destroys** human relationships." (quá tuyệt đối)
- ✅ "Technology **tends to undermine** human relationships **in some cases**." (hedged)

## Tại sao quan trọng

Trong IELTS Writing và Speaking, hedging language giúp:
1. **Band 7+:** Examiners đánh giá cao khả năng phân biệt fact vs opinion, certain vs uncertain
2. **Độ chính xác:** Tránh overgeneralisation — lỗi phổ biến khiến mất điểm Task Response
3. **Văn phong học thuật:** Academic writing luôn dùng hedges; không có hedges = không đủ sophisticated
4. **Speaking Band 7+:** Sử dụng hedging khi thảo luận ý kiến trừu tượng trong Part 3

## Nhóm 1: Modal Verbs (Động từ khuyết thiếu)

Modal verbs là cách hedging phổ biến nhất:

| Modal | Mức chắc chắn | Ví dụ |
|-------|--------------|-------|
| **will** | ~95% | Technology **will** continue to advance. |
| **should** | ~80% | This approach **should** reduce costs. |
| **would** | ~75% (giả định) | This **would** be beneficial in theory. |
| **may** | ~50% | Social media **may** have negative effects. |
| **might** | ~40% | This trend **might** reverse in the future. |
| **could** | ~35% | The policy **could** have unintended consequences. |
| **can** | khả năng/khái quát | Stress **can** lead to serious health problems. |

**Dùng trong IELTS Writing Task 2:**
- This **could** lead to greater inequality.
- There **may** be long-term consequences that we cannot yet foresee.
- Such policies **might** prove counterproductive if implemented without proper planning.

## Nhóm 2: Hedging Verbs (Động từ thận trọng)

### Verbs thể hiện "có vẻ / dường như":

| Verb | Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|------|----------|-------|
| **seem** | seem(s) to + V / seem(s) + adj | This **seems to** support the argument. |
| **appear** | appear(s) to + V | The data **appears to** show a decline. |
| **tend** | tend(s) to + V | Young people **tend to** prefer urban lifestyles. |
| **suggest** | suggest(s) that | The evidence **suggests that** stress is a factor. |
| **indicate** | indicate(s) that | Research **indicates that** sleep affects performance. |
| **imply** | imply/implies that | This **implies that** further study is needed. |

### Verbs thể hiện quan điểm cá nhân:

| Verb | Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|------|----------|-------|
| **argue** | argue(s) that | Some experts **argue that** technology is neutral. |
| **suggest** | suggest(s) that | I **would suggest that** a balanced approach is needed. |
| **believe** | believe(s) that | Many people **believe that** education is the key. |
| **claim** | claim(s) that | Critics **claim that** this policy will fail. |
| **maintain** | maintain(s) that | Proponents **maintain that** benefits outweigh costs. |

## Nhóm 3: Hedging Adverbs và Phrases

### Adverbs chỉ mức độ chắc chắn:

| Mức chắc chắn | Adverb/Phrase |
|--------------|----------------|
| Rất chắc chắn | **clearly, obviously, certainly, undoubtedly** |
| Khá chắc chắn | **probably, generally, typically, largely** |
| Không chắc | **possibly, perhaps, apparently, presumably** |
| Ít chắc hơn | **conceivably, theoretically, potentially** |

**Ví dụ:**
- This is **probably** the most significant factor.
- **Generally**, people prefer stability over change.
- **Perhaps** the most important consideration is cost.
- The side effects are **potentially** serious.

### Phrases hạn định phạm vi:

| Phrase | Chức năng | Ví dụ |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| **in many cases** | không phải tất cả | **In many cases**, this approach is effective. |
| **to some extent** | một phần | **To some extent**, the government is responsible. |
| **in some respects** | một số mặt | **In some respects**, technology has improved life. |
| **for the most part** | phần lớn | **For the most part**, the results were positive. |
| **under certain conditions** | điều kiện nhất định | This works **under certain conditions**. |
| **in certain contexts** | bối cảnh nhất định | **In certain contexts**, this may not apply. |

## Nhóm 4: Noun Phrases Thận Trọng

Dùng danh từ trừu tượng để tránh khẳng định mạnh:

| Cách nói mạnh | Cách nói thận trọng |
|--------------|-------------------|
| "Technology destroys..." | "There is **a tendency for** technology to..." |
| "This proves..." | "This **provides evidence for**..." |
| "Clearly, it causes..." | "There **appears to be** a link between..." |
| "Everyone knows..." | "It is **widely believed** that..." |

**Cấu trúc hữu dụng:**
- There **tends to be** a relationship between X and Y.
- There **appears to be** evidence that...
- There **is a possibility** that...
- There **is a degree of** uncertainty about...
- **A growing body of evidence suggests** that...

## Nhóm 5: Passive Reporting Structures

Cấu trúc bị động trích dẫn nghiên cứu một cách thận trọng — rất phổ biến trong IELTS Writing:

```
It + passive verb + that-clause
Subject + passive verb + to-infinitive
```

| Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|----------|-------|
| **It is believed that** | **It is believed that** exercise improves mental health. |
| **It is argued that** | **It is argued that** inequality is rising. |
| **It is widely accepted that** | **It is widely accepted that** education is a right. |
| **It has been suggested that** | **It has been suggested that** diet affects behaviour. |
| **It is generally assumed that** | **It is generally assumed that** growth is desirable. |
| **It is often claimed that** | **It is often claimed that** technology creates jobs. |

**Chuyển từ active sang passive:**
- "Researchers believe that..." → "**It is believed that...**"
- "Some argue that..." → "**It is argued that...**"
- "Studies show that..." → "**It has been shown that...**"

**Subject + passive + to-infinitive:**
- Technology **is thought to** be both beneficial and harmful.
- Education **is considered to** be a fundamental right.
- Income inequality **is known to** affect social mobility.

## Nhóm 6: Approximators và Limiters

Tránh số liệu tuyệt đối hoặc khẳng định vô điều kiện:

| Từ tuyệt đối | Hedged alternative |
|-------------|-------------------|
| **all** | most, the majority of, a large proportion of |
| **never** | rarely, seldom, in very few cases |
| **always** | generally, typically, in most instances |
| **everyone** | many people, a large number of, the majority |
| **nothing** | very little, relatively few |
| **impossible** | extremely difficult, highly unlikely |
| **proves** | suggests, indicates, implies, points to |

**Số liệu gần đúng:**
- **approximately** 30% of respondents
- **around** half of the population
- **roughly** one in three people
- **more than** / **fewer than** + số
- **up to** / **at least** + số

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Overgeneralisation — khẳng định tuyệt đối

- ❌ Social media **always** leads to depression.
- ✅ Social media **can** lead to depression **in some cases**.
- ❌ Technology **destroys** human connection.
- ✅ Technology **may undermine** human connection **to some extent**.

### Lỗi 2: Under-hedging trong Task 2 opinion essays

- ❌ This is the best solution to poverty.
- ✅ This **would appear to be** one of the most effective solutions to poverty.

### Lỗi 3: Nhầm "may" và "might" với "maybe"

- ❌ **Maybe** the government should invest more. (informal — tránh trong Writing)
- ✅ The government **may** need to invest more. (modal verb — formal)
- ✅ **Perhaps** the government should consider increasing investment. (adverb — formal)

### Lỗi 4: Lạm dụng hedging — quá nhiều hedges làm mất điểm Task Achievement

- ❌ It **might possibly perhaps** be the case that technology **could** potentially have **some** effects.
- ✅ Technology **may** have significant effects on mental health **in certain contexts**.

### Lỗi 5: Dùng "seem" sai cấu trúc

- ❌ It seems **like** the policy has failed.
- ✅ It seems **that** the policy has failed. (formal writing)
- ✅ The policy **seems to have** failed. (subject + seem + to-inf)

## Ứng dụng trong IELTS

### Writing Task 2 — trình bày quan điểm thận trọng:

**Đoạn mở đầu:**
> While it is **often argued** that technology has improved our lives, it **could be suggested** that this comes at a social cost. This essay will **attempt to** examine both perspectives.

**Thân bài — đưa ra lập luận:**
> Online learning **appears to** offer greater flexibility for working adults. Research **suggests that** students who study remotely **tend to** perform as well as, if not better than, their classroom-based peers. However, this **may** not hold true across all demographics, particularly those without reliable internet access.

**Thân bài — thừa nhận góc nhìn khác:**
> **It is widely believed** that face-to-face interaction is essential for social development. **To some extent**, this **seems to** be supported by psychological research, which **indicates** that isolation **can** lead to poorer mental health outcomes.

**Kết luận:**
> **On balance**, it **would appear** that a hybrid approach **is likely to** be the most effective solution. **In most cases**, policies that combine technological innovation with human-centred design **tend to** yield the best results.

### Writing Task 1 — mô tả dữ liệu không chắc chắn:

- The figures **suggest** a correlation between income and education levels.
- There **appears to be** a steady upward trend throughout the period.
- The data **may** reflect seasonal variation rather than a long-term shift.

### Speaking Part 3 — thảo luận ý kiến học thuật:

**Thay vì:** "Yes, technology is good for education."
**Dùng:** "Well, I **think** technology **tends to** be beneficial for education, **although** it **depends on** how it's used. For younger learners, **it seems** that some screen time **might** actually hinder development rather than help."

**Thay vì:** "The government should always provide free education."
**Dùng:** "**In an ideal world**, governments **would** provide free education for everyone, **but** the reality is that this **may** not always be financially feasible. **That said**, there **do appear to be** strong economic arguments for subsidising higher education **to some degree**."

## Bài tập luyện

### Thêm hedging vào câu sau

1. Technology causes young people to be less social.
2. The government's policy will solve unemployment.
3. Everyone prefers working from home.
4. Social media is responsible for mental health problems in teenagers.
5. The economy will recover within two years.

### Chọn hedge phù hợp

1. The data ___ (show/suggests/prove) a link between diet and cancer.
2. This approach ___ (is/tends to be/will always be) effective for adult learners.
3. It ___ (is argued/is obvious/everyone knows) that automation will change the labour market.
4. Online shopping ___ (destroys/may undermine/never helps) local businesses ___ (always/in some cases).

### Đáp án

**Thêm hedging:**
1. Technology **tends to make** young people **somewhat** less social, **in certain contexts**.
2. The government's policy **may help to** reduce unemployment **to some extent**.
3. **Many** people / **The majority of** people **appear to** prefer working from home.
4. Social media **may be** a contributing factor to mental health problems **in some** teenagers.
5. The economy **is likely to** recover **within approximately** two years, **assuming** conditions remain stable.

**Chọn hedge:**
1. suggests | 2. tends to be | 3. It is argued | 4. may undermine / in some cases

## Tóm tắt nhanh

| Kỹ thuật | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|
| **Modal verbs** | may, might, could, would, should |
| **Hedging verbs** | seem, appear, tend to, suggest, indicate |
| **Adverbs** | probably, generally, perhaps, possibly, apparently |
| **Limiters** | to some extent, in many cases, in certain contexts |
| **Approximators** | approximately, around, roughly, up to |
| **Passive reporting** | It is believed/argued/suggested that... |
| **Passive + to-inf** | is thought to, is considered to, is known to |
| **Noun phrases** | there tends to be, there appears to be a link |

**Quy tắc vàng:**
- Khẳng định tuyệt đối (all, never, always, proves) → hầu như luôn cần được hedge
- Dùng 1–2 hedges mỗi câu — quá nhiều làm câu lủng củng
- Trong IELTS Writing: hedging không phải là "thiếu tự tin" — nó là **văn phong học thuật chuẩn**
- "May/might/could + V" → hedge mạnh nhất và tự nhiên nhất trong Writing
