## Tóm tắt

| Từ/cụm | Từ loại | Mức độ nhấn mạnh | Vị trí | Register |
|--------|---------|-----------------|--------|---------|
| **although** | Liên từ phụ (subordinating conjunction) | Trung bình | Đầu câu hoặc giữa | Neutral/Formal |
| **though** | Liên từ phụ | Trung bình | Đầu, giữa, **hoặc cuối** | Informal/Neutral |
| **even though** | Liên từ phụ | Mạnh nhất | Đầu câu hoặc giữa | Neutral |
| **however** | Trạng từ liên kết | — | Đầu câu mới | Formal |
| **despite / in spite of** | Giới từ | — | Đầu hoặc giữa | Formal |

## Tại sao quan trọng

Khi phân tích trong IELTS Writing Task 2, bạn cần thừa nhận quan điểm đối lập trước khi phản bác — đây gọi là **concession + counter-argument**. Đây là kỹ năng cốt lõi để đạt Band 7+. Chỉ biết một trong số các từ này sẽ khiến bài viết lặp đi lặp lại. Biết tất cả và dùng đúng ngữ cảnh là thể hiện của Cohesion & Coherence và Lexical Resource cao.

---

## PHẦN 1: ALTHOUGH — Trung lập, trang trọng

```
Although + [subject + verb], [main clause].
[Main clause] although [subject + verb].
```

"Although" là liên từ phụ trung lập — phù hợp cả writing lẫn speaking. Nó giới thiệu mệnh đề phụ (subordinate clause) diễn đạt sự nhượng bộ.

### Vị trí:

**Đứng đầu câu (phổ biến hơn trong writing):**

- **Although** the economy has grown, inequality remains a serious problem.
- **Although** technology has many benefits, it also poses significant risks.
- **Although** access to education has improved, quality remains uneven.
- **Although** she worked hard, she did not pass the exam.

**Đứng giữa câu (sau mệnh đề chính):**

- The policy achieved some success, **although** its long-term effects are unclear.
- Many people support the idea, **although** implementation remains a challenge.
- He accepted the offer, **although** the salary was lower than expected.

> **Lưu ý quan trọng:** Không dùng "but" cùng "although" trong một câu:
> - ❌ "**Although** he is rich, **but** he is not happy."
> - ✅ "**Although** he is rich, he is not happy."
> - ✅ "He is rich, **but** he is not happy."

## PHẦN 2: THOUGH — Linh hoạt hơn, informal hơn

```
Though + [subject + verb], [main clause].
[Main clause] though [subject + verb].
[Main clause], though.    ← cuối câu (đặc biệt)
```

"Though" có thể thay thế "although" trong hầu hết các ngữ cảnh — nhưng có thêm khả năng đứng **cuối câu** như trạng từ để bổ sung ý nhượng bộ.

### Đứng đầu và giữa câu (giống although):

- **Though** the results were disappointing, the team remained motivated.
- She agreed to help, **though** she had very little time.

### Đứng cuối câu (đặc trưng của "though"):

```
[Statement]. [Contrasting statement], though.
```

- The project was challenging. It was rewarding, **though**.
- He didn't win. He learned a lot, **though**.
- The plan is ambitious. It is feasible, **though**.

> Đây là cách rất tự nhiên trong Speaking Part 3: đưa ra quan điểm, rồi thêm ý nhượng bộ nhẹ ở cuối.

### "Though" trong "as though" và "even though":

- She spoke **as though** she had lived there all her life.
- She passed **even though** she hadn't prepared at all. *(xem phần 3)*

## PHẦN 3: EVEN THOUGH — Nhấn mạnh nhất

```
Even though + [subject + verb], [main clause].
[Main clause] even though [subject + verb].
```

"Even though" nhấn mạnh rằng kết quả **bất ngờ hoặc đặc biệt mạnh** — "thậm chí cho dù", "ngay cả khi":

- **Even though** he trained for months, he still failed the test. *(kết quả bất ngờ)*
- **Even though** the evidence is clear, some people still deny climate change.
- She continued working **even though** she was exhausted.
- **Even though** the treatment is expensive, many patients cannot access it.
- He donated the money **even though** he could barely afford it himself.

### So sánh mức độ nhấn mạnh:

| Câu | Cảm giác |
|-----|---------|
| Although she studied hard, she failed. | Trung lập — đơn thuần tương phản |
| Though she studied hard, she failed. | Nhẹ hơn một chút, informal hơn |
| **Even though** she studied hard, she failed. | Bất ngờ — học rất chăm mà vẫn trượt |

## PHẦN 4: SO SÁNH VỚI CÁC CÔNG CỤ TƯƠNG PHẢN KHÁC

### However (trạng từ liên kết — formal):

```
[Sentence 1]. However, [Sentence 2].
```

"However" nối **hai câu riêng biệt** — không phải hai mệnh đề trong một câu.

- The government invested heavily in education. **However**, the results have been disappointing.
- Many people support the policy. **However**, its implementation remains problematic.

> ❌ Không dùng however như liên từ:
> - ❌ "I like this idea, **however** it has some drawbacks."
> - ✅ "I like this idea. **However**, it has some drawbacks."
> - ✅ "I like this idea**;** **however**, it has some drawbacks."

### Despite / In spite of (giới từ — + noun phrase):

```
Despite / In spite of + noun phrase / V-ing, [main clause].
```

- **Despite** the economic growth, poverty remains widespread.
- **In spite of** working hard, she did not achieve the desired result.

> So sánh:
> - "**Although** the economy grew, poverty remains..." *(+ subject + verb)*
> - "**Despite** economic growth, poverty remains..." *(+ noun phrase)*

### While / Whereas (so sánh trực tiếp hai vế đối lập):

```
While / Whereas [clause A], [clause B].
```

"While/Whereas" thường dùng để **đối chiếu trực tiếp hai nhóm hoặc hai quan điểm**:

- **While** urban areas have benefited from development, rural communities remain underserved.
- **Whereas** some argue that technology creates jobs, others contend that it leads to unemployment.

## PHẦN 5: CONCESSION + COUNTER-ARGUMENT (Kỹ thuật IELTS)

Đây là cấu trúc quan trọng nhất — thừa nhận ý đối lập, sau đó phản bác:

### Pattern 1: Although/Though + concession, counter-argument

- **Although** it is true that technology has created many jobs, it has simultaneously displaced a far greater number of workers in traditional industries.
- **Though** there are clear environmental benefits to renewable energy, the initial costs of implementation remain prohibitively high for many developing nations.

### Pattern 2: Admittedly/Granted + concession. However/Nevertheless + counter

- **Admittedly**, economic growth does bring some short-term benefits to the poorest communities. **However**, these gains are frequently outweighed by rising inequality and environmental degradation.
- **Granted**, some young people do develop valuable skills through social media. **Nevertheless**, the overall impact on mental health and academic performance appears to be largely negative.

### Pattern 3: While ... , ... (đối chiếu đồng thời)

- **While** renewable energy is not yet cost-competitive in all markets, the rapid pace of technological improvement suggests that this disparity will narrow significantly in the coming decade.

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Dùng "although" và "but" cùng một câu

- ❌ "**Although** the policy is costly, **but** it is necessary."
- ✅ "**Although** the policy is costly, it is necessary."
- ✅ "The policy is costly, **but** it is necessary."

### Lỗi 2: Nhầm "although" và "however"

- ❌ "**Although**, the results were disappointing." *(although không thể bắt đầu câu độc lập)*
- ✅ "**However**, the results were disappointing."
- ✅ "**Although** the approach was promising, the results were disappointing."

### Lỗi 3: Dùng "despite" + subject + verb

- ❌ "**Despite** she worked hard, she failed."
- ✅ "**Despite** working hard, she failed."
- ✅ "**Although** she worked hard, she failed."

### Lỗi 4: Dùng "even though" khi tương phản không đủ mạnh

- ❌ "**Even though** it was raining, I took an umbrella." *(mang ô khi mưa là bình thường — không bất ngờ)*
- ✅ "**Although** it was raining, I took an umbrella."
- ✅ "**Even though** it was raining heavily, she walked all the way to school." *(bất ngờ hơn)*

### Lỗi 5: Quên dấu phẩy sau mệnh đề phụ đứng đầu câu

- ❌ "Although the results were positive the challenges remain significant."
- ✅ "Although the results were positive**,** the challenges remain significant."

## Bài tập luyện

### Điền although, though, hoặc even though

1. ___ the government increased spending on healthcare, the quality of services declined.
2. She managed to finish the project on time, ___. *(cuối câu)*
3. ___ he had no experience in the field, he was given the position.
4. The proposal is controversial, ___ most experts agree it is necessary.
5. ___ the treatment is painful, many patients choose it over alternatives.

### Viết lại dùng "despite" hoặc "although"

6. "Even though she was tired, she continued working." → dùng "despite"
7. "Despite the high cost, many governments support the programme." → dùng "although"

### Hoàn thành câu IELTS-style

8. "Although renewable energy is more expensive to install initially, ___."
9. "___ technology offers many advantages in the classroom, ___."

### Đáp án

1. Although | 2. though | 3. Even though | 4. though | 5. Although / Even though
6. "Despite being tired, she continued working." / "Despite her tiredness, she continued working."
7. "Although the cost is high, many governments support the programme."
8. *(gợi ý)* "...it generates significant long-term savings in energy costs."
9. *(gợi ý)* "Although technology offers many advantages in the classroom, it can also be a significant source of distraction."

## Tóm tắt nhanh

```
ALTHOUGH + clause (neutral, formal):
  Although the economy grew, inequality persists.
  The policy failed, although intentions were good.
  → KHÔNG dùng "but" cùng "although"

THOUGH + clause (informal, linh hoạt):
  Though she studied hard, she failed.
  She was tired. She kept going, though. ← cuối câu

EVEN THOUGH + clause (mạnh nhất, nhấn mạnh bất ngờ):
  Even though he worked hard, he still failed.
  She donated money even though she had very little.

SO SÁNH VỚI:
  HOWEVER → nối hai câu riêng biệt (formal)
  DESPITE / IN SPITE OF + noun phrase (không phải clause)
  WHILE / WHEREAS → đối chiếu trực tiếp hai vế

IELTS CONCESSION PATTERN:
  Although it is true that [X], [counter-argument].
  Admittedly, [concession]. However, [main point].
  While [view A], [view B].
```
