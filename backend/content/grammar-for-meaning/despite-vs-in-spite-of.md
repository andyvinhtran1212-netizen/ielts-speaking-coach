## Tóm tắt

| | **DESPITE** | **IN SPITE OF** | **ALTHOUGH / EVEN THOUGH** |
|--|-------------|-----------------|---------------------------|
| **Theo sau là** | Noun / Noun phrase / Gerund | Noun / Noun phrase / Gerund | Subject + Verb (clause) |
| **Loại từ** | Giới từ (preposition) | Giới từ kép (preposition) | Liên từ (conjunction) |
| **Ý nghĩa** | Mặc dù | Mặc dù | Mặc dù |
| **Ví dụ** | despite the **rain** | in spite of the **rain** | although it **rained** |

---

## Tại sao quan trọng

Trong IELTS Writing Band 7+, examiners muốn thấy bạn dùng linh hoạt các cấu trúc tương phản — không chỉ "however" và "but". Hiểu rõ cấu trúc sau **despite / in spite of** (luôn là noun phrase, không phải clause) giúp bạn tránh một lỗi ngữ pháp rất phổ biến: "despite it was raining" (sai hoàn toàn).

---

## DESPITE và IN SPITE OF — hoàn toàn giống nhau về nghĩa

Cả hai đều nghĩa là **"mặc dù"** và **cần danh từ/cụm danh từ** theo sau:

```
Despite + noun/noun phrase/gerund, [main clause]
In spite of + noun/noun phrase/gerund, [main clause]
```

- **Despite** the bad weather, we went ahead with the picnic.
- **In spite of** the bad weather, we went ahead with the picnic. ✅ (hoàn toàn như nhau)

- **Despite** her nervousness, she gave an excellent presentation.
- **In spite of** her nervousness, she gave an excellent presentation. ✅

Hoặc đặt ở cuối câu:
- She gave an excellent presentation, **despite** her nervousness.
- He passed the exam **in spite of** studying very little.

> **Chọn cái nào?**
> Trong thực tế, "despite" ngắn hơn và phổ biến hơn trong văn viết học thuật. "In spite of" cũng đúng 100% nhưng dài hơn. Cả hai đều được chấp nhận trong IELTS.

## Cấu trúc đi theo sau

### 1. Danh từ (Noun)

- Despite **criticism**, the policy was implemented.
- In spite of **opposition**, the bill was passed.
- Despite **the cost**, she enrolled in the programme.

### 2. Cụm danh từ (Noun phrase)

- Despite **the heavy traffic**, we arrived on time.
- In spite of **his best efforts**, the project failed.
- Despite **a lack of resources**, they achieved remarkable results.
- In spite of **the government's promises**, little has changed.

### 3. Danh động từ / V-ing (Gerund)

Đây là trường hợp hay bị nhầm nhất:

- Despite **working** hard, she didn't get promoted.
- In spite of **trying** many times, he couldn't pass.
- Despite **being** tired, she stayed until the end.

### 4. "Despite the fact that" — trường hợp đặc biệt

Khi **bắt buộc** phải dùng mệnh đề sau despite, thêm "the fact that":

```
despite the fact that + subject + verb
in spite of the fact that + subject + verb
```

- Despite **the fact that** it **was** raining, they continued the game.
- In spite of **the fact that** she **had** worked all night, she looked fresh.

> **Lưu ý:** "Despite the fact that" = dài và hơi cứng. Trong hầu hết trường hợp, tốt hơn là chuyển sang dùng **although/even though**.

## ALTHOUGH / EVEN THOUGH — cấu trúc song song

**Although** và **even though** cũng có nghĩa "mặc dù" nhưng là **liên từ** — theo sau là **mệnh đề** (subject + verb):

```
Although + subject + verb, [main clause]
Even though + subject + verb, [main clause]
```

- **Although** it **was** raining, they continued the game.
- **Even though** she **was** nervous, she spoke confidently.
- He went to work **even though** he **felt** ill.

### So sánh trực tiếp:

| DESPITE/IN SPITE OF (+ noun) | ALTHOUGH/EVEN THOUGH (+ clause) |
|------------------------------|----------------------------------|
| Despite the **rain**, they played. | Although it **rained**, they played. |
| In spite of **her nerves**, she spoke well. | Even though she **was** nervous, she spoke well. |
| Despite **the high cost**, he bought it. | Although it **cost** a lot, he bought it. |
| Despite **working** all night, she was late. | Even though she **had worked** all night, she was late. |

## Bảng chuyển đổi giữa các cấu trúc

| Câu gốc | Chuyển sang despite | Chuyển sang although |
|---------|---------------------|----------------------|
| ...the weather was bad... | despite the bad weather | although the weather was bad |
| ...he didn't have experience... | despite his lack of experience | although he didn't have experience |
| ...she tried many times... | despite trying many times | although she tried many times |
| ...prices rose sharply... | despite the sharp rise in prices | although prices rose sharply |

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: "Despite" + mệnh đề (không có "the fact that")

- ❌ Despite **she was** tired, she kept working.
- ✅ **Despite** her tiredness, she kept working.
- ✅ Despite **the fact that** she **was** tired, she kept working.
- ✅ **Although** she **was** tired, she kept working.

### Lỗi 2: "Although" + cụm danh từ

- ❌ Although **the heavy traffic**, we arrived on time.
- ✅ **Despite** the heavy traffic, we arrived on time.
- ✅ Although **there was** heavy traffic, we arrived on time.

### Lỗi 3: "Despite of" (thêm "of" thừa vào despite)

- ❌ **Despite of** the difficulties, she persevered.
- ✅ **Despite** the difficulties, she persevered. (không cần "of")
- ✅ **In spite of** the difficulties, she persevered. (cần "of")

> **Lưu ý quan trọng:** "Despite" KHÔNG có "of". Chỉ "in spite **of**" mới có "of".

### Lỗi 4: Nhầm vị trí

- ❌ She was successful, in spite of she worked hard.
- ✅ She was successful, **in spite of** working hard.
- ✅ She was successful, **even though** she worked hard.

## Tăng cường: các cấu trúc tương phản khác

| Cấu trúc | Ví dụ | Ghi chú |
|----------|-------|---------|
| **Despite / In spite of** + noun | Despite the difficulties, ... | Formal, phổ biến trong writing |
| **Although / Even though** + clause | Although it was hard, ... | Linh hoạt, cả writing & speaking |
| **However** (đầu câu) | It was hard. However, she succeeded. | Đầu câu mới |
| **Nevertheless / Nonetheless** | It failed. Nevertheless, they tried again. | Mạnh hơn however |
| **Yet** | It was expensive, yet worth every penny. | Thường đứng giữa câu |
| **While** | While costs are high, benefits are significant. | Đồng thời / đối lập |
| **Whereas** | Urban areas are crowded, whereas rural areas are peaceful. | So sánh hai đối lập |

## Ứng dụng trong IELTS

### Writing Task 1 — mô tả xu hướng tương phản:

- **Despite** an overall upward trend, figures dipped sharply in 2009.
- **In spite of** significant investment, output remained low throughout the period.
- **Although** the overall pattern was positive, the figures for 2015 were notably lower.

### Writing Task 2 — lập luận:

- **Despite** the clear benefits of technology, its overuse can have serious consequences for mental health.
- **In spite of** widespread awareness of climate change, global carbon emissions continue to rise.
- **Although** education is widely regarded as a right, millions of children still lack access to quality schooling.
- **Even though** governments have introduced stricter regulations, enforcement remains inconsistent in many regions.

### Speaking Part 3 — thảo luận:

- "**Despite** the risks, many young people prefer starting their own businesses."
- "**In spite of** government campaigns, smoking rates haven't fallen significantly."
- "**Although** people know exercise is important, they often don't make time for it."
- "I think, **despite** all its drawbacks, social media has genuinely brought people closer together."

## Bài tập luyện

### Chọn cấu trúc đúng

1. ___ (despite / although) the cold weather, we decided to go hiking.
2. ___ (despite / although) it was very cold, we decided to go hiking.
3. ___ (despite of / despite) her young age, she was highly skilled.
4. She passed the exam ___ (in spite of / in spite) studying very little.
5. ___ (although / despite) the government's efforts, poverty rates remain high.
6. ___ (although / despite) the government tried hard, poverty rates remain high.

### Viết lại câu

Dùng "despite/in spite of" thay cho "although":

1. Although she was exhausted, she finished the marathon.
2. Although the plan was risky, they decided to proceed.
3. Although he had very little money, he was generous.

Dùng "although/even though" thay cho "despite":

4. Despite the noise, she managed to concentrate.
5. Despite his inexperience, he performed brilliantly.
6. Despite facing many obstacles, the team succeeded.

### Đáp án

**Chọn cấu trúc:**
1. Despite | 2. Although | 3. despite | 4. in spite of | 5. Despite | 6. Although

**Viết lại:**
1. Despite her exhaustion, she finished the marathon.
   / In spite of being exhausted, she finished the marathon.
2. Despite the risk, they decided to proceed.
   / In spite of the risky plan, they decided to proceed.
3. Despite having very little money, he was generous.
   / In spite of his lack of money, he was generous.
4. Although it was noisy, she managed to concentrate.
5. Even though he was inexperienced, he performed brilliantly.
6. Although/Even though the team faced many obstacles, they succeeded.

## Tóm tắt nhanh

| | DESPITE | IN SPITE OF | ALTHOUGH / EVEN THOUGH |
|--|---------|-------------|------------------------|
| **Theo sau** | noun / V-ing | noun / V-ing | subject + verb |
| **Lỗi phổ biến** | "despite of..." ❌ | — | "although the rain..." ❌ |
| **Trang trọng** | Cao | Cao | Trung bình–cao |
| **Thay thế** | in spite of | despite | however, nevertheless |

**Quy tắc vàng:**
- **Despite / In spite of** + danh từ → *despite the rain*, *in spite of her effort*
- **Although / Even though** + mệnh đề → *although it rained*, *even though she tried*
- **Không bao giờ viết "despite of"** — đây là lỗi cực kỳ phổ biến
- Khi muốn dùng mệnh đề sau despite: **"despite the fact that** + S + V"
