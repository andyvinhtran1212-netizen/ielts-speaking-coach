## Tóm tắt

| Công cụ | Từ loại | Vị trí | Register | Dùng khi |
|---------|---------|--------|---------|---------|
| **so** | Liên từ | Giữa hai vế | Informal | Speaking Part 1/2 |
| **therefore** | Trạng từ | Đầu câu mới | Formal | Writing, Speaking Part 3 |
| **as a result** | Cụm trạng từ | Đầu câu mới | Neutral/Formal | Writing & Speaking |
| **this means that** | Cụm | Đầu câu mới | Neutral | Giải thích tác động |
| **which leads to / which means** | Mệnh đề quan hệ | Giữa câu | Neutral/Formal | Chuỗi nhân-quả |
| **consequently** | Trạng từ | Đầu câu mới | Formal | Writing Task 2 |

## Tại sao quan trọng

Một câu trả lời hay không chỉ nêu nguyên nhân — nó còn cho thấy **điều đó dẫn đến điều gì**. Đây chính là phần tạo ra sự khác biệt giữa Band 5 và Band 7 trong IELTS Speaking: Band 5 nêu ý, Band 7 **giải thích tác động và hệ quả**. Trong Writing Task 2, thể hiện được chuỗi nguyên nhân → kết quả là yêu cầu cốt lõi.

---

## PHẦN 1: SO — Kết quả đơn giản, tự nhiên nhất

```
[Cause], so [Result].
```

"So" là công cụ đơn giản nhất để diễn đạt kết quả. Rất tự nhiên trong speaking.

- She studied hard, **so** she passed the exam.
- The traffic was terrible, **so** I arrived late.
- Living costs are rising, **so** many people are struggling to save money.
- The restaurant was fully booked, **so** we went somewhere else.

### Dùng "so" trong Speaking Part 1:

> Q: "Do you use public transport?"
> "Yes, I use the bus every day. My workplace is quite far from home, **so** it's the most practical option."

### Dùng "so" trong Speaking Part 2:

> "I remember this trip really well because it was the first time I'd been abroad. I'd never spoken English in a real context before, **so** it was both exciting and nerve-wracking."

> **Lưu ý:** "So" quá thông dụng — trong Writing Task 2, tránh lạm dụng và thay bằng các cụm formal hơn.

## PHẦN 2: THEREFORE / AS A RESULT / CONSEQUENTLY — Formal hơn

### Therefore:

```
[Cause]. Therefore, [Result].
[Cause]; therefore, [Result].
```

- Air pollution has worsened significantly in urban areas. **Therefore**, governments must introduce stricter vehicle emission standards.
- The research yielded inconclusive results. **Therefore**, further investigation is required.
- She had extensive experience in the field; **therefore**, she was the most suitable candidate.

### As a result:

```
[Cause]. As a result, [Result].
```

"As a result" có thể dùng ở đầu câu mới hoặc giữa câu (với dấu phẩy):

- Investment in education has declined. **As a result**, graduate unemployment has risen sharply.
- The factory was relocated overseas, **and as a result**, hundreds of local workers lost their jobs.
- Traffic congestion has worsened. **As a result**, many commuters are spending over two hours a day travelling.

### Consequently:

```
[Cause]. Consequently, [Result].
```

"Consequently" là dạng formal nhất — phù hợp nhất cho IELTS Writing Task 2:

- The population is ageing rapidly. **Consequently**, the demand for healthcare and social services is projected to increase dramatically over the next two decades.
- Deforestation has accelerated. **Consequently**, many species face the threat of extinction.

## PHẦN 3: THIS MEANS THAT — Giải thích tác động

```
[Fact/Cause]. This means that [Implication/Result].
```

"This means that" hữu ích khi bạn muốn **giải thích ý nghĩa thực tế** của một thực tế — thay vì chỉ liệt kê kết quả, bạn làm rõ nó tác động như thế nào đến người/xã hội:

- University tuition fees have tripled over the past decade. **This means that** many students now graduate with significant debt that affects their financial decisions for years.
- Automation is replacing many manual jobs. **This means that** workers without technical skills risk becoming unemployable in the near future.
- Public transport in this city is unreliable. **This means that** people have little choice but to rely on private vehicles, which worsens congestion.

### Trong speaking (informal version):

Dùng dạng ngắn hơn: "**which means**" hoặc "**so that means**":

> "I work from home three days a week, which means I save a lot of time on commuting — probably about two hours a day."

## PHẦN 4: WHICH LEADS TO / WHICH RESULTS IN — Chuỗi nhân-quả trong một câu

```
[Cause], which leads to / which results in [Result].
[Cause], leading to [Result].
```

Những cụm này cho phép diễn đạt nguyên nhân và kết quả **trong cùng một câu**, tạo câu văn chảy hơn:

- A sedentary lifestyle increases the risk of obesity, **which leads to** a range of serious health problems including heart disease and diabetes.
- Rising sea levels are flooding coastal areas, **which results in** the displacement of millions of people.
- Many young people delay marriage to focus on their careers, **leading to** a decline in birth rates in many developed countries.
- Poor diet combined with lack of exercise **can lead to** long-term health complications.

### Sự khác biệt nhỏ giữa "lead to" và "result in":

| Cụm | Ví dụ |
|-----|-------|
| **lead to** | The stress **led to** a breakdown. *(thường chỉ một chuỗi hành động/quá trình)* |
| **result in** | The accident **resulted in** three deaths. *(thường chỉ kết quả cụ thể/rõ ràng hơn)* |

## PHẦN 5: KẾT HỢP NGUYÊN NHÂN + KẾT QUẢ — Câu hoàn chỉnh

### Mẫu cơ bản (Speaking Part 1/2):

```
[Reason], so [result].
[Reason]. This means [result].
```

> "I don't own a car. It's just too expensive in the city, **so** I use the metro every day. **This means** I also get a bit of walking in, which is great."

### Mẫu phân tích (Speaking Part 3 / Writing):

```
[Problem/Cause]. As a result/Therefore/Consequently, [Result]. This means that [Further implication].
```

> "Many young people cannot afford to buy homes due to rising property prices. **As a result**, they are renting for much longer than previous generations. **This means that** they struggle to build long-term financial security, which has knock-on effects for retirement planning."

### Chuỗi nhân-quả (Writing Task 2):

```
[Cause A] leads to [Effect B], which in turn [leads to / results in] [Effect C].
```

> "Urbanisation **leads to** increased demand for housing, **which in turn results in** rising property prices that price out lower-income residents, **ultimately leading to** greater socioeconomic segregation within cities."

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Dùng "so" quá nhiều trong Writing

- ❌ "The population is growing, **so** more food is needed, **so** more land is used, **so** forests are destroyed."
- ✅ "The growing population has increased demand for food. **Consequently**, agricultural land has expanded, **resulting in** widespread deforestation."

### Lỗi 2: Nhầm "therefore" như liên từ

- ❌ "She worked hard, **therefore** she succeeded."
- ✅ "She worked hard. **Therefore**, she succeeded."
- ✅ "She worked hard; **therefore**, she succeeded."
- ✅ "She worked hard, **so** she succeeded." *(dùng "so" thay thế)*

### Lỗi 3: Nêu kết quả mà không giải thích tại sao

- ❌ "Social media is popular. Young people use it a lot."
- ✅ "Social media is extremely popular among young people. **This means that** news, trends, and opinions can spread to millions of users within hours — which has both positive and negative implications."

### Lỗi 4: Dùng "lead to" với sai dạng từ theo sau

- ❌ "This **leads to** damaged the environment."
- ✅ "This **leads to** damage to the environment." *(lead to + noun)*
- ✅ "This **leads to** the environment being damaged." *(lead to + V-ing)*

## Bài tập luyện

### Nối nguyên nhân và kết quả bằng công cụ phù hợp:

1. "Many people are working longer hours." + "They have less time for family." → ___
2. "Air pollution has increased significantly." + "Respiratory diseases are more common." → ___
3. "She missed the last train." + "She had to take a taxi." → ___
4. "The company expanded into new markets." + "Revenue increased by 40%." → ___

### Hoàn thành câu trả lời Speaking:

5. Q: "Why do you think so many young people are moving to cities?"
   "There are more job opportunities in cities, ___ many young people feel they have no choice but to move there."

6. Q: "What are the effects of social media on society?"
   "Social media has made communication much faster and easier. ___, people can now stay in touch with friends and family on the other side of the world."

### Đáp án

1. "Many people are working longer hours, **so** they have less time for family." / "Many people are working longer hours. **As a result**, they have less time for family."
2. "Air pollution has increased significantly. **Consequently**, respiratory diseases are more common." / "Air pollution has increased, **which has resulted in** a rise in respiratory disease rates."
3. "She missed the last train, **so** she had to take a taxi."
4. "The company expanded into new markets. **As a result**, revenue increased by 40%." / "The company's expansion into new markets **resulted in** a 40% increase in revenue."
5. "...so..."
6. "**As a result** / **This means that**..."

## Tóm tắt nhanh

```
SO → kết quả trong một câu (informal, speaking-friendly):
  She was tired, so she went to bed early.

THEREFORE / AS A RESULT / CONSEQUENTLY → đầu câu mới (formal):
  Prices rose. Therefore, / As a result, / Consequently, demand fell.
  → KHÔNG viết: "..., therefore ..."

THIS MEANS THAT → giải thích tác động thực tế:
  The bus is unreliable. This means that people must use cars.

WHICH LEADS TO / RESULTS IN → nhân-quả trong một câu:
  Stress leads to health problems, which results in higher healthcare costs.

CHUỖI NHÂN-QUẢ:
  [A] leads to [B], which in turn results in [C].

IELTS WRITING TIP:
  Tránh "so" trong Task 2 — dùng therefore/consequently/as a result
  Tránh chuỗi "so...so...so" — thay bằng chuỗi động từ causal
```
