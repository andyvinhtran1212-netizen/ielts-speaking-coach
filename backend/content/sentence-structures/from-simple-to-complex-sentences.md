## Tóm tắt

| Bước nâng cấp | Công cụ | Ví dụ ngắn |
|---------------|---------|-----------|
| **+Lý do** | because / since / as | ...because it saves time |
| **+Kết quả** | so / therefore / which means | ...so productivity increases |
| **+Tương phản** | but / although / while | ...but it also has drawbacks |
| **+Relative clause** | which / who / that / where | ...which affects millions of people |
| **+Điều kiện** | if / unless / as long as | ...if managed correctly |
| **+Thời gian/Bối cảnh** | Over the past decade / In recent years | Over the past decade, X has... |
| **+Nhượng bộ** | Although / Despite / While | Although X, Y remains... |

---

## Tại sao quan trọng

Câu đơn không sai. Nhưng nếu mọi câu trong bài đều là câu đơn, examiner sẽ thấy ngay bạn đang ở Band 5 hoặc thấp hơn. **Grammatical Range** đòi hỏi bạn chứng minh rằng bạn có thể xây dựng câu phức có nhiều tầng ý nghĩa.

Mục tiêu không phải là câu dài — mà là câu **chính xác và có chiều sâu**. Bài này cho bạn thấy con đường từng bước từ câu đơn đến câu phức.

---

## PHẦN 1: BẮT ĐẦU — CÂU ĐƠN

Câu đơn có một chủ ngữ và một động từ:

```
[Subject] + [Verb] + [Object/Complement].
```

- "Technology **is** useful."
- "Cities **are** growing."
- "Students **use** social media."
- "Exercise **reduces** stress."

Không sai, nhưng quá đơn giản. Hãy nâng cấp từng bước.

---

## PHẦN 2: BƯỚC 1 — THÊM LÝ DO

```
[Simple sentence] because / since / as [reason].
```

### Transformation:

| Câu đơn | + Lý do |
|---------|---------|
| "Technology is useful." | "Technology is useful **because** it allows people to access information instantly." |
| "Cities are growing." | "Cities are growing **as** more people migrate from rural areas in search of better opportunities." |
| "Exercise reduces stress." | "Exercise reduces stress **since** it triggers the release of endorphins in the brain." |

> "**Because**" = lý do cụ thể, trực tiếp
> "**Since**" / "**As**" = lý do được xem là đã biết (slightly softer, academic)

**Lưu ý:** "Because" mạnh nhất trong ba từ — dùng khi lý do là trọng tâm của ý bạn muốn nói.

## PHẦN 3: BƯỚC 2 — THÊM KẾT QUẢ

```
[Simple sentence], so [result].
[Simple sentence]. Therefore, / As a result, / Consequently, [result].
[Simple sentence], which means (that) [result].
```

### Transformation:

| Câu đơn + lý do | + Kết quả |
|----------------|---------|
| "Technology allows instant access to information." | "...**so** people can now learn almost anything without leaving home." |
| "More people are moving to cities." | "...**which means that** urban infrastructure is under increasing strain." |
| "Exercise releases endorphins." | "**As a result**, regular physical activity can significantly improve mental wellbeing." |

### Before/after (đầy đủ):

- **Before:** "Exercise is good. It makes you feel better."
- **After:** "Regular exercise **releases endorphins**, **which means that** it can significantly improve mood and reduce feelings of anxiety — **particularly** for individuals under chronic stress."

---

## PHẦN 4: BƯỚC 3 — THÊM TƯƠNG PHẢN

```
[Main point], but [contrast].
Although [contrast], [main point].
While [side A], [side B].
[Main point]. However, [contrast].
```

### Transformation:

| Câu không có tương phản | + Tương phản |
|------------------------|------------|
| "Technology is useful." | "Technology is useful, **but** it can also be a source of distraction and dependency." |
| "Cities offer opportunities." | "**While** cities offer greater career opportunities, they also come with higher living costs and significant social pressures." |
| "Social media connects people." | "Social media **can connect** people across vast distances. **However**, it has also been linked to rising rates of loneliness and anxiety, particularly among teenagers." |

---

## PHẦN 5: BƯỚC 4 — THÊM RELATIVE CLAUSE

```
[Noun] + which/that [adds information about noun].
[Noun] + who [adds information about a person].
[Place] + where [adds information about location].
[Time] + when [adds information about a time].
```

Relative clause giúp **bổ sung thông tin** về danh từ ngay bên trong câu — không cần tách ra thành câu mới.

### Transformation:

| Câu không có relative clause | + Relative clause |
|-----------------------------|-----------------|
| "Social media is popular." | "Social media, **which has transformed how billions of people communicate**, is now a dominant force in public discourse." |
| "Cities attract young people." | "Cities attract young people **who are looking for career opportunities and a more dynamic social environment**." |
| "The pandemic changed work culture." | "The pandemic, **which forced millions to work from home**, fundamentally altered expectations around workplace flexibility." |

### Thêm relative clause để thay thế câu thứ hai:

- **Before:** "Many countries face housing shortages. This creates social inequality."
- **After:** "Many countries face housing shortages, **which** contribute directly to **rising social inequality** and reduced social mobility."

## PHẦN 6: BƯỚC 5 — THÊM ĐIỀU KIỆN

```
[Main clause] if / unless / as long as / provided that [condition].
If [condition], [main clause].
```

Điều kiện thêm **sắc thái** và **tính chính xác** vào lập luận.

### Transformation:

| Câu không có điều kiện | + Điều kiện |
|-----------------------|-----------|
| "Technology improves productivity." | "Technology **can improve** productivity significantly, **provided that** employees receive adequate training and the right tools." |
| "Remote work is beneficial." | "Remote work **can be** highly beneficial, **as long as** clear communication structures are maintained." |
| "Stricter laws will reduce crime." | "Stricter laws **may** reduce certain forms of crime, **if** they are enforced consistently and fairly." |

## PHẦN 7: KẾT HỢP — CÂU PHỨC HOÀN CHỈNH

Bây giờ, kết hợp nhiều bước để tạo ra một câu phức thực sự có chiều sâu.

### Transformation hoàn chỉnh — Example 1:

**Câu đơn gốc:** "Technology changes work."

**Bước 1 (+reason):** "Technology changes work **because** it automates many routine tasks."

**Bước 2 (+result):** "Technology automates many routine tasks, **which means that** workers must continually update their skills."

**Bước 3 (+contrast):** "**While** technology automates many routine tasks, **which reduces labour costs for companies**, it also demands that workers continually invest in upskilling."

**Bước 4 (+condition):** "**While** technology undoubtedly automates many routine tasks, **reducing costs for businesses**, its overall impact on employment **is likely to be positive**, **provided that** governments invest adequately in retraining programmes."

---

### Transformation hoàn chỉnh — Example 2:

**Câu đơn gốc:** "Cities are noisy."

**+Lý do:** "Cities are noisy **because** of the constant flow of traffic and large concentrations of people."

**+Kết quả:** "...noise pollution, **which** can disrupt sleep and contribute to chronic stress."

**+Tương phản:** "**While** urban environments are inevitably noisier than rural areas, **which** some residents find deeply disruptive, others argue that the energy and vibrancy of city life **more than compensates** for the drawback of noise."

**+Điều kiện:** "**While** urban noise levels **are unlikely to** decrease significantly in the near future, their health impacts **can be** mitigated **if** city planners prioritise green spaces, noise barriers, and pedestrianised zones in urban design."

---

### Transformation hoàn chỉnh — Example 3 (Speaking answer):

**Q: "Do you think social media is good for society?"**

**Band 5 answer:** "I think social media is good. It helps people communicate. But it also has problems."

**Band 7 answer:** "I think social media **has real benefits**, **particularly** in terms of enabling people to stay connected with friends and family across long distances. **However**, platforms **that** prioritise engagement over accuracy **have also contributed** to the spread of misinformation — **which is a problem that governments and tech companies are increasingly struggling to address**. On balance, I'd say the benefits outweigh the drawbacks, **as long as** users develop critical media literacy skills."

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Câu quá dài và mất mạch

Câu phức không phải là câu dài vô tội vạ. Nếu một câu có hơn 3-4 mệnh đề phụ, hãy tách ra.

- ❌ "Technology is useful because it helps people communicate, which means they can work remotely, which reduces costs, which is why companies prefer it."
- ✅ Chia thành 2 câu: "Technology enables remote communication, significantly reducing operational costs for businesses. **As a result**, it has become an increasingly attractive option for employers worldwide."

### Lỗi 2: Thêm relative clause nhưng sai đại từ

- ❌ "The policy which it was introduced last year..."
- ✅ "The policy **which was introduced** last year..." / "The policy **that** was introduced last year..."

### Lỗi 3: Quá nhiều "because" trong một đoạn

- Đa dạng hóa: because → since → as → given that → due to the fact that

### Lỗi 4: "Although" + "but" trong cùng một câu

- ❌ "**Although** technology is useful, **but** it has drawbacks."
- ✅ "**Although** technology is useful, it has drawbacks." / "Technology is useful, **but** it has drawbacks."

## Bài tập luyện

### Nâng cấp các câu đơn sau qua từng bước:

1. "Exercise is important." → Thêm lý do → Thêm kết quả
2. "Young people use social media." → Thêm relative clause → Thêm tương phản
3. "Governments should act." → Thêm điều kiện → Thêm nhượng bộ

### Đáp án gợi ý:

1. "Exercise is important **because** it improves both physical and mental health. **As a result**, incorporating regular activity into daily routines **has been shown to** reduce the risk of numerous chronic diseases."

2. "Young people **who have grown up with social media** tend to use it as their primary source of news and social interaction. **However**, there are growing concerns about its impact on attention spans and the quality of real-world relationships."

3. "Governments **should** act decisively to address climate change, **provided that** such action is coordinated internationally and does not unfairly burden developing economies. **While** the short-term economic costs may be significant, the long-term consequences of inaction **would be** far more severe."

## Tóm tắt nhanh

```
TỪNG BƯỚC NÂNG CẤP CÂU:

BƯỚC 1 — +LÝ DO:
  "Technology is useful because it saves time."
  "...since / as [reason]"

BƯỚC 2 — +KẾT QUẢ:
  "...so people learn faster."
  "..., which means that / therefore / as a result, ..."

BƯỚC 3 — +TƯƠNG PHẢN:
  "..., but / although / while / however, ..."

BƯỚC 4 — +RELATIVE CLAUSE:
  "Social media, which connects billions of people, ..."
  "...a problem that governments must address."

BƯỚC 5 — +ĐIỀU KIỆN:
  "...provided that / if / as long as / unless ..."

KẾT HỢP:
  "While [A — contrast], [B — main point],
  which [result], provided that [condition]."

MỤC TIÊU: Câu CHÍNH XÁC và CÓ CHIỀU SÂU — không phải câu DÀI.
```
