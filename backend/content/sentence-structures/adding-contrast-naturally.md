## Tại sao quan trọng

Trong IELTS Speaking Part 3, examiner thường hỏi những câu như "Do you think technology is always good?" hay "Should governments control the internet?" — những câu này không có câu trả lời đơn giản một chiều. Người đạt Band 7+ luôn đưa ra **cả hai phía** và **đặt chúng đối nhau**. Biết dùng contrast language là kỹ năng thiết yếu để câu trả lời nghe có chiều sâu chứ không đơn điệu.

---

## PHẦN 1: BUT — Tương phản trực tiếp, tự nhiên nhất

```
[Statement A], but [contrasting Statement B].
```

"But" là công cụ tương phản đơn giản và phổ biến nhất trong speaking. Dùng thoải mái ở mọi level.

### Dùng "but" để đưa ra hai phía:

- Technology has improved communication, **but** it has also reduced face-to-face interaction.
- The job pays well, **but** the working hours are extremely long.
- Living in a city is convenient, **but** it can feel very isolating.
- This policy benefits businesses, **but** ordinary workers often lose out.

### Mẫu one side + but + another side (speaking answer):

```
[Your main point], but [acknowledgement of the other side].
```

> Câu hỏi: "Do you think people spend too much time on their phones?"
> "I think phones can definitely be overused, **but** they also allow people to stay connected with family and friends, which is really valuable."

### "But" + cụm thay thế để tránh lặp:

Thay vì luôn dùng "but", xen kẽ với:
- **yet** *(formal but)*: The plan is expensive, **yet** many argue it is necessary.
- **still**: It's a long commute. She **still** prefers to live outside the city, though.
- **even so**: The risks are clear. **Even so**, many people choose to proceed.

## PHẦN 2: HOWEVER — Trang trọng hơn, nối hai câu

```
[Sentence A]. However, [contrasting Sentence B].
[Sentence A]; however, [contrasting Sentence B].
```

"However" **không phải liên từ** — nó không thể nối hai vế trong một câu theo kiểu "but". Nó đứng đầu một câu mới hoặc sau dấu chấm phẩy.

- Technology has improved communication. **However**, it has also led to increased social isolation.
- The government invested heavily in public transport. **However**, traffic congestion has continued to worsen.
- Many young people aspire to move abroad. **However**, few have the resources to do so.

### ❌ Lỗi phổ biến:

- ❌ "Technology is useful, **however** it can be addictive."
- ✅ "Technology is useful. **However**, it can be addictive."
- ✅ "Technology is useful; **however**, it can be addictive."
- ✅ "Technology is useful, **but** it can be addictive."

### Vị trí linh hoạt của "however":

"However" cũng có thể đứng giữa hoặc cuối câu để nhấn mạnh khác:
- "The solution is not, **however**, as simple as it might appear."
- "This approach has achieved some success. The results, **however**, are far from consistent."

## PHẦN 3: WHILE / WHEREAS — Đối chiếu trực tiếp hai phía

```
While [Side A], [Side B].
[Side A], while [Side B].
[Side A], whereas [Side B].
```

"While" và "whereas" dùng để **đặt hai thứ cạnh nhau** một cách tương phản trực tiếp — đặc biệt hữu ích khi so sánh hai nhóm người, hai quan điểm, hay hai tình huống.

- **While** urban areas benefit from better infrastructure, rural communities are often left behind.
- **While** some people thrive working from home, others struggle with the lack of social interaction.
- Younger generations tend to embrace new technology readily, **whereas** older people often find it more challenging.
- **While** exercise has clear physical benefits, its positive effects on mental health are equally significant.

### Phân biệt "while" tương phản vs "while" thời gian:

- "**While** I was studying, my phone rang." *(while = thời gian — cùng lúc)*
- "**While** studying has benefits, it can also be stressful." *(while = tương phản)*

Ngữ cảnh thường làm rõ nghĩa. Nếu muốn nhấn mạnh tương phản, "whereas" ít bị nhầm hơn.

## PHẦN 4: ON THE OTHER HAND — Công cụ cân bằng hai phía

```
On one hand, [Side A]. On the other hand, [Side B].
[Discussion of Side A]. On the other hand, [Side B].
```

"On the other hand" thường dùng cùng với "on one hand" để tạo cấu trúc cân bằng rõ ràng.

- **On one hand**, living in a big city offers unparalleled career opportunities. **On the other hand**, the cost of living and the daily stress can take a serious toll on quality of life.
- **On one hand**, social media connects people across the world. **On the other hand**, it can spread misinformation at an alarming speed.

### Dùng trong Speaking Part 3:

> Câu hỏi: "Do you think it's better for children to grow up in the city or the countryside?"
> "That's a tricky one. **On one hand**, cities have better schools and more opportunities. **On the other hand**, the countryside gives children space to play and a closer connection to nature. I think it really depends on the child and the family."

## PHẦN 5: MẪU ĐẦY ĐỦ — One side + contrast + another side

Đây là cấu trúc nền tảng cho IELTS Speaking Part 3:

### Mẫu A — Câu ngắn (Part 1):

```
[Main point], but [contrast].
```

> "I enjoy living in the city, **but** I do miss the peace and quiet of the countryside sometimes."

### Mẫu B — Câu phân tích (Part 3):

```
[Side A has merit/is true]. However, [Side B is also valid/important].
```

> "It's true that stricter laws can reduce crime. **However**, many experts argue that addressing the root causes — poverty, inequality, lack of education — is ultimately more effective than punitive measures alone."

### Mẫu C — Cân bằng có lập trường:

```
While [Side A is true/has advantages], [Side B ultimately outweighs it / is a more pressing concern].
```

> "**While** remote working does offer greater flexibility, I think most people still benefit from the structure and human connection that a physical workplace provides."

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Dùng "however" như liên từ (không có dấu chấm hoặc chấm phẩy)

- ❌ "The job is well-paid, however the hours are terrible."
- ✅ "The job is well-paid. **However**, the hours are terrible."
- ✅ "The job is well-paid, **but** the hours are terrible."

### Lỗi 2: Không có ý tương phản — chỉ liệt kê

- ❌ "Technology is good. It helps us communicate. It is also fast."
- ✅ "Technology is incredibly useful for communication and productivity. **However**, excessive screen time has been linked to anxiety and sleep problems, which is a growing concern."

### Lỗi 3: Lặp cùng một từ tương phản

- ❌ "...but... but... but..."
- ✅ Xen kẽ: but → however → while → on the other hand → yet

### Lỗi 4: Contrast quá nhẹ, không thực sự đối lập

- ❌ "Cats are cute, but dogs are also cute."
- ✅ "Cats are generally more independent, **whereas** dogs require more attention and regular exercise."

## Bài tập luyện

### Nối hai câu bằng từ tương phản phù hợp (but / however / while / whereas / on the other hand):

1. "City life is exciting." + "It is also very stressful." → ___
2. "Some students prefer group work." + "Others learn better alone." → ___
3. "The new policy creates jobs." + "It has a negative impact on the environment." → ___
4. "Young people are comfortable with technology." + "Elderly people often struggle." → ___

### Hoàn thành câu trả lời Part 3:

5. Q: "Do you think social media has more advantages or disadvantages?"
   "I think social media has some real benefits — it connects people and spreads information quickly. ___"

### Đáp án gợi ý

1. "City life is exciting, **but** it is also very stressful." / "City life is exciting. **However**, it is also very stressful."
2. "**While** some students prefer group work, others learn better alone." / "Some students prefer group work, **whereas** others learn better alone."
3. "The new policy creates jobs. **However**, it has a negative impact on the environment."
4. "**While** young people are comfortable with technology, elderly people often struggle." / "Young people are generally comfortable with technology, **whereas** elderly people often find it more challenging."
5. "...But **on the other hand**, it can also spread misinformation very quickly, and the pressure to present a perfect life online is having a real impact on young people's mental health."

## Tóm tắt nhanh

```
BUT → nối hai vế trong một câu (informal/neutral, speaking-friendly):
  Technology is useful, but it can be distracting.

HOWEVER → nối hai câu riêng biệt (formal):
  It is useful. However, it can be distracting.
  → KHÔNG viết: "..., however ..."

WHILE / WHEREAS → đối chiếu trực tiếp hai phía:
  While city life is exciting, it can be stressful.
  City life is exciting, whereas rural life is peaceful.

ON THE OTHER HAND → cân bằng hai phía rõ ràng:
  On one hand, [A]. On the other hand, [B].

MẪU SPEAKING:
  [Main point], but [contrast].
  [Side A is true]. However, [Side B is also important].
  While [A], [B] is ultimately more significant.
```
