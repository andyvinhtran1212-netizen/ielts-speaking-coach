## Tóm tắt

Mệnh đề quan hệ (Relative Clause) là một mệnh đề phụ đứng ngay sau danh từ để **bổ sung thông tin** về danh từ đó. Nó được mở đầu bởi **relative pronoun** (who, which, that, whose, whom) hoặc **relative adverb** (where, when, why).

- The student **who won the prize** is from my class. (mô tả "student" nào)
- The book **that I borrowed** was very interesting. (mô tả cuốn sách nào)
- This is the city **where I was born**. (mô tả thành phố)

## Tại sao quan trọng

Mệnh đề quan hệ cho phép kết hợp hai câu ngắn thành một câu phức, tạo ra văn phong học thuật và tự nhiên hơn. Trong IELTS Writing và Speaking, thiếu cấu trúc này sẽ giới hạn điểm Grammatical Range.

## Relative Pronouns và Relative Adverbs

| Từ | Thay thế cho | Chức năng | Ví dụ |
|----|-------------|-----------|-------|
| **who** | người | chủ ngữ | The man **who** called you is my boss. |
| **whom** | người | tân ngữ (trang trọng) | The manager **whom** I met was helpful. |
| **which** | vật / ý | chủ ngữ hoặc tân ngữ | The car **which** I bought last year broke down. |
| **that** | người hoặc vật | chủ ngữ hoặc tân ngữ | The book **that** she recommended was amazing. |
| **whose** | sở hữu (người/vật) | | The student **whose** work I read was talented. |
| **where** | nơi chốn | | The city **where** I grew up has changed a lot. |
| **when** | thời gian | | I remember the day **when** we first met. |
| **why** | lý do | | That's the reason **why** I quit. |

### Who vs. Whom

- **Who** = chủ ngữ (subject) của mệnh đề quan hệ:
  - The teacher **who** taught me English retired last year. (who = chủ ngữ: "who taught me")
- **Whom** = tân ngữ (object) — trang trọng, hay bị thay bằng "who" trong giao tiếp:
  - The doctor **whom** I consulted recommended rest. (whom = tân ngữ: "I consulted whom")

### That vs. Which / Who

- **That** dùng trong mệnh đề quan hệ xác định (defining relative clause)
- **Which** dùng trong mệnh đề quan hệ không xác định (non-defining) hoặc thay thế "that"
- **That** KHÔNG dùng trong non-defining clause

## Loại 1: Defining Relative Clauses — Mệnh đề xác định

Cung cấp thông tin **thiết yếu** để xác định danh từ — **không có dấu phẩy**.

Nếu bỏ mệnh đề này, câu mất nghĩa hoặc không rõ đề cập đến ai/cái gì.

- The woman **who lives next door** is a nurse. (xác định người phụ nữ nào)
- I want to buy the car **that/which is in the window**. (xác định chiếc xe nào)
- Children **who eat well** tend to be healthier. (xác định nhóm trẻ em nào)

**Có thể bỏ relative pronoun** khi nó là tân ngữ của mệnh đề quan hệ:

- The book **(that/which)** I read was fascinating. (that/which = tân ngữ → có thể bỏ)
- The man **(who/that)** she married is very kind. (who/that = tân ngữ → có thể bỏ)
- The car **(that)** I bought broke down. ✅

**Không thể bỏ** khi relative pronoun là **chủ ngữ**:
- The book **that** is on the table is mine. ✅ (that = chủ ngữ, không bỏ được)

## Loại 2: Non-defining Relative Clauses — Mệnh đề không xác định

Cung cấp thông tin **bổ sung** (có thể bỏ mà không ảnh hưởng đến nghĩa chính) — **có dấu phẩy cả hai đầu**.

- My sister, **who lives in London**, is visiting next week. (đã biết tôi chỉ có một chị → thông tin thêm)
- The Eiffel Tower, **which was built in 1889**, is one of the most visited monuments in the world.
- Last summer, **when I was in Paris**, I visited several museums.

**Luật quan trọng:**
- KHÔNG dùng "that" trong non-defining clause
- KHÔNG bỏ relative pronoun trong non-defining clause
- Luôn có dấu phẩy

❌ My sister, that lives in London, is visiting. (sai — dùng "that" trong non-defining)
✅ My sister, **who** lives in London, is visiting.

## Whose — sở hữu

"Whose" thay thế cho 's hoặc his/her/its:

- I know a student. **His** work won first prize. → I know a student **whose work won first prize**.
- The company **whose products I use** has just launched a new app.
- She lives in a house **whose roof is painted red**.

## Where, When, Why

- **Where** = in which / at which (nơi chốn):
  - This is the school **where** I studied for ten years.
  - This is the school **in which** I studied. (trang trọng hơn)

- **When** = at which / during which (thời gian):
  - I remember the summer **when** we travelled across Europe.
  - There are times **when** I feel overwhelmed.

- **Why** = for which (lý do — chỉ dùng sau "reason"):
  - That's the reason **why** she left the company.
  - That's the reason **(why/that/∅)** she left.

## Rút gọn mệnh đề quan hệ (Reduced Relative Clauses)

Khi relative pronoun là chủ ngữ, có thể rút gọn bằng cách:

**→ V-ing** (khi mệnh đề ở thể chủ động):
- The man **who is standing** at the door is my father.
- → The man **standing** at the door is my father.

- Students **who want** to improve should practise daily.
- → Students **wanting** to improve should practise daily.

**→ V3 (past participle)** (khi mệnh đề ở thể bị động):
- The letter **that was written** in French was hard to read.
- → The letter **written** in French was hard to read.

- The report **which was published** last year has been updated.
- → The report **published** last year has been updated.

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Dùng "that" trong non-defining clause

- ❌ My brother, **that** is a doctor, lives in Hanoi.
- ✅ My brother, **who** is a doctor, lives in Hanoi.

### Lỗi 2: Dùng lại đại từ sau relative pronoun (double pronoun)

- ❌ The student who **he** won the prize is from my class.
- ✅ The student **who** won the prize is from my class. (bỏ "he")
- ❌ The book which I read **it** was amazing.
- ✅ The book **which** I read was amazing. (bỏ "it")

### Lỗi 3: Dùng "which" thay vì "whose"

- ❌ The company **which** products are famous worldwide...
- ✅ The company **whose** products are famous worldwide...

### Lỗi 4: Quên dấu phẩy trong non-defining clause

- ❌ My mother who is 60 years old still works full time.
- ✅ My mother, **who is 60 years old**, still works full time.

### Lỗi 5: Dùng "which" để thay toàn bộ mệnh đề (Sentence relative clause)

Có thể dùng "which" để bình luận về toàn bộ ý của mệnh đề trước — nhưng chỉ trong non-defining:

- She passed all her exams, **which** made her parents very proud.
- He refused to apologise, **which** surprised everyone.

✅ Cấu trúc này rất hữu ích cho IELTS Writing.

## Ứng dụng trong IELTS

### Writing Task 2 — thêm thông tin về chủ đề:

- Young people **who grow up in cities** often have more opportunities than those **who live in rural areas**.
- Technology, **which has transformed every aspect of modern life**, brings both benefits and challenges.
- The country **whose economy grew fastest** last decade is now facing new challenges.

### Writing Task 1 — mô tả dữ liệu:

- The year **when** sales reached their peak was 2019.
- Countries **where** literacy rates are high tend to have stronger economies.

### Speaking — mô tả người và nơi:

- She's the kind of person **who** always makes you feel welcome.
- It's a place **where** you can completely relax and forget about work.
- I remember a time **when** we had no internet and had to look everything up in books.

## Bài tập luyện

### Nối hai câu bằng mệnh đề quan hệ

1. The woman works at the airport. She helped us find our gate.
2. I live in a small town. The town has a beautiful old castle.
3. I have a friend. Her father is a famous artist.
4. 1990 was the year. I was born that year.
5. The report was very useful. I read the report yesterday.

### Sửa lỗi

1. My car, that I bought last year, has already broken down.
2. The student who she won the prize is from Hanoi.
3. This is the city which I grew up in it.
4. He's someone which I really admire.
5. The man, whose lives next door, is a doctor.

### Đáp án

**Nối câu:**
1. The woman **who helped us find our gate** works at the airport.
2. I live in a small town **that/which has a beautiful old castle**.
3. I have a friend **whose father is a famous artist**.
4. 1990 was the year **when I was born** / **in which I was born**.
5. The report **(that/which) I read yesterday** was very useful.

**Sửa lỗi:**
1. My car, **which** I bought last year, has already broken down. (non-defining → which, không phải that)
2. The student **who** won the prize is from Hanoi. (bỏ "she")
3. This is the city **where I grew up** / **in which I grew up**. (bỏ "in it")
4. He's someone **who/whom** I really admire. (người → who/whom, không phải which)
5. The man **who** lives next door is a doctor. (non-defining sai: nếu đây là mệnh đề xác định → không dấu phẩy; nếu non-defining → The man next door, who is a doctor, ...)

## Tóm tắt nhanh

**Relative pronouns:**
- **who** → người (chủ ngữ)
- **whom** → người (tân ngữ, trang trọng)
- **which** → vật
- **that** → người hoặc vật (chỉ dùng trong defining clause)
- **whose** → sở hữu

**Relative adverbs:**
- **where** → nơi chốn | **when** → thời gian | **why** → lý do (sau "reason")

**Hai loại:**

| | Defining | Non-defining |
|--|----------|--------------|
| Dấu phẩy | Không | Có |
| "that" | Được | Không được |
| Bỏ được không | Bỏ được (nếu là object) | Không bỏ được |
| Ý nghĩa | Thiết yếu | Bổ sung |

**Rút gọn:** relative pronoun (subject) + be → bỏ hết; hoặc dùng V-ing / V3
