## Tóm tắt

**Complex sentence** = một **independent clause** (mệnh đề chính) + ít nhất một **dependent clause** (mệnh đề phụ), nối bằng **subordinating conjunction** (*because, although, when, if, since, unless...*) hoặc **relative pronoun** (*who, which, that, where*).

## Tại sao quan trọng

Complex sentences là cấu trúc **bắt buộc** cho điểm cao trong IELTS:

| IELTS Band | Đặc điểm cấu trúc câu |
|-----------|----------------------|
| Band 5 | Chủ yếu simple sentences, một số compound |
| Band 6 | Dùng được compound sentences, bắt đầu có complex |
| Band 7 | Dùng thành thạo complex sentences với variety |
| Band 8–9 | Complex sentences phong phú, linh hoạt, ít lỗi |

Theo Band Descriptors của IELTS, Band 7 yêu cầu: *"uses a variety of complex structures"*. Không có complex sentences = không thể đạt Band 7.

Trong IELTS Writing Task 2:
- ❌ Band 5: "There are many benefits of technology. Technology helps communication. It also helps education."
- ✅ Band 7: "**Although technology has transformed communication**, its impact on education **—especially in areas where access is limited—** is still debated by researchers **who emphasise the importance** of equal opportunity."

## Định nghĩa

### Independent Clause (Main Clause)
Mệnh đề có thể đứng độc lập thành câu hoàn chỉnh:
- *The economy grew.*
- *Students work hard.*
- *Governments should invest in education.*

### Dependent Clause (Subordinate Clause)
Mệnh đề có S + V nhưng **không đứng độc lập** được vì bắt đầu bằng từ nối phụ:
- *because the economy grew* → cần câu chính
- *although students work hard* → cần câu chính
- *who invented the telephone* → mô tả noun, cần câu chính

### Complex Sentence
**[Independent Clause] + [Dependent Clause]**
hoặc
**[Dependent Clause] + [Independent Clause]**

(Khi dependent clause đứng đầu câu, dùng dấu phẩy sau nó)

## Cấu trúc

### Loại 1: Adverb Clause (Mệnh đề trạng ngữ)

Bắt đầu bằng **subordinating conjunction**, mô tả điều kiện, thời gian, lý do, mục đích, kết quả, nhượng bộ...

**Vị trí:**
- Cuối câu: không cần dấu phẩy
  - *She succeeded **because she worked hard**.*
- Đầu câu: dùng dấu phẩy sau dependent clause
  - ***Because she worked hard**, she succeeded.*

| Loại | Subordinating Conjunctions | Ví dụ |
|------|--------------------------|-------|
| **Lý do (Reason)** | because, since, as | *She left **because she was tired**.* |
| **Nhượng bộ (Concession)** | although, though, even though, while, whereas | ***Although it was raining**, they continued.* |
| **Điều kiện (Condition)** | if, unless, provided that, as long as, on condition that | ***If governments invest**, outcomes improve.* |
| **Thời gian (Time)** | when, while, after, before, until, as soon as, once, whenever | ***When she arrived**, the meeting had started.* |
| **Mục đích (Purpose)** | so that, in order that | *She studied **so that she could improve**.* |
| **Kết quả (Result)** | so...that, such...that | *It was **so hot that** no one went outside.* |
| **Tương phản (Contrast)** | whereas, while | ***Whereas cities expand**, rural areas shrink.* |

### Loại 2: Relative Clause (Mệnh đề quan hệ)

Bắt đầu bằng **relative pronoun** (*who, whom, which, that, whose, where, when*), mô tả một danh từ trong câu.

| Relative Pronoun | Dùng cho | Ví dụ |
|-----------------|---------|-------|
| **who / whom** | Người (who = subject, whom = object) | *The student **who studied hardest** passed.* |
| **which** | Vật, ý tưởng | *The book **which I borrowed** was excellent.* |
| **that** | Người hoặc vật (restrictive) | *The house **that he built** is still standing.* |
| **whose** | Sở hữu (người hoặc vật) | *The student **whose work was outstanding** won.* |
| **where** | Nơi chốn | *The city **where I grew up** has changed.* |
| **when** | Thời gian | *The year **when she graduated** was 2020.* |

**Hai loại relative clause:**

**Restrictive (Defining):** Cần thiết để xác định danh từ — **không có dấu phẩy**
- *The students **who study regularly** tend to do well.* (không phải tất cả sinh viên — chỉ những em học đều)

**Non-restrictive (Non-defining):** Thêm thông tin, không cần thiết — **có dấu phẩy**
- *My sister, **who lives in London**, is a doctor.* (ta đã biết ai là chị gái — thêm thông tin phụ)

Lưu ý: *that* **không dùng** trong non-restrictive clause — phải dùng *which* hoặc *who*.
- ❌ *My car, **that** I bought last year, is already broken.*
- ✅ *My car, **which** I bought last year, is already broken.*

### Loại 3: Noun Clause (Mệnh đề danh từ)

Mệnh đề đóng vai trò như một danh từ trong câu — làm chủ ngữ, tân ngữ, hoặc bổ ngữ.

| Vị trí | Từ nối | Ví dụ |
|--------|--------|-------|
| **Chủ ngữ** | What, That, Whether | ***What matters most** is consistent effort.* |
| **Tân ngữ** | that, what, whether, how, why, when | *I believe **that education is a right**.* |
| **Sau linking verb** | that, what | *The problem is **that funding is limited**.* |

## Cách dùng phổ biến

### 1. Adverb clauses — Thể hiện lập luận logic

**Diễn đạt nguyên nhân (Band 7+):**
- *Since many young people lack financial literacy, banks should offer educational programs.* (since = because, formal hơn)
- *As the population continues to age, healthcare systems face increasing pressure.*

**Diễn đạt nhượng bộ — công nhận ý đối lập (Band 7 signature):**
- *Although social media has numerous benefits, its negative effects on mental health cannot be ignored.*
- *Even though technology creates jobs, it also displaces many workers.*
- *While some argue that globalization benefits all nations, evidence suggests otherwise.*

**Diễn đạt điều kiện — lập luận có điều kiện:**
- *Unless governments take immediate action, climate change will become irreversible.*
- *Provided that adequate funding is available, the project could be highly successful.*

### 2. Relative clauses — Mô tả và hạn định danh từ

**Thêm thông tin về người/vật:**
- *Countries **that invest heavily in education** tend to have stronger economies.*
- *The researchers **whose findings challenged the consensus** faced significant scrutiny.*

**Non-restrictive — thêm chi tiết không thiết yếu:**
- *Finland, **which consistently tops international education rankings**, invests significantly in teacher training.*

### 3. Noun clauses — Diễn đạt ý kiến, niềm tin

Đặc biệt hữu ích trong IELTS Writing khi nêu quan điểm:
- *I believe **that stricter regulations are necessary** to address this issue.*
- ***What concerns me most** is the long-term economic impact.*
- *It is widely accepted **that education is the foundation of development**.*
- *The question is **whether governments should intervene** in the market.*

### 4. Phối hợp trong một câu

Complex sentences có thể chứa nhiều dependent clauses:
- ***Although the government introduced reforms that aimed to reduce inequality**, many economists argue **that the measures were insufficient**.*
  - *Although... reforms* → Adverb clause (nhượng bộ)
  - *that aimed to reduce inequality* → Relative clause
  - *that the measures were insufficient* → Noun clause

## Dấu hiệu nhận biết

### Nhận ra Dependent Clause → Complex Sentence

| Loại | Từ mở đầu |
|------|-----------|
| **Adverb clause** | *because, since, as, although, though, even though, while, whereas, if, unless, when, whenever, before, after, until, once, so that, provided that, as long as* |
| **Relative clause** | *who, whom, which, that, whose, where, when* (sau danh từ) |
| **Noun clause** | *that, what, whether, if, how, why, when, where* (làm subject/object) |

**Quy tắc nhanh:** Nếu thấy một trong các từ trên và sau nó là S + V → đó là dependent clause → câu chứa nó là complex sentence.

## Ví dụ

### Phân tích câu thực tế

**Câu 1:**
> *"Although governments have made considerable efforts to reduce carbon emissions, scientists warn that climate change is accelerating at an unprecedented rate."*

- *"Although governments have made considerable efforts to reduce carbon emissions"* → Dependent adverb clause (nhượng bộ, bắt đầu bằng *although*)
- *"scientists warn"* → Independent clause chính
- *"that climate change is accelerating at an unprecedented rate"* → Dependent noun clause (tân ngữ của *warn*)
- → **Complex sentence** với 2 dependent clauses

**Câu 2:**
> *"The cities that experience the fastest economic growth are often those where inequality is most severe."*

- *"The cities...are often those"* → Independent clause
- *"that experience the fastest economic growth"* → Relative clause (restrictive, mô tả *cities*)
- *"where inequality is most severe"* → Relative clause (mô tả *those*)
- → **Complex sentence** với 2 relative clauses

### Ví dụ IELTS Writing Task 2

**Band 5 (simple + basic compound):**
> "Social media has benefits. It connects people. But it also has problems. Young people spend too much time on it."

**Band 7 (complex sentences):**
> "**Although social media has revolutionised the way people communicate**, enabling individuals **who live in different countries** to maintain close relationships, there is growing concern **that excessive use is damaging young people's mental health**. **Since the brain is still developing during adolescence**, exposure to carefully curated online images — **which often present an unrealistic ideal** — can lead to anxiety and low self-esteem."

Phân tích câu 1:
- *Although...communicate* → Adverb clause (nhượng bộ)
- *who live in different countries* → Relative clause
- *that excessive use is damaging...* → Noun clause

Phân tích câu 2:
- *Since the brain is still developing...* → Adverb clause (lý do)
- *which often present an unrealistic ideal* → Relative clause (non-restrictive, non-essential info)

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Sentence fragment — dùng dependent clause như câu đơn

- ❌ SAI: *Although technology has many benefits.* (dependent clause đứng một mình)
- ✅ ĐÚNG: *Although technology has many benefits, it also poses risks.*
- **TẠI SAO:** Dependent clause BẮT BUỘC có mệnh đề chính đi kèm. Đây là lỗi nghiêm trọng nhất trong IELTS Writing.

### Lỗi 2: Dùng sai dấu phẩy với relative clause

- ❌ SAI: *The students, who study hard, tend to succeed.* (nếu muốn nói về tất cả sinh viên nói chung — restrictive)
- ✅ ĐÚNG: *The students who study hard tend to succeed.* (restrictive — chỉ những em học chăm)
- ❌ SAI: *My brother who lives in Hanoi is a teacher.* (nếu chỉ có một người anh — non-restrictive)
- ✅ ĐÚNG: *My brother, who lives in Hanoi, is a teacher.* (non-restrictive — thêm thông tin về người đã biết)
- **TẠI SAO:** Dấu phẩy thay đổi nghĩa của relative clause.

### Lỗi 3: Dùng "that" trong non-restrictive relative clause

- ❌ SAI: *The Eiffel Tower, **that** was built in 1889, is in Paris.*
- ✅ ĐÚNG: *The Eiffel Tower, **which** was built in 1889, is in Paris.*
- **TẠI SAO:** *that* không dùng trong non-restrictive (non-defining) relative clause.

### Lỗi 4: Dùng sai subordinating conjunction

- ❌ SAI: *Despite she worked hard, she failed.* (*despite* là preposition, không phải conjunction)
- ✅ ĐÚNG: *Although she worked hard, she failed.*
- ✅ ĐÚNG: *Despite working hard, she failed.* (*despite + gerund/noun*)
- ❌ SAI: *Because of the economy is growing...* (*because of* + noun, không phải clause)
- ✅ ĐÚNG: *Because the economy is growing...* (*because* + clause)
- ✅ ĐÚNG: *Because of economic growth...* (*because of* + noun phrase)
- **TẠI SAO:** Phân biệt *because* (conjunction + S + V) vs *because of* (preposition + noun/gerund); *although* (conjunction) vs *despite/in spite of* (preposition).

### Lỗi 5: "Although... but..." — dùng hai liên từ đối lập

- ❌ SAI: *Although she worked hard, **but** she failed.*
- ✅ ĐÚNG: *Although she worked hard, she failed.*
- ✅ ĐÚNG: *She worked hard, **but** she failed.*
- **TẠI SAO:** *although* và *but* đều diễn đạt tương phản — dùng cả hai là thừa và sai ngữ pháp. Chọn một.

### Lỗi 6: Thiếu dấu phẩy khi dependent clause đứng đầu

- ❌ SAI: *Although the economy grew inequality remained high.*
- ✅ ĐÚNG: *Although the economy grew**,** inequality remained high.*
- **TẠI SAO:** Khi dependent clause đứng trước independent clause, bắt buộc có dấu phẩy ngăn cách.

## So sánh với

| | Simple | Compound | Complex |
|--|--------|----------|---------|
| **Clauses** | 1 independent | 2+ independent | 1 independent + 1+ dependent |
| **Liên từ** | Không | FANBOYS | Subordinating conj. / relative pronoun |
| **Ví dụ** | *She works.* | *She works hard, so she succeeds.* | *Because she works hard, she succeeds.* |
| **Hai vế bình đẳng?** | — | Có | Không (dependent phụ thuộc main) |
| **Emphasizes** | Một ý | Hai ý bình đẳng | Một ý chính + ý phụ/bổ trợ |
| **IELTS target** | Band 5 | Band 6 | Band 7+ |

**Khi nào dùng compound vs. complex:**
- **Compound** (*She studied hard, **so** she passed*): Hai ý ngang nhau, bình đẳng về tầm quan trọng
- **Complex** (***Because** she studied hard, she passed*): Một ý chính (kết quả), một ý phụ (lý do); hoặc khi muốn nhấn mạnh mối quan hệ cụ thể (nhượng bộ, điều kiện, thời gian...)

## Lưu ý cho người học Việt Nam

Tiếng Việt có cấu trúc tương tự complex sentence — "Vì... nên...", "Mặc dù... nhưng...", "Khi... thì...". Tuy nhiên có hai điểm khác biệt quan trọng:

**1. "Although... but..." trong tiếng Anh là SAI:**
- Tiếng Việt: "Mặc dù cô ấy học chăm, **nhưng** cô ấy không đỗ." → Tự nhiên hoàn toàn
- Tiếng Anh: "Although she studied hard, she didn't pass." → Không có *but*
- Lỗi này rất phổ biến ở người học Việt.

**2. Despite vs. Although:**
- *despite / in spite of* + **noun / gerund**
- *although / though / even though* + **S + V**

| Tiếng Việt | Tiếng Anh (đúng) |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Mặc dù có nhiều thách thức... | *Despite the many challenges...* (noun) |
| Mặc dù có nhiều thách thức... | *Although there are many challenges...* (clause) |
| ❌ Mặc dù có nhiều thách thức... | *Despite there are many challenges...* (SAI) |

**Mẹo:** Nếu sau "mặc dù" là chủ ngữ + động từ → dùng *although*. Nếu là danh từ/cụm danh từ → dùng *despite/in spite of*.

## Ứng dụng trong IELTS

### IELTS Speaking — Complex sentences tạo độ phức tạp ngữ pháp

Trong Speaking Part 3, các examiner tìm kiếm:
- *"I think **that** access to quality education should be considered a basic human right, **which** means governments have a responsibility to fund it."*
- *"**Although I understand why** some people prefer online learning, I personally believe **that** face-to-face interaction is irreplaceable."*
- *"**Since technology is advancing so rapidly**, it's difficult to predict **what** jobs will exist in 20 years."*

### IELTS Writing Task 2 — Building a Band 7 paragraph

**Cấu trúc một luận điểm Band 7:**
1. **Topic sentence** (có thể simple hoặc compound)
2. **Explanation** (complex sentence với *because/since/as*)
3. **Concession** (complex sentence với *although/while/even though*)
4. **Example + elaboration** (relative clause để thêm chi tiết)

Ví dụ:
> "*(1) Remote work has fundamentally altered the modern workplace. (2) **Since employees no longer need to commute**, they gain additional hours **that can be used for personal development or family time**. (3) **While some workers thrive in home environments**, others struggle with the isolation **that remote work can create**. (4) Countries **where flexible work arrangements were common before the pandemic**, such as the Netherlands, have reported higher levels of employee satisfaction **than those where rigid office cultures persist**."*

## From simple to complex (worked examples)

## PHẦN 1: BẮT ĐẦU — CÂU ĐƠN

Câu đơn có một chủ ngữ và một động từ:

```
[Subject] + [Verb] + [Object/Complement].
```

- "Technology **is** useful."
- "Cities **are** growing."
- "Students **use** social media."
- "Exercise **reduces** stress."

Không sai, nhưng quá đơn giản. Hãy nâng cấp từng bước.

---

## PHẦN 2: BƯỚC 1 — THÊM LÝ DO

```
[Simple sentence] because / since / as [reason].
```

### Transformation:

| Câu đơn | + Lý do |
|---------|---------|
| "Technology is useful." | "Technology is useful **because** it allows people to access information instantly." |
| "Cities are growing." | "Cities are growing **as** more people migrate from rural areas in search of better opportunities." |
| "Exercise reduces stress." | "Exercise reduces stress **since** it triggers the release of endorphins in the brain." |

> "**Because**" = lý do cụ thể, trực tiếp
> "**Since**" / "**As**" = lý do được xem là đã biết (slightly softer, academic)

**Lưu ý:** "Because" mạnh nhất trong ba từ — dùng khi lý do là trọng tâm của ý bạn muốn nói.

---

## PHẦN 3: BƯỚC 2 — THÊM KẾT QUẢ

```
[Simple sentence], so [result].
[Simple sentence]. Therefore, / As a result, / Consequently, [result].
[Simple sentence], which means (that) [result].
```

### Transformation:

| Câu đơn + lý do | + Kết quả |
|----------------|---------|
| "Technology allows instant access to information." | "...**so** people can now learn almost anything without leaving home." |
| "More people are moving to cities." | "...**which means that** urban infrastructure is under increasing strain." |
| "Exercise releases endorphins." | "**As a result**, regular physical activity can significantly improve mental wellbeing." |

### Before/after (đầy đủ):

- **Before:** "Exercise is good. It makes you feel better."
- **After:** "Regular exercise **releases endorphins**, **which means that** it can significantly improve mood and reduce feelings of anxiety — **particularly** for individuals under chronic stress."

---

## PHẦN 4: BƯỚC 3 — THÊM TƯƠNG PHẢN

```
[Main point], but [contrast].
Although [contrast], [main point].
While [side A], [side B].
[Main point]. However, [contrast].
```

### Transformation:

| Câu không có tương phản | + Tương phản |
|------------------------|------------|
| "Technology is useful." | "Technology is useful, **but** it can also be a source of distraction and dependency." |
| "Cities offer opportunities." | "**While** cities offer greater career opportunities, they also come with higher living costs and significant social pressures." |
| "Social media connects people." | "Social media **can connect** people across vast distances. **However**, it has also been linked to rising rates of loneliness and anxiety, particularly among teenagers." |

---

## PHẦN 5: BƯỚC 4 — THÊM RELATIVE CLAUSE

```
[Noun] + which/that [adds information about noun].
[Noun] + who [adds information about a person].
[Place] + where [adds information about location].
[Time] + when [adds information about a time].
```

Relative clause giúp **bổ sung thông tin** về danh từ ngay bên trong câu — không cần tách ra thành câu mới.

### Transformation:

| Câu không có relative clause | + Relative clause |
|-----------------------------|-----------------|
| "Social media is popular." | "Social media, **which has transformed how billions of people communicate**, is now a dominant force in public discourse." |
| "Cities attract young people." | "Cities attract young people **who are looking for career opportunities and a more dynamic social environment**." |
| "The pandemic changed work culture." | "The pandemic, **which forced millions to work from home**, fundamentally altered expectations around workplace flexibility." |

### Thêm relative clause để thay thế câu thứ hai:

- **Before:** "Many countries face housing shortages. This creates social inequality."
- **After:** "Many countries face housing shortages, **which** contribute directly to **rising social inequality** and reduced social mobility."

---

## PHẦN 6: BƯỚC 5 — THÊM ĐIỀU KIỆN

```
[Main clause] if / unless / as long as / provided that [condition].
If [condition], [main clause].
```

Điều kiện thêm **sắc thái** và **tính chính xác** vào lập luận.

### Transformation:

| Câu không có điều kiện | + Điều kiện |
|-----------------------|-----------|
| "Technology improves productivity." | "Technology **can improve** productivity significantly, **provided that** employees receive adequate training and the right tools." |
| "Remote work is beneficial." | "Remote work **can be** highly beneficial, **as long as** clear communication structures are maintained." |
| "Stricter laws will reduce crime." | "Stricter laws **may** reduce certain forms of crime, **if** they are enforced consistently and fairly." |

---

## PHẦN 7: KẾT HỢP — CÂU PHỨC HOÀN CHỈNH

Bây giờ, kết hợp nhiều bước để tạo ra một câu phức thực sự có chiều sâu.

### Transformation hoàn chỉnh — Example 1:

**Câu đơn gốc:** "Technology changes work."

**Bước 1 (+reason):** "Technology changes work **because** it automates many routine tasks."

**Bước 2 (+result):** "Technology automates many routine tasks, **which means that** workers must continually update their skills."

**Bước 3 (+contrast):** "**While** technology automates many routine tasks, **which reduces labour costs for companies**, it also demands that workers continually invest in upskilling."

**Bước 4 (+condition):** "**While** technology undoubtedly automates many routine tasks, **reducing costs for businesses**, its overall impact on employment **is likely to be positive**, **provided that** governments invest adequately in retraining programmes."

---

### Transformation hoàn chỉnh — Example 2:

**Câu đơn gốc:** "Cities are noisy."

**+Lý do:** "Cities are noisy **because** of the constant flow of traffic and large concentrations of people."

**+Kết quả:** "...noise pollution, **which** can disrupt sleep and contribute to chronic stress."

**+Tương phản:** "**While** urban environments are inevitably noisier than rural areas, **which** some residents find deeply disruptive, others argue that the energy and vibrancy of city life **more than compensates** for the drawback of noise."

**+Điều kiện:** "**While** urban noise levels **are unlikely to** decrease significantly in the near future, their health impacts **can be** mitigated **if** city planners prioritise green spaces, noise barriers, and pedestrianised zones in urban design."

---

### Transformation hoàn chỉnh — Example 3 (Speaking answer):

**Q: "Do you think social media is good for society?"**

**Band 5 answer:** "I think social media is good. It helps people communicate. But it also has problems."

**Band 7 answer:** "I think social media **has real benefits**, **particularly** in terms of enabling people to stay connected with friends and family across long distances. **However**, platforms **that** prioritise engagement over accuracy **have also contributed** to the spread of misinformation — **which is a problem that governments and tech companies are increasingly struggling to address**. On balance, I'd say the benefits outweigh the drawbacks, **as long as** users develop critical media literacy skills."

| Điều kiện hoặc nhượng bộ (nếu phù hợp) | □ |

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Câu quá dài và mất mạch

Câu phức không phải là câu dài vô tội vạ. Nếu một câu có hơn 3-4 mệnh đề phụ, hãy tách ra.

- ❌ "Technology is useful because it helps people communicate, which means they can work remotely, which reduces costs, which is why companies prefer it."
- ✅ Chia thành 2 câu: "Technology enables remote communication, significantly reducing operational costs for businesses. **As a result**, it has become an increasingly attractive option for employers worldwide."

### Lỗi 2: Thêm relative clause nhưng sai đại từ

- ❌ "The policy which it was introduced last year..."
- ✅ "The policy **which was introduced** last year..." / "The policy **that** was introduced last year..."

### Lỗi 3: Quá nhiều "because" trong một đoạn

- Đa dạng hóa: because → since → as → given that → due to the fact that

### Lỗi 4: "Although" + "but" trong cùng một câu

- ❌ "**Although** technology is useful, **but** it has drawbacks."
- ✅ "**Although** technology is useful, it has drawbacks." / "Technology is useful, **but** it has drawbacks."

## Bài tập luyện

### Nâng cấp các câu đơn sau qua từng bước:

1. "Exercise is important." → Thêm lý do → Thêm kết quả
2. "Young people use social media." → Thêm relative clause → Thêm tương phản
3. "Governments should act." → Thêm điều kiện → Thêm nhượng bộ

### Đáp án gợi ý:

1. "Exercise is important **because** it improves both physical and mental health. **As a result**, incorporating regular activity into daily routines **has been shown to** reduce the risk of numerous chronic diseases."

2. "Young people **who have grown up with social media** tend to use it as their primary source of news and social interaction. **However**, there are growing concerns about its impact on attention spans and the quality of real-world relationships."

3. "Governments **should** act decisively to address climate change, **provided that** such action is coordinated internationally and does not unfairly burden developing economies. **While** the short-term economic costs may be significant, the long-term consequences of inaction **would be** far more severe."

## Tóm tắt nhanh

```
TỪNG BƯỚC NÂNG CẤP CÂU:

BƯỚC 1 — +LÝ DO:
  "Technology is useful because it saves time."
  "...since / as [reason]"

BƯỚC 2 — +KẾT QUẢ:
  "...so people learn faster."
  "..., which means that / therefore / as a result, ..."

BƯỚC 3 — +TƯƠNG PHẢN:
  "..., but / although / while / however, ..."

BƯỚC 4 — +RELATIVE CLAUSE:
  "Social media, which connects billions of people, ..."
  "...a problem that governments must address."

BƯỚC 5 — +ĐIỀU KIỆN:
  "...provided that / if / as long as / unless ..."

KẾT HỢP:
  "While [A — contrast], [B — main point],
  which [result], provided that [condition]."

MỤC TIÊU: Câu CHÍNH XÁC và CÓ CHIỀU SÂU — không phải câu DÀI.
```

## PHẦN 1: AND / BUT — Nối thêm thông tin hoặc tương phản

```
[Sentence A] + and/but + [Sentence B without repeated subject]
```

### Before → After với "and":

| ❌ Trước | ✅ Sau |
|--------|------|
| "I study English. I also practice speaking." | "I study English **and** practice speaking regularly." |
| "She is kind. She is hardworking." | "She is kind **and** hardworking." |
| "He moved to the city. He found a job there." | "He moved to the city **and** found a job there." |

### Before → After với "but":

| ❌ Trước | ✅ Sau |
|--------|------|
| "The city is exciting. It is expensive." | "The city is exciting **but** expensive." |
| "I like coffee. I don't drink it at night." | "I like coffee **but** don't drink it at night." |
| "The plan was good. It didn't work." | "The plan was good **but** didn't work out." |

### Tránh lặp chủ ngữ khi dùng "and/but":

- ❌ "She studied hard. She passed the exam."
- ✅ "She studied hard **and** passed the exam." *(bỏ "she" thứ hai vì cùng chủ ngữ)*

---

## PHẦN 2: BECAUSE / SINCE / AS — Nối lý do

```
[Main idea] because/since/as [reason].
Because/Since/As [reason], [main idea].
```

### Before → After với "because":

| ❌ Trước | ✅ Sau |
|--------|------|
| "I take the bus. My office is far away." | "I take the bus **because** my office is quite far from home." |
| "She was nervous. It was her first interview." | "She was nervous **because** it was her first interview." |
| "He started exercising. He wanted to feel healthier." | "He started exercising **because** he wanted to feel healthier." |

### "Since" và "as" — thay thế "because" (trang trọng hơn một chút):

- "**Since** public transport is unreliable, most people prefer to drive."
- "**As** the weather was so beautiful, we decided to walk instead."
- "**Since** she had lived there for ten years, she knew the city very well."

> **Lưu ý:** "Since" cũng có nghĩa thời gian ("since 2010"). Ngữ cảnh thường làm rõ. Nếu muốn chắc chắn, dùng "because".

---

## PHẦN 3: WHO / WHICH / THAT — Relative Clauses

```
[Noun] + who/which/that + [additional information about that noun]
```

Relative clauses cho phép bạn **thêm thông tin về một người hoặc vật** ngay trong câu, thay vì tách thành câu riêng.

### Before → After với "who":

| ❌ Trước | ✅ Sau |
|--------|------|
| "I have a teacher. She inspires me a lot." | "I have a teacher **who** inspires me a lot." |
| "My friend lives in London. He works in finance." | "My friend, **who** lives in London, works in finance." |

### Before → After với "which":

| ❌ Trước | ✅ Sau |
|--------|------|
| "I read a book. It changed my perspective." | "I read a book **that** changed my perspective." |
| "The city has great public transport. It makes commuting easy." | "The city has great public transport, **which** makes commuting easy." |

### Before → After với "that":

- "I found an app **that** helps me practice vocabulary every day."
- "She chose a career **that** she is genuinely passionate about."
- "This is the kind of problem **that** requires a long-term solution."

> **Mẹo:** Dùng "who" cho người, "which" cho vật (non-defining), "that" cho người hoặc vật (defining — không có dấu phẩy).

---

## PHẦN 4: V-ING PHRASE — Nối hành động đồng thời hoặc nguyên nhân

```
[Main clause], + V-ing [additional action / reason].
V-ing [reason/context], [main clause].
```

Đây là cấu trúc nâng cao hơn nhưng rất tự nhiên và hiệu quả:

### Before → After:

| ❌ Trước | ✅ Sau |
|--------|------|
| "I walk to work. I listen to podcasts at the same time." | "I walk to work, **listening to podcasts** along the way." |
| "She sat by the window. She was reading a book." | "She sat by the window, **reading a book**." |
| "He grew up in a small town. He learned to appreciate nature." | "**Growing up in a small town**, he learned to appreciate nature." |
| "I was tired. I still finished the report." | "**Feeling tired**, I still managed to finish the report." |

> **Chú ý:** Chủ ngữ của V-ing phrase phải là **chủ ngữ của mệnh đề chính**. Nếu khác, câu sẽ sai (dangling modifier):
> - ❌ "**Walking home**, the rain started." *(mưa không thể "walking home")*
> - ✅ "**Walking home**, I got caught in the rain."

---

## PHẦN 5: WHERE / WHEN — Thêm bối cảnh địa điểm hoặc thời gian

```
[Noun] + where [location information]
[Noun/Time] + when [time/circumstance information]
```

### Before → After:

| ❌ Trước | ✅ Sau |
|--------|------|
| "I grew up in Hanoi. There were few tall buildings then." | "I grew up in Hanoi, **where** there were few tall buildings at the time." |
| "I remember a day. My father took me to the sea." | "I remember a day **when** my father took me to the sea for the first time." |
| "There's a café near my office. I go there every morning." | "There's a café near my office **where** I go every morning." |

---

## PHẦN 6: BEFORE / AFTER / WHILE / WHEN — Nối sự kiện theo thứ tự thời gian

```
Before/After/When + [Sentence], [Main sentence].
[Main sentence] before/after/when + [Sentence].
```

### Before → After:

| ❌ Trước | ✅ Sau |
|--------|------|
| "I finished work. Then I went to the gym." | "**After** finishing work, I went to the gym." / "I went to the gym **after** finishing work." |
| "I was a student. I lived in a dormitory." | "**When** I was a student, I lived in a dormitory." |
| "She arrived home. Then she made dinner." | "**As soon as** she arrived home, she made dinner." |

---

## Áp dụng vào IELTS Speaking

### Part 2 — Kể về trải nghiệm (before/after rõ ràng):

**❌ Rời rạc:**
> "I visited Hội An. It was beautiful. There were many old buildings. I liked the lanterns. I took many photos."

**✅ Câu kết hợp tự nhiên:**
> "I visited Hội An last year, **which** is a small ancient town in central Vietnam **that** is famous for its colourful lanterns and well-preserved architecture. I was genuinely amazed by the old buildings **lining** the riverside, **where** I spent most of the afternoon **taking photos and exploring** the local streets."

### Part 3 — Phân tích có chiều sâu:

**❌ Rời rạc:**
> "People use phones a lot. It is a problem. They don't talk to each other."

**✅ Câu kết hợp:**
> "People use their phones so frequently **that** they often fail to engage with those around them, **which** is particularly noticeable in public spaces **where** people sit together without making any real conversation."

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Dùng "which" khi nên dùng "who"

- ❌ "She is a teacher **which** works at my school."
- ✅ "She is a teacher **who** works at my school."

### Lỗi 2: Nối quá nhiều ý với "and...and...and"

- ❌ "I went to the market **and** bought vegetables **and** came home **and** cooked dinner **and** watched TV."
- ✅ "I went to the market to buy vegetables, **then** came home to cook dinner **before** settling in for the evening."

### Lỗi 3: V-ing phrase với sai chủ ngữ

- ❌ "**Walking to school**, the weather suddenly changed."
- ✅ "**Walking to school**, I noticed the weather suddenly change."

### Lỗi 4: Lặp chủ ngữ khi không cần

- ❌ "She studied hard and **she** passed the exam."
- ✅ "She studied hard and passed the exam."

## Tóm tắt nhanh

```
AND / BUT → thêm thông tin hoặc tương phản (cùng chủ ngữ: bỏ "she/he/it" thứ hai):
  She studied and passed. | The city is exciting but expensive.

BECAUSE / SINCE / AS → giải thích lý do:
  I take the bus because my office is far away.

WHO / WHICH / THAT → thêm thông tin về người/vật:
  a teacher who inspires me | a book that changed my life
  great transport, which makes commuting easy

V-ING PHRASE → hành động đồng thời hoặc lý do:
  Walking to work, I listen to podcasts.
  Growing up in the countryside, she learned to love nature.
  → Chủ ngữ V-ing PHẢI là chủ ngữ của mệnh đề chính

WHERE / WHEN → bối cảnh địa điểm/thời gian:
  a café where I go every morning
  a day when everything changed

BEFORE / AFTER / WHILE:
  After finishing work, I went to the gym.
  When I was a student, I lived in a dormitory.
```

## Bài tập luyện

### Nối bằng cách phù hợp

1. "She failed the exam." + "She studied very hard." (nhượng bộ — *although*)
2. "The city lacks clean water." + "The government must invest in infrastructure." (kết quả — *therefore*)
3. "He is the researcher." + "His work changed our understanding of climate change." (relative clause — *whose*)
4. "Technology creates jobs." + "It eliminates many traditional ones." (tương phản — *while*)
5. "We act now." + "The effects will be irreversible." (điều kiện — *unless*)

### Sửa lỗi

1. "Although she worked hard, but she didn't succeed."
2. "Despite she was tired, she continued working."
3. "The researchers, that discovered the vaccine, received a Nobel Prize."
4. "Because of the economy is growing, more jobs are available."
5. "When he arrived at the station. The train had already left."

### Đáp án

**Nối câu:**
1. *Although she studied very hard, she failed the exam.*
2. *The city lacks clean water; therefore, the government must invest in infrastructure.*
3. *He is the researcher whose work changed our understanding of climate change.*
4. *While technology creates jobs, it also eliminates many traditional ones.*
5. *Unless we act now, the effects will be irreversible.*

**Sửa lỗi:**
1. → *Although she worked hard, she didn't succeed.* (bỏ *but* — không dùng although + but cùng lúc)
2. → *Although she was tired, she continued working.* (*despite* + noun/gerund; *although* + clause)
3. → *The researchers **who** discovered the vaccine received a Nobel Prize.* (non-restrictive: *The researchers, **who** discovered...*; restrictive: không dấu phẩy — *that* có thể dùng nếu restrictive)
4. → *Because the economy is growing, more jobs are available.* (*because of* + noun; *because* + S + V)
5. → *When he arrived at the station, the train had already left.* (dependent clause không đứng một mình)

## Tóm tắt nhanh
1. **Complex sentence** = 1 independent clause + 1+ dependent clause
2. Ba loại dependent clause: **adverb clause** (*because, although, if, when...*), **relative clause** (*who, which, that*), **noun clause** (*that, what, whether*)
3. Dependent clause đứng đầu câu → **dấu phẩy** sau nó
4. Relative clause **restrictive** (xác định) → không dấu phẩy; **non-restrictive** (thêm thông tin) → có dấu phẩy
5. Lỗi phổ biến: *although...but* (chọn một), *despite + clause* (sai — dùng *despite + noun/gerund*), dependent clause đứng một mình
6. Complex sentences là **yêu cầu bắt buộc** cho IELTS Band 7+ (Grammatical Range & Accuracy)
