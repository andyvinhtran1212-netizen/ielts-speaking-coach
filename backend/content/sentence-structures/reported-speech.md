## Tóm tắt

Reported Speech thuật lại lời nói mà **không trích dẫn trực tiếp**, thường qua động từ tường thuật như: *say, tell, explain, claim, argue, suggest, ask, warn, advise...*

| Direct Speech | Reported Speech |
|--------------|----------------|
| "I **am** tired." | She said she **was** tired. |
| "I **will help** you." | He promised he **would help** me. |
| "I **have finished**." | She said she **had finished**. |
| "Do you **like** it?" | He asked if I **liked** it. |

## Tại sao quan trọng

- **IELTS Writing Task 2:** trích dẫn quan điểm từ nghiên cứu, chuyên gia, số đông
- **IELTS Speaking:** kể lại điều ai đó nói, khuyên, hỏi
- Là cấu trúc ngữ pháp phức tạp thể hiện Grammatical Range (Band 7+)
- Giúp viết đa dạng hơn thay vì chỉ dùng trích dẫn trực tiếp

## Backshift — Lùi thì động từ

### Quy tắc cơ bản: Thì lùi về quá khứ

| Direct Speech | Reported Speech |
|--------------|----------------|
| Present Simple | Past Simple |
| Present Continuous | Past Continuous |
| Present Perfect | Past Perfect |
| Past Simple | Past Perfect |
| will | would |
| can | could |
| may | might |
| must | had to |
| shall | would |

### Ví dụ backshift đầy đủ:

- "I **work** in London." → She said she **worked** in London.
- "I **am working** on it." → He said he **was working** on it.
- "I **have finished**." → She said she **had finished**.
- "I **finished** yesterday." → He said he **had finished** the day before.
- "I **will call** you." → She said she **would call** me.
- "I **can** help." → He said he **could** help.
- "You **must** leave." → She told me I **had to** leave.

### Khi KHÔNG cần backshift

1. **Sự thật luôn đúng** (scientific facts, general truths):
   - "The Earth **orbits** the sun." → He said the Earth **orbits** the sun. ✅
   - "Water **boils** at 100°C." → She explained that water **boils** at 100°C. ✅

2. **Tường thuật ngay lập tức** (vừa mới nói):
   - She says she **is** tired. (đang nói ngay lúc này)

3. **Dùng với "that" và thì hiện tại** (trong IELTS Writing, chấp nhận):
   - Researchers argue that technology **has** both benefits and drawbacks.

## Thay đổi đại từ và trạng từ

### Đại từ

| Direct | Reported |
|--------|---------|
| I / me / my / mine | he/she / him/her / his/her / his/hers |
| we / us / our | they / them / their |
| you / your | I/he/she, me/him/her (tùy ngữ cảnh) |

### Trạng từ thời gian và nơi chốn

| Direct | Reported |
|--------|---------|
| now | then |
| today | that day |
| yesterday | the day before / the previous day |
| tomorrow | the next day / the following day |
| this week | that week |
| last week | the week before |
| next year | the following year |
| here | there |
| this | that |
| these | those |

**Ví dụ:**

- "I will call you **tomorrow**." → She said she would call me **the next day**.
- "I finished **yesterday**." → He said he had finished **the day before**.
- "I'm working **here** **now**." → She said she was working **there** **then**.

## Câu tường thuật — Các loại câu

### 1. Tường thuật câu phát biểu (Statements)

Dùng: **say (that) / tell + O (that)**

- "I love this city." → She **said** (that) she loved that city.
- "We will attend the conference." → He **told** me (that) they would attend the conference.

> **say vs tell:**
> - **say**: say (that)... — không cần tân ngữ người
> - **tell**: tell + PERSON + (that)... — luôn cần tân ngữ người
> - ❌ She told that she was tired.
> - ✅ She **said** (that) she was tired. / She **told me** she was tired.

### 2. Tường thuật câu hỏi (Questions)

**Yes/No questions → if / whether**

```
asked + if/whether + Subject + verb (không đảo ngữ)
```

- "Do you like music?" → He **asked** if I **liked** music.
- "Are you coming?" → She **asked** whether I **was coming**.
- "Have you seen the film?" → He **asked** if I **had seen** the film.

**Wh- questions → wh-word + Subject + verb (không đảo ngữ)**

```
asked + wh-word + Subject + verb
```

- "Where do you live?" → She **asked** where I **lived**.
- "What are you doing?" → He **asked** what I **was doing**.
- "Why did you leave?" → She **asked** why I **had left**.

> **Quan trọng:** Trong tường thuật câu hỏi, **không đảo ngữ** — viết như câu thông thường.
> - ❌ She asked where **did I live**.
> - ✅ She asked where **I lived**.

### 3. Tường thuật mệnh lệnh và yêu cầu (Commands/Requests)

```
told/asked/advised/ordered + Object + to-infinitive
Phủ định: told + Object + NOT to + infinitive
```

- "Close the door!" → He **told** me **to close** the door.
- "Please sit down." → She **asked** me **to sit** down.
- "Don't be late!" → He **told** me **not to be** late.
- "You should apply for the job." → She **advised** me **to apply** for the job.

## Reporting Verbs — Động từ tường thuật

Thay vì chỉ dùng "say" và "tell", dùng đa dạng reporting verbs:

| Reporting verb | Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---------------|---------|-------|
| **claim** | claim (that) | khẳng định (ngụ ý có thể sai) |
| **argue** | argue (that) | lập luận |
| **suggest** | suggest + V-ing / suggest (that) + subj | đề xuất |
| **admit** | admit (that) / admit + V-ing | thừa nhận |
| **deny** | deny + V-ing | phủ nhận |
| **promise** | promise (that) / promise + to-inf | hứa |
| **warn** | warn + O + (not) to-inf | cảnh báo |
| **explain** | explain (that) | giải thích |
| **insist** | insist (that) | khẳng định mạnh |
| **complain** | complain (that) | phàn nàn |
| **confirm** | confirm (that) | xác nhận |
| **refuse** | refuse + to-inf | từ chối |

**Ví dụ:**

- "I didn't steal anything." → He **denied stealing** anything.
- "You should exercise more." → The doctor **advised** him **to exercise** more.
- "I will definitely finish it." → She **promised to finish** it / **promised (that) she would finish** it.
- "This approach is wrong." → They **argued (that)** this approach **was** wrong.

## Lỗi thường gặp

### Lỗi 1: Quên backshift thì

- ❌ He said he **is** tired.
- ✅ He said he **was** tired.
- ❌ She told me she **will** come.
- ✅ She told me she **would** come.

### Lỗi 2: Đảo ngữ trong tường thuật câu hỏi

- ❌ She asked where **did I live**.
- ✅ She asked where **I lived**.
- ❌ He asked what **was I doing**.
- ✅ He asked what **I was doing**.

### Lỗi 3: Dùng "say" thay vì "tell" khi có tân ngữ

- ❌ She said me that she was tired.
- ✅ She **told me** (that) she was tired. / She **said** (that) she was tired.

### Lỗi 4: Quên thay đổi đại từ và trạng từ

- ❌ He said "I will come **tomorrow**." → He said he would come **tomorrow**.
- ✅ He said he would come **the next day**.

### Lỗi 5: Dùng cấu trúc sai với "suggest"

- ❌ She suggested me to try again.
- ✅ She **suggested (that) I try** again. / She **suggested trying** again.

## Ứng dụng trong IELTS

### Writing Task 2 — Trích dẫn quan điểm và nghiên cứu:

- Research **suggests** that regular exercise **can reduce** the risk of chronic disease.
- Many economists **argue** that free trade **benefits** all participating nations.
- Critics **claim** that social media **has damaged** interpersonal relationships.
- The report **concludes** that governments **need** to invest more in renewable energy.
- Experts **warn** that if action **is not taken** immediately, the consequences **will be** severe.

### Speaking Part 2/3 — Kể lại điều ai nói:

- "My teacher once **told** me **to read** widely in English — best advice I ever received."
- "A friend of mine **suggested** that I **try** speaking English every day, even to myself."
- "My parents always **said** that education **was** the most important investment."

### Academic Reporting — Passive structures:

- It **is argued** that technology has transformed society.
- It **has been suggested** that diet plays a major role.
- **It is widely believed** that climate change poses the greatest threat.

## Bài tập luyện

### Chuyển sang Reported Speech

1. "I am very happy today," she said.
2. "Did you finish your homework?" the teacher asked me.
3. "Don't touch the equipment," the supervisor told us.
4. "We will announce the results tomorrow," the company said.
5. "Why did you leave early?" he asked her.

### Sửa lỗi

1. She said me that she was tired.
2. He asked where did she live.
3. They told that they would return next week.
4. She suggested me to apply for the job.
5. He said he will help me the next day.

### Đáp án

**Reported Speech:**
1. She said (that) she was very happy that day.
2. The teacher asked me if I had finished my homework.
3. The supervisor told us not to touch the equipment.
4. The company said (that) they would announce the results the next/following day.
5. He asked her why she had left early.

**Sửa lỗi:**
1. She **told me** (that) she was tired. / She **said** (that) she was tired.
2. He asked where **she lived**. (không đảo ngữ)
3. They **said** (that) they would return the following week. (say không cần tân ngữ người)
4. She **suggested that I apply** for the job. / She **suggested applying** for the job.
5. He said he **would** help me **the next day**. (backshift + thay trạng từ)

## Tóm tắt nhanh

| Loại câu | Reporting verb | Cấu trúc |
|---------|---------------|---------|
| Phát biểu | say / tell | said (that) + backshifted clause |
| Yes/No question | ask | asked if/whether + S + V |
| Wh- question | ask | asked + wh-word + S + V |
| Mệnh lệnh | tell / order | told + O + (not) to-inf |
| Lời khuyên | advise | advised + O + (not) to-inf |
| Đề xuất | suggest | suggested + V-ing / that S (should) V |

**Backshift nhanh:**
- Present Simple → Past Simple
- will → would | can → could | must → had to
- Present Perfect → Past Perfect
- Past Simple → Past Perfect

**Quy tắc vàng:**
- **say** (that): không cần tân ngữ người
- **tell** + NGƯỜI + (that): luôn cần tân ngữ người
- Câu hỏi tường thuật: **KHÔNG đảo ngữ**
- Sự thật khoa học: **có thể không backshift**
