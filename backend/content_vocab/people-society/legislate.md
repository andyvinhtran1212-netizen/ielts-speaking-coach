**Ban hành luật, lập pháp** — làm ra luật.

## Ví dụ IELTS Speaking Part 3

> Governments should **legislate** against harmful pollution.

> New **legislation** is needed to keep up with rapid technological change.

## Ghi nhớ nhanh

*legislate* (v) → *legislation* (n, luật) → *legislative* (adj) → *legislature* (n). *legislate against/for* = ra luật chống/ủng hộ.
