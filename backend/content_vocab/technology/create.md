**Tạo ra, sáng tạo** — làm cho một thứ gì đó tồn tại hoặc ra đời.

## Ví dụ IELTS Speaking Part 3

> Automation may **create** as many jobs as it destroys, provided that workers are retrained for new roles.

> A supportive environment allows children to develop the **creativity** they need to solve unfamiliar problems.

## Ghi nhớ nhanh

Họ từ: **create** (v) → *creation* (n, -tion) → *creative* (adj, -ive) → *creativity* (n, -ity) → *creator* (n). Câu word-form hay hỏi: *the ____ of jobs* → **creation**; *a ____ solution* → **creative**.
